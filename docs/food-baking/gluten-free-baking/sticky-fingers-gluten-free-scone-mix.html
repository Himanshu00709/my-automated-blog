How to Use Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix

Using Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix is incredibly straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Start by preheating your oven to the temperature specified on the package, usually around 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Mix: In a large mixing bowl, combine the scone mix with cold butter (cut into small pieces) and your choice of milk or a milk alternative. Mix until the dough comes together.
  3. Add Mix-Ins: If you’re adding any mix-ins like dried fruit or chocolate chips, fold them into the dough at this stage.
  4. Shape the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and shape it into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference. Cut the dough into wedges or squares.
  5. Bake: Place the scones on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for the recommended time, usually around 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve and Enjoy: Allow the scones to cool slightly before serving. They’re best enjoyed warm with a cup of tea or coffee.

FAQs About Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix

1. Is Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix is made with gluten-free ingredients and is safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. However, always check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings if you have severe sensitivities.

2. Can I use dairy-free alternatives with this mix?

Absolutely! You can use dairy-free butter and milk alternatives like almond milk, soy milk, or oat milk to make the scones dairy-free as well.

3. How should I store the scones after baking?

Once baked, allow the scones to cool completely before storing them in an airtight container. They can be kept at room temperature for up to 2 days or refrigerated for up to a week. You can also freeze the scones for longer storage.

4. Can I add my own flavors to the mix?

Yes, the mix is highly versatile. You can add your favorite mix-ins like dried fruits, nuts, chocolate chips, or even savory ingredients like cheese and herbs to create unique scone flavors.
