Why Gluten-Free Matters at Airports

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and long-term complications. Airports, with their fast-paced environment and limited food options, can be particularly challenging for gluten-free travelers. However, Minneapolis Airport has recognized the importance of catering to diverse dietary needs, ensuring that gluten-free travelers can enjoy a stress-free dining experience.

Tips for Safe Gluten-Free Eating at MSP

While Minneapolis Airport offers several gluten-free options, it's essential to take precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips to help you navigate gluten-free dining at MSP:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Always inform the staff about your gluten-free requirements. Many restaurants are willing to accommodate special requests, but clear communication is key.
  • Check Labels: When purchasing packaged snacks, always check the labels for gluten-free certification or ingredients that may contain gluten.
  • Choose Naturally Gluten-Free Foods: Opt for foods that are naturally gluten-free, such as fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins, to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Be Aware of Cross-Contamination: Even if a restaurant offers gluten-free options, cross-contamination can occur in shared kitchens. Ask about their preparation methods to ensure your food is safe.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Minneapolis Airport

1. Are there dedicated gluten-free restaurants at MSP?

2. Can I find gluten-free snacks at MSP?

Yes, Minneapolis Airport has several stores and kiosks that sell gluten-free snacks. Look for packaged items like gluten-free granola bars, nuts, and chips at convenience stores and newsstands throughout the airport.

3. Is it safe to eat gluten-free at fast-food restaurants in MSP?

Some fast-food restaurants at MSP, like Shake Shack and McDonald's, offer gluten-free options. However, it's important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff and inquire about their preparation methods to avoid cross-contamination.

4. Are there gluten-free options for breakfast at MSP?

Yes, several restaurants at MSP offer gluten-free breakfast options. French Meadow Bakery & Cafe, for example, serves gluten-free bread, muffins, and breakfast bowls.
