Best Gluten-Free Options at Disney Springs

Disney Springs is a vibrant shopping, dining, and entertainment district located at Walt Disney World Resort in Florida. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating dining options can be challenging. However, Disney Springs offers a variety of gluten-free options that cater to different tastes and preferences. In this article, we’ll explore the best gluten-free dining options at Disney Springs, ensuring you can enjoy your visit without compromising your dietary needs.

Why Disney Springs is a Gluten-Free Haven

Disney Springs is known for its diverse culinary offerings, and many restaurants here are well-equipped to handle gluten-free requests. The chefs are trained to understand cross-contamination risks and can often modify dishes to meet gluten-free requirements. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a sit-down meal, Disney Springs has something for everyone.

Erin McKenna's Bakery NYC

Erin McKenna's Bakery NYC is a must-visit for anyone with gluten sensitivities. This bakery is entirely gluten-free and vegan, offering a wide range of sweet treats, including cupcakes, cookies, donuts, and more. The bakery uses high-quality ingredients to ensure that every bite is delicious and safe for those with gluten intolerance.

Blaze Fast-Fire'd Pizza

Blaze Fast-Fire'd Pizza is a great option for those craving a quick and customizable meal. They offer a gluten-free crust that is both crispy and flavorful. You can choose from a variety of toppings to create your perfect pizza, all while ensuring it remains gluten-free. The staff is knowledgeable about cross-contamination, making it a safe choice for gluten-free diners.

The Polite Pig

The Polite Pig is a modern barbecue restaurant that offers a variety of gluten-free options. From smoked meats to fresh salads, there’s something for everyone. They also provide gluten-free buns and sauces, ensuring that you can enjoy a full barbecue experience without worrying about gluten.

Raglan Road Irish Pub

Raglan Road Irish Pub is a popular spot for those looking for a hearty meal. They offer gluten-free fish and chips, which are cooked in a dedicated gluten-free fryer to avoid cross-contamination. The pub also has a variety of other gluten-free options, making it a great choice for a sit-down meal.

Chef Art Smith's Homecomin'

Chef Art Smith's Homecomin' is known for its Southern comfort food, and they offer a gluten-free fried chicken option that is cooked in a dedicated fryer. The restaurant also provides gluten-free sides and desserts, ensuring a complete and satisfying meal for gluten-free diners.

FAQs

Are there gluten-free options at Disney Springs?

Yes, Disney Springs offers a variety of gluten-free options at many of its restaurants. From dedicated gluten-free bakeries to restaurants with gluten-free menus, there are plenty of choices for those with gluten sensitivities.

How do I know if a restaurant is safe for gluten-free dining?

Most restaurants at Disney Springs are well-trained in handling gluten-free requests. It’s always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary needs and ask about their procedures to avoid cross-contamination.

Can I find gluten-free desserts at Disney Springs?

Absolutely! Erin McKenna's Bakery NYC is a dedicated gluten-free and vegan bakery that offers a wide range of desserts, including cupcakes, cookies, and donuts.

Conclusion

Disney Springs is a fantastic destination for gluten-free dining, offering a wide range of options that cater to different tastes and preferences. Whether you're in the mood for a sweet treat, a quick pizza, or a hearty meal, you’ll find plenty of gluten-free options to enjoy. With knowledgeable staff and dedicated gluten-free practices, you can dine with confidence and focus on making magical memories at Disney Springs.
