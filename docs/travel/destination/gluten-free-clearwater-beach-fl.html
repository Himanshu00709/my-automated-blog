Tips for Dining Gluten-Free in Clearwater Beach

When dining out gluten-free, it’s important to take precautions to avoid cross-contamination and ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips for a successful gluten-free dining experience in Clearwater Beach:

  • Communicate with Staff: Always inform your server about your gluten-free needs. Ask questions about ingredients and preparation methods.
  • Check Menus Online: Many restaurants in Clearwater Beach offer gluten-free menus online. Review them before your visit to ensure they meet your dietary requirements.
  • Look for Gluten-Free Certifications: Some restaurants have gluten-free certifications or are part of gluten-free dining programs, which can provide additional assurance.
  • Be Cautious with Fried Foods: Cross-contamination is common in fryers. Opt for grilled or baked options instead.
  • Bring Your Own Snacks: If you’re unsure about the options available, bring your own gluten-free snacks to tide you over.
