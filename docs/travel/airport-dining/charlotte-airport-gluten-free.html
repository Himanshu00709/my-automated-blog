Charlotte Airport Gluten-Free Guide: A Comprehensive Resource

Traveling with dietary restrictions can be challenging, especially when navigating busy airports. For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding safe and delicious gluten-free options is a top priority. Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT), one of the busiest airports in the United States, has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free travelers. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about enjoying a gluten-free experience at Charlotte Airport, from dining options to tips for a stress-free journey.

Tips for Gluten-Free Travelers at Charlotte Airport

Traveling gluten-free requires a bit of planning, but with these tips, you can ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience at Charlotte Airport:

  • Research Ahead: Before your trip, check the airport's website or contact restaurants directly to confirm their gluten-free offerings.
  • Pack Snacks: Bring your own gluten-free snacks to avoid any uncertainty. Nuts, fruit, and gluten-free granola bars are great options.
  • Communicate Clearly: When ordering, clearly communicate your dietary needs to the staff. Don't hesitate to ask about cross-contamination risks.
  • Use Apps: Apps like Find Me Gluten Free can help you locate gluten-free-friendly restaurants in and around the airport.

  • Hudson News: Offers a selection of gluten-free snacks, including chips, cookies, and protein bars.
  • The Body Shop: While primarily a beauty store, they also carry gluten-free skincare products for those with sensitivities.
  • CNBC Smart Shop: Features a variety of travel-friendly gluten-free snacks and beverages.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Charlotte Airport

1. Are there dedicated gluten-free menus at Charlotte Airport?

While not all restaurants have dedicated gluten-free menus, many offer gluten-free options. It's always best to ask the staff for assistance.

2. How can I avoid cross-contamination at the airport?

To minimize the risk of cross-contamination, inform the staff about your dietary restrictions and ask about their food preparation practices. Opt for packaged snacks when in doubt.

3. Can I bring my own gluten-free food through security?

Yes, you can bring your own gluten-free food through security. Just ensure that it complies with TSA guidelines for carry-on items.

4. Are there gluten-free options for breakfast at Charlotte Airport?

Yes, several restaurants, such as Starbucks and Einstein Bros. Bagels, offer gluten-free breakfast options like gluten-free bagels and oatmeal.

Conclusion

Traveling gluten-free doesn't have to be stressful, especially at Charlotte Airport. With a growing number of gluten-free dining options, helpful staff, and a variety of retail stores offering gluten-free products, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying experience.
