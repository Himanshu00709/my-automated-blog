Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to avoid gluten in all forms, including medications and supplements.

Ingredients to Watch Out For

While many Halls cough drops are gluten-free, it is essential to be aware of ingredients that may contain gluten or be derived from gluten-containing grains. Some ingredients to watch out for include:

  • Maltodextrin: This is a common ingredient in many processed foods and medications. While it is usually derived from corn, it can sometimes be made from wheat. If the source is not specified, it is best to avoid it.
  • Modified Food Starch: This ingredient can be derived from various sources, including wheat. If the label does not specify the source, it may be best to avoid the product.
  • Natural Flavors: Natural flavors can sometimes contain gluten, depending on their source. If you are unsure, it is best to contact the manufacturer for clarification.
