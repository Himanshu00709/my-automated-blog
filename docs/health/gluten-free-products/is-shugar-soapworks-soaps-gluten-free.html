Understanding Gluten in Personal Care Products

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. While it’s commonly associated with food, gluten can also be present in personal care products like soaps, shampoos, and lotions. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even topical exposure to gluten can sometimes cause skin irritation or other reactions. This is why it’s crucial to scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes of skincare products.
