Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe immune responses. Therefore, it’s essential for these individuals to ensure that the products they consume, including cough drops, are free from gluten.

Ricola, a Swiss brand, is known for its commitment to natural ingredients. But does this commitment extend to being gluten-free? Let’s explore the details.
