Understanding Gluten

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. It is responsible for the elastic texture of dough and is commonly found in bread, pasta, and other grain-based products. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, skin rashes, and even neurological problems.

Given the potential health risks associated with gluten consumption, it's no wonder that people are cautious about its presence in various substances, including bodily fluids like semen.
