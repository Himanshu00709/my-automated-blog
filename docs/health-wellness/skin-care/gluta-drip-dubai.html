What is Gluta Drip?

Gluta Drip is an intravenous (IV) therapy that delivers high doses of glutathione directly into the bloodstream. Glutathione is a powerful antioxidant naturally produced by the body, known for its ability to detoxify, brighten skin, and boost overall health. The treatment is widely sought after for its skin-whitening effects, as it inhibits the production of melanin, the pigment responsible for skin color. Additionally, Gluta Drip is known to improve skin texture, reduce signs of aging, and enhance overall well-being.

How Does Gluta Drip Work?

The Gluta Drip works by delivering glutathione directly into the bloodstream, ensuring maximum absorption and effectiveness. When administered intravenously, glutathione bypasses the digestive system, allowing it to reach higher concentrations in the body. This method is more effective than oral supplements, which may lose potency during digestion. The treatment typically takes 30 to 60 minutes, and patients may require multiple sessions to achieve their desired results.
