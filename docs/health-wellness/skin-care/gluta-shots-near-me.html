What Are Gluta Shots?

Gluta shots are injections that contain glutathione, a powerful antioxidant naturally produced in the body. Glutathione plays a crucial role in detoxification, immune function, and the maintenance of healthy skin. When administered as a shot, glutathione is believed to be more effective than oral supplements because it bypasses the digestive system and goes directly into the bloodstream.

Glutathione is often referred to as the "master antioxidant" due to its ability to neutralize free radicals, reduce oxidative stress, and support the body's natural detoxification processes. In addition to its antioxidant properties, glutathione is also known for its skin-whitening effects, which is why it has become a popular treatment for those looking to achieve a brighter, more even complexion.
