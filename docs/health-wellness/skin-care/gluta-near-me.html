What is Glutathione?

Glutathione is a naturally occurring antioxidant found in every cell of the human body. It plays a crucial role in detoxification, immune function, and the maintenance of cellular health. In recent years, glutathione has become a popular ingredient in skincare and wellness treatments due to its ability to lighten skin, reduce oxidative stress, and promote overall health.

Glutathione treatments are available in various forms, including oral supplements, intravenous (IV) injections, and topical creams. Here are some of the key benefits:
