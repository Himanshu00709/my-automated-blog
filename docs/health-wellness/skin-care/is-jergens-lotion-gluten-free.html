Understanding Gluten in Skincare Products

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. While it’s commonly associated with food, gluten can also be present in skincare and cosmetic products. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even topical exposure to gluten can cause adverse reactions. This is why it’s essential to scrutinize the ingredients in lotions and other skincare products.

Gluten in skincare products can come from various sources, including wheat germ oil, hydrolyzed wheat protein, and other derivatives. While the risk of gluten absorption through the skin is low, those with severe sensitivities may still experience reactions. Therefore, knowing whether a product like Jergens lotion is gluten-free is vital for many consumers.
