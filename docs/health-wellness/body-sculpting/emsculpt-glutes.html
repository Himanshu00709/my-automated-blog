What is Emsculpt?

Emsculpt is a non-invasive body contouring treatment that uses High-Intensity Focused Electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology to induce powerful muscle contractions. These contractions are far more intense than what you can achieve through voluntary exercise, leading to muscle building and fat reduction. The treatment is FDA-approved and has been clinically proven to increase muscle mass and reduce fat in targeted areas.

How Does Emsculpt Work on Glutes?

When it comes to the glutes, Emsculpt works by targeting the muscles in the buttocks, including the gluteus maximus, medius, and minimus. The HIFEM technology induces supramaximal contractions, which are essentially super-intense muscle contractions that cannot be achieved through regular exercise. These contractions lead to muscle hypertrophy (growth) and fat apoptosis (cell death), resulting in a more toned and lifted appearance.

The treatment typically involves four 30-minute sessions spaced over two weeks. During each session, you will lie down while the Emsculpt device is placed on your buttocks. The device will then deliver electromagnetic energy, causing your muscles to contract. The experience is often described as a deep, intense workout, but without the physical exertion.

What to Expect During and After Treatment
