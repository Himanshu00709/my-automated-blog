What is Emsculpt Neo?

Emsculpt Neo is an advanced non-invasive body contouring treatment that uses high-intensity focused electromagnetic (HIFEM) technology and radiofrequency (RF) energy to simultaneously build muscle and reduce fat. Unlike traditional Emsculpt, which primarily focuses on muscle building, Emsculpt Neo combines two powerful technologies to deliver more comprehensive results. The HIFEM technology induces supramaximal muscle contractions, which are not achievable through voluntary exercise, while the RF energy heats the fat cells, causing them to break down and be naturally eliminated by the body.
