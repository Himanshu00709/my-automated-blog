What is Emsculpt Neo?

Emsculpt Neo is an advanced body contouring treatment that combines two powerful technologies: High-Intensity Focused Electromagnetic (HIFEM) energy and Radiofrequency (RF) heating. This dual-action approach not only builds muscle but also reduces fat, making it a comprehensive solution for body sculpting. The treatment is non-invasive, meaning there are no incisions, needles, or downtime required. Emsculpt Neo is FDA-approved and has been clinically proven to deliver significant results in muscle building and fat reduction.

How Does Emsculpt Neo Work on Glutes?

Emsculpt Neo targets the gluteal muscles, which include the gluteus maximus, gluteus medius, and gluteus minimus. These muscles are responsible for the shape, lift, and overall appearance of the buttocks. The treatment works by delivering HIFEM energy to the muscles, causing them to contract at a rate that is impossible to achieve through voluntary exercise. These supramaximal contractions lead to muscle hypertrophy, or the growth of muscle fibers, resulting in a more toned and lifted appearance.

In addition to muscle building, Emsculpt Neo also uses RF heating to target and reduce fat cells in the treatment area. The RF energy heats the fat cells, causing them to break down and be naturally eliminated by the body. This dual-action approach ensures that you not only build muscle but also reduce fat, leading to a more sculpted and defined appearance.

What to Expect During the Treatment

Emsculpt Neo treatments are typically performed in a clinical setting and take about 30 minutes per session. During the procedure, you will lie down while a specialized applicator is placed on the treatment area. The applicator delivers HIFEM energy and RF heating to the glutes, causing the muscles to contract and the fat cells to heat up. Most patients describe the sensation as a deep, intense muscle contraction, similar to the feeling of an intense workout. The treatment is generally well-tolerated, and there is no downtime required afterward.

Most patients require a series of 4-6 treatments, spaced about 5-7 days apart, to achieve optimal results. After completing the treatment series, you can expect to see a noticeable improvement in the shape, tone, and lift of your glutes. Results typically continue to improve over the following weeks as the muscles continue to grow and the fat cells are eliminated from the body.
