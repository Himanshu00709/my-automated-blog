Understanding Gluten and Its Presence in Personal Care Products

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, and rye. While it is most commonly associated with food, gluten can also be found in various personal care products, including shampoos, lotions, and body washes. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even topical exposure to gluten can sometimes cause skin reactions or other symptoms. Therefore, it is crucial to understand whether the products you use on your skin contain gluten.
