What is C4?

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and long-term health complications. As a result, many people seek out gluten-free products to avoid these adverse effects.

When it comes to supplements, the presence of gluten can be a concern, especially for those who are highly sensitive. Cross-contamination during manufacturing or the use of gluten-containing ingredients can make a product unsafe for individuals with gluten-related disorders.

Is C4 Gluten-Free?

The good news for those following a gluten-free diet is that most formulations of C4 are indeed gluten-free. Cellucor, the manufacturer of C4, has taken steps to ensure that their products meet the needs of individuals with gluten sensitivities. However, it's important to note that not all versions of C4 are created equal, and some may contain trace amounts of gluten due to cross-contamination.
