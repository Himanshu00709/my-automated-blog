Are Fairlife Shakes Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has surged, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity. What Are Fairlife Shakes?

Fairlife shakes are a line of protein-rich beverages produced by Fairlife, LLC, a subsidiary of Coca-Cola. These shakes are marketed as a convenient and nutritious option for individuals looking to increase their protein intake. They are made using ultra-filtered milk, which removes some of the lactose and increases the protein content. Fairlife shakes come in various flavors, including chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry, and are often used as meal replacements, post-workout recovery drinks, or simply as a tasty snack.

Are Fairlife Shakes Gluten-Free?

Yes, Fairlife shakes are gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Fairlife shakes do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients. This makes them a safe option for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, it’s important to note that while the shakes themselves are gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during the manufacturing process. Fairlife does not explicitly state that their products are produced in a gluten-free facility, so those with severe gluten intolerance should exercise caution.
