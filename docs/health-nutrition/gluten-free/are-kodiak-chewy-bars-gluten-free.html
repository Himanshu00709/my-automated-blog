Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues. Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Symptoms can range from digestive problems to fatigue, skin rashes, and even neurological issues. Therefore, it's crucial for individuals with these conditions to avoid gluten-containing foods.

Are Kodiak Chewy Bars Gluten-Free?

The short answer is no, Kodiak Chewy Bars are not gluten-free. The primary reason is that they are made with whole grains, including wheat, which contains gluten. While Kodiak Cakes, the company behind Kodiak Chewy Bars, offers some gluten-free products, the Chewy Bars are not among them. It's important to read the ingredient list and nutrition label carefully if you are avoiding gluten.

2. Can I eat Kodiak Chewy Bars if I have celiac disease?
