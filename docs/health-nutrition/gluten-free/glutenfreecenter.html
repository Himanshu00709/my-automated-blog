Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, consuming gluten is harmless. However, for individuals with celiac disease, gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Non-celiac gluten sensitivity and wheat allergy are other conditions that necessitate a gluten-free diet. Beyond medical reasons, some people choose to go gluten-free to improve digestion, boost energy levels, or manage weight.
