Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For people with celiac disease, consuming gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even small amounts of gluten can cause symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. Therefore, it's essential for those affected to avoid gluten entirely.

Gluten sensitivity, while not as severe as celiac disease, can also cause discomfort and health issues. As a result, many people choose to follow a gluten-free diet to improve their overall well-being.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

Even if a product is made without gluten-containing ingredients, cross-contamination can occur during manufacturing. This happens when gluten-free products come into contact with gluten-containing products or surfaces. For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, even trace amounts of gluten can be harmful.

David Sunflower Seeds are produced in facilities that may also process other products containing gluten. While the company takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, there is always a risk. If you have a severe gluten intolerance, it's advisable to contact the manufacturer directly for the most accurate information regarding their production practices.
