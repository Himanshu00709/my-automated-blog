What Are ZYNs?

ZYNs are a popular brand of nicotine pouches that offer a smoke-free, tobacco-free alternative to traditional smoking products. These pouches are placed between the gum and lip, where they release nicotine slowly, providing a satisfying experience without the need for combustion. ZYNs are known for their convenience, discretion, and variety of flavors, making them a favorite among those looking to quit smoking or reduce their tobacco intake.

Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and long-term health complications. Therefore, it's essential for these individuals to ensure that the products they use are free from gluten.

Are ZYNs Gluten-Free?

The good news for those who need to avoid gluten is that ZYNs are indeed gluten-free. The manufacturers of ZYNs have confirmed that their products do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients. This makes ZYNs a safe option for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity who are looking for a nicotine alternative.
