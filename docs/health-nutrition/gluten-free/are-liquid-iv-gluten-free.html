Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless and can be consumed without any issues. However, for individuals with celiac disease or non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to carefully scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes of the products they consume.

The product is available in several flavors, each containing a blend of electrolytes, vitamins, and minerals.

Is Liquid I.V. Gluten-Free?

The short answer is yes, Liquid I.V. is gluten-free. According to the company, all Liquid I.V. products are formulated without gluten-containing ingredients. Additionally, Liquid I.V. is certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO), which is one of the most stringent certification bodies for gluten-free products. This certification ensures that the product contains less than 10 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for individuals with celiac disease.

Manufacturing Process and Cross-Contamination

One of the key concerns for individuals with gluten sensitivities is the risk of cross-contamination during the manufacturing process. Even if a product does not contain gluten-containing ingredients, it can still be contaminated if it is produced in a facility that also processes gluten-containing products. Liquid I.V. addresses this concern by manufacturing its products in a gluten-free facility.
