In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has surged, driven by the growing awareness of celiac disease, gluten intolerance, and the general preference for healthier snack options. What Are Quest Protein Chips?

Quest Protein Chips are a high-protein, low-carb snack designed to cater to fitness enthusiasts, health-conscious individuals, and those following specific dietary regimens like keto or gluten-free diets. Manufacturing Process and Cross-Contamination

Even if the ingredients are gluten-free, cross-contamination during the manufacturing process can introduce gluten into the product. Quest Nutrition states that they follow strict manufacturing protocols to prevent cross-contamination. Their facilities are designed to minimize the risk of gluten exposure, and they regularly test their products to ensure they meet gluten-free standards.

However, it's important to note that Quest Protein Chips are not certified gluten-free by organizations like the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO). While the company asserts that their products are gluten-free, the lack of certification may be a concern for individuals with severe gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.
