In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, and the general preference for healthier dietary choices. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many individuals seek out gluten-free products to avoid these adverse effects.

Herbalife recognizes the importance of catering to diverse dietary needs, including those who require gluten-free options. The company has taken steps to ensure that many of its products are gluten-free, but it's essential to understand which products meet this criterion and how to identify them.
