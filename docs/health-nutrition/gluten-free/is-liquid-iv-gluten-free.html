What is Liquid IV?

Liquid IV is a hydration multiplier that uses Cellular Transport Technology (CTT) to deliver hydration to your bloodstream faster and more efficiently than water alone. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to carefully scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes of any product they consume.

Is Liquid IV Gluten-Free?

The short answer is yes, Liquid IV is gluten-free. According to the company, Liquid IV products do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients. Additionally, the manufacturing facilities where Liquid IV is produced are designed to prevent cross-contamination with gluten. This is an important consideration for those with celiac disease, as even trace amounts of gluten can trigger symptoms.
