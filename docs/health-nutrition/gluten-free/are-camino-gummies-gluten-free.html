Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many people seek out gluten-free alternatives to avoid these adverse effects.
