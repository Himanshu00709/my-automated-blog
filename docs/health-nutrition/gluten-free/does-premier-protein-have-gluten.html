Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and long-term health complications. As a result, many people are vigilant about avoiding gluten in their diets. This has led to a growing market for gluten-free products, including protein supplements like those offered by Premier Protein.
