Are Breath Savers Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, navigating the world of food and snacks can be a daunting task. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to skin rashes and even neurological problems. Therefore, it's crucial for these individuals to avoid gluten-containing products.

When it comes to mints and candies, the ingredients can sometimes be a bit ambiguous. While some products are clearly labeled as gluten-free, others may contain hidden sources of gluten. This is why it's essential to scrutinize the ingredient list and understand the manufacturing processes involved.

At first glance, none of these ingredients contain gluten. However, it's important to consider the possibility of cross-contamination during the manufacturing process. Cross-contamination can occur if the same equipment is used to process gluten-containing products.

Manufacturing Processes
