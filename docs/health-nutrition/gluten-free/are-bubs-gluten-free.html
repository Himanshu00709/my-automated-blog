Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, skin rashes, and even neurological symptoms. For babies and young children, ensuring a gluten-free diet is crucial if they have been diagnosed with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. This is where Bubs comes into the picture.
