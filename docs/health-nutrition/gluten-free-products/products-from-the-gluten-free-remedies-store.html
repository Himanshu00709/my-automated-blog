FAQs About Gluten-Free Products

1. What does "gluten-free" mean?

Gluten-free means that a product does not contain gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. Gluten-free products are safe for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies.

2. Are gluten-free products healthier?

Not necessarily. While gluten-free products are essential for those with gluten-related disorders, they are not inherently healthier than their gluten-containing counterparts. However, many gluten-free products are made with whole, nutrient-dense ingredients, which can offer additional health benefits.

3. Can I lose weight on a gluten-free diet?

A gluten-free diet is not a weight-loss diet. However, some people may lose weight initially because they are cutting out processed foods and refined carbohydrates. It’s important to focus on a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

4. Are gluten-free products more expensive?

Gluten-free products can be more expensive due to the cost of sourcing and producing gluten-free ingredients. However, the Gluten-Free Remedies Store offers competitive pricing and often has discounts and promotions to make gluten-free living more affordable.
