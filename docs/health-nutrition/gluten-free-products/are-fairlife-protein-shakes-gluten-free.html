Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many people seek out gluten-free products to avoid these adverse effects. When it comes to protein shakes, it’s essential to scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes to ensure they meet gluten-free standards.
