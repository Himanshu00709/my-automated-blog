Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Even for those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, gluten can cause discomfort, bloating, and other digestive problems. As a result, many people seek out gluten-free products to avoid these adverse effects.

When it comes to protein shakes, the presence of gluten can be a concern, especially if the product contains additives, flavorings, or thickeners derived from gluten-containing grains. Therefore, it's crucial to examine the ingredients and manufacturing processes of any protein shake to determine its gluten-free status.
