FAQs About Premier Protein Shakes

Are Premier Protein Shakes really gluten-free?

Yes, Premier Protein Shakes are certified gluten-free, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

How much protein is in each Premier Protein Shake?

Each Premier Protein Shake contains 30 grams of protein, making it an excellent source of high-quality protein.

Can I use Premier Protein Shakes as a meal replacement?

While Premier Protein Shakes are nutrient-dense, they are best used as a supplement or snack rather than a complete meal replacement. However, they can be part of a balanced diet.

Are Premier Protein Shakes suitable for vegetarians?

Premier Protein Shakes contain milk-based proteins, so they are not suitable for vegans. However, they can be consumed by lacto-vegetarians.

Do Premier Protein Shakes contain artificial sweeteners?

Premier Protein Shakes use natural flavors and sweeteners like sucralose to enhance taste without adding extra sugar.
