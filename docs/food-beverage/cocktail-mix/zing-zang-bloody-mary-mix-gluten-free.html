How to Make the Perfect Gluten-Free Bloody Mary with Zing Zang

Creating a delicious gluten-free Bloody Mary with Zing Zang is simple and straightforward. Here’s a basic recipe to get you started:

Ingredient Quantity
Zing Zang Bloody Mary Mix 4 oz
Vodka (gluten-free) 1.5 oz
Ice As needed
Celery stalk (for garnish) 1
Lemon or lime wedge (for garnish) 1

Instructions:

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice.
  2. Pour the Zing Zang Bloody Mary Mix over the ice.
  3. Add the gluten-free vodka and stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with a celery stalk and a lemon or lime wedge.
  5. Enjoy your gluten-free Bloody Mary!
