What is Powerade?

Powerade is a sports drink manufactured by The Coca-Cola Company. It is designed to replenish electrolytes, carbohydrates, and fluids lost during physical activity. Powerade comes in various flavors and formulations, including Powerade Zero, which is a low-calorie option. The drink is widely consumed by athletes, gym-goers, and anyone looking to stay hydrated during intense physical activity.

Is Powerade Gluten-Free?

Yes, Powerade is gluten-free. The Coca-Cola Company has confirmed that all Powerade products, including Powerade Zero, are free from gluten-containing ingredients. This makes Powerade a safe option for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, it’s always a good practice to check the label for any potential changes in ingredients or manufacturing processes.
