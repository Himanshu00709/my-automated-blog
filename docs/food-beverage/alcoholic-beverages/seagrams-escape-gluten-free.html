What Makes Seagram's Escape Gluten-Free?

Seagram's Escape is a line of flavored malt beverages that are crafted to be gluten-free. The key to its gluten-free status lies in the ingredients used during the brewing process. Unlike traditional beers that are made from barley, wheat, or rye—all of which contain gluten—Seagram's Escape uses a base of malted barley that has been processed to remove gluten. This ensures that the final product contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold set by the FDA for a product to be labeled as gluten-free.
