What is Ashwood Hard Root Beer?

Ashwood Hard Root Beer is a craft alcoholic beverage that blends the rich, creamy flavors of traditional root beer with a hint of alcohol. It’s a unique offering that caters to those who enjoy the taste of root beer but are looking for something a bit more adult. What sets Ashwood apart is its commitment to quality and inclusivity—it’s gluten-free, making it a great option for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have become increasingly popular, not just among those with celiac disease but also among individuals who experience gluten sensitivity or simply prefer to avoid gluten for health reasons. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause digestive issues and other health problems for some people. By offering a gluten-free option, Ashwood Hard Root Beer ensures that more people can enjoy their product without worry.
