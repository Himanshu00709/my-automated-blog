In recent years, hard seltzers have taken the beverage industry by storm, offering a refreshing and low-calorie alternative to traditional alcoholic drinks. What is Press Seltzer?

Press Seltzer is a premium hard seltzer brand known for its clean, crisp taste and high-quality ingredients. Unlike many other hard seltzers that use malted barley or other gluten-containing grains, Press Seltzer is crafted with a base of fermented cane sugar. This not only gives it a distinct flavor profile but also makes it a safer option for those avoiding gluten.

Press Seltzer offers a variety of flavors, including Blackberry Hibiscus, Grapefruit, Lime, and Pomegranate Ginger. Each flavor is carefully crafted to provide a balanced taste that appeals to a wide range of palates.

Is Press Seltzer Gluten-Free?

The short answer is yes, Press Seltzer is gluten-free. The brand takes pride in using a fermentation process that avoids gluten-containing grains entirely. Instead, Press Seltzer is made from fermented cane sugar, which is naturally gluten-free. This makes it a suitable choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

To ensure the safety of their products, Press Seltzer undergoes rigorous testing to confirm that their beverages contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold set by the FDA for gluten-free labeling. This level is considered safe for most people with gluten-related disorders.
