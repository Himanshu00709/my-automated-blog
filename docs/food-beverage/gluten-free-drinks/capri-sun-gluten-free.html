What is Gluten and Why Does It Matter?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. Therefore, it’s crucial for individuals with these conditions to ensure that the foods and beverages they consume are gluten-free.

Is Capri Sun Gluten-Free?

The good news is that Capri Sun is indeed gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Capri Sun beverages do not contain any gluten ingredients. This makes them a safe option for those who need to avoid gluten in their diet. However, it’s always a good idea to check the label for any changes in ingredients or manufacturing processes, as formulations can change over time.
