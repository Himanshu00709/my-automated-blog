What is Jarritos Hard Soda?

Jarritos Hard Soda is a line of alcoholic beverages that takes inspiration from the classic Jarritos soft drinks. Known for their bold and authentic flavors, Jarritos has been a staple in Mexican households for decades. The hard soda version retains the same vibrant taste but adds a 4.5% alcohol by volume (ABV), making it a perfect choice for those looking to enjoy a refreshing drink with a bit of a buzz.

The hard soda comes in a variety of flavors, including Lime, Guava, Tamarind, and Mandarin. Each flavor is crafted to deliver a burst of authentic taste, reminiscent of the original Jarritos sodas. The addition of alcohol doesn't overpower the natural flavors, ensuring that each sip is as delightful as the last.

Why Gluten-Free Matters

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding gluten-free alcoholic beverages can be a challenge. Many beers and malt beverages contain gluten, which can cause adverse reactions in those who are intolerant. Jarritos Hard Soda is made with a gluten-free base, ensuring that it's safe for those who need to avoid gluten in their diet.

Gluten-free beverages are not just a necessity for some; they are also a preference for many who choose to avoid gluten for health or lifestyle reasons. By offering a gluten-free option, Jarritos Hard Soda caters to a broader audience, making it an inclusive choice for social gatherings and celebrations.
