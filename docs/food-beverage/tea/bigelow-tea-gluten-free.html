Bigelow Tea: A Gluten-Free Delight

For tea enthusiasts who are mindful of their dietary restrictions, finding a brand that aligns with gluten-free needs can be a challenge. Bigelow Tea, a household name in the tea industry, has emerged as a trusted choice for those seeking gluten-free options. With a rich history dating back to 1945, Bigelow Tea has consistently delivered high-quality, flavorful teas that cater to a variety of dietary preferences. In this article, we’ll explore why Bigelow Tea is a gluten-free favorite, its product range, and how it ensures safety for gluten-sensitive individuals.

What Makes Bigelow Tea Gluten-Free?

Bigelow Tea takes pride in offering a wide range of gluten-free teas. The company adheres to strict manufacturing processes to ensure that their teas are free from gluten contamination. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Bigelow Tea’s commitment to quality means that their teas are crafted in facilities that prioritize cross-contamination prevention, making them a safe choice for gluten-free consumers.

Bigelow Tea’s Gluten-Free Certification

Bigelow Tea is certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO), one of the most reputable certification bodies in the industry. This certification ensures that Bigelow Tea products contain less than 10 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for most individuals with gluten intolerance. This rigorous certification process involves regular testing and facility inspections, giving consumers peace of mind.

FAQs About Bigelow Tea and Gluten-Free Diets

1. Is all Bigelow Tea gluten-free?

Yes, all Bigelow Tea products are gluten-free and certified by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO).

2. Can individuals with celiac disease safely consume Bigelow Tea?

Absolutely. Bigelow Tea’s gluten-free certification ensures that their products contain less than 10 ppm of gluten, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease.

3. Are Bigelow Tea bags gluten-free?

Yes, both the tea and the tea bags are gluten-free. Bigelow Tea uses materials that are free from gluten contamination.

4. Does Bigelow Tea test for gluten?

Yes, Bigelow Tea conducts regular testing to ensure their products meet gluten-free standards and are safe for consumption.
