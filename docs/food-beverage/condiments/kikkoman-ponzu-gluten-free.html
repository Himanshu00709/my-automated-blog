What is Kikkoman Ponzu Gluten-Free?

Kikkoman Ponzu Gluten-Free is a Japanese citrus-based sauce that combines the tangy zest of yuzu, sudachi, and other citrus fruits with the umami richness of soy sauce. Traditionally, ponzu sauce is used as a dipping sauce for sashimi, shabu-shabu, and other Japanese dishes. However, Kikkoman's gluten-free version opens up a world of possibilities for those who need to avoid gluten without compromising on flavor.

The sauce is crafted using Kikkoman's proprietary brewing process, which ensures a consistent and high-quality product. The gluten-free aspect is achieved by using tamari, a type of soy sauce that is naturally gluten-free, making it suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.
