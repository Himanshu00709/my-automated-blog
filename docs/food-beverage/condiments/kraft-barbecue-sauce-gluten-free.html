What Makes a Barbecue Sauce Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For a barbecue sauce to be considered gluten-free, it must not contain any ingredients derived from these grains. Additionally, the sauce must be processed in a facility that avoids cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. Many barbecue sauces use ingredients like soy sauce or malt vinegar, which often contain gluten, so it’s essential to read labels carefully.

Kraft has been proactive in labeling their products clearly, making it easier for consumers to identify gluten-free options. However, it’s always a good idea to double-check the ingredients list and look for any gluten-free certifications on the packaging.
