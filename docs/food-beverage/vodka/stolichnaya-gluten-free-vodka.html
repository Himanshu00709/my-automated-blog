However, the distillation process used by Stolichnaya removes the gluten proteins, making the final product gluten-free. According to the FDA, distilled spirits made from gluten-containing grains can be labeled as gluten-free if the distillation process removes the gluten proteins to below 20 parts per million (ppm), which is the threshold considered safe for most people with celiac disease.

Stolichnaya's distillation process involves multiple steps to ensure the purity and quality of the vodka. The grains are fermented, and the resulting liquid is distilled multiple times to remove impurities, including gluten proteins.
