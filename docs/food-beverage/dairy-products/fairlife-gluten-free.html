What is Fairlife?

Fairlife is a premium dairy brand known for its innovative approach to milk production. The company utilizes a unique filtration process that separates milk into its core components—water, lactose, protein, fat, vitamins, and minerals. This process allows Fairlife to create products that are not only lactose-free but also higher in protein and calcium while being lower in sugar compared to traditional milk. Fairlife's commitment to quality and nutrition has made it a favorite among health-conscious consumers.
