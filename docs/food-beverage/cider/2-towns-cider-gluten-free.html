2 Towns Ciderhouse: A Gluten-Free Delight

When it comes to gluten-free beverages, 2 Towns Ciderhouse stands out as a beacon of quality and innovation. Known for their handcrafted ciders, this Oregon-based company has been delighting taste buds since 2010. With a commitment to using locally sourced ingredients and sustainable practices, 2 Towns Ciderhouse has become a favorite among cider enthusiasts and those with gluten sensitivities alike. In this article, we’ll explore what makes 2 Towns Ciderhouse a top choice for gluten-free cider lovers, delve into their unique offerings, and answer some frequently asked questions.

The Gluten-Free Advantage

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding a safe and enjoyable beverage can be a challenge. Traditional beers are off-limits due to their gluten content, but ciders, which are made from fermented apples, are naturally gluten-free. 2 Towns Ciderhouse takes this a step further by ensuring that their ciders are crafted in a gluten-free environment, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. This dedication to quality and safety makes their ciders a reliable choice for those who need to avoid gluten.

What Makes 2 Towns Ciderhouse Unique?

2 Towns Ciderhouse prides itself on its commitment to quality and innovation. Here are some key factors that set them apart:

Locally Sourced Ingredients

2 Towns Ciderhouse sources its apples from local orchards in the Pacific Northwest, ensuring that only the freshest and highest-quality fruit is used in their ciders. This not only supports local farmers but also results in a superior product that reflects the unique terroir of the region.

Creative Flavor Profiles

One of the hallmarks of 2 Towns Ciderhouse is their willingness to experiment with flavors. From classic apple ciders to more adventurous blends featuring fruits like marionberries, cherries, and even hops, there’s something for everyone. Their seasonal offerings, such as the popular "BrightCider" and "Cosmic Crisp," keep customers coming back for more.

Sustainability

2 Towns Ciderhouse is committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly practices throughout their production process. They recycle apple pomace (the leftover pulp from pressing apples) into compost, and their cans are made from recycled materials. This dedication to the environment resonates with consumers who are increasingly conscious of their ecological footprint.

FAQs About 2 Towns Ciderhouse

Is 2 Towns Ciderhouse completely gluten-free?

Yes, all ciders produced by 2 Towns Ciderhouse are gluten-free. They are made from apples and other fruits, with no gluten-containing ingredients. Additionally, the ciders are crafted in a gluten-free environment to prevent cross-contamination.

Where can I buy 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders?

2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders are widely available in the Pacific Northwest and can be found in many grocery stores, liquor stores, and specialty beverage shops. They also offer online ordering and shipping to select states through their website.

Are 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders vegan?

Yes, all 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders are vegan. They do not use any animal products or by-products in their production process.

What is the shelf life of 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders?

Most 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders have a shelf life of about one year when stored in a cool, dark place. However, for the best flavor, it’s recommended to consume them within six months of purchase.
