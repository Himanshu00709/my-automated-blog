Understanding Gluten in Alcoholic Beverages

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. It is commonly present in beers and malt beverages, which are made from these grains. However, many alcoholic beverages, such as wine, cider, and certain spirits, are naturally gluten-free. Spiked Simply Lemonade falls into the category of gluten-free alcoholic beverages because it does not contain any gluten-containing grains in its production process.
