What is Celsius?

Celsius is a fitness drink brand that markets itself as a healthier alternative to traditional energy drinks. Celsius drinks come in a variety of flavors and are marketed as being free from sugar, preservatives, and artificial flavors. But what about gluten?

Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to ensure that the products they consume are gluten-free.

Are Celsius Drinks Gluten-Free?

The short answer is yes, Celsius drinks are gluten-free. According to the company, all Celsius products are formulated without gluten-containing ingredients and are manufactured in facilities that adhere to strict gluten-free protocols. This makes Celsius a safe option for individuals who need to avoid gluten.
