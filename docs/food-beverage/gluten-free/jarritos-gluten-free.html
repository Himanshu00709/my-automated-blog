What Makes Jarritos Gluten-Free?

Jarritos is a Mexican soda brand that has been delighting taste buds since 1950. One of the key reasons for its popularity is its commitment to using natural ingredients. Unlike many sodas that rely on artificial flavors and additives, Jarritos is made with real sugar and natural fruit flavors. This not only enhances the taste but also ensures that the beverages are free from gluten-containing ingredients.

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Jarritos’ gluten-free status is a result of its careful ingredient selection and production process. The brand avoids using any gluten-containing additives, making it a safe choice for those who need to avoid gluten.
