Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to ensure that the foods they consume are free from gluten.

What is Hershey's Cocoa?

Hershey's Cocoa is a popular brand of unsweetened cocoa powder that has been a staple in American kitchens for over a century. It is commonly used in baking, cooking, and beverage preparation. The product is known for its rich chocolate flavor and versatility in various recipes.
