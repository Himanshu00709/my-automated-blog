What Are Downeast Cider Donuts?

Downeast Cider Donuts are a delightful treat made with real apple cider, giving them a unique, tangy sweetness that sets them apart from traditional donuts. What makes them even more special is that they are gluten-free, catering to those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

The donuts are crafted using a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour and almond flour, which provide a light and airy texture.
