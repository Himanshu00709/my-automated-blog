What is Estrella Gluten-Free?

Estrella Gluten-Free is a brand that specializes in producing high-quality, gluten-free snacks. Originating from Sweden, Estrella has a long-standing reputation for crafting delicious and nutritious snacks that cater to a variety of dietary needs. The brand's gluten-free line is particularly popular, offering a range of products that are free from gluten, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

The History of Estrella

Estrella was founded in 1946 in Sweden and has since grown to become one of the leading snack brands in Scandinavia. The company initially focused on traditional snacks but recognized the growing need for gluten-free options as awareness of gluten-related health issues increased. In response, Estrella launched its gluten-free product line, which has been well-received by consumers worldwide.
