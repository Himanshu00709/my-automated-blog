How to Use Pioneer Gluten-Free Gravy Mix

One of the standout features of Pioneer Gluten-Free Gravy Mix is its ease of use. Here’s a step-by-step guide to preparing the perfect gravy:

  1. Measure the Mix: Start by measuring out the desired amount of gravy mix. Typically, 1 tablespoon of mix is used per cup of water.
  2. Combine with Water: In a saucepan, combine the gravy mix with cold water. Stir until the mixture is smooth and free of lumps.
  3. Heat and Stir: Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent clumping.
  4. Simmer: Once the mixture reaches a boil, reduce the heat and let it simmer for 1-2 minutes, or until the gravy reaches your desired thickness.
  5. Serve: Remove from heat and serve immediately over your favorite dishes.

For added flavor, you can also use broth instead of water or incorporate pan drippings from roasted meats.
