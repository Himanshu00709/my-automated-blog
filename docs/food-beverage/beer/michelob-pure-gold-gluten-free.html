What Makes Michelob Pure Gold Unique?

Michelob Pure Gold is not just another gluten-free beer; it’s a meticulously crafted lager that combines quality ingredients with a commitment to health and sustainability. Here’s what sets it apart:

1. Gluten-Free Certification

Michelob Pure Gold is certified gluten-free by the Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG). This certification ensures that the beer contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The beer is brewed using organic grains, primarily organic rice and organic barley, which are carefully processed to remove gluten.

2. Organic Ingredients

One of the standout features of Michelob Pure Gold is its use of organic ingredients. The beer is made with organic grains, which are grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. This not only makes the beer healthier but also more environmentally friendly. The organic certification ensures that the ingredients meet strict standards, providing a cleaner and more natural product.

3. Low Calorie and Low Carb

For those watching their calorie and carbohydrate intake, Michelob Pure Gold is an excellent choice. With only 85 calories and 2.5 grams of carbohydrates per 12-ounce serving, it’s a lighter option compared to many traditional beers. This makes it a great choice for those who want to enjoy a beer without the guilt.

4. Sustainability

Michelob Pure Gold is not just good for you; it’s good for the planet. The beer is brewed using renewable electricity and is part of Anheuser-Busch’s commitment to sustainability. The packaging is also eco-friendly, with the use of recycled materials and a focus on reducing waste.
