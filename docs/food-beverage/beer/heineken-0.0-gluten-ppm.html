Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and other grains. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health problems. The gluten content in food and beverages is typically measured in parts per million (PPM), with lower PPM values indicating less gluten.
