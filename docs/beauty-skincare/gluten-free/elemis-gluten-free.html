What Does Gluten-Free Skincare Mean?

Gluten-free skincare refers to products that do not contain gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. While gluten is primarily a concern for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, some individuals prefer to avoid it in their skincare products due to concerns about skin irritation or absorption. ELEMIS ensures that its gluten-free products are safe for those with gluten-related disorders, providing peace of mind and effective skincare solutions.
