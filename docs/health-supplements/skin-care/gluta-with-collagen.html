What is Glutathione (Gluta)?

Glutathione, often referred to as Gluta, is a naturally occurring antioxidant found in every cell of the human body. It plays a crucial role in detoxification, immune function, and the neutralization of free radicals. Gluta is composed of three amino acids: cysteine, glutamine, and glycine. Its primary function is to protect cells from oxidative stress, which can lead to premature aging and various health issues.

In the context of beauty and wellness, Gluta is renowned for its skin-lightening properties. It inhibits the production of melanin, the pigment responsible for skin color, thereby promoting a brighter and more even complexion. Additionally, Gluta helps to reduce the appearance of dark spots, acne scars, and hyperpigmentation, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products and supplements.

What is Collagen?

Collagen is the most abundant protein in the human body, making up a significant portion of our skin, hair, nails, bones, and connective tissues. It provides structural support, elasticity, and strength to these tissues. As we age, our body's natural collagen production declines, leading to wrinkles, sagging skin, and joint pain.

Supplementing with collagen can help to counteract these effects by replenishing the body's collagen levels. Collagen supplements are derived from various sources, including bovine, marine, and chicken. They are available in different forms, such as powders, capsules, and drinks, making it easy to incorporate into your daily routine.
