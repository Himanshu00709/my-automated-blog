What is Gluta Niacinamide?

Gluta Niacinamide is a skincare ingredient that combines two potent compounds: glutathione and niacinamide. Glutathione is a naturally occurring antioxidant in the body, known for its ability to combat oxidative stress and promote skin brightening. Niacinamide, also known as Vitamin B3, is a versatile ingredient that addresses a wide range of skin concerns, including hyperpigmentation, acne, and aging. Together, these ingredients work synergistically to deliver a multitude of benefits for the skin.

The Science Behind Gluta Niacinamide

To understand why Gluta Niacinamide is so effective, it’s important to break down the individual components and their mechanisms of action.

Glutathione: The Master Antioxidant

Glutathione is often referred to as the "master antioxidant" due to its critical role in neutralizing free radicals and protecting cells from damage. In skincare, glutathione is prized for its ability to inhibit the production of melanin, the pigment responsible for skin color. By reducing melanin synthesis, glutathione helps to lighten dark spots, even out skin tone, and impart a radiant glow.

Niacinamide: The Multi-Tasking Marvel

Niacinamide is a water-soluble vitamin that plays a key role in maintaining the skin’s barrier function. It helps to reduce inflammation, regulate sebum production, and improve the appearance of enlarged pores. Additionally, niacinamide has been shown to boost collagen production, which can help to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Its ability to address multiple skin concerns makes it a favorite among dermatologists and skincare enthusiasts.
