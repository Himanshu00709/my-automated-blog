Understanding Gluten and Dairy Intolerance

Gluten and dairy are two of the most common dietary triggers for digestive discomfort. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause issues for those with celiac disease or non-celiac gluten sensitivity. Similarly, lactose, the sugar found in dairy products, can be difficult to digest for individuals with lactose intolerance. Symptoms of these intolerances can range from bloating and gas to more severe gastrointestinal distress.

For those who experience these symptoms, avoiding gluten and dairy entirely may not always be practical or desirable.
