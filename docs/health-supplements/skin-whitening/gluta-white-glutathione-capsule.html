What is Glutathione?

Glutathione is a naturally occurring antioxidant found in every cell of the human body. It plays a crucial role in detoxification, immune function, and the maintenance of cellular health. Composed of three amino acids—cysteine, glutamine, and glycine—glutathione helps neutralize free radicals, reduce oxidative stress, and support the body's natural detoxification processes.

In recent years, glutathione has gained significant attention for its skin-lightening properties. By inhibiting the production of melanin, the pigment responsible for skin color, glutathione can help achieve a brighter, more even complexion.
