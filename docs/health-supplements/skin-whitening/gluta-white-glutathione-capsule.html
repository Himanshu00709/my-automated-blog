What is Glutathione?

Glutathione is a naturally occurring antioxidant found in the human body. It plays a crucial role in detoxification, immune function, and the maintenance of cellular health. Composed of three amino acids—cysteine, glutamine, and glycine—glutathione helps neutralize free radicals, reduce oxidative stress, and support the body's natural detoxification processes.
