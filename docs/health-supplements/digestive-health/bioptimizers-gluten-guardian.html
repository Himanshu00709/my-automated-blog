How Does Gluten Guardian Work?

Gluten Guardian works by targeting the proteins found in gluten, such as gliadin and glutenin, which are often difficult for the body to digest. The supplement contains a proprietary enzyme blend, including DPP-IV (Dipeptidyl Peptidase-IV), which is specifically designed to break down these proteins into smaller, more manageable peptides. This process helps to reduce the inflammatory response that gluten can trigger in sensitive individuals.

Additionally, Gluten Guardian includes probiotics that support gut health, promoting a balanced microbiome. A healthy gut is essential for proper digestion and immune function, and the probiotics in Gluten Guardian help to maintain this balance, further reducing the risk of gluten-related discomfort.
