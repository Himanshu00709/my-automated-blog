1. Glute Ham Raise

The glute ham raise is one of the most effective exercises for targeting the hamstrings and glutes. To perform this exercise:

  1. Position the glider under your feet while kneeling on a soft surface.
  2. Engage your core and slowly lower your torso towards the ground, keeping your back straight.
  3. Use your hamstrings and glutes to pull yourself back up to the starting position.

2. Sliding Leg Curl

The sliding leg curl is another excellent exercise for the hamstrings. Here's how to do it:

  1. Lie on your back with the glider under your heels.
  2. Lift your hips off the ground, forming a straight line from your shoulders to your knees.
  3. Slide your heels towards your glutes, then slowly extend your legs back to the starting position.

3. Mountain Climbers

Mountain climbers are a great way to incorporate cardio into your workout while also engaging the core and lower body. To perform mountain climbers with a glider:

  1. Place the glider under one foot and assume a plank position.
  2. Quickly alternate sliding your knees towards your chest, as if you're climbing a mountain.
  3. Maintain a steady pace and keep your core engaged throughout the exercise.
