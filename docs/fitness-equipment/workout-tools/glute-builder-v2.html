How to Use the Glute Builder V2

Using the Glute Builder V2 is straightforward, but proper form is essential to maximize its benefits. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting started:

  1. Set Up: Attach the resistance bands to the Glute Builder V2 and adjust the tension according to your fitness level. Ensure the equipment is stable before you begin.
  2. Positioning: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and place the Glute Builder V2 around your thighs, just above your knees. Keep your back straight and engage your core.
  3. Movement: Slowly push your knees outward against the resistance bands, focusing on squeezing your glutes. Hold the position for a few seconds, then return to the starting position.
