How to Use the Precor Glute Builder

Using the Precor Glute Builder is straightforward, but it’s important to use proper form to maximize the benefits and avoid injury. Here’s a step-by-step guide to using the machine:

  1. Adjust the Machine: Start by adjusting the seat and footplates to fit your body. Make sure your knees are aligned with the pivot point of the machine.
  2. Position Your Feet: Place your feet on the footplates, ensuring that they are shoulder-width apart. Your heels should be slightly elevated to engage the glutes more effectively.
  3. Engage Your Core: Before starting the movement, engage your core muscles to stabilize your body.
  4. Perform the Movement: Push through your heels to extend your legs, focusing on squeezing your glutes at the top of the movement. Slowly return to the starting position.
  5. Repeat: Perform 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions, depending on your fitness level.
