How to Use the Cap Barbell Glute Ham Developer

To get the most out of your Cap Barbell GHD, it’s important to use proper form and technique. Here’s a step-by-step guide to performing a glute-ham raise, one of the most popular exercises on this machine:

  1. Adjust the Footplate: Start by adjusting the footplate to a comfortable position that aligns with your height.
  2. Position Your Body: Kneel on the padded platform with your feet secured under the footplate. Your knees should be positioned just behind the edge of the pad.
  3. Engage Your Core: Tighten your core muscles to stabilize your body and maintain proper alignment.
  4. Lower Your Torso: Slowly lower your torso toward the floor, keeping your back straight and your hamstrings engaged.
  5. Raise Your Body: Use your hamstrings and glutes to pull your body back up to the starting position. Avoid using momentum to complete the movement.
  6. Repeat: Perform 8-12 repetitions, focusing on controlled movements and full range of motion.
