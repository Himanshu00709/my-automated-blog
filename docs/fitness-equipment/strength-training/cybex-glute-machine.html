What is the Cybex Glute Machine?

The Cybex Glute Machine is a specialized piece of gym equipment designed to target the gluteal muscles, which include the gluteus maximus, gluteus medius, and gluteus minimus. Unlike traditional leg press machines or squat racks, the Cybex Glute Machine is engineered to isolate the glutes, reducing the involvement of other muscle groups such as the quadriceps and hamstrings. This isolation allows for more focused and effective glute training, making it an excellent choice for those looking to build stronger, more defined glutes.
