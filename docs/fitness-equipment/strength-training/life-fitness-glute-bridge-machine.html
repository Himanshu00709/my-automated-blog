How to Use the Life Fitness Glute Bridge Machine

Using the Life Fitness Glute Bridge Machine is straightforward, but it's essential to maintain proper form to maximize the benefits and avoid injury. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Adjust the Machine: Start by adjusting the machine to fit your body. Set the resistance level and range of motion according to your fitness level and goals.
  2. Position Yourself: Sit on the machine with your back against the padded support and your feet flat on the footplate. Ensure your knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.
  3. Engage Your Core: Tighten your core muscles to stabilize your body and protect your lower back.
  4. Perform the Glute Bridge: Push through your heels to lift your hips towards the ceiling, squeezing your glutes at the top of the movement. Lower your hips back down to the starting position in a controlled manner.
  5. Repeat: Perform the desired number of repetitions, maintaining proper form throughout the exercise.
