What is Hard Mountain Dew?

Hard Mountain Dew is an alcoholic version of the classic Mountain Dew soda. It is produced by PepsiCo in collaboration with the Boston Beer Company, the makers of Samuel Adams. This beverage is designed to appeal to those who enjoy the taste of Mountain Dew but are looking for an alcoholic twist. Hard Mountain Dew comes in a variety of flavors, including Original, Baja Blast, and Black Cherry, each offering a unique taste experience.

Is Hard Mountain Dew Gluten-Free?

Yes, Hard Mountain Dew is gluten-free. The beverage is made using a malt base that is derived from gluten-free ingredients. This makes it a suitable option for individuals with celiac disease or those who follow a gluten-free diet. However, it is always important to check the label and verify the gluten-free status of any product, as formulations can change over time.
