What is Corona Refresca?

Corona Refresca is a line of flavored malt beverages produced by Grupo Modelo, the same company behind the iconic Corona Extra beer. Unlike traditional beers, Corona Refresca is made with a malt base that is naturally gluten-free, making it suitable for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. The drink is available in three tropical flavors: Guava Lime, Passionfruit Lime, and Coconut Lime. Each flavor is designed to evoke the feeling of a beachside vacation, with a light, refreshing taste that’s perfect for warm weather.
