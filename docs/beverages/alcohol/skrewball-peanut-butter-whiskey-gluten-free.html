What is Skrewball Peanut Butter Whiskey?

Skrewball Peanut Butter Whiskey is a flavored whiskey that combines the smooth, rich taste of peanut butter with the warmth of traditional whiskey. Created by Steven Yeng and his wife Brittany in San Diego, California, Skrewball was born out of a love for peanut butter and a desire to create something truly unique in the spirits industry. The result is a whiskey that is both indulgent and versatile, perfect for sipping neat, on the rocks, or as a key ingredient in creative cocktails.

Why Skrewball is Gluten-Free

One of the most appealing aspects of Skrewball Peanut Butter Whiskey is its gluten-free status. Traditional whiskeys are often made from grains like barley, rye, or wheat, which contain gluten. However, Skrewball is crafted using a base of corn and other gluten-free ingredients, ensuring that it is safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. This makes Skrewball a rare find in the whiskey world, where gluten-free options are limited.
