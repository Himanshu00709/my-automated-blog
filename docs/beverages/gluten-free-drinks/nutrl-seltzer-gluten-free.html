What is Nutrl Seltzer?

Nutrl Seltzer is a brand of hard seltzer that has gained popularity for its clean, crisp taste and gluten-free formulation. Hard seltzers are alcoholic beverages made by fermenting sugar and combining it with carbonated water and natural flavors. Nutrl Seltzer stands out in the crowded market of hard seltzers due to its commitment to using high-quality ingredients and maintaining a gluten-free profile.

Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free alternatives to avoid these adverse effects.

Nutrl Seltzer is crafted to be gluten-free, making it a safe and enjoyable option for those who need to avoid gluten. The brand ensures that its products are free from gluten-containing ingredients and are produced in a facility that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination.
