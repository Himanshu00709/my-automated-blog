Is Mike's Hard Lemonade Gluten-Free?

One of the most common questions about Mike's Hard Lemonade is whether it is gluten-free. The answer is yes, Mike's Hard Lemonade is considered gluten-free. The beverage is made from a malt base, which typically contains gluten. However, the gluten content in Mike's Hard Lemonade is reduced to less than 20 parts per million (ppm), which is the threshold set by the FDA for a product to be labeled as gluten-free. This makes it safe for most people with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease to consume.
