The Origins of the Arnold Palmer

The Arnold Palmer drink was named after the legendary golfer who popularized the combination of iced tea and lemonade. The simplicity of the drink, combined with its refreshing taste, made it a hit among golfers and non-golfers alike. Fast forward to today, and the Arnold Palmer has evolved into a versatile beverage that can be enjoyed in various forms, including the spiked version.
