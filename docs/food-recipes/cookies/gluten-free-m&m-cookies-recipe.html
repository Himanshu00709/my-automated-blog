Gluten-Free M&M Cookies Recipe

If you're looking for a delicious treat that caters to gluten-free diets, you've come to the right place. These gluten-free M&M cookies are not only easy to make but also incredibly tasty. Whether you're hosting a party, preparing a snack for your kids, or simply craving something sweet, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds. Let's dive into the details of how to make these delightful cookies.

Ingredients

Before we get started, let's gather all the ingredients you'll need. Here's a list of everything required to make these gluten-free M&M cookies:

Ingredient Quantity
Gluten-free all-purpose flour 2 cups
Baking soda 1/2 teaspoon
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Unsalted butter (softened) 1/2 cup
Granulated sugar 1/2 cup
Brown sugar 1/2 cup
Egg 1 large
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
M&M candies 1 cup

Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let's move on to the step-by-step instructions to make these gluten-free M&M cookies:

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your oven is at the right temperature when you're ready to bake the cookies.

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the gluten-free all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. This helps to evenly distribute the leavening agent and salt throughout the flour, ensuring a consistent texture in your cookies.

Step 3: Cream Butter and Sugars

In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened unsalted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. This process incorporates air into the butter, which helps to create a tender cookie.

Step 4: Add Egg and Vanilla

Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined. The egg acts as a binder, while the vanilla extract adds a rich flavor to the cookies.

Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can lead to tough cookies.

Step 6: Fold in M&M Candies

Gently fold in the M&M candies until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough. This ensures that each cookie has a colorful and tasty surprise in every bite.

Step 7: Scoop and Bake

Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

Step 8: Cool and Enjoy

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, enjoy your delicious gluten-free M&M cookies!

FAQs

Can I use regular flour instead of gluten-free flour?

Yes, you can use regular all-purpose flour if you don't need the cookies to be gluten-free. However, if you or someone you're baking for has a gluten intolerance or celiac disease, it's important to stick with gluten-free flour.

Can I use a different type of candy instead of M&Ms?

Absolutely! You can substitute M&Ms with other candies like chocolate chips, Reese's Pieces, or even chopped nuts. Just make sure the candies you choose are gluten-free if needed.

How should I store these cookies?

Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week. You can also freeze the dough or baked cookies for longer storage. If freezing the dough, scoop it into balls and freeze on a baking sheet before transferring to a freezer bag. Bake from frozen, adding a couple of extra minutes to the baking time.

Can I make these cookies vegan?

Yes, you can make these cookies vegan by substituting the butter with a plant-based alternative and using a flax egg (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water) instead of a regular egg. Make sure to use vegan-friendly M&Ms or another type of candy.

Conclusion

These gluten-free M&M cookies are a delightful treat that everyone can enjoy, regardless of dietary restrictions. With their soft and chewy texture, combined with the colorful and sweet M&M candies, they are sure to be a hit at any gathering or as a special treat for yourself. The recipe is straightforward and easy to follow, making it perfect for both novice and experienced bakers. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to bake a batch of these irresistible cookies. Happy baking!
