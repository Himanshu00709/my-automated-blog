Gluten-Free Dairy-Free Pumpkin Bars: A Delicious Fall Treat

As the leaves change color and the air turns crisp, there's nothing quite like the comforting flavors of fall. Pumpkin is a quintessential ingredient during this season, and what better way to enjoy it than in a delicious, gluten-free, and dairy-free pumpkin bar? These bars are not only a treat for your taste buds but also cater to those with dietary restrictions, making them a perfect dessert for everyone to enjoy.

Why Choose Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free?

Gluten-free and dairy-free diets have gained popularity for various reasons, including food intolerances, allergies, and personal health choices. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause digestive issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Similarly, dairy products can be problematic for individuals with lactose intolerance or a dairy allergy. By opting for gluten-free and dairy-free recipes, you can create inclusive dishes that everyone can enjoy without compromising on flavor.

Ingredients You'll Need

To make these delectable pumpkin bars, you'll need the following ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Gluten-free flour blend 1 ½ cups
Almond flour ½ cup
Pumpkin puree 1 cup
Coconut sugar ¾ cup
Maple syrup ¼ cup
Coconut oil (melted) ⅓ cup
Flaxseed meal (for egg substitute) 1 tbsp + 3 tbsp water
Pumpkin pie spice 1 tsp
Baking powder 1 tsp
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Salt ¼ tsp

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create your gluten-free dairy-free pumpkin bars:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare the Flax Egg: In a small bowl, mix 1 tablespoon of flaxseed meal with 3 tablespoons of water. Let it sit for 5 minutes to thicken.
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour blend, almond flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, combine the pumpkin puree, coconut sugar, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and the prepared flax egg.
  5. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool and Serve: Allow the bars to cool completely before cutting them into squares. Serve and enjoy!

Nutritional Benefits

These gluten-free dairy-free pumpkin bars are not only delicious but also packed with nutritional benefits. Pumpkin is rich in vitamins A and C, which are essential for immune health and skin health. Almond flour provides a good source of healthy fats and protein, while flaxseed meal adds a boost of omega-3 fatty acids. By using coconut sugar and maple syrup, you're opting for natural sweeteners that have a lower glycemic index compared to refined sugar.

FAQs

Can I use a different flour blend?

Yes, you can use any gluten-free flour blend that you prefer. Just make sure it’s a 1:1 substitute for regular flour to maintain the right texture.

Can I make these bars nut-free?

Absolutely! You can replace the almond flour with an additional ½ cup of gluten-free flour blend or use a nut-free alternative like sunflower seed flour.

How should I store these pumpkin bars?

Store the bars in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week. You can also freeze them for up to 3 months.
