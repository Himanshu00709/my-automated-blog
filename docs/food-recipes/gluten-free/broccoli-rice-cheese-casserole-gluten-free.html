Broccoli Rice Cheese Casserole: A Gluten-Free Delight

When it comes to comfort food, few dishes can rival the creamy, cheesy goodness of a casserole. But for those who follow a gluten-free diet, finding a satisfying casserole recipe can be a challenge. Enter the Broccoli Rice Cheese Casserole—a gluten-free dish that doesn’t compromise on flavor or texture. This hearty, wholesome casserole is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or even meal prep. In this article, we’ll dive into the details of this delicious recipe, explore its health benefits, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Why Choose a Gluten-Free Casserole?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity in recent years, not only among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity but also among individuals seeking healthier eating habits. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause digestive issues and inflammation in some people. By opting for gluten-free recipes like this Broccoli Rice Cheese Casserole, you can enjoy a comforting meal without the worry of gluten-related discomfort.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this gluten-free casserole, you’ll need the following ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Gluten-free rice 2 cups (cooked)
Broccoli florets 3 cups
Shredded cheddar cheese 2 cups
Gluten-free cream of mushroom soup 1 can (10.5 oz)
Milk (or dairy-free alternative) 1 cup
Butter (or dairy-free alternative) 2 tablespoons
Garlic powder 1 teaspoon
Onion powder 1 teaspoon
Salt and pepper To taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create your gluten-free Broccoli Rice Cheese Casserole:

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the rice: Prepare 2 cups of gluten-free rice according to the package instructions. Set aside.
  3. Steam the broccoli: Steam the broccoli florets until they are tender but still crisp. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Prepare the sauce: In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free cream of mushroom soup, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Combine ingredients: Add the cooked rice, steamed broccoli, and 1 ½ cups of shredded cheddar cheese to the sauce mixture. Stir until everything is evenly coated.
  6. Transfer to baking dish: Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  7. Top with cheese: Sprinkle the remaining ½ cup of shredded cheddar cheese on top of the casserole.
  8. Bake: Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve: Let the casserole cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make this casserole dairy-free?

Yes! Simply substitute the cheddar cheese with a dairy-free alternative and use a dairy-free cream of mushroom soup. You can also replace the milk with almond, soy, or oat milk.

How long does this casserole last in the fridge?

This casserole can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Can I freeze this casserole?

Absolutely! Allow the casserole to cool completely, then wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and aluminum foil. It can be frozen for up to 2 months. Thaw in the refrigerator overnight before reheating.

What can I serve with this casserole?

This casserole pairs well with a fresh green salad, roasted vegetables, or a side of gluten-free bread. It’s a versatile dish that complements many meals.

With its easy preparation and wholesome ingredients, it’s a dish that everyone can enjoy. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to savor every bite of this gluten-free delight!
