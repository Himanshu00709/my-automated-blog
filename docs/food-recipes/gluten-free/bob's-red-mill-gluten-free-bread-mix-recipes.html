Basic Recipe: Classic Gluten-Free Sandwich Bread

Let's start with a basic recipe that will yield a soft, fluffy loaf perfect for sandwiches. This recipe is straightforward and requires minimal effort, making it ideal for everyday baking.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Bread Mix 1 package
Eggs 3 large
Water 1 1/2 cups
Vegetable Oil 1/4 cup
Apple Cider Vinegar 1 tablespoon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread mix, eggs, water, oil, and apple cider vinegar. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

Advanced Recipe: Gluten-Free Artisan Bread

If you're looking to elevate your gluten-free baking game, this artisan bread recipe is for you. With a crispy crust and a soft, chewy interior, this bread is perfect for serving with soups, salads, or simply slathered with butter.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Bread Mix 1 package
Eggs 3 large
Water 1 1/2 cups
Olive Oil 1/4 cup
Apple Cider Vinegar 1 tablespoon
Cornmeal (for dusting) 1 tablespoon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C). Dust a baking sheet with cornmeal and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread mix, eggs, water, olive oil, and apple cider vinegar. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the dough into a round loaf and place it on the prepared baking sheet. Use a sharp knife to make a few shallow slashes on the top of the loaf.
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the bread sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  5. Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving.

FAQs About Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Bread Mix

1. Is Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Bread Mix suitable for vegans?

No, the mix contains eggs, so it is not suitable for vegans. However, you can experiment with egg substitutes like flax eggs or chia eggs to make it vegan-friendly.

2. Can I add additional ingredients to the mix?

Absolutely! You can customize your bread by adding ingredients like nuts, seeds, dried fruits, or herbs. Just be sure to adjust the baking time accordingly.

3. How should I store the bread?

Store the bread in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, wrap the bread tightly in plastic wrap and freeze for up to 3 months.

4. Can I use this mix to make other baked goods?

While the mix is specifically designed for bread, you can experiment with using it for other baked goods like muffins or rolls. Keep in mind that the texture and rise may vary.
