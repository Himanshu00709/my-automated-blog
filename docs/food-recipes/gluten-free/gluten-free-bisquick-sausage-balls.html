Gluten-Free Bisquick Sausage Balls: A Delicious and Easy Recipe

If you're looking for a crowd-pleasing appetizer or snack that's both gluten-free and incredibly easy to make, gluten-free Bisquick sausage balls are the perfect choice. These bite-sized treats are a staple at parties, holiday gatherings, and even as a quick snack. They combine the savory flavor of sausage with the convenience of gluten-free Bisquick, making them a hit for anyone, whether they follow a gluten-free diet or not.

What Are Gluten-Free Bisquick Sausage Balls?

Gluten-free Bisquick sausage balls are a variation of the classic sausage ball recipe, which traditionally uses regular Bisquick baking mix. However, for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the gluten-free version of Bisquick is a game-changer. These sausage balls are made by combining gluten-free Bisquick, ground sausage, and cheese, then baking them to golden perfection. The result is a savory, cheesy, and slightly crispy snack that's hard to resist.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Bisquick?

Gluten-free Bisquick is a versatile baking mix that allows those with gluten intolerances to enjoy their favorite recipes without compromise. It's made with a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour and potato starch, and is fortified with essential vitamins and minerals. Using gluten-free Bisquick ensures that your sausage balls are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, while still delivering the same great taste and texture as the original recipe.

Ingredients You'll Need

To make gluten-free Bisquick sausage balls, you'll need the following ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Gluten-Free Bisquick 2 cups
Ground Sausage 1 pound
Shredded Cheddar Cheese 2 cups
Milk 1/2 cup
Garlic Powder 1 teaspoon
Onion Powder 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Black Pepper 1/4 teaspoon

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Preheat Your Oven

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). This ensures that your sausage balls will bake evenly and achieve that perfect golden-brown color.

Step 2: Combine the Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free Bisquick, ground sausage, shredded cheddar cheese, milk, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Use your hands or a stand mixer to mix the ingredients until they are well combined. The mixture should be slightly sticky but hold together when formed into balls.

Step 3: Form the Sausage Balls

Using a tablespoon or a small cookie scoop, portion out the mixture and roll it into 1-inch balls. Place the balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving about an inch of space between each ball to allow for even baking.

Step 4: Bake to Perfection

Bake the sausage balls in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. You can check for doneness by cutting one open to ensure the sausage is fully cooked.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Once the sausage balls are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving. They can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature, making them a versatile option for any occasion.

Tips for Perfect Gluten-Free Sausage Balls

  • Use High-Quality Sausage: The flavor of your sausage balls largely depends on the quality of the sausage you use. Opt for a high-quality, flavorful sausage to ensure the best results.
  • Don't Overmix: Overmixing the dough can result in tough sausage balls. Mix just until the ingredients are combined.
  • Experiment with Cheese: While cheddar cheese is the classic choice, you can experiment with other types of cheese, such as pepper jack or mozzarella, for a different flavor profile.
  • Make Ahead: You can prepare the sausage balls ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator or freezer until you're ready to bake them. This makes them a convenient option for busy days.

FAQs

Can I use a different type of sausage?

Yes, you can use any type of ground sausage you prefer, such as spicy sausage, turkey sausage, or even plant-based sausage for a vegetarian option.

Can I freeze gluten-free Bisquick sausage balls?

Absolutely! You can freeze the unbaked sausage balls on a baking sheet, then transfer them to a freezer-safe bag once they're frozen. When you're ready to bake them, simply place them on a baking sheet and bake as directed, adding a few extra minutes to the cooking time if needed.

What can I serve with gluten-free sausage balls?

Gluten-free sausage balls are delicious on their own, but they can also be served with dipping sauces such as marinara, ranch, or honey mustard for added flavor.

Can I make these sausage balls dairy-free?

Yes, you can substitute the cheddar cheese with a dairy-free cheese alternative and use a non-dairy milk, such as almond or oat milk, to make the recipe dairy-free.

With the convenience of gluten-free Bisquick, you can enjoy this classic recipe without worrying about gluten.
