Why Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have gained immense popularity for a variety of reasons. For some, it’s a medical necessity due to conditions like celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For others, it’s a lifestyle choice aimed at improving digestion, reducing inflammation, or simply exploring new culinary horizons. Whatever the reason, the challenge has always been finding gluten-free recipes that don’t compromise on taste or texture. This is where Half Baked Harvest shines.

Tieghan Gerard’s approach to gluten-free cooking is rooted in simplicity and creativity. She doesn’t rely on overly processed gluten-free substitutes but instead focuses on whole, natural ingredients that are inherently gluten-free. From hearty grain bowls to decadent desserts, her recipes prove that gluten-free eating can be both satisfying and indulgent.
