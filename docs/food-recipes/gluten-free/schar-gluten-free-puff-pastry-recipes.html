Delicious Recipes Using Schar Gluten-Free Puff Pastry

1. Gluten-Free Spinach and Feta Pastries

These savory pastries are perfect for a quick snack or a light meal. The combination of spinach and feta cheese is a classic, and the flaky puff pastry adds a delightful crunch.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Schar Gluten-Free Puff Pastry 1 sheet
Fresh spinach 200g
Feta cheese 100g
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves
Salt and pepper To taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Thaw the puff pastry according to the package instructions.
  3. In a pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the garlic until fragrant. Add the spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Crumble the feta cheese into the spinach mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Roll out the puff pastry and cut it into squares. Place a spoonful of the spinach and feta mixture in the center of each square.
  6. Fold the pastry over the filling to form triangles and press the edges to seal.
  7. Place the pastries on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.

2. Gluten-Free Apple Turnovers

These sweet turnovers are a delightful treat that can be enjoyed any time of the day. The combination of tender apples and flaky pastry is simply irresistible.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Schar Gluten-Free Puff Pastry 1 sheet
Apples 2 medium
Sugar 50g
Cinnamon 1 tsp
Lemon juice 1 tbsp
Egg 1 (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Thaw the puff pastry according to the package instructions.
  3. Peel and dice the apples, then toss them with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice.
  4. Roll out the puff pastry and cut it into squares. Place a spoonful of the apple mixture in the center of each square.
  5. Fold the pastry over the filling to form triangles and press the edges to seal.
  6. Brush the pastries with beaten egg for a golden finish.
  7. Place the turnovers on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.

3. How should I store Schar Gluten-Free Puff Pastry?
