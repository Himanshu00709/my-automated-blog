Gluten-Free Salisbury Steak Recipe

If you're looking for a comforting, hearty meal that's both delicious and gluten-free, you've come to the right place. Salisbury steak is a classic dish that brings back memories of home-cooked meals. Traditionally made with breadcrumbs, this recipe has been adapted to be gluten-free without sacrificing any of the flavor or texture. Whether you're following a gluten-free diet or simply want to try something new, this gluten-free Salisbury steak recipe is sure to become a family favorite.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Salisbury Steak?

Salisbury steak is a dish that typically consists of ground beef patties mixed with breadcrumbs and seasonings, served with a rich gravy. For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, traditional Salisbury steak is off the menu due to the breadcrumbs and flour used in the gravy. However, with a few simple substitutions, you can enjoy this classic dish without worrying about gluten. This recipe uses gluten-free breadcrumbs and a gluten-free flour blend to create a dish that's just as satisfying as the original.

Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to make gluten-free Salisbury steak:

Ingredient Quantity
Ground beef 1 pound
Gluten-free breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
Onion (finely chopped) 1/2 cup
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Egg 1
Worcestershire sauce (gluten-free) 1 tablespoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Beef broth (gluten-free) 2 cups
Gluten-free flour blend 2 tablespoons
Ketchup (gluten-free) 2 tablespoons
Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon
Fresh parsley (chopped) For garnish

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Steak Patties

In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, gluten-free breadcrumbs, chopped onion, minced garlic, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix until all ingredients are well incorporated. Shape the mixture into 4 oval-shaped patties, about 1/2 inch thick.

Step 2: Cook the Patties

Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the patties and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are browned and cooked through. Remove the patties from the skillet and set aside.

Step 3: Make the Gravy

In the same skillet, add the gluten-free flour blend and whisk it into the remaining oil and juices. Cook for about 1 minute to remove the raw flour taste. Gradually add the beef broth, whisking constantly to prevent lumps. Stir in the ketchup and Dijon mustard. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the gravy thickens, about 5 minutes.

Step 4: Combine and Serve

Return the cooked patties to the skillet, spooning the gravy over them. Reduce the heat to low and let the patties simmer in the gravy for an additional 5 minutes. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Gluten-free Salisbury steak pairs wonderfully with mashed potatoes, steamed vegetables, or a fresh green salad. For a complete gluten-free meal, consider serving it with gluten-free dinner rolls or rice.

FAQs

Can I use a different type of meat?

Yes, you can substitute ground beef with ground turkey, chicken, or even a plant-based ground meat alternative. Just be sure to adjust the cooking time as needed.

What can I use instead of gluten-free breadcrumbs?

If you don’t have gluten-free breadcrumbs, you can use crushed gluten-free crackers or even almond flour as a substitute.

Can I make this recipe ahead of time?

Absolutely! You can prepare the patties and gravy ahead of time and store them separately in the refrigerator. When ready to serve, simply reheat the patties in the gravy on the stovetop.

Is Worcestershire sauce gluten-free?

Not all Worcestershire sauces are gluten-free, so be sure to check the label. There are several brands that offer gluten-free versions, such as Lea & Perrins.

Conclusion

With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow instructions, you can create a meal that’s both satisfying and safe for those with gluten sensitivities. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or your family, this dish is sure to be a hit. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your ingredients, and get ready to enjoy a delicious, gluten-free Salisbury steak dinner!
