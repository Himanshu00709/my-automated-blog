How to Enjoy Jovial Gluten-Free Dairy-Free Mac and Cheese

One of the best things about Jovial’s mac and cheese is its versatility. While it’s delicious on its own, there are countless ways to customize it to suit your taste preferences. Here are a few ideas:

1. Add Vegetables

Boost the nutritional value of your mac and cheese by adding steamed or roasted vegetables. Broccoli, spinach, and peas are great options that pair well with the creamy cheese sauce.

2. Incorporate Protein

For a more filling meal, consider adding a source of protein. Grilled chicken, tofu, or even beans can be mixed in to create a balanced dish.

3. Spice It Up

If you enjoy a bit of heat, try adding some chili flakes, hot sauce, or a dash of cayenne pepper to your mac and cheese. This will give it an extra kick and elevate the flavors.

4. Top It Off

For added texture and flavor, consider topping your mac and cheese with breadcrumbs, crushed nuts, or even a sprinkle of fresh herbs like parsley or chives.
