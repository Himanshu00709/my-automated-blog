Gluten-Free Cinnamon Bread Machine Recipe

Gluten-free baking has become increasingly popular, and for good reason. Whether you have celiac disease, a gluten intolerance, or simply want to explore healthier baking options, gluten-free recipes can be both delicious and satisfying. One such recipe that has gained a lot of attention is gluten-free cinnamon bread made in a bread machine. This recipe is not only easy to prepare but also yields a soft, flavorful loaf that’s perfect for breakfast or a snack. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to create this delightful treat, along with tips, FAQs, and more.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Cinnamon Bread?

Gluten-free cinnamon bread is a fantastic alternative to traditional cinnamon bread, especially for those who need to avoid gluten. The combination of warm cinnamon and sweet bread is a classic flavor that’s hard to resist. By using a bread machine, you can simplify the process and ensure consistent results every time. Plus, gluten-free flours like almond flour, rice flour, or a gluten-free flour blend can add unique textures and flavors that make this bread stand out.

Ingredients You’ll Need

Before you start, it’s important to gather all the necessary ingredients. Here’s a list of what you’ll need to make gluten-free cinnamon bread in a bread machine:

Ingredient Quantity
Gluten-free flour blend 2 1/2 cups
Xanthan gum (if not included in flour blend) 1 tsp
Sugar 1/4 cup
Salt 1/2 tsp
Active dry yeast 2 1/4 tsp
Warm water 1 cup
Eggs 2 large
Unsalted butter (melted) 1/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Cinnamon 1 tbsp
Brown sugar (for filling) 1/4 cup

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you have all your ingredients ready, let’s dive into the step-by-step process of making gluten-free cinnamon bread in a bread machine.

Step 1: Prepare the Bread Machine

Start by ensuring your bread machine is clean and ready to use. If your machine has a gluten-free setting, select that option. If not, the basic or sweet bread setting will work just fine.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour blend, xanthan gum (if needed), sugar, and salt. Mix these ingredients thoroughly to ensure even distribution.

Step 3: Add Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the warm water, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Make sure the water is warm but not hot, as extreme temperatures can kill the yeast.

Step 4: Combine Ingredients in the Bread Machine

Pour the wet ingredients into the bread machine pan first, followed by the dry ingredients. Make a small well in the center of the dry ingredients and add the yeast. This helps to activate the yeast properly.

Step 5: Add the Cinnamon Filling

In a small bowl, mix the cinnamon and brown sugar. Once the bread machine has mixed the dough for a few minutes, pause the cycle and add the cinnamon-sugar mixture. This will create a swirl effect in the bread.

Step 6: Bake the Bread

Close the lid of the bread machine and let it do the rest of the work. The machine will mix, knead, rise, and bake the bread. Once the cycle is complete, carefully remove the bread from the machine and let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.

Tips for Perfect Gluten-Free Cinnamon Bread

Here are some tips to ensure your gluten-free cinnamon bread turns out perfectly every time:

  • Use a Gluten-Free Flour Blend: Not all gluten-free flours are created equal. Choose a high-quality blend that’s specifically designed for baking.
  • Check the Yeast: Make sure your yeast is fresh and active. Old yeast can result in a dense loaf.
  • Don’t Overmix: Overmixing can lead to a tough texture. Let the bread machine handle the mixing and kneading.
  • Let It Cool: Allow the bread to cool completely before slicing. This helps to set the structure and makes it easier to cut.

FAQs

Can I use a different type of flour?

Yes, you can experiment with different gluten-free flours like almond flour, coconut flour, or oat flour. However, keep in mind that the texture and flavor may vary.

Can I make this recipe without a bread machine?

Absolutely! You can make this recipe by hand or using a stand mixer. Just follow the same steps, but you’ll need to knead the dough and let it rise before baking in a conventional oven.

How should I store the bread?

Store the bread in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, you can freeze the bread for up to 3 months.

Can I add nuts or raisins?

Yes, you can add nuts, raisins, or other mix-ins to the dough. Just be sure to add them during the mixing process so they’re evenly distributed.

Conclusion

Gluten-free cinnamon bread made in a bread machine is a delightful and easy-to-make treat that’s perfect for anyone following a gluten-free diet. With the right ingredients and a few simple steps, you can enjoy a warm, flavorful loaf that’s perfect for breakfast, snacks, or even dessert. Whether you’re new to gluten-free baking or a seasoned pro, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your household. Happy baking!
