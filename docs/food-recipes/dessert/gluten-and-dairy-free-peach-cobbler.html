Gluten and Dairy-Free Peach Cobbler: A Delicious and Healthy Dessert

Peach cobbler is a classic dessert that brings warmth and comfort to any table. However, for those with dietary restrictions, enjoying this treat can be a challenge. Enter the gluten and dairy-free peach cobbler—a delightful alternative that doesn’t compromise on flavor or texture. Whether you’re gluten-intolerant, lactose-intolerant, or simply looking for a healthier dessert option, this recipe is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Why Choose Gluten and Dairy-Free?

Gluten and dairy are common allergens that can cause discomfort for many people. Gluten, found in wheat, barley, and rye, can trigger symptoms in those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Dairy, on the other hand, can cause digestive issues for those who are lactose intolerant or have a dairy allergy. By opting for gluten and dairy-free recipes, you can enjoy your favorite dishes without the worry of adverse reactions.

Moreover, gluten and dairy-free diets have gained popularity for their potential health benefits, including improved digestion, reduced inflammation, and better skin health. This peach cobbler recipe is not only delicious but also aligns with these health-conscious choices.

Ingredients You’ll Need

To make this gluten and dairy-free peach cobbler, you’ll need the following ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity Notes
Fresh peaches 6-8 medium-sized Peeled and sliced
Gluten-free flour 1 cup Almond flour or oat flour works well
Coconut sugar 1/2 cup Can substitute with maple syrup
Dairy-free butter 1/2 cup Melted
Almond milk 1/2 cup Unsweetened
Baking powder 1 tsp Ensure it’s gluten-free
Vanilla extract 1 tsp Pure vanilla extract is best
Cinnamon 1 tsp Optional, for added flavor

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Preheat and Prepare

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). While the oven is heating, peel and slice your fresh peaches. Place them in a baking dish and sprinkle with a little coconut sugar and cinnamon if desired. This will help bring out the natural sweetness of the peaches.

2. Make the Cobbler Topping

In a mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Add the melted dairy-free butter, almond milk, and vanilla extract. Stir until the mixture is well combined and forms a thick batter.

3. Assemble the Cobbler

Pour the batter evenly over the sliced peaches in the baking dish. Spread it out with a spatula to ensure even coverage. The batter will rise and form a delicious, golden crust as it bakes.

4. Bake to Perfection

Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the peaches are bubbling. The aroma of baked peaches and cinnamon will fill your kitchen, signaling that your cobbler is ready.

5. Serve and Enjoy

Allow the cobbler to cool slightly before serving. It pairs wonderfully with a scoop of dairy-free vanilla ice cream or a dollop of coconut whipped cream. Enjoy this gluten and dairy-free peach cobbler warm, and savor every bite!

FAQs

Can I use frozen peaches instead of fresh ones?

Yes, you can use frozen peaches if fresh ones are not available. Just make sure to thaw and drain them before using to avoid excess moisture in your cobbler.

What other fruits can I use in this recipe?

This recipe is versatile and works well with other fruits like berries, apples, or pears. Adjust the sugar quantity based on the sweetness of the fruit you choose.

Can I make this recipe vegan?

This recipe is already vegan as it uses dairy-free butter and almond milk. Just ensure that all other ingredients, like the baking powder, are vegan-friendly.

How should I store leftovers?

Store any leftover cobbler in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion

Gluten and dairy-free peach cobbler is a delightful dessert that proves you don’t have to sacrifice flavor for dietary restrictions. With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, this recipe is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re hosting a dinner party or simply craving a sweet treat, this cobbler is sure to impress. So, gather your ingredients, preheat your oven, and get ready to enjoy a healthier, equally delicious version of a classic dessert.
