Gluten-Free Ice Cream Sandwich Recipe

Ice cream sandwiches are a classic treat that combines the creamy goodness of ice cream with the satisfying crunch of cookies. For those who follow a gluten-free diet, finding a delicious and safe option can be a challenge. But fear not! This gluten-free ice cream sandwich recipe is here to save the day. Whether you're gluten-intolerant, celiac, or simply looking for a healthier alternative, this recipe is sure to delight your taste buds.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Ice Cream Sandwiches?

Gluten-free diets have become increasingly popular, not only among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity but also among individuals seeking a healthier lifestyle. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause digestive issues and other health problems for some people. By opting for gluten-free ice cream sandwiches, you can enjoy a delicious treat without compromising your dietary needs.

Ingredients You'll Need

Before we dive into the recipe, let's gather all the necessary ingredients. Here's what you'll need to make these delightful gluten-free ice cream sandwiches:

Ingredient Quantity
Gluten-free flour blend 1 ½ cups
Almond flour ½ cup
Unsweetened cocoa powder ½ cup
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt ½ tsp
Unsalted butter (softened) ½ cup
Granulated sugar ¾ cup
Egg 1 large
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Gluten-free ice cream (your choice of flavor) 1 pint

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that we have all the ingredients ready, let's get started with the step-by-step instructions to make these gluten-free ice cream sandwiches.

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat to prevent the cookies from sticking.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the gluten-free flour blend, almond flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Ensure that all the dry ingredients are well combined.

Step 3: Cream the Butter and Sugar

In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. This should take about 2-3 minutes with an electric mixer on medium speed.

Step 4: Add the Egg and Vanilla

Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter-sugar mixture. Beat until well combined. The mixture should be smooth and slightly creamy.

Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.

Step 6: Shape the Cookies

Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each ball slightly with the back of a spoon or your fingers. Make sure to leave enough space between each cookie as they will spread during baking.

Step 7: Bake the Cookies

Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set but the centers are still soft. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 8: Assemble the Ice Cream Sandwiches

Once the cookies have cooled completely, it's time to assemble the ice cream sandwiches. Take a scoop of your favorite gluten-free ice cream and place it on the flat side of one cookie. Top with another cookie, flat side down, and gently press together. Repeat with the remaining cookies and ice cream.

Step 9: Freeze the Sandwiches

Place the assembled ice cream sandwiches on a baking sheet and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm. This will help the sandwiches hold their shape and make them easier to eat.

FAQs

Can I use a different type of flour?

Yes, you can experiment with different gluten-free flour blends, such as rice flour, oat flour, or coconut flour. However, keep in mind that the texture and flavor of the cookies may vary depending on the flour you choose.

Can I make these sandwiches ahead of time?

Absolutely! These gluten-free ice cream sandwiches can be made ahead of time and stored in the freezer for up to 2 weeks. Just make sure to wrap them individually in plastic wrap or place them in an airtight container to prevent freezer burn.

What if I don't have an electric mixer?

If you don't have an electric mixer, you can mix the ingredients by hand using a wooden spoon or a whisk. It may take a bit more effort, but the results will be just as delicious.

Can I use dairy-free ice cream?

Yes, you can use dairy-free ice cream if you're looking for a vegan or lactose-free option. There are many delicious dairy-free ice cream brands available that work perfectly in this recipe.

With a rich, chocolatey cookie and your favorite gluten-free ice cream, these sandwiches are the perfect combination of texture and flavor.
