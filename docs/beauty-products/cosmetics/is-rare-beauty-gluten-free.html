Understanding Gluten in Cosmetics

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, is commonly associated with food products. However, it can also be present in cosmetics, particularly in products that contain wheat-derived ingredients. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even topical exposure to gluten can cause adverse reactions, making it crucial to choose gluten-free beauty products.

While the FDA does not require cosmetics to be labeled as gluten-free, many brands, including Rare Beauty, are taking steps to ensure their products are safe for those with gluten-related disorders. Let’s explore whether Rare Beauty meets this standard.
