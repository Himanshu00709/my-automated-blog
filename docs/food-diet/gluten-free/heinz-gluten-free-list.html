Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein composite found in certain grains, primarily wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can trigger a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe autoimmune reactions. The only effective treatment for these conditions is a strict gluten-free diet. This makes it crucial for individuals to identify and consume products that are certified gluten-free.
