What is Two Chicks?

Two Chicks is a UK-based brand that specializes in egg-based products. Founded by Anna Richey and Anna Henshaw, the company was born out of a desire to create healthier, more convenient food options. Their flagship product, liquid egg whites, has become a staple for fitness enthusiasts, health-conscious individuals, and anyone looking to reduce their calorie intake without sacrificing protein. In addition to liquid egg whites, Two Chicks offers a variety of other products, including ready-to-eat omelets, egg white chips, and baking mixes.

Understanding Gluten and Gluten-Free Diets

Before we dive into the specifics of Two Chicks products, it's essential to understand what gluten is and why some people need to avoid it. Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Additionally, some people may have non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. As a result, many people choose to follow a gluten-free diet to manage their health and well-being.
