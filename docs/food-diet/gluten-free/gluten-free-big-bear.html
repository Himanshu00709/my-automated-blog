Tips for a Gluten-Free Big Bear Experience

Navigating a gluten-free lifestyle in Big Bear can be seamless with a few tips:

  • Plan Ahead: Research restaurants and grocery stores before your trip to ensure they offer gluten-free options.
  • Communicate: Always inform restaurant staff about your gluten-free needs to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Pack Snacks: Bring gluten-free snacks for outdoor activities like hiking or skiing.
  • Check Labels: When shopping, always read labels to confirm products are gluten-free.
