Is Krackel Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, determining whether a product is gluten-free is crucial. One popular treat that often comes into question is Krackel, a chocolate bar produced by Hershey's. In this article, we will delve into whether Krackel is gluten-free, explore its ingredients, and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

What is Krackel?

Krackel is a chocolate bar made by Hershey's, known for its unique combination of milk chocolate and crispy rice. The crispy rice gives the bar a delightful crunch, making it a favorite among chocolate lovers. However, for those who need to avoid gluten, the presence of crispy rice raises questions about the bar's safety.

Ingredients in Krackel

To determine whether Krackel is gluten-free, it's essential to examine its ingredients. Here is a list of the primary ingredients found in a standard Krackel bar:

  • Milk Chocolate (Sugar, Cocoa Butter, Chocolate, Nonfat Milk, Milk Fat, Lactose, Soy Lecithin, PGPR, Emulsifier, Vanillin, Artificial Flavor)
  • Crisped Rice (Rice, Sugar, Salt, Malt Flavoring)

The key ingredient to focus on here is the crisped rice, which contains malt flavoring. Malt flavoring is often derived from barley, a gluten-containing grain. This raises concerns about whether Krackel is safe for those on a gluten-free diet.

Is Krackel Gluten-Free?

According to Hershey's, Krackel is not considered gluten-free. The presence of malt flavoring in the crisped rice means that the product contains gluten. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming Krackel could lead to adverse health effects.

It's important to note that while some products may contain gluten, they may still be labeled as "gluten-free" if they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten. However, in the case of Krackel, Hershey's has explicitly stated that the product is not gluten-free due to the malt flavoring.

FAQs About Krackel and Gluten

1. Is Krackel safe for people with celiac disease?

No, Krackel is not safe for people with celiac disease due to the presence of malt flavoring, which contains gluten.

2. Can I find a gluten-free version of Krackel?

Currently, Hershey's does not offer a gluten-free version of Krackel. However, there are gluten-free alternatives available that provide a similar taste and texture.

3. What should I look for on the label to determine if a product is gluten-free?

Look for a gluten-free certification label on the packaging. Additionally, check the ingredients list for any gluten-containing ingredients such as wheat, barley, rye, or malt.

4. Are there any Hershey's products that are gluten-free?

Yes, some Hershey's products are gluten-free, such as Hershey's Milk Chocolate Bar and Hershey's Kisses. Always check the packaging for gluten-free certification.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Krackel is not gluten-free due to the presence of malt flavoring in its crisped rice. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it's essential to avoid Krackel and opt for gluten-free alternatives. Always read product labels carefully and look for gluten-free certification to ensure the safety of your dietary choices. By staying informed and making mindful decisions, you can enjoy delicious treats without compromising your health.
