Why Choose a Gluten-Free Lifestyle?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity for various reasons, including celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, and personal health choices. Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. For those with gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. Even for those without these conditions, a gluten-free diet can lead to improved digestion and overall well-being.

Tips for Maintaining a Gluten-Free Lifestyle

Living gluten-free in Pawleys Island is easier than ever, but it still requires some planning and awareness. Here are some tips to help you stay on track:

  1. Plan Ahead: Before dining out, check restaurant menus online or call ahead to inquire about gluten-free options.
  2. Read Labels: When shopping, always read food labels to ensure products are gluten-free. Look for certifications like "Certified Gluten-Free."
  3. Communicate: When dining out, inform your server about your dietary restrictions to avoid cross-contamination.
  4. Cook at Home: Preparing meals at home allows you to control ingredients and ensure your food is gluten-free.

Q: Are there gluten-free bakeries in Pawleys Island?

Q: Can I find gluten-free beer in Pawleys Island?

A: Yes, many local bars and restaurants offer gluten-free beer options. Some popular brands include Omission and Glutenberg.

Q: Is it safe to eat seafood in Pawleys Island if I'm gluten-free?

A: Yes, seafood is naturally gluten-free. However, be cautious of sauces and breading, which may contain gluten. Always ask your server for details.
