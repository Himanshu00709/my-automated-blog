Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Before we delve into Surfside’s gluten-free status, it’s essential to understand what gluten is and why it matters. Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine.

Given the potential health risks, many individuals are opting for gluten-free diets, even if they don’t have a diagnosed gluten-related disorder. This has led to a surge in gluten-free product offerings across various food categories, including snacks, baked goods, and ready-to-eat meals.
