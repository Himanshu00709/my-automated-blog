The Health Benefits of Going Gluten-Free

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Going gluten-free can alleviate these symptoms and improve overall health. Even for those without gluten-related disorders, reducing gluten intake can lead to benefits such as improved digestion, increased energy levels, and better nutrient absorption.
