Blue 32 has earned a reputation for its commitment to quality and inclusivity. FAQs About the Blue 32 Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Blue 32 gluten-free menu safe for celiac patients?

Yes, Blue 32 takes great care to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchen. They use separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free dishes, making it a safe choice for individuals with celiac disease.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Blue 32 offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the vegan gluten-free pasta and the roasted vegetable platter. Be sure to inform your server of any dietary restrictions.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Many dishes on the regular menu can be customized to be gluten-free. Simply inform your server, and they will guide you through the available options.

4. Does Blue 32 offer gluten-free beer or beverages?

Yes, Blue 32 has a selection of gluten-free beers and other beverages to complement your meal. Ask your server for recommendations.
