Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, individuals with these conditions must avoid gluten entirely, making it crucial to carefully scrutinize food labels and ingredients.
