FAQs About Zoup! Gluten-Free Options

1. Are all Zoup! soups gluten-free?

Not all Zoup! soups are gluten-free, but the chain offers a wide variety of gluten-free options. Be sure to check the ingredient list and allergen information before ordering.

2. How does Zoup! prevent cross-contamination?

Zoup! takes several precautions to minimize cross-contamination, including using separate utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free items. However, if you have severe gluten intolerance, it's always best to inform the staff so they can take extra care.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, many of Zoup!'s menu items can be customized to be gluten-free. Just let the staff know your dietary needs, and they will do their best to accommodate you.
