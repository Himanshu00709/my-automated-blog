What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. As a result, many people are opting for gluten-free diets to improve their health and well-being.

Ponderosa Steakhouse recognizes the importance of catering to this growing demographic. By offering a variety of gluten-free options, they ensure that everyone can enjoy a delicious meal without compromising their dietary needs.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Ponderosa

Dining out gluten-free can be challenging, but with a few precautions, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying meal at Ponderosa. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  1. Communicate Clearly: Inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. Ask them to communicate your dietary needs to the kitchen staff.
  2. Check for Cross-Contamination: Ensure that your food is prepared in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  3. Review the Menu: Familiarize yourself with the gluten-free options available and ask about any seasonal or special items that may be offered.
  4. Bring Your Own Condiments: If you're concerned about gluten in sauces or dressings, consider bringing your own gluten-free condiments.

By following these tips, you can enjoy a worry-free dining experience at Ponderosa Steakhouse.
