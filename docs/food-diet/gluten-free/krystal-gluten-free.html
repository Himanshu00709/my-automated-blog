Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people have turned to gluten-free diets to manage their health. However, finding gluten-free options, especially in fast-food restaurants, can be challenging. This is where Krystal steps in, offering a variety of gluten-free menu items that allow customers to enjoy their favorite fast-food flavors without the worry of gluten exposure.

FAQs About Krystal's Gluten-Free Options

1. Are Krystal's gluten-free options safe for people with celiac disease?

While Krystal offers gluten-free menu items, cross-contamination is possible in the kitchen. Individuals with celiac disease should consult with the restaurant staff and consider their own sensitivity levels before ordering.

2. Does Krystal offer gluten-free buns?

As of now, Krystal does not offer gluten-free buns. However, many of their menu items can be enjoyed without the bun, making them a viable option for those avoiding gluten.

3. Are Krystal's fries cooked in a dedicated fryer?

Yes, Krystal's fries are cooked in a dedicated fryer, which reduces the risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing items.

4. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Absolutely! Krystal allows customers to customize their orders. You can request to remove certain ingredients or ask for gluten-free alternatives when available.

5. Does Krystal provide nutritional information for their gluten-free items?

Yes, Krystal provides detailed nutritional information for all their menu items, including gluten-free options. This information can be found on their website or by asking in-store.
