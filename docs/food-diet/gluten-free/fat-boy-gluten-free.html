The Benefits of Going Gluten-Free

Before diving deeper into what Fat Boy Gluten Free has to offer, it's important to understand the benefits of a gluten-free diet. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and while it is harmless for most people, it can cause serious health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to damage in the small intestine, resulting in a range of symptoms from digestive issues to nutrient deficiencies. Even for those without celiac disease, a gluten-free diet can help alleviate symptoms of gluten sensitivity, such as bloating, fatigue, and joint pain. Additionally, many people find that a gluten-free diet helps them feel more energetic and improves their overall well-being.
