What Are Against the Grain Gluten-Free Rolls?

Against the Grain Gluten-Free Rolls are a type of bread product made without gluten-containing grains such as wheat, barley, or rye. Instead, they are crafted from a blend of alternative flours and ingredients that mimic the texture and taste of traditional bread. These rolls are known for their soft, chewy texture and rich flavor, making them a popular choice for sandwiches, burgers, or simply as a side to your favorite meal.
