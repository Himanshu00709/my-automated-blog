Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health. Birdcall recognizes this need and has made a concerted effort to cater to gluten-free diners without compromising on taste or quality.

Preparation Practices at Birdcall

One of the most critical aspects of offering gluten-free options is ensuring that cross-contamination is minimized. Birdcall takes this seriously by implementing strict preparation practices:

  • Dedicated Fryers: Birdcall uses separate fryers for gluten-free items, such as their fries and gluten-free tenders, to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in designated areas to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Birdcall staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all precautions are taken to maintain the integrity of the gluten-free items.
