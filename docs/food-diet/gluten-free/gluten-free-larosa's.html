Gluten-Free LaRosa's: A Delicious and Safe Dining Option

Understanding Gluten Sensitivity and Celiac Disease

Before diving into LaRosa's gluten-free options, it’s essential to understand what gluten sensitivity and celiac disease are. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. Gluten sensitivity, while not as severe, can still cause discomfort and digestive issues. Both conditions require strict adherence to a gluten-free diet, making it crucial for restaurants to offer safe and reliable options.

Importantly, LaRosa's takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free pizzas are prepared separately from regular pizzas.

In addition to pizza, LaRosa's offers gluten-free pasta dishes. Their gluten-free pasta is made from rice or corn, providing a safe alternative for those avoiding wheat. Ensuring Safety: LaRosa's Gluten-Free Protocols

One of the most critical aspects of offering gluten-free options is ensuring that the food is safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. LaRosa's takes this responsibility seriously and has implemented several protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Gluten-free pizzas and pasta are prepared in separate areas to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Separate Utensils: LaRosa's uses dedicated utensils and equipment for gluten-free orders to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and the steps needed to ensure safety.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.
