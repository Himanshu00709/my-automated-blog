What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergy, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues. Symptoms can range from digestive problems and fatigue to more severe conditions like nutrient malabsorption and autoimmune reactions. Going gluten-free is not just a trend; for many, it’s a medical necessity.

Simple Truth Gluten-Free products cater to this need by offering a wide range of foods that are free from gluten, ensuring that individuals with gluten-related disorders can enjoy a varied and nutritious diet without compromising on taste or quality.
