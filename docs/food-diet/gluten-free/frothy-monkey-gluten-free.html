FAQs About Frothy Monkey Gluten-Free Options

1. Are all Frothy Monkey locations gluten-free friendly?

Yes, all Frothy Monkey locations offer gluten-free options. However, it's always a good idea to check with your local Frothy Monkey for specific menu items and preparation practices.

2. Does Frothy Monkey use separate equipment for gluten-free dishes?

Frothy Monkey takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, including using separate equipment and utensils for gluten-free dishes. However, they do operate in a shared kitchen, so there is always a slight risk of cross-contamination.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Frothy Monkey is happy to accommodate dietary restrictions and can often customize dishes to be gluten-free. Just let your server know about your needs, and they will do their best to accommodate you.

4. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Frothy Monkey offers gluten-free options for kids, including gluten-free pancakes and sandwiches. Be sure to ask your server for the kid's gluten-free menu.
