In recent years, the demand for gluten-free dining options has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity. Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into Carmine's gluten-free options, it's essential to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, gluten-free diets have become a necessity for many, and restaurants like Carmine's have adapted their menus to accommodate these dietary needs.

The Gluten-Free Preparation Process

One of the critical aspects of gluten-free dining is ensuring that the food is prepared in a way that avoids cross-contamination. Carmine's takes this responsibility seriously by implementing strict protocols in their kitchens. The restaurant uses separate cooking utensils, pots, and pans for gluten-free dishes, and staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care. Additionally, Carmine's sources high-quality gluten-free ingredients to ensure that every dish meets the highest standards of taste and safety.
