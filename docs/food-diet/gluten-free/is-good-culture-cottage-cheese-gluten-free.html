What is Gluten and Why Does It Matter?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in grains such as wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless and can be part of a healthy diet. However, for individuals with celiac disease or non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health and well-being.

When it comes to dairy products like cottage cheese, gluten is not typically an ingredient. However, cross-contamination during processing or the addition of gluten-containing additives can make some products unsafe for those avoiding gluten. This is why it’s essential to examine the specific brand and product in question.
