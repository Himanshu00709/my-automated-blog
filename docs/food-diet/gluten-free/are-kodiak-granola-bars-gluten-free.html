Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to skin rashes and even neurological problems. As a result, many individuals seek out gluten-free alternatives to avoid these adverse effects.

When it comes to granola bars, the presence of gluten can be a concern, especially since many traditional granola bars contain oats, which are often cross-contaminated with gluten-containing grains. This makes it crucial to scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes of any granola bar brand, including Kodiak.

2. What makes Kodiak gluten-free granola bars different?

Yes, individuals with celiac disease can safely consume Kodiak gluten-free granola bars, as they are made with gluten-free ingredients and processed to avoid cross-contamination.
