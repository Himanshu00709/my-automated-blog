Understanding Gluten and Its Sources

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. It’s commonly found in bread, pasta, and baked goods, but it can also be hidden in less obvious products like sauces, soups, and broths. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms such as bloating, diarrhea, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine.

When evaluating whether a product like Pacific Organic Chicken Broth is gluten-free, it’s essential to consider both the ingredients and the manufacturing process. Cross-contamination can occur if the product is processed in a facility that also handles gluten-containing foods.
