What Makes 3 Musketeers Gluten-Free?

3 Musketeers bars are known for their light, fluffy nougat center covered in rich milk chocolate. The primary ingredients in 3 Musketeers bars include sugar, corn syrup, hydrogenated palm kernel oil, cocoa powder, and skim milk. Importantly, these ingredients do not contain gluten, making the candy safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

However, it’s always important to check the label for any potential cross-contamination or changes in ingredients. As of the latest information, 3 Musketeers bars are manufactured in facilities that do not process gluten-containing products, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.
