Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless and can be part of a healthy diet. However, for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to avoid gluten-containing foods.

Cross-Contamination Risks

Even if a product does not contain gluten-containing ingredients, there is still a risk of cross-contamination during the manufacturing process. Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free products are processed on the same equipment as gluten-containing products. UTZ has not made any specific claims about their pretzels being processed in a gluten-free facility, so there is a potential risk of cross-contamination.
