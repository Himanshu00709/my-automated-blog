Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. As a result, many restaurants, including Newk's, have adapted their menus to cater to this growing demographic.

Newk's commitment to gluten-free dining is evident in their menu, which features a variety of salads, soups, and sandwiches that can be customized to meet gluten-free requirements. However, it’s important to note that while Newk's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, their kitchens are not entirely gluten-free environments. Therefore, diners with severe gluten allergies should exercise caution and communicate their needs clearly to the staff.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Newk's

While Newk's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s essential to take certain precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and suggest safe options.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Although Newk's takes steps to avoid cross-contamination, their kitchens handle gluten-containing ingredients. If you have a severe allergy, ask about their preparation methods.
  • Review the Menu Online: Before visiting, review Newk's gluten-free menu online to familiarize yourself with the available options.
  • Ask About Dressings and Sauces: Some dressings and sauces may contain gluten. Always ask for gluten-free alternatives.
