Small Cheval Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

Small Cheval, the beloved Chicago-based burger joint, has earned a reputation for its mouthwatering burgers, crispy fries, and laid-back atmosphere. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. Fortunately, Small Cheval has made strides to accommodate gluten-free diners, offering options that don’t compromise on flavor or quality. In this guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about Small Cheval’s gluten-free offerings, from their menu options to cross-contamination precautions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Small Cheval

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend—it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Small Cheval recognizes this and has taken steps to ensure that gluten-free diners can enjoy their meals safely and deliciously.

While Small Cheval is primarily known for its classic burgers and buns, they have adapted their menu to cater to gluten-free needs. However, it’s important to note that their kitchen is not entirely gluten-free, so cross-contamination is a possibility. Always inform the staff of your dietary restrictions to ensure they take extra precautions.

Milkshakes Yes Check with staff to ensure no gluten-containing ingredients are used.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

One of the biggest challenges for gluten-free diners is the risk of cross-contamination. Even if a menu item is inherently gluten-free, it can come into contact with gluten during preparation. At Small Cheval, the kitchen handles gluten-containing ingredients, so cross-contamination is a possibility. Here are some tips to minimize the risk:

  • Communicate Clearly: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can take extra precautions, such as using separate utensils or cleaning surfaces.
  • Avoid Shared Fryers: While the fries are gluten-free, they are cooked in a shared fryer, which may also be used for gluten-containing items.
  • Double-Check Ingredients: Always ask about the ingredients in sauces, dressings, and toppings to ensure they are gluten-free.

FAQs About Small Cheval’s Gluten-Free Options

1. Does Small Cheval have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen?

No, Small Cheval does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. Cross-contamination is possible, so it’s important to inform the staff of your dietary needs.

2. Are the fries at Small Cheval gluten-free?

Yes, the fries are gluten-free, but they are cooked in a shared fryer, which may pose a risk of cross-contamination.

3. Can I get a gluten-free bun for my burger?

Yes, Small Cheval offers gluten-free buns upon request. Be sure to inform the staff when placing your order.

4. Are the milkshakes gluten-free?

Most milkshakes at Small Cheval are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to double-check with the staff to ensure no gluten-containing ingredients are used.
