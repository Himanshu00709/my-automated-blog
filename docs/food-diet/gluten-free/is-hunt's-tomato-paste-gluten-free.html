Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. Therefore, it is essential to ensure that the foods they consume are free from gluten.

Tomato paste, in its purest form, is made from tomatoes that have been cooked down and concentrated. However, some brands may add additional ingredients or use processing methods that introduce gluten. This is why it's important to scrutinize the labels and understand the manufacturing processes of the products you consume.

Hunt's Tomato Paste is a popular choice for many households due to its rich flavor and versatility in cooking. The primary ingredients in Hunt's Tomato Paste are tomatoes, water, and salt. According to the manufacturer, Hunt's Tomato Paste does not contain any gluten-containing ingredients.

Hunt's has stated that they follow strict manufacturing practices to prevent cross-contamination, making their tomato paste a safe option for those avoiding gluten.
