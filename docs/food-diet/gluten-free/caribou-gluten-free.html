What is Caribou Coffee?

Caribou Coffee is a well-known coffeehouse chain that originated in Minnesota, USA. Founded in 1992, it has grown to become one of the largest coffee chains in the country, rivaling even Starbucks in some regions. Caribou Coffee is renowned for its high-quality coffee beans, cozy ambiance, and a wide array of beverages and snacks. In response to the growing demand for gluten-free options, Caribou has expanded its menu to include a variety of gluten-free items, ensuring that everyone can enjoy their offerings without worry.

FAQs About Caribou Gluten-Free

1. Is Caribou Coffee completely gluten-free?

While Caribou Coffee offers a variety of gluten-free items, not all items on their menu are gluten-free. It's important to check the menu or ask the staff for gluten-free options.

2. Are Caribou's gluten-free items safe for people with celiac disease?

Caribou Coffee takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but individuals with celiac disease should always inform the staff about their condition to ensure the safest possible experience.

3. Can I find gluten-free pastries at Caribou Coffee?

Currently, Caribou Coffee does not offer gluten-free pastries. However, they do have other gluten-free snack options like Kind Bars and popcorn.

4. Are Caribou's syrups and sauces gluten-free?

Most of Caribou's syrups and sauces are gluten-free, but it's always best to check the ingredients or consult with the staff to be sure.

5. Does Caribou Coffee offer gluten-free milk alternatives?

Yes, Caribou Coffee offers gluten-free milk alternatives such as almond milk, soy milk, and oat milk, which can be used in any of their beverages.
