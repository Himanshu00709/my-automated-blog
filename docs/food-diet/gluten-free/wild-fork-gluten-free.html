Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of health issues, from digestive problems to more severe autoimmune reactions. Even for those without gluten-related disorders, reducing gluten intake can sometimes lead to improved digestion and overall well-being. This is where Wild Fork's gluten-free products come into play, offering a safe and delicious alternative for those looking to avoid gluten.
