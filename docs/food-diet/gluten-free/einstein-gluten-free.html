What is Einstein Gluten Free?

Einstein Gluten Free is a brand dedicated to providing gluten-free baked goods that do not compromise on taste or texture. The brand is an offshoot of the popular Einstein Bros. Bagels, a well-known chain of bagel shops across the United States. Recognizing the growing need for gluten-free options, Einstein Bros. Bagels introduced a line of gluten-free products under the Einstein Gluten Free label, catering to individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those simply looking to reduce their gluten intake.
