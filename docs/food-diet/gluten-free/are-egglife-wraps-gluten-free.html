The short answer is yes, Egglife Wraps are gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Egglife Wraps are made without any gluten-containing ingredients. This makes them a safe option for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those simply avoiding gluten for other health reasons.

However, it’s important to note that while the ingredients themselves are gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during the manufacturing process. This is particularly relevant for those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease. To ensure the safety of their products, Egglife states that they follow strict manufacturing protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. Nevertheless, if you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s always a good idea to contact the manufacturer directly for the most up-to-date information on their production practices.
