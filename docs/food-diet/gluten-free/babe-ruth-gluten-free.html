What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe allergic reactions. Going gluten-free is not just a trend; for many, it's a medical necessity. However, even for those without gluten-related health issues, a gluten-free diet can offer benefits such as improved digestion, increased energy levels, and better overall health.
