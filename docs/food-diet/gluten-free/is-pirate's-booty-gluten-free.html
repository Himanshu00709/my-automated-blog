Understanding Gluten and Gluten Sensitivity

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, those with gluten-related disorders must be vigilant about avoiding gluten in their diet.

When it comes to snacks like Pirate's Booty, it’s essential to scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes to ensure that the product is truly gluten-free. Even trace amounts of gluten can be harmful to those with celiac disease, so it’s crucial to look for products that are certified gluten-free.
