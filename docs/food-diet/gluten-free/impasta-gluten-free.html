How to Cook Impasta Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking Impasta gluten-free pasta is similar to cooking traditional pasta, but there are a few tips to ensure the best results:

  1. Use Plenty of Water: Boil a large pot of water to prevent the pasta from sticking together.
  2. Add Salt: Add a generous amount of salt to the boiling water to enhance the pasta's flavor.
  3. Follow Cooking Time: Gluten-free pasta can cook faster than traditional pasta, so follow the package instructions carefully to avoid overcooking.
  4. Rinse with Caution: Rinsing gluten-free pasta is generally not recommended, as it can remove the starch that helps sauces adhere to the pasta.
  5. Pair with the Right Sauce: Choose sauces that complement the flavor and texture of the gluten-free pasta you're using.
