Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into Au Cheval’s gluten-free offerings, it’s important to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to digestive issues, fatigue, and other health problems. As a result, many restaurants have started to offer gluten-free options to cater to this growing demographic.

Au Cheval has embraced this trend by offering a variety of gluten-free options, ensuring that everyone can enjoy their delicious burgers without compromising their health. Let’s take a closer look at what they have to offer.

How Au Cheval Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a major concern. Even a small amount of gluten can trigger a reaction, so it’s crucial that restaurants take steps to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchens.

Au Cheval takes this responsibility seriously. They have implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free options are safe for those with gluten-related disorders:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in a separate area of the kitchen to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils and equipment are used exclusively for gluten-free orders.
  • Staff Training: The staff at Au Cheval are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all precautions are taken.

These measures provide peace of mind for gluten-free diners, allowing them to enjoy their meal without fear of adverse reactions.
