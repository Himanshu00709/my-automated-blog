How Eggspectation Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Eggspectation takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in designated areas to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils and cookware are used exclusively for gluten-free dishes.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly marked on the menu, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.
