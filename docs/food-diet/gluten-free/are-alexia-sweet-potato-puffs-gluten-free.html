Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Even for those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can cause discomfort, bloating, and other digestive problems. Therefore, it’s crucial for these individuals to ensure that the foods they consume are free from gluten.
