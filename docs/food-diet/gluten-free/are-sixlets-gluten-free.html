Are Sixlets Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

Sixlets are a popular candy known for their crunchy chocolatey shells and colorful appearance. They are often used in baking, party favors, and as a sweet treat. What Are Sixlets?

Sixlets are small, round candies that consist of a chocolatey center covered in a crunchy shell. Are Sixlets Gluten-Free?

According to the manufacturer, Oak Leaf Confections, Sixlets do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients. However, they are not certified gluten-free. This means that while the ingredients themselves are gluten-free, there is a risk of cross-contamination during production. Cross-contamination can occur if the same equipment is used to process gluten-containing products, or if the facility is not dedicated to gluten-free production.

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, even trace amounts of gluten can cause adverse reactions. Therefore, it's essential to consider the potential for cross-contamination when deciding whether to consume Sixlets.

Gluten-Free Certification

Gluten-free certification is a process by which a third-party organization verifies that a product meets strict gluten-free standards. These standards typically require that the product contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is considered safe for most people with celiac disease.

Sixlets are not certified gluten-free, which means they have not undergone this rigorous testing process. While the absence of gluten-containing ingredients is a positive sign, the lack of certification may be a concern for some individuals.
