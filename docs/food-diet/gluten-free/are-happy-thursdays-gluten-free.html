Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many individuals are turning to gluten-free products to avoid these adverse effects.

When it comes to Happy Thursdays, the brand has positioned itself as a provider of healthy, natural, and delicious snacks. But are these snacks gluten-free? Let’s explore this question in detail.
