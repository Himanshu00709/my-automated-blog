FAQs About Gluten-Free Avalon

1. Are all Avalon products gluten-free?

No, not all Avalon products are gluten-free. However, Avalon has a dedicated line of gluten-free products that are clearly labeled. Always check the packaging to ensure the product is gluten-free.

2. Is Avalon's gluten-free bread suitable for vegans?

Yes, Avalon's gluten-free bread is vegan-friendly. It does not contain any animal-derived ingredients.

3. Where can I buy gluten-free Avalon products?

Gluten-free Avalon products are available at most health food stores, organic markets, and online retailers. You can also check Avalon's official website for a store locator.

4. How does Avalon ensure their products are gluten-free?

Avalon follows strict manufacturing processes to prevent cross-contamination. Their gluten-free products are tested to ensure they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.
