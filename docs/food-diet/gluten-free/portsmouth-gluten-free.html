Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those who are intolerant. Even if you don't have a medical condition, many people choose to go gluten-free for its potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Living in Portsmouth

1. Are there gluten-free options at mainstream restaurants in Portsmouth?

Yes, many mainstream restaurants in Portsmouth offer gluten-free options. However, it's always a good idea to call ahead and confirm with the restaurant to ensure they can accommodate your dietary needs.

2. Can I find gluten-free products in regular supermarkets?

Most supermarkets in Portsmouth have a dedicated gluten-free section. However, for a wider variety, it's best to visit specialized gluten-free stores.

3. Are gluten-free products more expensive?

Gluten-free products can be more expensive due to the specialized ingredients and production processes. However, prices are gradually becoming more competitive as demand increases.

4. How do I know if a product is truly gluten-free?

Always check the label for a gluten-free certification. In the UK, products labeled as gluten-free must contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is considered safe for most people with celiac disease.
