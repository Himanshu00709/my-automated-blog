What is Fluff Gluten-Free?

Fluff Gluten-Free is a versatile, gluten-free version of the classic marshmallow creme. It is a light, fluffy, and sweet spread that can be used in a variety of recipes, from desserts to breakfast dishes. Unlike traditional marshmallow creme, Fluff Gluten-Free is made without any gluten-containing ingredients, making it safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Fluff Gluten-Free should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it can be kept in the refrigerator to maintain its freshness.
