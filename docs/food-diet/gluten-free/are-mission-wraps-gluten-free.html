Are Mission Wraps Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity. For those who must avoid gluten, finding safe and delicious alternatives to traditional wheat-based products is essential. One popular product that often comes under scrutiny is Mission Wraps. Are Mission Wraps gluten-free? This article delves into the details, providing a comprehensive guide to help you make informed decisions.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe autoimmune reactions. Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Non-celiac gluten sensitivity, on the other hand, involves symptoms similar to celiac disease but without the intestinal damage.

Given the potential health risks, it's crucial for individuals with these conditions to avoid gluten-containing foods. This has led to a surge in the availability of gluten-free products, including wraps, bread, and pasta. However, not all products labeled as "gluten-free" are created equal, and understanding the specifics is key.

Are Mission Wraps Gluten-Free?
