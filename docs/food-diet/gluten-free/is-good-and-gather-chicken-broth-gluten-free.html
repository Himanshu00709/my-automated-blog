Understanding Gluten and Its Presence in Food Products

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, it’s essential for these individuals to carefully scrutinize food labels to ensure that the products they consume are gluten-free.

When it comes to chicken broth, gluten can sometimes be present due to additives, flavorings, or cross-contamination during manufacturing. This is why it’s important to verify whether a specific brand of chicken broth is gluten-free.
