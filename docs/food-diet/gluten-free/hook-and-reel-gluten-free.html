How Hook and Reel Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Hook and Reel addresses this issue by implementing strict kitchen protocols. Separate fryers are used for gluten-free items, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care. Additionally, the restaurant provides detailed allergen information on their menu, allowing diners to make informed choices.
