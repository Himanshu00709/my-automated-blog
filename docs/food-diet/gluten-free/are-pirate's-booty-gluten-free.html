Understanding Gluten and Gluten-Free Diets

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many people follow a strict gluten-free diet, which requires careful scrutiny of food labels and ingredients.

Is Pirate's Booty Gluten-Free?

Additionally, Pirate's Booty is manufactured in a facility that does not process wheat, barley, or rye, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.

However, it’s always important to check the packaging for any updates or changes in ingredients, as formulations can change over time. Pirate's Booty also carries a gluten-free certification on many of its products, providing an extra layer of assurance for consumers.
