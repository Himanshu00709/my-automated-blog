What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. A gluten-free diet eliminates all sources of gluten, which can be challenging given its prevalence in many common foods.
