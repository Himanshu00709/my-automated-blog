Are Just Bare Chicken Nuggets Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, gluten-free diets have gained significant popularity, not only among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity but also among health-conscious individuals. For those who love chicken nuggets but need to avoid gluten, finding a safe and delicious option can be a challenge. Enter Just Bare Chicken Nuggets—a product that has been making waves in the gluten-free community. But are they truly gluten-free? In this article, we’ll dive deep into the ingredients, manufacturing processes, and certifications to determine whether Just Bare Chicken Nuggets are a safe choice for those avoiding gluten.

What Are Just Bare Chicken Nuggets?

Just Bare Chicken Nuggets are a product of the Just Bare brand, which prides itself on offering high-quality, minimally processed chicken products. These nuggets are made from 100% all-natural chicken breast meat, with no artificial ingredients, preservatives, or antibiotics. They are also free from added hormones and steroids, making them a popular choice for health-conscious consumers.

The nuggets are lightly breaded and seasoned, offering a crispy exterior while maintaining a juicy and tender interior. They are available in both regular and organic varieties, catering to a wide range of dietary preferences.

Manufacturing Process and Cross-Contamination

While the ingredients themselves are gluten-free, it’s crucial to consider the manufacturing process. Cross-contamination can occur if the product is processed in a facility that also handles gluten-containing ingredients. This is a significant concern for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity.

According to the manufacturer, Just Bare Chicken Nuggets are produced in a facility that also processes wheat and other gluten-containing products. However, the company follows strict cleaning protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. Despite these precautions, there is still a possibility of trace amounts of gluten being present in the final product.

Certifications and Labeling

Just Bare Chicken Nuggets are not certified gluten-free by any third-party organizations, such as the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO). However, the product is labeled as "gluten-free" on the packaging, which means it meets the FDA’s requirements for gluten-free labeling. According to the FDA, a product can be labeled gluten-free if it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten.

For most people with gluten sensitivity, this level is considered safe. However, individuals with celiac disease should exercise caution and consult with their healthcare provider before consuming products that are not certified gluten-free.
