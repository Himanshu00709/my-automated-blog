Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient malabsorption, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, it’s crucial for these individuals to carefully scrutinize food labels and understand the potential for cross-contamination.
