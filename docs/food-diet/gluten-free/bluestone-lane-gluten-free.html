Understanding Gluten-Free at Bluestone Lane

For those new to gluten-free dining, it’s important to understand what “gluten-free” truly means. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Bluestone Lane takes this seriously by ensuring that their gluten-free items are prepared with care to avoid cross-contamination.

The café uses separate utensils, cutting boards, and preparation areas for gluten-free items. Additionally, they source their gluten-free ingredients from trusted suppliers, ensuring that every bite is safe and enjoyable. This level of attention to detail is what sets Bluestone Lane apart from other casual dining options.

3. How does Bluestone Lane prevent cross-contamination?

Bluestone Lane uses separate preparation areas, utensils, and equipment for gluten-free items. Staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
