Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it is crucial for these individuals to carefully scrutinize food labels and understand the potential sources of gluten in their diet.

Hershey's Ice Cream does not make a blanket statement that all of its products are gluten-free.
