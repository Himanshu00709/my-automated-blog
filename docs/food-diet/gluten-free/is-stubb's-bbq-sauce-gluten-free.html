Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe autoimmune reactions. Therefore, it’s crucial to carefully examine food labels and understand the manufacturing processes of products like BBQ sauce to ensure they are gluten-free.
