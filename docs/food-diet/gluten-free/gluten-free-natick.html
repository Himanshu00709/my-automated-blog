Gluten-Free Living in Natick: A Comprehensive Guide

Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those who are intolerant. Even for those without gluten-related health conditions, many people choose a gluten-free diet for its potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels.

1. Is it easy to find gluten-free options in Natick?

Yes, Natick has a growing number of restaurants and grocery stores that offer gluten-free options. Many establishments are well-informed about gluten-free needs and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

While there are no dedicated gluten-free bakeries in Natick, many local bakeries offer gluten-free options. Whole Foods Market and Wegmans also have gluten-free bakery sections.

4. Are there any gluten-free events in Natick?
