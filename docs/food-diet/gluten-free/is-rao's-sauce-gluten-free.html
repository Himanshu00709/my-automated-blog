Rao's Homemade is a brand that has gained a loyal following for its high-quality, authentic Italian sauces. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. Therefore, it’s crucial for these individuals to ensure that the foods they consume are free from gluten contamination.

When it comes to sauces, gluten can often be hidden in ingredients like thickeners, flavorings, or even in the manufacturing process. This makes it essential to scrutinize product labels and understand the sourcing of ingredients.

Manufacturing Process and Gluten Contamination

Rao's Homemade takes great care in ensuring that their products meet the highest quality standards. According to the company, their sauces are produced in facilities that are dedicated to gluten-free production. This significantly reduces the risk of cross-contamination.

Additionally, Rao's Homemade sauces are certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO). This certification requires that products contain less than 10 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for individuals with celiac disease.
