The Importance of Gluten-Free Diets

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of health issues, from digestive problems to more severe autoimmune reactions. A gluten-free diet is essential for managing these conditions, and brands like Atly Gluten-Free play a crucial role in making this diet more accessible and enjoyable.
