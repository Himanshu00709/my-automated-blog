Tips for Gluten-Free Travelers in Pigeon Forge

Traveling with a gluten-free diet requires some planning, but with these tips, you can enjoy your trip to Pigeon Forge without any worries:

  1. Research Ahead: Before your trip, research restaurants and grocery stores that offer gluten-free options. Many restaurants have their menus available online, so you can plan your meals in advance.
  2. Communicate with Staff: When dining out, always inform the staff about your gluten-free needs. Most restaurants in Pigeon Forge are familiar with gluten-free diets and will accommodate your requests.
  3. Pack Snacks: Bring along some gluten-free snacks, especially if you’re planning to spend the day exploring attractions. This ensures you have something safe to eat if you’re unable to find gluten-free options on the go.
  4. Check Labels: When shopping for groceries, always check the labels for gluten-free certification. Some products may be naturally gluten-free but could be processed in facilities that handle gluten.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in Pigeon Forge

1. Are there gluten-free options at Dollywood?

Yes, Dollywood offers several gluten-free options at its restaurants and snack stands. Be sure to ask for a gluten-free menu or speak with a staff member to ensure your meal is safe.

2. Can I find gluten-free desserts in Pigeon Forge?

Absolutely! Many restaurants in Pigeon Forge offer gluten-free desserts, including cakes, cookies, and pies. The Old Mill Restaurant is particularly known for its gluten-free desserts.

3. Is it safe to eat gluten-free at buffets in Pigeon Forge?

While buffets can be challenging for gluten-free diners due to cross-contamination, some buffets in Pigeon Forge offer gluten-free options and take precautions to prevent cross-contact. Always communicate your dietary needs with the staff.

4. Are there gluten-free bakeries in Pigeon Forge?

Yes, there are a few bakeries in Pigeon Forge that offer gluten-free baked goods. Be sure to call ahead to confirm availability and to ensure that the bakery follows strict gluten-free protocols.
