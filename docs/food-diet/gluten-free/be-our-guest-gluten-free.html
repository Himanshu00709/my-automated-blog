Be Our Guest Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

Dining out can be a challenge for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. However, Disney World's "Be Our Guest" restaurant has made it a priority to cater to gluten-free diners. Located in the heart of Magic Kingdom, this enchanting dining experience offers a variety of gluten-free options that are both delicious and safe for those with dietary restrictions. In this guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about enjoying a gluten-free meal at Be Our Guest, from menu options to dining tips.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Be Our Guest

Be Our Guest is one of the most popular dining destinations in Disney World, known for its immersive Beauty and the Beast-themed atmosphere. The restaurant offers a unique dining experience with three distinct rooms: the Grand Ballroom, the West Wing, and the Castle Gallery. But beyond the ambiance, Be Our Guest is also celebrated for its commitment to accommodating dietary needs, including gluten-free options.

Disney World has a reputation for being one of the most accommodating places for guests with food allergies and intolerances. At Be Our Guest, the culinary team takes extra care to ensure that gluten-free meals are prepared separately to avoid cross-contamination. This attention to detail makes it a safe and enjoyable dining experience for those who need to avoid gluten.

Gluten-Free Menu Options at Be Our Guest

The gluten-free menu at Be Our Guest is extensive, offering a variety of options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Here’s a breakdown of what you can expect:

Breakfast

Breakfast at Be Our Guest is a magical way to start your day at Magic Kingdom. The gluten-free options include:

  • Open-Faced Bacon and Egg Sandwich: Served on gluten-free bread, this dish features scrambled eggs, bacon, and béchamel sauce.
  • Assorted Fresh Fruits: A refreshing and healthy option to start your day.
  • Gluten-Free Pastries: Enjoy a selection of gluten-free muffins and croissants.

Lunch

For lunch, Be Our Guest offers a variety of gluten-free entrees and sides:

  • Grilled Steak: Served with garlic butter, pommes frites, and seasonal vegetables.
  • Ratatouille: A flavorful vegetarian option made with seasonal vegetables and served with quinoa.
  • French Onion Soup: A classic favorite, made gluten-free with a special recipe.

Dinner

Dinner at Be Our Guest is a more formal affair, with a focus on French-inspired cuisine. Gluten-free options include:

  • Herb-Salted Pork Tenderloin: Served with au gratin potatoes and seasonal vegetables.
  • Pan-Seared Salmon: Accompanied by roasted potatoes and a lemon-dill butter sauce.
  • Braised Beef: A hearty dish served with mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Be Our Guest

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable dining experience, here are some tips for dining gluten-free at Be Our Guest:

Tip Description
Notify the Staff When making your reservation, inform the staff about your gluten-free dietary needs. This allows the kitchen to prepare in advance.
Ask Questions Don’t hesitate to ask your server about the ingredients and preparation methods to ensure your meal is gluten-free.
Check for Cross-Contamination Confirm with the staff that your meal will be prepared in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination.
Review the Menu in Advance Take a look at the gluten-free menu options online before your visit to plan your meal.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Be Our Guest safe for celiacs?

Yes, Be Our Guest is considered safe for celiacs. The restaurant takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination and offers a dedicated gluten-free menu.

Can I make special requests for my gluten-free meal?

Absolutely! Be Our Guest is known for accommodating special requests. Just inform your server, and they will work with the kitchen to meet your needs.

Are there gluten-free desserts available?

Yes, Be Our Guest offers gluten-free dessert options, including a flourless chocolate cake and a selection of sorbets.

Do I need to make a reservation for gluten-free dining?

While reservations are not required, they are highly recommended, especially if you have specific dietary needs. This allows the restaurant to prepare for your visit.

Conclusion

Dining at Be Our Guest is a magical experience, and for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, it’s a safe and enjoyable option. With a dedicated gluten-free menu and a commitment to avoiding cross-contamination, Be Our Guest ensures that all guests can enjoy a delicious meal in a truly enchanting setting. Whether you’re visiting for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, you can rest assured that your dietary needs will be met with care and attention. So, the next time you’re at Disney World, don’t hesitate to "be our guest" and indulge in a gluten-free feast fit for royalty!
