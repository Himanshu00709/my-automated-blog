Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe allergic reactions. This makes it crucial for restaurants to offer gluten-free options and to ensure that these options are prepared in a way that avoids cross-contamination.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. has recognized the importance of catering to gluten-sensitive diners. They have taken steps to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are not only delicious but also safe for those who need to avoid gluten.

How Bubba Gump Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. understands the importance of providing safe dining options for gluten-sensitive individuals. Here are some of the steps they take to ensure gluten-free safety:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Bubba Gump has designated areas in their kitchens for preparing gluten-free meals. This helps to reduce the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Separate Utensils and Cookware: Gluten-free dishes are prepared using separate utensils and cookware to avoid any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: The staff at Bubba Gump are trained to understand the importance of gluten-free dining and how to handle gluten-free orders properly.
  • Clear Menu Labeling: Bubba Gump’s menu clearly labels gluten-free options, making it easier for diners to identify safe choices.

These measures help to create a safe and enjoyable dining experience for those who need to avoid gluten.
