What Makes Surfside Vodka Gluten-Free?

Surfside Vodka is crafted using a meticulous distillation process that ensures the final product is free from gluten. Unlike traditional vodkas that may be made from wheat, barley, or rye, Surfside Vodka is distilled from corn. Corn is naturally gluten-free, making it an ideal base for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. The distillation process further removes any trace amounts of gluten, ensuring that the vodka is safe for consumption by those with gluten-related disorders.

It's important to note that not all vodkas labeled as "gluten-free" are created equal. Some vodkas may be made from gluten-containing grains but are marketed as gluten-free due to the distillation process. However, for individuals with severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, even trace amounts can be problematic. Surfside Vodka, on the other hand, is made from 100% corn, providing an extra layer of assurance for those who need to avoid gluten entirely.

The Production Process: Crafting Perfection

Surfside Vodka's production process is a blend of traditional techniques and modern innovation. The journey begins with the selection of high-quality, non-GMO corn. The corn is then fermented and distilled multiple times to achieve a pure, clean spirit. After distillation, the vodka is filtered through a proprietary filtration system that further enhances its smoothness and clarity.
