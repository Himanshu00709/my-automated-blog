What Makes Surfside Vodka Gluten-Free?

Surfside Vodka is crafted with a meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that it meets the highest standards of gluten-free production. Unlike traditional vodkas that are made from grains like wheat, barley, or rye, Surfside Vodka is distilled from 100% corn. Corn is naturally gluten-free, making it an ideal base for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The distillation process further removes any trace amounts of gluten, resulting in a vodka that is not only safe but also incredibly smooth and versatile.

The Distillation Process

The distillation process is a critical factor in ensuring that Surfside Vodka remains gluten-free. The vodka undergoes multiple distillations, which not only purify the spirit but also eliminate any potential contaminants, including gluten. This rigorous process ensures that the final product is as clean and pure as possible, making it a safe choice for those who need to avoid gluten in their diet.
