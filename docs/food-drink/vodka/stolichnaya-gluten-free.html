What Makes Stolichnaya Vodka Gluten-Free?

Stolichnaya Vodka is made from high-quality wheat and rye grains, which are naturally gluten-containing. However, the distillation process plays a crucial role in making the final product gluten-free. During distillation, the liquid is heated to a point where alcohol vaporizes, separating it from the solid components, including gluten proteins. The vapor is then condensed back into liquid form, resulting in a pure, gluten-free spirit.

It’s important to note that while the distillation process removes gluten, some individuals with severe gluten sensitivities or celiac disease may still experience reactions. However, Stolichnaya Vodka is widely recognized as safe for most people with gluten intolerance.

The Production Process of Stolichnaya Vodka

Stolichnaya Vodka is produced using a traditional Russian method that has been refined over centuries. The process begins with the selection of the finest wheat and rye grains, which are then fermented to create a mash. This mash is distilled multiple times to ensure purity and remove impurities, including gluten proteins.

After distillation, the vodka is filtered through quartz sand and activated charcoal, which further refines the spirit and enhances its smoothness. The result is a clean, crisp vodka that is both gluten-free and exceptionally smooth.

Flavor Profile of Stolichnaya Gluten-Free Vodka
