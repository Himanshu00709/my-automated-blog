FAQs About Simply Lemonade and Gluten

Is Simply Lemonade certified gluten-free?

While Simply Lemonade is not certified gluten-free, its ingredients are naturally gluten-free, and the brand has not reported any cross-contamination issues.

Can people with celiac disease drink Simply Lemonade?

Yes, people with celiac disease can safely consume Simply Lemonade as it does not contain gluten or any gluten-derived ingredients.

Does Simply Lemonade contain any allergens?

Simply Lemonade is free from common allergens like gluten, nuts, and dairy. However, always check the label for the most up-to-date information.

Is Simply Lemonade suitable for vegans?

Yes, Simply Lemonade is vegan-friendly as it contains no animal products or by-products.
