Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause serious health issues for people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Even for those without these conditions, reducing gluten intake can lead to better digestion, reduced inflammation, and overall improved health. Happy Thursday Spiked Refreshers are made with gluten-free ingredients, ensuring that everyone can enjoy them without worry.
