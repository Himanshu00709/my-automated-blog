What Are Aguas Frescas?

Aguas frescas, which translates to "fresh waters," are traditional Mexican beverages made by blending fruits, flowers, or seeds with water and sugar. These drinks are known for their light, refreshing taste and are often enjoyed as a way to cool down in hot weather. Common flavors include horchata (made from rice and cinnamon), tamarind, hibiscus, and various fruit combinations like watermelon, pineapple, and cucumber.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Beverages?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and skin problems. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can be present in many processed foods and beverages. By choosing gluten-free options like Modelo Aguas Frescas, individuals can enjoy their favorite drinks without worrying about adverse health effects.
