Are Truly Seltzers Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, hard seltzers have taken the beverage industry by storm, offering a refreshing alternative to traditional beers and cocktails. Among the most popular brands is Truly, known for its wide range of flavors and low-calorie options. However, for individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the question remains: Are Truly seltzers gluten-free? In this article, we’ll dive deep into the ingredients, brewing process, and certifications to provide a clear answer.

What Are Truly Seltzers?

Truly Hard Seltzers are alcoholic beverages made by fermenting sugar with yeast, resulting in a light, bubbly drink with a relatively low alcohol content (around 5% ABV). They are known for their crisp taste and variety of flavors, ranging from classic citrus to exotic fruit blends. But what sets Truly apart from other alcoholic beverages is its claim to be gluten-free. Let’s explore this claim in detail.

Ingredients in Truly Seltzers

To determine whether Truly seltzers are gluten-free, it’s essential to examine their ingredients. The primary components of Truly seltzers include:

  • Carbonated water
  • Cane sugar
  • Natural flavors
  • Yeast
  • Citric acid

None of these ingredients inherently contain gluten. However, the brewing process and potential cross-contamination are critical factors to consider.

The Brewing Process: Is Gluten Involved?

Traditional beers are brewed using barley, wheat, or rye—all of which contain gluten. In contrast, Truly seltzers are made by fermenting cane sugar, which is naturally gluten-free. This distinction is crucial because it means that Truly seltzers do not rely on gluten-containing grains during production.

However, some hard seltzers are brewed in facilities that also process gluten-containing products, raising concerns about cross-contamination. Truly addresses this issue by ensuring that their products are brewed in dedicated gluten-free facilities, minimizing the risk of contamination.

Certifications and Testing

Truly Hard Seltzers are certified gluten-free by the Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG), a reputable organization that sets stringent standards for gluten-free products. To earn this certification, Truly seltzers must contain less than 10 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for individuals with celiac disease.

Additionally, Truly conducts regular testing to ensure compliance with gluten-free standards. This rigorous testing process provides peace of mind for consumers who need to avoid gluten for health reasons.

FAQs About Truly Seltzers and Gluten

1. Are Truly seltzers safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Truly seltzers are certified gluten-free and contain less than 10 ppm of gluten, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease.

2. Can I drink Truly seltzers if I have a gluten sensitivity?

Absolutely. Truly seltzers are made without gluten-containing ingredients and are brewed in gluten-free facilities, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.

3. Do Truly seltzers taste different because they are gluten-free?

No, the absence of gluten does not affect the taste of Truly seltzers. They are known for their crisp, refreshing flavors, just like other hard seltzers.

4. Are all hard seltzers gluten-free?

Not all hard seltzers are gluten-free. It’s essential to check the label or certification to ensure the product meets gluten-free standards.

Conclusion

Truly Hard Seltzers are a fantastic option for anyone looking to enjoy a refreshing, low-calorie alcoholic beverage without worrying about gluten. With their gluten-free certification, dedicated brewing facilities, and rigorous testing, Truly seltzers provide a safe and delicious choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. Whether you’re at a party, relaxing at home, or enjoying the outdoors, you can confidently reach for a Truly seltzer and savor the moment.
