What is Gluten-Free Beer?

Gluten-free beer is brewed without the use of gluten-containing grains like barley, wheat, or rye. Instead, it uses alternative grains such as sorghum, rice, millet, or corn. For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, gluten-free beer offers a safe and enjoyable way to enjoy a cold one without the adverse health effects.
