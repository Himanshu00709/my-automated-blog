Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause serious health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from digestive discomfort to severe autoimmune reactions. As a result, many restaurants have started to offer gluten-free options to accommodate these dietary restrictions.

Bricktown Brewery has taken significant steps to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are not only safe but also delicious. From their menu items to their brewing process, they have made a concerted effort to cater to the needs of gluten-free diners.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Bricktown Brewery

While Bricktown Brewery offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take certain precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server of your gluten-free dietary needs. They can provide guidance on menu items and ensure that your meal is prepared safely.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing foods. Ask the staff about their procedures for preventing cross-contamination in the kitchen.
  • Check for Gluten-Free Labels: Some menu items may be labeled as gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to double-check with your server to confirm.
  • Be Mindful of Sauces and Dressings: Some sauces and dressings may contain gluten. Ask for gluten-free options or request that your dish be prepared without these ingredients.
