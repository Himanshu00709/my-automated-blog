What is Carnation Instant Breakfast Gluten-Free?

Carnation Instant Breakfast Gluten-Free is a powdered nutritional drink mix designed to provide a balanced breakfast in a convenient format. It is specifically formulated to be free from gluten, making it suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The product is packed with essential vitamins and minerals, offering a quick and easy way to fuel your body in the morning.
