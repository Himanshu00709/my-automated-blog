What Makes Surfside Iced Tea Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For a product to be considered gluten-free, it must contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, as per the FDA guidelines. Surfside Iced Tea meets this criterion, ensuring that it is safe for those who need to avoid gluten.

The primary ingredients in Surfside Iced Tea include brewed tea, natural flavors, and sweeteners. None of these ingredients contain gluten, which is why the product is labeled as gluten-free. Additionally, Surfside Iced Tea is produced in facilities that adhere to strict gluten-free protocols, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.
