What is Green's Gluten-Free Beer?

Green’s Gluten-Free Beer is a product of the Belgian brewery, Green’s, which has been crafting beers since 1999. The brewery specializes in producing gluten-free beers that cater to individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. Green’s uses alternative grains like millet, buckwheat, and rice to create beers that are not only safe for those avoiding gluten but also delicious and satisfying.
