What Makes Stateside Vodka Gluten-Free?

Stateside Vodka is crafted from 100% corn, making it naturally gluten-free. Unlike traditional vodkas that are often made from wheat, rye, or barley—grains that contain gluten—Stateside Vodka uses corn as its primary ingredient. This not only ensures that the vodka is safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance but also gives it a distinct, smooth flavor profile that sets it apart from other vodkas.

Gluten-free vodka is not just a trend; it's a necessity for many people. For those who must avoid gluten, finding a vodka that doesn't compromise on taste or quality can be a challenge. Stateside Vodka meets this need by offering a product that is both gluten-free and exceptionally crafted. The distillation process further ensures that any potential gluten proteins are removed, making it a safe choice for everyone.

The Production Process of Stateside Vodka

The production of Stateside Vodka is a meticulous process that begins with the selection of the finest corn. The corn is fermented and then distilled multiple times to achieve a pure, clean spirit. The distillation process is crucial in removing impurities and ensuring that the final product is smooth and free from any unwanted flavors.

After distillation, the vodka is filtered through charcoal to further refine its taste. This step is essential in creating the signature smoothness that Stateside Vodka is known for. The result is a vodka that is not only gluten-free but also of exceptional quality, making it a favorite among both casual drinkers and connoisseurs.
