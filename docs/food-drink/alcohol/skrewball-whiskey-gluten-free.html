What is Skrewball Whiskey?

Skrewball Whiskey is a peanut butter-flavored whiskey that was first introduced in 2018 by Steven Yeng and his wife Brittany. The idea for Skrewball was born out of a desire to create a unique and flavorful whiskey that could stand out in a crowded market. The result was a smooth, creamy, and slightly sweet whiskey that has since gained a loyal following. What sets Skrewball apart from other flavored whiskeys is its commitment to being gluten-free, making it accessible to a wider audience, including those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

Is Skrewball Whiskey Really Gluten-Free?

Yes, Skrewball Whiskey is indeed gluten-free. The whiskey is made from a base of corn, which is naturally gluten-free, and is then flavored with natural peanut butter flavors. Unlike some other whiskeys that are made from barley, wheat, or rye—grains that contain gluten—Skrewball’s corn-based recipe ensures that it is safe for those who need to avoid gluten. However, it’s important to note that while Skrewball is gluten-free, it is always a good idea to check the label or contact the manufacturer if you have severe gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.
