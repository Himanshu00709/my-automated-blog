Understanding Gluten and Gluten-Free Diets

Before we delve into whether Cayman Jack Margarita is gluten-free, it’s essential to understand what gluten is and why some people need to avoid it. Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications.

A gluten-free diet eliminates all sources of gluten, which means avoiding foods and beverages that contain these grains or are processed in facilities that handle them. For many, this requires careful scrutiny of ingredient labels and production processes.
