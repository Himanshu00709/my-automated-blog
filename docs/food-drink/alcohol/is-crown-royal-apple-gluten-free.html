Understanding Gluten and Its Presence in Alcoholic Beverages

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. When it comes to alcoholic beverages, gluten can be present in beers, ales, and certain spirits that are made from gluten-containing grains.

However, the distillation process used in the production of many spirits, including whiskey, can remove gluten proteins. This is because gluten proteins are too large to pass through the distillation process. But the question remains: Is Crown Royal Apple gluten-free?
