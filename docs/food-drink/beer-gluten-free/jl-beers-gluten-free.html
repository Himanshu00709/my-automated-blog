Understanding Gluten-Free Beer

Gluten-free beer is brewed without the use of gluten-containing grains such as wheat, barley, or rye. Instead, these beers are made from alternative grains like sorghum, rice, millet, or corn. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, making gluten-free beer a crucial option for those who still want to enjoy a cold brew.

JL Beers has recognized the importance of offering gluten-free options and has curated a selection of beers that cater to this need. Their commitment to inclusivity ensures that everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions, can enjoy a great beer experience.
