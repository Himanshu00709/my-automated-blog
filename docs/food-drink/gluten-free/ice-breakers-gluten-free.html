Understanding Gluten and Gluten-Free Diets

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. A gluten-free diet eliminates all sources of gluten, requiring careful scrutiny of food labels and ingredients.

For those on a gluten-free diet, finding safe and enjoyable snacks can be a challenge. This is where Ice Breakers gluten-free products come into play. By offering gluten-free mints and gums, Ice Breakers provides a convenient and safe option for those who need to avoid gluten.
