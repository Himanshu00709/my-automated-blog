Understanding Gluten and Gluten-Free Products

Before we delve into whether Lone River Ranch Rita is gluten-free, it’s essential to understand what gluten is and why it matters. Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many people seek out gluten-free products to avoid these adverse effects.

Gluten-free products are those that contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, as defined by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA). This threshold is considered safe for most individuals with gluten-related disorders.
