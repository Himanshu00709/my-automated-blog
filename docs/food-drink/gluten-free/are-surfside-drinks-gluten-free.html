Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health.

When it comes to beverages, gluten can sometimes be a hidden ingredient, especially in flavored drinks or those with added ingredients. Therefore, it's crucial to scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes of any drink to ensure it's safe for those avoiding gluten.
