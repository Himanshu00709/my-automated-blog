Why Choose Gluten-Free Drinks?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, consuming gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to skin rashes and fatigue. Even small amounts of gluten can trigger these symptoms, making it essential for affected individuals to avoid gluten-containing products.

Alcoholic beverages are a common source of hidden gluten. Many beers, ales, and lagers are made from barley or wheat, and even some spirits and liqueurs may contain gluten due to their production processes. This is why gluten-free drinks like Malibu Splash are so important—they provide a safe and enjoyable option for those who need to avoid gluten.
