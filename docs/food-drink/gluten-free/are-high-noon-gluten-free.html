Are High Noon Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

High Noon has become a popular choice for those seeking a refreshing, low-calorie alcoholic beverage. But for individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the question of whether High Noon is gluten-free is crucial. In this article, we’ll dive deep into the ingredients, brewing process, and certifications to determine if High Noon is safe for a gluten-free diet.

What is High Noon?

High Noon is a brand of hard seltzers that has gained significant popularity in recent years. Known for its crisp, refreshing taste and low-calorie content, High Noon is made with real fruit juice and vodka. Unlike many other hard seltzers that use malted barley or other gluten-containing grains, High Noon is crafted with a focus on simplicity and purity, which makes it a strong candidate for being gluten-free.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe autoimmune reactions. Therefore, it’s essential for those with gluten-related disorders to carefully scrutinize the ingredients and production processes of the foods and beverages they consume.

Is High Noon Gluten-Free?

Yes, High Noon is gluten-free. The brand uses vodka made from corn, which is naturally gluten-free, and real fruit juice, ensuring that no gluten-containing ingredients are used in the production process. Additionally, High Noon is certified gluten-free by the Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG), which provides an extra layer of assurance for consumers.

Ingredients in High Noon

High Noon’s ingredients are straightforward and free from gluten-containing grains. Here’s a breakdown of the primary components:

  • Vodka: Made from corn, which is gluten-free.
  • Real Fruit Juice: Adds natural flavor without the need for gluten-based additives.
  • Carbonated Water: Provides the refreshing fizz.
  • Natural Flavors: Derived from fruits and other natural sources.

Certifications and Testing

High Noon’s gluten-free status is backed by rigorous testing and certification. The Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG) has certified High Noon as gluten-free, meaning it meets strict standards for gluten content (less than 20 parts per million). This certification is crucial for individuals with celiac disease, as it ensures that the product is safe for consumption.

FAQs About High Noon and Gluten

1. Is High Noon safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, High Noon is safe for people with celiac disease. It is certified gluten-free by the Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG), ensuring that it contains less than 20 parts per million of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for those with celiac disease.

2. Does High Noon contain any gluten-containing ingredients?

No, High Noon does not contain any gluten-containing ingredients. The vodka is made from corn, and the other ingredients, such as real fruit juice and carbonated water, are naturally gluten-free.

3. How does High Noon compare to other gluten-free alcoholic beverages?

High Noon stands out among gluten-free alcoholic beverages due to its use of real fruit juice and corn-based vodka. Many other gluten-free options may use alternative grains or lack the refreshing taste that High Noon offers.

4. Can I find High Noon in most liquor stores?

Yes, High Noon is widely available in most liquor stores and supermarkets. Its popularity has made it a staple in the hard seltzer section, and it’s often found alongside other gluten-free beverages.

Conclusion

High Noon is a gluten-free hard seltzer that offers a refreshing and safe option for individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. With its use of corn-based vodka, real fruit juice, and certification from the Gluten Intolerance Group, High Noon provides a delicious and worry-free choice for those looking to enjoy a gluten-free alcoholic beverage. Whether you’re at a party, relaxing at home, or enjoying a day out, High Noon is a reliable and tasty option that you can feel good about consuming.
