Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into the specifics of Bricktown Brewery’s gluten-free menu, it’s important to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from mild discomfort to severe health issues. Therefore, restaurants that offer gluten-free options must take extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination and ensure that their dishes are truly gluten-free.

Bricktown Brewery’s Commitment to Gluten-Free Dining

Bricktown Brewery has taken significant steps to accommodate gluten-free diners. Their gluten-free menu is carefully curated to include a variety of appetizers, entrees, and even desserts. This commitment to gluten-free dining has made Bricktown Brewery a popular choice for those who need to avoid gluten.
