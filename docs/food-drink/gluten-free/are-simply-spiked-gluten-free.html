Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Before we dive into the specifics of Simply Spiked, it's essential to understand what gluten is and why it matters. Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Even for those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can cause symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue.

Given the potential health risks, it's crucial for individuals with gluten-related disorders to carefully scrutinize the ingredients and production processes of the foods and beverages they consume. This is especially true for alcoholic beverages, which can sometimes contain hidden sources of gluten.

Are Simply Spiked Drinks Gluten-Free?

The short answer is yes, Simply Spiked drinks are gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Simply Spiked beverages are made with gluten-free ingredients and are produced in a facility that takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. For example, if the facility also processes gluten-containing products, there is a possibility of cross-contamination. To mitigate this risk, Simply Beverages has implemented strict protocols to ensure that their Simply Spiked line remains gluten-free.
