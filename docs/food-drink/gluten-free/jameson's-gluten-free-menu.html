Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into Jameson's menu, it's essential to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. A gluten-free diet eliminates these grains and any products derived from them. For restaurants, this means ensuring that all ingredients, preparation methods, and cooking surfaces are free from gluten contamination.

Jameson's Commitment to Gluten-Free Dining

Jameson's has made a significant commitment to providing safe and enjoyable dining experiences for gluten-free customers. The restaurant has implemented strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination, including separate preparation areas and dedicated cooking utensils. Additionally, Jameson's staff is trained to understand the needs of gluten-free diners, ensuring that every meal is prepared with care and attention to detail.

FAQs About Jameson's Gluten-Free Menu

Is Jameson's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Jameson's takes great care to prevent cross-contamination, making their gluten-free menu safe for those with celiac disease. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions.

Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

While Jameson's gluten-free menu is extensive, vegan options may be limited. It's best to check with the restaurant for specific vegan-friendly dishes.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, Jameson's is happy to accommodate customizations to make dishes gluten-free. Be sure to communicate your needs clearly to your server.

Are the gluten-free desserts made in-house?

Many of Jameson's gluten-free desserts are made in-house, ensuring freshness and quality. However, some items may be sourced from trusted gluten-free suppliers.
