What is Lone River Ranch Rita?

Lone River Ranch Rita is a premium, ready-to-drink cocktail inspired by the flavors of the West Texas ranch lifestyle. Crafted with real lime juice and a blend of premium spirits, Ranch Rita delivers the perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and refreshing flavors. It’s a margarita-style drink that comes in a variety of flavors, including Classic Lime, Spicy, and Rio Red Grapefruit, catering to a wide range of palates.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Cocktails?

For individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding gluten-free alcoholic beverages can be a challenge. Many traditional cocktails and beers contain gluten, which can cause adverse reactions. Lone River Ranch Rita is proudly gluten-free, making it a safe and enjoyable option for those who need to avoid gluten. But even if you don’t have dietary restrictions, gluten-free cocktails like Ranch Rita are often lighter and easier to digest, making them a great choice for anyone looking to enjoy a refreshing drink without the heaviness of gluten.
