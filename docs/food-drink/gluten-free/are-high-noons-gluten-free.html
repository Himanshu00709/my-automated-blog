Are High Noons Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

High Noon is a popular brand of hard seltzers that has gained a significant following for its refreshing taste and low-calorie content. As more people become health-conscious and adopt specific dietary restrictions, such as gluten-free diets, it's essential to know whether your favorite beverages align with these requirements. In this article, we'll dive deep into whether High Noons are gluten-free, explore the ingredients, and provide answers to frequently asked questions.

What Are High Noons?

High Noon is a brand of hard seltzers that combines real fruit juice with vodka to create a light, refreshing alcoholic beverage. Known for its crisp taste and low calorie count, High Noon has become a go-to choice for those looking to enjoy a drink without the guilt. The brand offers a variety of flavors, including Pineapple, Watermelon, Black Cherry, and Grapefruit, making it a versatile option for different taste preferences.

Are High Noons Gluten-Free?

Yes, High Noons are gluten-free. The primary ingredients in High Noon hard seltzers are vodka, sparkling water, and real fruit juice. Vodka, when distilled from gluten-containing grains like wheat, barley, or rye, is generally considered gluten-free because the distillation process removes gluten proteins. However, it's important to note that some individuals with severe gluten sensitivities or celiac disease may still react to distilled spirits made from gluten-containing grains. High Noon uses vodka distilled from corn, which is naturally gluten-free, making it a safe choice for those avoiding gluten.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Beverages?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and skin problems. Even for those without a diagnosed condition, some people choose to avoid gluten as part of a healthier lifestyle. Gluten-free beverages like High Noon provide a safe and enjoyable option for those who want to indulge without worrying about adverse reactions.

FAQs About High Noons and Gluten

1. Are all High Noon flavors gluten-free?

Yes, all High Noon flavors are gluten-free. The brand uses the same base ingredients across all its products, ensuring consistency in their gluten-free status.

2. Can people with celiac disease drink High Noon?

Yes, people with celiac disease can safely consume High Noon hard seltzers, as they are made with gluten-free ingredients and distilled from corn.

3. Does High Noon contain any allergens?

High Noon does not contain common allergens like gluten, nuts, or dairy. However, it's always a good idea to check the label for any specific concerns.

4. How many calories are in a High Noon?

Each High Noon hard seltzer contains approximately 100 calories, making it a low-calorie option compared to many other alcoholic beverages.

5. Is High Noon suitable for a keto diet?

High Noon is relatively low in carbs, with about 2 grams per can, making it a suitable option for those following a keto diet.

Conclusion

High Noon hard seltzers are a fantastic choice for anyone looking for a refreshing, gluten-free alcoholic beverage. Made with real fruit juice and distilled from corn, they offer a safe and enjoyable option for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. With a variety of flavors and a low-calorie count, High Noon is a versatile drink that can be enjoyed on various occasions. Whether you're at a party, relaxing at home, or enjoying a day out, you can sip on a High Noon with confidence, knowing it aligns with your dietary needs.
