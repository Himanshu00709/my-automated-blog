Why Choose Gluten-Free Beverages?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Even for those without gluten intolerance, reducing gluten intake can contribute to better overall health. Cayman Jack’s gluten-free beverages provide a safe and delicious option for anyone looking to avoid gluten while still enjoying a flavorful drink.
