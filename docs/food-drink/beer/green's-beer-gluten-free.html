The Rise of Gluten-Free Beer

Gluten-free beer has gained significant popularity over the past decade, driven by the increasing awareness of gluten-related disorders and the growing demand for dietary alternatives. Green's Beer has been at the forefront of this movement, producing beers that are not only safe for those with gluten intolerance but also delicious and satisfying for all beer lovers.

What Makes Green's Beer Gluten-Free?

Green's Beer is brewed using gluten-free grains such as millet, buckwheat, and rice. These grains are carefully selected to ensure that the final product is free from gluten, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The brewing process is meticulously controlled to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that every bottle of Green's Beer meets the highest standards of gluten-free certification.

The Brewing Process

Green's Beer is brewed using traditional Belgian brewing techniques, combined with modern innovations to ensure a gluten-free product. The process begins with the selection of gluten-free grains, which are then malted and mashed to extract fermentable sugars. The wort is boiled, hops are added for bitterness and aroma, and the mixture is fermented using specialized yeast strains. The beer is then carefully filtered and bottled, with each step monitored to maintain gluten-free integrity.
