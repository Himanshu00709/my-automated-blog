Redd's Apple Ale has become a popular choice for those who enjoy a refreshing, fruity twist on traditional beer. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and other grains. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many individuals seek out gluten-free alternatives to their favorite foods and beverages, including beer.

What is Redd's Apple Ale?

Ingredients and Brewing Process

To determine whether Redd's Apple Ale is gluten-free, it's essential to examine its ingredients and brewing process. The primary ingredients in Redd's Apple Ale include:

  • Malted barley
  • Corn syrup

Malted barley is a key ingredient in traditional beer brewing and contains gluten. The presence of malted barley in Redd's Apple Ale suggests that it is not gluten-free. Gluten Content in Redd's Apple Ale

Redd's Apple Ale is brewed using malted barley, which means it contains gluten. The brewing process does not include any steps to remove gluten, so the final product retains its gluten content.
