The History of Estrella Damm Daura

Estrella Damm, founded in 1876 in Barcelona, Spain, has a long-standing reputation for producing high-quality beers. The brewery has always been at the forefront of innovation, and their commitment to inclusivity led to the creation of Daura, a gluten-free beer that doesn't compromise on taste or quality. Launched in 2006, Daura was developed in collaboration with the Spanish Celiac Association to ensure it meets the strictest gluten-free standards.

Daura is brewed using traditional methods but with a unique twist: the gluten is removed during the brewing process, making it safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. The result is a beer that retains the rich flavors and aromas of a traditional lager, making it a favorite among both gluten-free and regular beer drinkers.

What Makes Estrella Damm Daura Unique?

Estrella Damm Daura stands out for several reasons:

  • Gluten-Free Certification: Daura is certified gluten-free by the European Union and the Spanish Federation of Celiac Associations. It contains less than 6 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, making it safe for those with celiac disease.
  • Traditional Brewing Methods: Despite being gluten-free, Daura is brewed using traditional methods, including the use of malted barley. The gluten is removed through a proprietary process, ensuring the beer retains its authentic flavor.
  • Rich Flavor Profile: Daura boasts a balanced flavor profile with notes of malt, hops, and a slight sweetness. It has a smooth, crisp finish that makes it highly drinkable.
  • Versatility: Daura pairs well with a variety of foods, making it a versatile choice for different occasions. Whether you're enjoying a casual meal or a formal dinner, Daura complements a wide range of dishes.

What is the alcohol content of Daura?

Can I find Estrella Damm Daura outside of Spain?

How should I serve Estrella Damm Daura?
