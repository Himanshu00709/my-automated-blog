What is Estrella Damm Daura?

Estrella Damm Daura is a gluten-free beer produced by the Spanish brewery Estrella Damm. It was specifically crafted to cater to individuals who cannot consume gluten due to medical conditions like celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. What sets Daura apart is its unique brewing process, which reduces the gluten content to less than 6 parts per million (ppm), well below the 20 ppm threshold considered safe for most people with gluten intolerance.

The Brewing Process

The brewing process of Estrella Damm Daura is a testament to the innovation and dedication of the Estrella Damm team. The beer is brewed using traditional ingredients like barley and hops, but it undergoes a specialized filtration process to remove gluten. This process ensures that the beer retains its rich, malty flavor while being safe for those who need to avoid gluten.
