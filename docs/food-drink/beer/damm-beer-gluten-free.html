Damm Beer Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

What is Damm Beer Gluten-Free?

Damm Beer Gluten-Free is a product of the Spanish brewery, Damm. Known for its high-quality beers, Damm has been a staple in the Spanish beer market for over a century. The gluten-free variant is a testament to the brewery's commitment to innovation and inclusivity, catering to the growing number of consumers who require gluten-free options.

Damm Beer Gluten-Free is crafted using a unique brewing process that ensures the final product contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, making it safe for most people with gluten intolerance. The beer is made from a blend of malted cereals, including rice and millet, which are naturally gluten-free. This combination results in a light, refreshing beer with a crisp finish, perfect for any occasion.

Step Description
