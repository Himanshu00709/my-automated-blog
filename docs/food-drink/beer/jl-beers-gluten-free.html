Understanding Gluten-Free Beer

Gluten-free beer is brewed without the use of barley, wheat, or rye—grains that contain gluten. Instead, brewers use alternative grains like sorghum, rice, millet, or even gluten-free oats. The brewing process for gluten-free beer is meticulous, as cross-contamination can easily occur. JL Beers takes extra precautions to ensure their gluten-free beers are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.
