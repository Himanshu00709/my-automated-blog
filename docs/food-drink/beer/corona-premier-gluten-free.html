What is Corona Premier?

Corona Premier is a light beer introduced by Grupo Modelo, the Mexican brewery known for its iconic Corona Extra. Launched in 2018, Corona Premier is marketed as a lighter, lower-calorie alternative to Corona Extra, with a crisp and refreshing taste. It is designed to appeal to health-conscious consumers who are looking for a beer that fits into a balanced lifestyle without compromising on flavor.

Is Corona Premier Gluten-Free?

One of the most common questions about Corona Premier is whether it is gluten-free. The answer is yes, Corona Premier is considered gluten-free. The beer is brewed using traditional ingredients like barley, which contains gluten, but it undergoes a process that removes the gluten to levels below 20 parts per million (ppm), the threshold set by the FDA for gluten-free labeling. This makes Corona Premier a safe option for individuals with gluten sensitivity or celiac disease.
