Creating a gluten-free beer that retains the rich flavors and textures of traditional beer is no small feat. Deschutes Brewery employs a meticulous brewing process to ensure their gluten-free beers meet the highest standards. Here's a breakdown of the process:

Step Description
1. Ingredient Selection Deschutes uses gluten-free grains such as sorghum, millet, and rice. These grains are carefully selected to ensure they are free from gluten contamination.

5. Packaging The final product is packaged in a controlled environment to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring the beer remains gluten-free from brewery to glass.

Health Benefits of Gluten-Free Beer

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and skin rashes. Switching to gluten-free beer like those offered by Deschutes can help alleviate these symptoms and improve overall well-being.

Additionally, gluten-free beers are often made with alternative grains that can offer their own nutritional benefits. For example, sorghum is rich in antioxidants, while millet is a good source of magnesium and phosphorus. These grains can contribute to a healthier diet, making gluten-free beer a smart choice for health-conscious consumers.
