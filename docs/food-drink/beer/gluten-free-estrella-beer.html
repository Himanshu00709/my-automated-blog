What is Estrella Damm Daura?

Estrella Damm Daura is a gluten-free beer produced by the renowned Spanish brewery, Estrella Damm. It is crafted using traditional brewing methods but with a unique twist: the gluten content is reduced to less than 6 parts per million (ppm), making it safe for individuals with celiac disease. The beer is brewed with barley, but the gluten is removed during the brewing process, ensuring that the final product retains the classic taste of beer without the harmful effects of gluten.

The Brewing Process

Estrella Damm Daura is brewed using a meticulous process that ensures the gluten content is minimized while preserving the beer’s flavor and aroma. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown of how this gluten-free beer is made:

Step Description
1. Malting Barley is malted to produce the sugars needed for fermentation.
2. Mashing The malted barley is mixed with water to create a mash, which is then heated to extract fermentable sugars.
3. Fermentation Yeast is added to the mash to ferment the sugars, producing alcohol and carbon dioxide.
4. Gluten Removal After fermentation, the beer undergoes a specialized process to remove gluten, reducing it to less than 6 ppm.
5. Filtration The beer is filtered to remove any remaining solids, resulting in a clear and crisp final product.
6. Packaging The beer is bottled or canned, ready to be enjoyed by gluten-free beer lovers.
