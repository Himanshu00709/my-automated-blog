Gluten-Free Beer: A Deep Dive into New Grist

For beer enthusiasts with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding a beer that doesn’t compromise on taste while being safe to consume can be a challenge. Enter New Grist, a pioneering gluten-free beer that has been making waves in the craft beer community. In this article, we’ll explore what makes New Grist stand out, its brewing process, nutritional profile, and why it’s a top choice for those seeking a gluten-free beer option.

What is New Grist?

New Grist is a gluten-free beer produced by Lakefront Brewery, a well-known craft brewery based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Launched in 2006, New Grist was one of the first gluten-free beers to hit the market, and it quickly gained a reputation for its crisp, refreshing taste and high-quality ingredients. Unlike traditional beers that are brewed with barley or wheat, New Grist is made from sorghum and rice, making it safe for those with gluten intolerances.

The Brewing Process

The brewing process of New Grist is a testament to the innovation and dedication of Lakefront Brewery. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown of how this gluten-free beer is crafted:

Step Description
1. Milling Sorghum and rice are milled to break down the grains, preparing them for the mashing process.
2. Mashing The milled grains are mixed with water and heated to convert the starches into fermentable sugars.
3. Lautering The liquid (wort) is separated from the grain solids, ensuring a clear and smooth base for fermentation.
4. Boiling The wort is boiled, and hops are added to impart bitterness and aroma to the beer.
5. Fermentation Yeast is added to the cooled wort, initiating the fermentation process where sugars are converted into alcohol and carbon dioxide.
6. Conditioning The beer is conditioned to allow flavors to mature and develop, resulting in a well-rounded taste.
7. Packaging Finally, the beer is filtered, carbonated, and packaged, ready to be enjoyed by consumers.

Why Choose New Grist?

New Grist has earned its place as a favorite among gluten-free beer drinkers for several reasons:

  • Great Taste: Unlike some gluten-free beers that can taste overly sweet or lack depth, New Grist offers a balanced, crisp flavor with a hint of citrus and a clean finish.
  • Safe for Gluten-Sensitive Individuals: Brewed without barley or wheat, New Grist is certified gluten-free, making it a safe choice for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities.
  • Wide Availability: New Grist is widely available in stores and online, making it accessible to a broad audience.
  • Support for Craft Brewing: By choosing New Grist, you’re supporting a craft brewery that prioritizes quality and innovation.

FAQs About New Grist

Is New Grist completely gluten-free?

Yes, New Grist is 100% gluten-free. It is brewed without barley or wheat and is certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO).

What does New Grist taste like?

New Grist has a crisp, refreshing taste with a slight citrus note. It is well-balanced and has a clean finish, making it a great option for those who enjoy light, easy-drinking beers.

Where can I buy New Grist?

New Grist is available at many liquor stores, grocery stores, and online retailers. You can also check Lakefront Brewery’s website for a store locator to find the nearest retailer.

Can I drink New Grist if I have celiac disease?

Yes, New Grist is safe for individuals with celiac disease as it is brewed without gluten-containing ingredients and is certified gluten-free.

How does New Grist compare to other gluten-free beers?

New Grist is often praised for its superior taste and quality compared to other gluten-free beers. Its balanced flavor and clean finish make it a standout option in the gluten-free beer market.

Whether you’re a seasoned beer enthusiast or someone exploring gluten-free options, New Grist is a beer worth trying.
