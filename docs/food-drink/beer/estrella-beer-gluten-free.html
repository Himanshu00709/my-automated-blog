What is Estrella Damm Daura?

Estrella Damm Daura is a gluten-free beer produced by the Spanish brewery Estrella Damm. It is specifically crafted for individuals who are sensitive to gluten or have celiac disease. The beer is made using barley, but through a unique brewing process, the gluten content is reduced to less than 6 parts per million (ppm), which is well below the threshold considered safe for most people with gluten intolerance.

The Brewing Process

The brewing process of Estrella Damm Daura is what sets it apart from traditional beers. The beer is brewed using barley, which contains gluten. However, during the brewing process, the gluten proteins are broken down using a special enzyme. This enzyme reduces the gluten content to a level that is safe for most people with gluten sensitivity. The result is a beer that retains the flavor and aroma of traditional beer but is safe for those who need to avoid gluten.

Estrella Damm Daura is considered gluten-free as it contains less than 6 ppm of gluten, which is below the threshold considered safe for most people with gluten intolerance. However, individuals with severe celiac disease should consult their healthcare provider before consuming.

Estrella Damm Daura is often praised for its flavor, which is closer to traditional beer than many other gluten-free options. Its brewing process ensures that it retains the characteristics of a classic lager while being safe for those avoiding gluten.
