New Belgium Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, and the beer industry is no exception. New Belgium Brewing, a pioneer in the craft beer movement, has responded to this growing demand with a range of gluten-free options that cater to those with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or anyone looking to reduce their gluten intake. In this article, we’ll dive deep into New Belgium’s gluten-free offerings, exploring their brewing process, flavor profiles, and what sets them apart in the competitive gluten-free beer market.

The Rise of Gluten-Free Beer

Gluten-free beer has come a long way since its inception. Initially, the options were limited and often lacked the depth of flavor found in traditional beers. However, with advancements in brewing technology and a better understanding of gluten-free grains, breweries like New Belgium have been able to create gluten-free beers that rival their gluten-containing counterparts in both taste and quality.

New Belgium’s Gluten-Free Brewing Process

New Belgium Brewing has always been at the forefront of innovation, and their approach to gluten-free beer is no different. The brewery uses a combination of gluten-free grains, such as sorghum, millet, and rice, to create a base for their beers. These grains are carefully selected to ensure they provide the necessary enzymes and sugars for fermentation, resulting in a beer that is both gluten-free and full of flavor.

One of the key aspects of New Belgium’s gluten-free brewing process is their commitment to quality control. The brewery employs rigorous testing methods to ensure that their gluten-free beers contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold set by the FDA for a product to be considered gluten-free. This ensures that even those with severe gluten sensitivities can enjoy New Belgium’s gluten-free offerings without worry.

1. Are New Belgium’s gluten-free beers safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, New Belgium’s gluten-free beers are tested to ensure they contain less than 20 ppm of gluten, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

2. What grains are used in New Belgium’s gluten-free beers?

New Belgium uses a combination of gluten-free grains, including sorghum, millet, and rice, to create their gluten-free beers.

3. Where can I buy New Belgium’s gluten-free beers?

New Belgium’s gluten-free beers are available at select retailers and can also be found in many bars and restaurants. You can use the brewery’s online beer finder to locate a retailer near you.

4. Do New Belgium’s gluten-free beers taste different from their regular beers?
