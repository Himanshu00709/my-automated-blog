What is Gluten-Free Beer?

Gluten-free beer is a type of beer that is brewed without the use of gluten-containing grains such as wheat, barley, or rye. Instead, these beers are made from alternative grains like sorghum, rice, or millet. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, gluten-free beer offers a safe and enjoyable way to indulge in a cold brew without the adverse health effects associated with gluten consumption.
