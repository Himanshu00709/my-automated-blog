What Makes Devil's Backbone Gluten-Free?

Devil's Backbone gluten-free products are crafted using alternative grains and ingredients that do not contain gluten. Traditional beers are made from barley, wheat, or rye, all of which contain gluten. However, Devil's Backbone uses gluten-free grains such as sorghum, millet, and rice to create their gluten-free beverages. These grains are carefully selected for their ability to produce a flavorful and satisfying beer without compromising on taste or quality.

The brewing process for gluten-free beers is similar to that of traditional beers, but with a few key differences. The grains are milled, mashed, and fermented, but special care is taken to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients. This ensures that the final product is safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.
