What is Gluten-Free Stella?

Gluten-Free Stella is a brand that specializes in producing high-quality, gluten-free food products. The brand is particularly known for its gluten-free beer, which has gained a loyal following among those who must avoid gluten due to health reasons or personal preference. Gluten-Free Stella is crafted to provide the same great taste and quality as traditional Stella Artois, but without the gluten that can cause issues for many people.

The brand is committed to using only the finest ingredients, ensuring that each product meets strict gluten-free standards. This dedication to quality has made Gluten-Free Stella a trusted name in the gluten-free community.

How Gluten-Free Stella is Made

The production process of Gluten-Free Stella is carefully designed to ensure that the final product is completely free from gluten. Here’s a step-by-step overview of how it’s made:

  1. Selection of Gluten-Free Ingredients: The process begins with the careful selection of gluten-free grains, such as sorghum, millet, or rice, which are used as the base for the beer.
  2. Brewing Process: The selected grains are then brewed using traditional methods, but with special attention to avoiding cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  3. Testing for Gluten: Throughout the brewing process, the beer is rigorously tested to ensure that it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold for being considered gluten-free.
  4. Packaging: Once the beer has been confirmed to be gluten-free, it is packaged in a facility that is dedicated to gluten-free products to prevent any risk of contamination.
