What is Estrella Damm Daura?

Estrella Damm Daura is a gluten-free beer produced by the Spanish brewery Estrella Damm. It is specifically crafted for individuals who are gluten intolerant or have celiac disease. Unlike many other gluten-free beers that use alternative grains like sorghum or rice, Daura is made from barley, which is traditionally a gluten-containing grain. However, through a specialized brewing process, the gluten content is reduced to below 6 parts per million (ppm), making it safe for most people with gluten sensitivities.

The Brewing Process

The brewing process of Estrella Damm Daura is what sets it apart from other gluten-free beers. The beer is brewed using barley, which is then subjected to a unique enzymatic process that breaks down the gluten proteins. This process ensures that the final product contains less than 6 ppm of gluten, which is well below the threshold considered safe for those with celiac disease. The result is a beer that retains the traditional flavors and aromas of a classic lager, without the harmful effects of gluten.
