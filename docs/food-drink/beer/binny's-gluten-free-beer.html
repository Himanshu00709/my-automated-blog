What is Binny's Gluten-Free Beer?

Binny's Gluten-Free Beer is a craft beer specifically designed to meet the needs of individuals who must avoid gluten in their diet. Unlike traditional beers that are brewed with barley, wheat, or rye—all of which contain gluten—Binny's uses alternative grains such as sorghum, millet, or rice. These grains are naturally gluten-free, making the beer safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

But Binny's doesn't just stop at being gluten-free. The brand is committed to delivering a beer that doesn't compromise on flavor. Whether you're a fan of light lagers, hoppy IPAs, or rich stouts, Binny's offers a variety of styles to suit different palates. The result is a beer that not only meets dietary restrictions but also satisfies the taste buds of even the most discerning beer enthusiasts.

The Brewing Process: How Binny's Achieves Gluten-Free Perfection

Brewing a gluten-free beer that tastes great is no small feat. Traditional beer brewing relies heavily on gluten-containing grains, which contribute to the beer's body, flavor, and mouthfeel. To create a gluten-free alternative, Binny's has had to rethink the entire brewing process.

First, Binny's selects high-quality gluten-free grains such as sorghum, millet, or rice. These grains are then malted and mashed to extract fermentable sugars. The mash is then boiled, and hops are added for bitterness and aroma. After boiling, the wort is cooled and fermented with yeast, which converts the sugars into alcohol and carbon dioxide.

One of the challenges of brewing gluten-free beer is achieving the same mouthfeel and body as traditional beer. To address this, Binny's often adds additional ingredients like quinoa or buckwheat, which help to enhance the beer's texture and complexity. The result is a beer that not only tastes great but also feels like a traditional beer in your mouth.
