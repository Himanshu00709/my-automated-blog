The Rise of Gluten-Free Beer

Gluten-free beer has come a long way since its inception. Initially, gluten-free beers were often criticized for their lack of flavor and poor texture. However, with advancements in brewing technology and a better understanding of gluten-free ingredients, breweries have been able to produce beers that rival their gluten-containing counterparts. New Grist is a prime example of this evolution, offering a beer that is not only safe for those with gluten sensitivities but also delicious and satisfying.

What Makes New Grist Unique?

New Grist is brewed by Lakefront Brewery, a well-respected name in the craft beer industry. What sets New Grist apart is its use of sorghum and rice as the primary ingredients, which are naturally gluten-free. This combination results in a beer that is light, crisp, and refreshing, with a clean finish that appeals to a wide range of beer drinkers.
