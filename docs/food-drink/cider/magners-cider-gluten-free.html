What Makes Magners Cider Gluten-Free?

Magners Cider is crafted from the finest apples, which are naturally gluten-free. Unlike beer, which is typically made from barley, wheat, or rye—all of which contain gluten—cider is made from fermented apple juice. This means that Magners Cider is inherently free from gluten, making it a safe and enjoyable option for those who need to avoid gluten in their diet.

To ensure that Magners Cider remains gluten-free, the production process is carefully monitored. The apples are pressed to extract the juice, which is then fermented using yeast. The fermentation process converts the natural sugars in the apple juice into alcohol, resulting in a crisp and refreshing cider. Throughout this process, no gluten-containing ingredients are introduced, ensuring that the final product is 100% gluten-free.
