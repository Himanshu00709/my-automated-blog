2 Towns Ciderhouse: A Gluten-Free Delight

For those who love cider but need to avoid gluten, 2 Towns Ciderhouse offers a refreshing and delicious solution. Based in Oregon, 2 Towns Ciderhouse has become a beloved name in the craft cider industry, known for its commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation. Their gluten-free ciders are crafted with care, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a crisp, flavorful beverage without worrying about gluten content. In this article, we’ll dive deep into what makes 2 Towns Ciderhouse a standout choice for gluten-free cider enthusiasts.

What Makes 2 Towns Ciderhouse Gluten-Free?

2 Towns Ciderhouse takes pride in producing ciders that are naturally gluten-free. Unlike beer, which is typically made from barley, wheat, or rye—all of which contain gluten—cider is made from fermented apples. This means that, by nature, cider is gluten-free. However, 2 Towns goes the extra mile to ensure that their ciders are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity by adhering to strict production practices that prevent cross-contamination.

Ingredients and Production Process

2 Towns Ciderhouse uses only the finest ingredients, sourcing apples from local orchards in the Pacific Northwest. Their ciders are crafted using traditional methods, with a modern twist. The apples are pressed, fermented, and then carefully blended to create a variety of flavors that cater to different palates. The entire process is designed to maintain the integrity of the cider while ensuring it remains gluten-free.

FAQs About 2 Towns Ciderhouse Gluten-Free Ciders

Are all 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders gluten-free?

Yes, all ciders produced by 2 Towns Ciderhouse are naturally gluten-free. They are made from apples and do not contain any gluten-containing grains.

Is 2 Towns Ciderhouse certified gluten-free?

While 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders are naturally gluten-free, they are not certified gluten-free. However, they adhere to strict production practices to prevent cross-contamination.

Where can I buy 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders?

2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders are available at many grocery stores, liquor stores, and online retailers. You can also visit their taproom in Corvallis, Oregon, to sample their offerings.

What is the alcohol content of 2 Towns Ciderhouse ciders?

The alcohol content varies by cider, but most range between 5.5% and 6.9% ABV. Check the label for specific details.
