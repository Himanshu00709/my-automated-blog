Gluten-Free Brewery Denver: A Haven for Beer Lovers with Dietary Restrictions

Denver, Colorado, is renowned for its vibrant craft beer scene, but for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding a brewery that caters to their needs can be a challenge. Fortunately, Denver is home to several exceptional gluten-free breweries that offer a wide range of delicious, safe-to-consume beers. In this article, we’ll explore the best gluten-free breweries in Denver, what makes them unique, and why they’re a must-visit for anyone looking to enjoy a cold brew without the gluten.

Why Gluten-Free Beer?

Gluten-free beer is crafted to exclude gluten-containing grains like barley, wheat, and rye, which are traditionally used in beer production. Instead, gluten-free breweries use alternative grains such as sorghum, millet, rice, and quinoa. This makes gluten-free beer a safe and enjoyable option for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. The rise in demand for gluten-free products has led to an increase in the quality and variety of gluten-free beers available, and Denver’s breweries are at the forefront of this movement.

Top Gluten-Free Breweries in Denver

1. Holidaily Brewing Company

Holidaily Brewing Company is one of the most well-known gluten-free breweries in Denver. Founded by Karen Hertz, who was diagnosed with celiac disease, Holidaily is dedicated to producing 100% gluten-free beer. Their taproom in Golden, just outside Denver, offers a wide variety of beers, from IPAs to stouts, all crafted with gluten-free ingredients. Holidaily’s commitment to quality and safety has earned them a loyal following among gluten-free beer enthusiasts.

2. New Planet Beer

New Planet Beer is another standout in the Denver gluten-free beer scene. Established in 2009, New Planet was one of the first breweries in the country to focus exclusively on gluten-free beer. Their lineup includes a variety of styles, including pale ales, amber ales, and even a gluten-free Belgian-style ale. New Planet’s beers are widely available in Denver and beyond, making them a convenient option for gluten-free beer lovers.

3. Ceria Brewing Company

Ceria Brewing Company, founded by the legendary brewer Keith Villa, offers a unique twist on gluten-free beer. Ceria specializes in THC-infused and non-alcoholic gluten-free beers, catering to a diverse audience. Their Grainwave Belgian-style white ale is a fan favorite, offering a refreshing and flavorful option for those avoiding gluten. Ceria’s innovative approach to brewing has made them a standout in Denver’s gluten-free beer market.

What Makes Denver’s Gluten-Free Breweries Unique?

Denver’s gluten-free breweries stand out for their commitment to quality, innovation, and inclusivity. These breweries go beyond simply removing gluten from their beers; they focus on creating flavorful, high-quality brews that can compete with traditional beers. Additionally, many of these breweries offer taprooms and tasting experiences, allowing visitors to enjoy their beers in a social setting. This emphasis on community and quality has helped Denver’s gluten-free breweries carve out a niche in the city’s thriving craft beer scene.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Breweries in Denver

1. Are all beers at gluten-free breweries safe for celiacs?

Yes, beers produced by dedicated gluten-free breweries like Holidaily and New Planet are crafted to be safe for individuals with celiac disease. These breweries use gluten-free ingredients and follow strict protocols to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Can I visit gluten-free breweries if I don’t have a gluten intolerance?

Absolutely! Gluten-free breweries welcome everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions. Many people enjoy the unique flavors and high-quality brews offered by these breweries, even if they don’t need to avoid gluten.

3. Where can I buy gluten-free beer in Denver?

Gluten-free beers from Denver’s breweries are available at local liquor stores, grocery stores, and online retailers. Additionally, you can visit the breweries’ taprooms to enjoy their beers on-site.

4. Are gluten-free beers more expensive than regular beers?

Gluten-free beers can be slightly more expensive due to the cost of alternative ingredients and specialized brewing processes. However, many beer lovers find the quality and taste to be well worth the price.

Conclusion

Denver’s gluten-free breweries offer a unique and inclusive experience for beer lovers with dietary restrictions. With a focus on quality, innovation, and community, these breweries have become a vital part of Denver’s craft beer scene. Whether you’re a celiac, gluten-intolerant, or simply curious about gluten-free beer, Denver’s breweries provide a welcoming environment to explore and enjoy a wide range of delicious brews. So, the next time you’re in Denver, be sure to raise a glass at one of these exceptional gluten-free breweries!
