Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Before we delve into the specifics of PJ's Coffee gluten-free menu, it's important to understand what a gluten-free diet entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people choose to avoid gluten entirely, making it essential for restaurants and coffee shops to offer gluten-free options.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at PJ's Coffee

While PJ's Coffee offers several gluten-free options, it's important to take precautions when ordering to ensure that your food and beverages are safe to consume. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Ask About Ingredients: Always ask the barista about the ingredients in your chosen beverage or food item. Some syrups, toppings, or additives may contain gluten.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free items come into contact with gluten-containing items. Ask the staff about their procedures for preventing cross-contamination.
  • Review the Menu Online: Before visiting PJ's Coffee, review their menu online to identify gluten-free options. This will help you make informed choices when you arrive.
