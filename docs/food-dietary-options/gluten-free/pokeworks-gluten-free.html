Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health. Pokeworks recognizes this need and has made significant efforts to accommodate gluten-free diners.

How Pokeworks Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Pokeworks takes gluten-free dining seriously. The restaurant has implemented several measures to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, which is crucial for those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance. Here are some of the steps Pokeworks takes to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Pokeworks uses separate utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free ingredients to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that gluten-free ingredients are not mixed with gluten-containing ones.
  • Clear Labeling: All gluten-free options are clearly labeled on the menu, making it easy for customers to identify safe choices.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Pokeworks

While Pokeworks offers a variety of gluten-free options, it's essential to take a few extra steps to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips for ordering gluten-free at Pokeworks:

  1. Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff that you require a gluten-free meal. They can guide you through the menu and ensure your order is prepared safely.
  2. Double-Check Ingredients: Even though many ingredients are gluten-free, it's always a good idea to double-check, especially with sauces and garnishes.
  3. Avoid Cross-Contamination: If you have severe gluten intolerance, ask the staff to change gloves and use clean utensils when preparing your bowl.
