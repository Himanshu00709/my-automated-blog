What is Gluta White?

Gluta White is a skin whitening and brightening supplement that primarily contains glutathione, a powerful antioxidant naturally produced in the body. Glutathione is known for its ability to detoxify the body, boost the immune system, and, most notably, lighten the skin by inhibiting the production of melanin. Melanin is the pigment responsible for the color of our skin, and its overproduction can lead to dark spots, uneven skin tone, and hyperpigmentation.

In addition to glutathione, Gluta White often contains other skin-beneficial ingredients such as collagen, vitamin C, and alpha-lipoic acid. These components work synergistically to enhance the skin's overall health, elasticity, and brightness.

How Does Gluta White Work?

The primary mechanism by which Gluta White works is through the action of glutathione. Glutathione inhibits the enzyme tyrosinase, which is crucial in the production of melanin. By reducing melanin production, glutathione helps to lighten the skin and reduce the appearance of dark spots and hyperpigmentation.

Moreover, glutathione is a potent antioxidant that helps to neutralize free radicals, which can cause oxidative stress and damage to the skin. By reducing oxidative stress, glutathione helps to maintain the skin's health and prevent premature aging.
