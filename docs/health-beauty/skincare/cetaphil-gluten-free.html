What Does Gluten-Free Skincare Mean?

Gluten-free skincare refers to products that do not contain gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. While gluten is primarily a concern for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, some individuals prefer gluten-free skincare to avoid potential skin reactions or cross-contamination. Cetaphil's gluten-free products are formulated to be safe for those who need to avoid gluten, ensuring that their skincare routine is both effective and worry-free.
