What is Gluten-Free Shampoo?

Gluten-free shampoo is exactly what it sounds like—a shampoo that does not contain any gluten-derived ingredients. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and while it’s most commonly associated with food, it can also be found in many personal care products, including shampoos. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even topical exposure to gluten can cause adverse reactions. Native Shampoo has recognized this need and has developed a line of shampoos that are not only gluten-free but also free from other harmful chemicals, making them a safe and effective choice for everyone.
