How to Use Gluta Berry Soap

Using Gluta Berry Soap is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to get the most out of your skincare routine:

  1. Wet your skin: Start by wetting your skin with lukewarm water. This helps to open up the pores and prepare the skin for cleansing.
  2. Lather the soap: Rub the Gluta Berry Soap between your hands or on a washcloth to create a rich lather.
  3. Apply to the skin: Gently massage the lather onto your skin in circular motions. Pay special attention to areas with dark spots or uneven skin tone.
  4. Rinse thoroughly: Rinse off the soap with lukewarm water, ensuring that all residue is removed.
  5. Pat dry: Gently pat your skin dry with a clean towel. Avoid rubbing, as this can cause irritation.
