What is Gluta White Soap?

Gluta White Soap is a skin-whitening soap that contains glutathione, a powerful antioxidant known for its skin-lightening properties. Glutathione works by inhibiting the production of melanin, the pigment responsible for skin color. By reducing melanin production, the soap helps to lighten the skin, reduce dark spots, and even out skin tone. In addition to glutathione, Gluta White Soap often contains other beneficial ingredients such as collagen, kojic acid, and vitamin C, which work together to nourish and rejuvenate the skin.

How to Use Gluta White Soap Effectively

To get the most out of Gluta White Soap, it’s important to use it correctly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wet Your Skin: Start by wetting your skin with lukewarm water. This helps to open up your pores and prepare your skin for cleansing.
  2. Lather the Soap: Rub the Gluta White Soap between your hands to create a rich lather. You can also use a loofah or washcloth to help generate more foam.
  3. Apply to Skin: Gently massage the lather onto your skin using circular motions. Focus on areas with dark spots or uneven skin tone.
  4. Leave On for a Few Minutes: Allow the soap to sit on your skin for 2-3 minutes. This gives the active ingredients time to penetrate the skin and work their magic.
  5. Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse off the soap with cool water to close your pores and lock in moisture.
