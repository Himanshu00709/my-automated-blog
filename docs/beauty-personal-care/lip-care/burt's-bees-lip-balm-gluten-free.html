Why Choose Gluten-Free Lip Balm?

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. While lip balms are not typically ingested, they can still pose a risk if they contain gluten. This is because lip balms are applied directly to the lips, and small amounts can be ingested accidentally. For those who are highly sensitive, even trace amounts of gluten can trigger symptoms. Therefore, choosing a gluten-free lip balm is essential for maintaining both lip health and overall well-being.
