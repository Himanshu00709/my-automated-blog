Why Choose Gluten-Free Products?

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Symptoms range from digestive issues to skin problems, making it crucial to avoid gluten in all forms, including skincare products. Even for those without gluten-related conditions, gluten-free products are often perceived as cleaner and safer, aligning with a holistic approach to health and wellness.
