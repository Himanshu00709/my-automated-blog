How to Use Gluta Body Wash Effectively

To get the most out of your Gluta Body Wash, it’s important to use it correctly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wet Your Skin: Start by wetting your skin with lukewarm water. This helps to open up your pores and prepare your skin for cleansing.
  2. Apply the Body Wash: Squeeze a small amount of Gluta Body Wash onto a loofah or your hands. Gently massage it onto your skin in circular motions, focusing on areas with dark spots or uneven tone.
  3. Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse off the body wash with lukewarm water, ensuring that all product is removed from your skin.
  4. Pat Dry: After rinsing, pat your skin dry with a clean towel. Avoid rubbing, as this can cause irritation.
  5. Moisturize: Follow up with a moisturizer to lock in hydration and keep your skin soft and supple.
