What is HelloFresh?

FAQs About Gluten-Free HelloFresh

1. Is HelloFresh safe for people with celiac disease?

HelloFresh takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free meals are safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, it’s important to note that HelloFresh’s facilities are not entirely gluten-free, so there is a slight risk of cross-contamination. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s recommended to review the ingredient labels and consult with HelloFresh’s customer service for additional information.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free meals?

Yes, HelloFresh allows you to customize your meal selections each week. You can choose from a variety of gluten-free options and swap out meals based on your preferences. This flexibility ensures that you always have meals that you’ll enjoy.

3. How much does gluten-free HelloFresh cost?

The cost of gluten-free HelloFresh varies depending on the meal plan you choose and the number of servings per meal. On average, meals start at around $8.99 per serving. HelloFresh often offers discounts and promotions for new customers, so be sure to check their website for the latest deals.

4. Are the gluten-free meals nutritionally balanced?

Yes, HelloFresh’s gluten-free meals are designed to be nutritionally balanced, providing a good mix of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats. Each recipe card includes detailed nutritional information, so you can make informed choices about your meals.
