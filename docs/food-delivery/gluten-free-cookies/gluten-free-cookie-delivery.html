Why Choose Gluten-Free Cookies?

Gluten-free cookies are more than just a trend; they are a necessity for many and a preference for others. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Even for those without these conditions, reducing gluten intake can lead to improved digestion, reduced inflammation, and better overall health. Gluten-free cookies are made with alternative flours like almond, coconut, or rice flour, which not only make them safe for gluten-sensitive individuals but also add unique flavors and textures that traditional cookies can't match.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Cookie Delivery

1. Are gluten-free cookies healthier than regular cookies?

Gluten-free cookies are not inherently healthier than regular cookies, but they are a better option for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. The healthiness of a cookie depends on its ingredients, such as the type of flour, sugar, and fats used.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free cookie order?

Many gluten-free cookie delivery services offer customization options, allowing you to choose specific flavors, ingredients, or dietary preferences. Be sure to check with the service provider for their customization options.

3. How long do gluten-free cookies stay fresh?

Gluten-free cookies typically stay fresh for about 5-7 days when stored in an airtight container. Some services may offer longer shelf-life options or provide storage instructions to maintain freshness.

4. Are gluten-free cookies more expensive?

Gluten-free cookies can be more expensive than regular cookies due to the cost of alternative flours and ingredients. However, the price varies depending on the brand and service you choose.
