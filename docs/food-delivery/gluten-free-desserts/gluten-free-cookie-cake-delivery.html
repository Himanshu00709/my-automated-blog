FAQs About Gluten-Free Cookie Cake Delivery

1. Are gluten-free cookie cakes safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, as long as the cake is made in a certified gluten-free facility and uses gluten-free ingredients, it is safe for individuals with celiac disease. Always check the bakery's certifications and ingredient list to ensure safety.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free cookie cake?

Absolutely! Many delivery services offer customization options, including personalized messages, choice of toppings, and even dietary preferences like vegan or nut-free.

3. How far in advance should I order a gluten-free cookie cake?

It's best to order at least 2-3 days in advance, especially if you need the cake for a specific event. Some services offer same-day or next-day delivery, but availability may vary.

4. Do gluten-free cookie cakes taste different from regular cookie cakes?

While the texture and flavor may vary slightly due to the absence of gluten, many people find gluten-free cookie cakes to be just as delicious, if not more so, than their traditional counterparts.

5. Can I freeze a gluten-free cookie cake?

Yes, most gluten-free cookie cakes can be frozen for up to a month. Just make sure to wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and store it in an airtight container to maintain freshness.
