Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, a condition where gluten—a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye—triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. For others, gluten sensitivity or intolerance can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. Even for those without a medical condition, many people choose gluten-free options for their potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels.

The Hub understands the importance of catering to these dietary needs, ensuring that their gluten-free menu is not only safe but also flavorful and satisfying. Let’s dive into what makes their gluten-free options stand out.

FAQs About the Hub Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Hub’s gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, the Hub takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! The Hub offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the vegan gluten-free pasta and the grilled vegetable platter.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, the Hub is happy to accommodate customizations. Just let your server know about your dietary restrictions, and they will guide you through the available options.

4. Are the gluten-free options clearly labeled on the menu?

Yes, the Hub clearly labels all gluten-free items on their menu, making it easy for customers to identify suitable options.
