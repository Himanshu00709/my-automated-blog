Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those who choose to avoid gluten for other health reasons. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in sensitive individuals. When dining out, it’s crucial to ensure that the food is prepared in a way that avoids cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.

Chicken Salad Chick’s Commitment to Gluten-Free Options

Chicken Salad Chick takes dietary restrictions seriously and offers a variety of gluten-free options on their menu. While they are not a 100% gluten-free establishment, they provide clear guidance on which items are safe for gluten-sensitive diners. Their staff is trained to handle food with care to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, making it a safer choice for those with gluten intolerance.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Chicken Salad Chick

To ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience, follow these tips when ordering gluten-free at Chicken Salad Chick:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Always inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you on the safest options and take extra precautions in the kitchen.
  • Avoid Cross-Contamination: Request that your food be prepared separately from gluten-containing items. This includes using clean utensils and preparation surfaces.
  • Check the Menu: While most chicken salads are gluten-free, some specialty flavors may contain gluten. Always verify with the staff before ordering.
  • Be Cautious with Sides: Some sides, like the Broccoli Salad, may contain gluten if croutons are added. Ask for them to be omitted if necessary.
