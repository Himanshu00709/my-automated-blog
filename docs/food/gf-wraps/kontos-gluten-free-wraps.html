In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease, gluten intolerance, and the general desire for healthier eating options. What Are Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps?

Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps are a type of flatbread made without gluten-containing ingredients, making them suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. These wraps are crafted using a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, which provide a soft and pliable texture that closely resembles traditional wheat-based wraps. Kontos is a well-known brand in the gluten-free community, recognized for its commitment to quality and taste.

How to Use Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps in Your Meals

Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps are incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes. Here are some creative ways to incorporate them into your meals:

FAQs About Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps

1. Are Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps suitable for vegans?

Yes, Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps are vegan-friendly as they do not contain any animal-derived ingredients. However, it's always a good idea to check the label for any updates or changes in the ingredient list.

2. Can I freeze Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps?

Absolutely! Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps can be frozen to extend their shelf life. Simply place them in an airtight container or freezer bag and store them in the freezer. When you're ready to use them, thaw them at room temperature or warm them in the microwave for a few seconds.

3. Are Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps certified gluten-free?

Yes, Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps are certified gluten-free, meaning they have been tested and verified to contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.

4. Where can I buy Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps?

Kontos Gluten-Free Wraps are widely available in most grocery stores, health food stores, and online retailers. You can check the Kontos website for a store locator to find a retailer near you.
