What Makes Uncle Ben's Wild Rice Gluten-Free?

Uncle Ben's Wild Rice is naturally gluten-free, making it a safe choice for individuals with gluten-related disorders. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, but wild rice is a completely different grain. It is actually the seed of an aquatic grass native to North America. This means that wild rice does not contain gluten, and Uncle Ben's ensures that their product is processed in a way that prevents cross-contamination with gluten-containing grains.

FAQs About Uncle Ben's Wild Rice

Is Uncle Ben's Wild Rice certified gluten-free?

Yes, Uncle Ben's Wild Rice is certified gluten-free, meaning it has been tested and verified to contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.

Can I use Uncle Ben's Wild Rice in place of other grains?

Absolutely! Uncle Ben's Wild Rice can be used as a substitute for other grains like quinoa, brown rice, or couscous in most recipes. Its unique texture and flavor can add a new dimension to your dishes.

How should I store Uncle Ben's Wild Rice?

Uncle Ben's Wild Rice should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it is best to transfer the rice to an airtight container to maintain its freshness.

Is Uncle Ben's Wild Rice suitable for vegans and vegetarians?

Yes, Uncle Ben's Wild Rice is suitable for both vegans and vegetarians. It is a plant-based product that does not contain any animal-derived ingredients.
