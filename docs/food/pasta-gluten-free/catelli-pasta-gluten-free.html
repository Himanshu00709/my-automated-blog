What is Catelli Pasta Gluten-Free?

Catelli Pasta Gluten-Free is a line of pasta products specifically designed for individuals who need or prefer to avoid gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Catelli's gluten-free pasta is made from a blend of non-gluten grains and legumes, such as corn, rice, and lentils, which provide a similar texture and taste to traditional wheat-based pasta.

Ingredients and Nutritional Profile

One of the key aspects of Catelli Pasta Gluten-Free is its ingredient list. The pasta is made from a combination of corn flour, rice flour, and lentil flour, which not only makes it gluten-free but also adds a nutritional boost. Lentils, for example, are a great source of plant-based protein and fiber, making this pasta a healthier option compared to traditional wheat pasta.
