How to Prepare Three Bakers Gluten-Free Stuffing

One of the best things about Three Bakers Gluten-Free Stuffing is how easy it is to prepare. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, you’ll find the process straightforward and hassle-free. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the stuffing mix with your choice of broth (chicken, vegetable, or turkey broth works well). Add melted butter or olive oil for extra richness, if desired.
  3. Transfer to Baking Dish: Spread the mixture evenly in a greased baking dish.
  4. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve: Let the stuffing cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy it as a side dish or as part of your main meal.

Creative Ways to Use Three Bakers Gluten-Free Stuffing

  • Stuffing Waffles: Transform your stuffing into a unique waffle by pressing the mixture into a waffle iron. Serve with gravy or cranberry sauce for a fun twist.
