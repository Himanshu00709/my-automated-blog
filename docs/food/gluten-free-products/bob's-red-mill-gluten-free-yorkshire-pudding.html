How to Make Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Yorkshire Pudding

Making Yorkshire pudding with Bob's Red Mill mix is a straightforward process that yields delicious results. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step Instructions
1 Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Place a muffin tin or Yorkshire pudding tin in the oven to heat up.
2 In a mixing bowl, combine the Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Yorkshire Pudding mix with eggs and milk. Whisk until smooth.
3 Carefully remove the hot tin from the oven and add a small amount of oil or fat to each cup.
4 Pour the batter into the hot tin, filling each cup about halfway.
5 Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the puddings are golden brown and puffed up.
6 Serve immediately with your favorite roast and gravy.

For best results, it's important to ensure that your oven is fully preheated and that the tin is hot before adding the batter. This helps to create the signature rise and crispiness that Yorkshire pudding is known for.
