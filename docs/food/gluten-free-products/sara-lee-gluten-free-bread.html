What is Sara Lee Gluten-Free Bread?

Sara Lee Gluten-Free Bread is a product line specifically designed for individuals who need or prefer to avoid gluten in their diet. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Sara Lee's gluten-free bread is made with alternative flours and ingredients that do not contain gluten, making it a safe and delicious option for those on a gluten-free diet.

The bread is known for its soft texture, which closely mimics traditional wheat bread, making it a favorite among those who miss the taste and feel of regular bread. It is available in various forms, including white bread, whole grain bread, and artisan-style loaves, catering to different tastes and dietary preferences.
