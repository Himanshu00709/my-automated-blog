What Makes Schär Baguettes Special?

Schär Baguettes are crafted with precision to ensure they meet the dietary needs of those avoiding gluten. Made from a blend of gluten-free flours, including corn and rice flour, these baguettes are designed to mimic the texture and flavor of traditional wheat-based baguettes. The result is a product that is not only safe for those with gluten intolerance but also delicious enough to satisfy even the most discerning bread lovers.

How to Enjoy Schär Baguettes

Schär Baguettes are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Classic Sandwiches

Slice the baguette horizontally and fill it with your favorite sandwich ingredients. Whether you prefer a classic ham and cheese or a veggie-packed option, Schär Baguettes provide the perfect base.

2. Toast

For a quick and easy breakfast or snack, toast slices of the baguette and top them with avocado, eggs, or your favorite spread.

3. Soup Companion

Pair a warm slice of Schär Baguette with a bowl of soup for a comforting meal. The slightly crispy crust and soft interior make it an ideal accompaniment.

4. Bruschetta

Slice the baguette into thin rounds, toast them, and top with a mixture of diced tomatoes, basil, and olive oil for a delicious gluten-free bruschetta.
