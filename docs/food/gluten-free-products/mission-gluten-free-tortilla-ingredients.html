Mission Gluten-Free Tortilla Ingredients: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of gluten intolerance and celiac disease. Among the many gluten-free options available, Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas have emerged as a popular choice for those seeking a delicious and versatile alternative to traditional wheat-based tortillas. But what exactly goes into these tortillas? In this article, we’ll take a deep dive into the ingredients of Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas, exploring their nutritional profile, benefits, and how they compare to other gluten-free options.

What Are Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas?

Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are a type of tortilla specifically designed for individuals who need or prefer to avoid gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Mission’s gluten-free tortillas are made with a blend of alternative flours and ingredients that mimic the texture and taste of traditional tortillas without the gluten.

Key Ingredients in Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas

The ingredients in Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are carefully selected to ensure they are safe for those with gluten intolerance while still delivering the taste and texture that consumers expect. Here’s a breakdown of the key ingredients:

Ingredient Purpose Benefits

Baking Powder Leavening agent Helps the tortilla rise slightly and maintain a soft texture.

Benefits of Choosing Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas

There are several reasons why Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are a great choice for those on a gluten-free diet:

  • Safe for Celiac Disease: These tortillas are certified gluten-free, meaning they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for most people with celiac disease.
  • Versatile: Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas can be used in a variety of dishes, from tacos and burritos to wraps and quesadillas. Their flexibility and mild flavor make them a great base for both savory and sweet recipes.
  • Nutritionally Balanced: With a good balance of carbohydrates, fats, and a small amount of protein, these tortillas can fit into a well-rounded diet.
  • Widely Available: Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are available in most grocery stores, making them an accessible option for those on a gluten-free diet.

FAQs About Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas

Are Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas vegan?

Yes, Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are vegan. They do not contain any animal-derived ingredients.

Can I freeze Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas?

Yes, you can freeze these tortillas. To freeze, place them in a resealable plastic bag and store them in the freezer for up to 6 months. When ready to use, thaw them at room temperature or heat them directly from frozen.

Are Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas keto-friendly?

While Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are low in calories and fat, they are not specifically designed for a keto diet due to their carbohydrate content. However, they can be enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

Do Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas contain any allergens?

Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas are free from gluten, dairy, and soy. However, they are produced in facilities that also process wheat, so there is a risk of cross-contamination. Always check the packaging for the most up-to-date allergen information.

Whether you’re making tacos, wraps, or quesadillas, Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas provide a delicious and convenient base for your favorite dishes. So, the next time you’re at the grocery store, consider picking up a pack of Mission Gluten-Free Tortillas and enjoy the freedom of gluten-free eating without compromising on taste or texture.
