What Are Bob's Gluten-Free Oats?

Bob's Gluten-Free Oats are a type of oat product that is specifically processed to ensure they are free from gluten contamination. Oats themselves are naturally gluten-free, but they are often processed in facilities that also handle wheat, barley, and rye, which can lead to cross-contamination. Bob's Red Mill, the company behind these oats, takes extra precautions to ensure that their gluten-free oats are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

The oats are grown in dedicated fields, harvested with dedicated equipment, and processed in a gluten-free facility. This rigorous process ensures that the final product contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold set by the FDA for a product to be labeled as gluten-free.
