What is 365 Gluten-Free Bread?

365 Gluten-Free Bread is a product line offered by Whole Foods Market under their 365 Everyday Value brand. This bread is specifically designed for individuals who need or prefer to avoid gluten in their diet. Made with a blend of gluten-free flours and other wholesome ingredients, 365 Gluten-Free Bread aims to provide a tasty and nutritious alternative to traditional wheat-based bread.

Can I freeze 365 Gluten-Free Bread?

Absolutely! Freezing 365 Gluten-Free Bread is a great way to extend its shelf life. Simply place the bread in a freezer-safe bag and store it in the freezer for up to three months. When you’re ready to use it, let it thaw at room temperature or toast it directly from the freezer.
