What is Udi's Gluten-Free Pizza Crust?

Udi's Gluten-Free Pizza Crust is a pre-made pizza crust designed for individuals who need or choose to avoid gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Udi's has become a trusted brand in the gluten-free community, offering a variety of products that cater to those with dietary restrictions.

The pizza crust is made from a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour and tapioca starch, which give it a texture that closely resembles traditional pizza crust. It's pre-baked, so all you need to do is add your favorite toppings and bake it in the oven for a quick and easy meal.
