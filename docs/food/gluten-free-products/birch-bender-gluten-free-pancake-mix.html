How to Use Birch Bender Gluten-Free Pancake Mix

Using Birch Bender Gluten-Free Pancake Mix is incredibly straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Mix the Ingredients: Combine the pancake mix with water or your preferred milk alternative. The ratio is typically 1:1, but you can adjust it to achieve your desired consistency.
  2. Heat the Pan: Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with oil or butter.
  3. Cook the Pancakes: Pour the batter onto the skillet, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  4. Serve and Enjoy: Top with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, or nut butter.
