Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Schär gluten-free bread safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Schär gluten-free bread is certified gluten-free and is safe for individuals with celiac disease. The brand follows strict manufacturing processes to avoid cross-contamination.

Where can I buy Schär gluten-free bread?

Schär products are widely available in grocery stores, health food stores, and online retailers. You can also use the store locator on the Schär website to find a retailer near you.

Does Schär gluten-free bread contain dairy?

Most Schär breads are dairy-free, but it’s always a good idea to check the ingredient list on the packaging to be sure, especially if you have a dairy allergy.

How should I store Schär gluten-free bread?

Schär bread should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it’s best to keep it in an airtight container or resealable bag to maintain freshness. Some varieties can also be frozen for longer storage.
