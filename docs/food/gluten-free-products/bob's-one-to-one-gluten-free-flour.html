What is Bob's One-to-One Gluten-Free Flour?

Bob's One-to-One Gluten-Free Flour is a specially formulated flour blend designed to replace traditional wheat flour in a 1:1 ratio. This means you can use it in any recipe that calls for regular flour without needing to adjust the measurements. The blend is made from a combination of gluten-free grains and starches, including brown rice flour, sweet white rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour. It also contains xanthan gum, which helps mimic the elasticity and structure that gluten provides in traditional baking.

How to Use Bob's One-to-One Gluten-Free Flour

Using Bob's One-to-One Gluten-Free Flour is straightforward, but there are a few tips and tricks to ensure the best results:

Recipe Type Tips for Best Results
Cakes and Cupcakes Ensure all ingredients are at room temperature before mixing. This helps create a smoother batter and a more even bake.
Cookies Chill the dough before baking to prevent spreading. This is especially important for gluten-free cookies.
Bread Allow the dough to rest before baking. This gives the xanthan gum time to hydrate and create a better structure.
Pizza Dough Roll the dough out between two sheets of parchment paper to prevent sticking. Pre-bake the crust before adding toppings.
