What Are Gefen Gluten-Free Chow Mein Noodles?

Gefen Gluten-Free Chow Mein Noodles are a type of Asian-style noodle made from a blend of gluten-free ingredients, such as rice flour and tapioca starch. These noodles are designed to mimic the texture and taste of traditional chow mein noodles, making them an excellent choice for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those simply looking to reduce their gluten intake. Gefen, a trusted brand in the kosher and gluten-free food industry, ensures that these noodles are produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.
