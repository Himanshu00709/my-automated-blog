How to Use Midel Gluten-Free Pie Crust

Using Midel Gluten-Free Pie Crust is straightforward, but here are some tips to ensure the best results:

  1. Thawing: If the pie crust is frozen, allow it to thaw at room temperature for about 15-20 minutes before use.
  2. Unrolling: Carefully unroll the crust and place it into your pie dish. Gently press it into the dish, ensuring it fits snugly.
  3. Trimming: Trim any excess crust from the edges and crimp as desired.
  4. Blind Baking: For recipes that require a pre-baked crust, prick the bottom with a fork and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 10-12 minutes, or until lightly golden.
  5. Filling: Add your desired filling and bake according to your recipe's instructions.
