What is Canyon Bakehouse Gluten-Free Bread?

Canyon Bakehouse is a dedicated gluten-free bakery that produces a wide range of gluten-free bread, bagels, and buns. Founded by a family with a personal connection to gluten-free living, the brand has grown to become a trusted name in the gluten-free community. Their products are certified gluten-free, non-GMO, and free from artificial preservatives, making them a healthier choice for those avoiding gluten.

The bread is made with a blend of whole grains, including millet, quinoa, and amaranth, which not only enhance the nutritional profile but also contribute to a satisfying texture and flavor. Unlike many gluten-free breads that can be dry or crumbly, Canyon Bakehouse bread is known for its soft, moist texture that closely resembles traditional bread.

FAQs About Canyon Bakehouse Gluten-Free Bread

1. Is Canyon Bakehouse bread really gluten-free?

Yes, Canyon Bakehouse bread is certified gluten-free, meaning it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.

2. Can I find Canyon Bakehouse bread in my local grocery store?

Canyon Bakehouse products are widely available in many grocery stores across the United States. You can also purchase them online through various retailers.

3. Is Canyon Bakehouse bread suitable for vegans?

Most Canyon Bakehouse breads are vegan, but it’s always a good idea to check the ingredient list to be sure, as some varieties may contain honey or other non-vegan ingredients.

4. How should I store Canyon Bakehouse bread?

Canyon Bakehouse bread should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it’s best to keep it in the refrigerator to maintain freshness.

5. Does Canyon Bakehouse bread contain any allergens?

Canyon Bakehouse bread is free from dairy, soy, and nuts, but it is made in a facility that processes these allergens, so cross-contamination is possible.
