FAQs About Udi's Gluten-Free Rolls

1. Are Udi's gluten-free rolls suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Udi's gluten-free rolls are certified gluten-free and are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. They are produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

2. Do Udi's gluten-free rolls contain dairy?

No, Udi's gluten-free rolls are dairy-free, making them suitable for those with lactose intolerance or a dairy allergy.

3. How should I store Udi's gluten-free rolls?

Udi's gluten-free rolls should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it’s best to keep them in an airtight container or resealable bag to maintain freshness. They can also be frozen for longer storage.

4. Can I toast Udi's gluten-free rolls?

Absolutely! Toasting Udi's gluten-free rolls enhances their texture and flavor. They can be toasted in a toaster, oven, or even on a grill for a crispy exterior.

5. Are Udi's gluten-free rolls vegan?

While Udi's gluten-free rolls are dairy-free, they do contain egg whites, so they are not considered vegan. However, Udi's offers other products that are vegan-friendly.
