What Makes Against the Grain Gluten-Free Baguette Special?

The Against the Grain Gluten-Free Baguette is crafted with a unique blend of ingredients that set it apart from other gluten-free bread options. Unlike many gluten-free products that rely heavily on rice flour or starches, this baguette is made primarily from tapioca starch and cheese. This combination gives the bread a chewy, airy texture that is reminiscent of traditional French baguettes. Additionally, the baguette is free from artificial preservatives, making it a healthier choice for those who are mindful of what they eat.
