What is Ener-G Gluten-Free Bread?

Ener-G Gluten-Free Bread is a specially formulated bread designed to meet the dietary needs of those who cannot consume gluten. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Ener-G's bread is made from a blend of gluten-free flours and starches, ensuring that it is safe for those with gluten-related disorders.

The bread is known for its light texture and versatility, making it suitable for sandwiches, toast, and even as a base for gluten-free recipes. Ener-G offers a variety of bread types, including white rice bread, brown rice bread, and tapioca loaf, catering to different taste preferences and dietary requirements.
