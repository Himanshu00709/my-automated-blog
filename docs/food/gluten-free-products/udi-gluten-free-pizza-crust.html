How to Use UDI's Gluten-Free Pizza Crust

Using UDI's Gluten-Free Pizza Crust is straightforward and convenient. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you create a delicious gluten-free pizza at home:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the packaging, usually around 425°F (220°C).
  2. Prepare Your Toppings: While the oven is heating, prepare your favorite pizza toppings. This could include tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, and meats.
  3. Assemble Your Pizza: Place the UDI's Gluten-Free Pizza Crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the crust, then add your toppings.
  4. Bake: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
