Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Bubba Gump

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. is known for its Southern-inspired seafood dishes, but what many people don’t realize is that the restaurant has a dedicated gluten-free menu. This menu is designed to accommodate individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, ensuring that they can enjoy a meal without worrying about cross-contamination or hidden gluten. The gluten-free options range from appetizers to main courses, and even desserts, making it a great choice for those with dietary restrictions.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Bubba Gump

While Bubba Gump offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten-free needs. Bubba Gump staff are trained to handle dietary restrictions and can guide you through the menu.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Ask about the kitchen’s procedures to prevent cross-contamination. Bubba Gump takes precautions, but it’s always good to double-check.
  • Review the Gluten-Free Menu: Bubba Gump provides a separate gluten-free menu, so make sure to request it when you arrive.
