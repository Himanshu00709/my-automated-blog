How Udi's Gluten-Free Muffins Are Made

Udi's takes great care in crafting their gluten-free muffins. The process begins with sourcing high-quality, gluten-free ingredients. The muffins are made with a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour and tapioca starch, which provide the perfect texture and consistency. Natural sweeteners, like cane sugar, are used to enhance the flavor without compromising on health.

One of the key aspects of Udi's production process is their commitment to avoiding cross-contamination. Their facilities are dedicated gluten-free, ensuring that there is no risk of gluten exposure during the manufacturing process. This is particularly important for individuals with celiac disease, who must avoid even trace amounts of gluten.
