Burgatory Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

Enter Burgatory, a popular burger joint that has embraced the gluten-free movement with open arms. In this article, we’ll dive deep into Burgatory’s gluten-free offerings, exploring everything from their menu options to their preparation practices.

Burgatory is not your average burger joint. Preparation Practices: Ensuring Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity is cross-contamination. Burgatory takes this issue seriously and has implemented several practices to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

Practice Description
Separate Preparation Areas Gluten-free items are prepared in a dedicated area to avoid cross-contamination.
Dedicated Utensils Separate utensils are used for gluten-free items to prevent any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
Staff Training All staff members are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and the risks of cross-contamination.
Clear Labeling Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, and staff are knowledgeable about which items are safe for those with gluten intolerance.

FAQs About Burgatory’s Gluten-Free Options

1. Are all Burgatory locations gluten-free friendly?

Absolutely! Burgatory is known for its customizable burgers. You can substitute the traditional bun with a gluten-free bun and choose from a variety of gluten-free toppings.

While the gluten-free buns are free from gluten, they may contain other allergens such as eggs or dairy. If you have additional dietary restrictions, it’s best to check with the staff before ordering.

4. How does Burgatory prevent cross-contamination?

Burgatory takes several precautions to prevent cross-contamination, including using separate preparation areas, dedicated utensils, and thorough staff training.

5. Are there gluten-free dessert options at Burgatory?

Yes, Burgatory offers a selection of gluten-free desserts, including flourless chocolate cake and gluten-free cookies.
