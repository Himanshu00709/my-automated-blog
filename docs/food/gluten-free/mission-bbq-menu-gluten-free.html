Mission BBQ Menu: Gluten-Free Options

When it comes to barbecue, Mission BBQ is a name that resonates with quality, flavor, and a deep sense of patriotism. Known for its commitment to honoring the military and first responders, Mission BBQ also strives to cater to a variety of dietary needs, including those who require gluten-free options. In this article, we’ll dive deep into the gluten-free offerings at Mission BBQ, exploring the menu, ingredients, and how you can enjoy a delicious, gluten-free meal without compromising on taste.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Mission BBQ

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals who suffer from celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Mission BBQ understands this and has made efforts to provide options that are safe and enjoyable for those avoiding gluten. However, it’s important to note that while Mission BBQ offers gluten-free menu items, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Therefore, those with severe gluten allergies should exercise caution and communicate their needs clearly when ordering.

Gluten-Free Menu Options at Mission BBQ

Mission BBQ’s menu is designed to offer a wide range of choices, and while not everything is gluten-free, there are several options that can be safely enjoyed by those avoiding gluten. Below is a detailed breakdown of the gluten-free items available:

Category Gluten-Free Options
Meats Pulled Pork, Smoked Turkey, Sausage (check for gluten-free varieties), Brisket
Sides Green Beans, Cole Slaw, Baked Beans (without bread crumbs), Cornbread (gluten-free version available upon request)
Sauces Most of the sauces are gluten-free, but always check the label or ask the staff for confirmation
Desserts Banana Pudding (without the cookie topping), Fresh Fruit

How to Order Gluten-Free at Mission BBQ

Ordering gluten-free at Mission BBQ is relatively straightforward, but it’s essential to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask the staff about the ingredients and preparation methods. They are trained to assist customers with dietary restrictions.
  • Specify Gluten-Free: When ordering, make sure to specify that you need a gluten-free meal. This will alert the kitchen to take extra precautions.
  • Check Sauces: While many of the sauces are gluten-free, some may contain gluten. Always double-check before adding them to your meal.
  • Opt for Simple Sides: Stick to simple sides like green beans or cole slaw, which are less likely to contain gluten.

Gluten-Free Sauces at Mission BBQ

Sauces are an integral part of the barbecue experience, and Mission BBQ offers a variety of options. Most of their sauces are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to verify with the staff. Here’s a list of some popular gluten-free sauces:

  • Memphis Belle: A sweet and tangy sauce that’s perfect for pulled pork.
  • Texas Twang: A bold and spicy sauce that pairs well with brisket.
  • KC Classic: A rich and smoky sauce that’s great on ribs.
  • Carolina Vinegar: A tangy and slightly spicy sauce that’s ideal for chicken.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Mission BBQ

1. Is Mission BBQ’s cornbread gluten-free?

Mission BBQ offers a gluten-free version of their cornbread upon request. Be sure to ask for it when ordering.

2. Are the fries at Mission BBQ gluten-free?

Unfortunately, the fries at Mission BBQ are not gluten-free as they are cooked in shared fryers, which may lead to cross-contamination.

3. Can I get a gluten-free bun for my sandwich?

Mission BBQ does not currently offer gluten-free buns, but you can enjoy your meal as a platter with gluten-free sides.

4. Are the baked beans gluten-free?

The baked beans at Mission BBQ are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to confirm with the staff as recipes can change.

Conclusion

Mission BBQ is a fantastic option for those looking to enjoy a gluten-free barbecue meal. With a variety of meats, sides, and sauces that cater to gluten-free diets, you can indulge in the rich flavors of barbecue without worry. However, it’s crucial to communicate your dietary needs clearly and be aware of the potential for cross-contamination. By following the tips provided in this article, you can enjoy a safe and delicious gluten-free meal at Mission BBQ. So, the next time you’re craving barbecue, don’t hesitate to visit Mission BBQ and explore their gluten-free options!
