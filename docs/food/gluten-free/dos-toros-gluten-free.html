Dos Toros Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free options has skyrocketed, and for good reason. With more people being diagnosed with celiac disease or choosing a gluten-free lifestyle for health reasons, restaurants have had to adapt. Dos Toros, a popular fast-casual Mexican restaurant chain, has stepped up to the plate by offering a variety of gluten-free options. In this blog post, we’ll dive deep into Dos Toros' gluten-free offerings, how they ensure safety for gluten-sensitive customers, and what you can expect when dining there.

What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe digestive issues, nutrient malabsorption, and long-term health complications. Others may have non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can cause similar symptoms without the autoimmune response. Additionally, some people choose a gluten-free diet for perceived health benefits, such as improved digestion or reduced inflammation.

Dos Toros recognizes the importance of catering to these dietary needs and has made significant efforts to provide safe and delicious gluten-free options.

How Dos Toros Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Dos Toros takes several steps to minimize the risk of cross-contamination in their kitchens:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Dos Toros uses dedicated preparation areas for gluten-free items to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that utensils and surfaces are properly cleaned before use.
  • Clear Labeling: Menu items that are gluten-free are clearly labeled, making it easier for customers to identify safe options.
  • Ingredient Transparency: Dos Toros provides detailed ingredient lists for all menu items, allowing customers to make informed choices.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Dos Toros

To ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience, here are some tips for ordering gluten-free at Dos Toros:

  1. Communicate Your Needs: Let the staff know about your gluten sensitivity or celiac disease so they can take extra precautions.
  2. Stick to Gluten-Free Items: Avoid items that are not labeled as gluten-free, as they may contain hidden sources of gluten.
  3. Double-Check Ingredients: If you’re unsure about an ingredient, ask the staff for clarification.
  4. Be Mindful of Cross-Contamination: Even if an item is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur if it’s prepared near gluten-containing foods.

FAQs About Dos Toros Gluten-Free Options

1. Are Dos Toros corn tortillas gluten-free?

Yes, Dos Toros corn tortillas are naturally gluten-free. However, always confirm with the staff to ensure there’s no cross-contamination.

2. Can I get a gluten-free burrito at Dos Toros?

While Dos Toros does not offer gluten-free flour tortillas, you can enjoy a burrito bowl, which includes all the fillings of a burrito without the tortilla.

3. Are Dos Toros chips gluten-free?

Yes, Dos Toros chips are made from corn and are gluten-free. They are fried in a dedicated fryer to avoid cross-contamination.

4. Does Dos Toros have gluten-free sauces?

Most of Dos Toros’ sauces, including their salsas and guacamole, are gluten-free. However, always check the ingredient list or ask the staff to be sure.

5. Is Dos Toros safe for people with celiac disease?

While Dos Toros takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, individuals with celiac disease should communicate their needs to the staff and exercise caution when ordering.

Conclusion

Dos Toros has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diners, offering a variety of safe and delicious options. From burrito bowls to tacos, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. By following the tips outlined in this guide and communicating your needs to the staff, you can have a worry-free dining experience at Dos Toros. Whether you’re gluten-free by choice or necessity, Dos Toros is a great option for satisfying your Mexican food cravings.
