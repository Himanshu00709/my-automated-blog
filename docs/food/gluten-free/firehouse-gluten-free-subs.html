Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including damage to the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause discomfort, bloating, and other digestive issues. As a result, many people are opting for gluten-free diets to improve their overall health and well-being.

Firehouse Subs recognizes the importance of catering to diverse dietary needs. Their gluten-free subs are made with high-quality ingredients and are prepared with care to avoid cross-contamination, making them a safe and delicious option for anyone avoiding gluten.

How Firehouse Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity is cross-contamination. Firehouse Subs takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free subs are safe for consumption:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free subs are prepared in a designated area to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils are used exclusively for gluten-free orders to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and how to handle gluten-free orders safely.

These precautions make Firehouse Subs a trusted choice for anyone who needs to avoid gluten.

FAQs About Firehouse Gluten-Free Subs

Are all Firehouse Subs gluten-free?

No, not all Firehouse Subs are gluten-free. However, they offer a selection of subs that can be made gluten-free by substituting the regular bread with a gluten-free roll.

Are the gluten-free rolls vegan?

The gluten-free rolls at Firehouse Subs are not vegan, as they contain eggs and dairy. However, they are suitable for those avoiding gluten.

Can I customize my gluten-free sub?

Yes, you can customize your gluten-free sub with a variety of toppings and condiments. Just be sure to inform the staff of any dietary restrictions to avoid cross-contamination.

Are Firehouse Subs safe for people with celiac disease?

Firehouse Subs takes precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, but they cannot guarantee that their gluten-free subs are 100% free from gluten. If you have celiac disease, it’s important to communicate your needs clearly to the staff.
