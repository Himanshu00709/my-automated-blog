What is Slapfish?

Slapfish is a fast-casual seafood restaurant that prides itself on serving high-quality, sustainable seafood. Founded by Chef Andrew Gruel, Slapfish has grown from a single food truck to a nationwide chain, offering a variety of seafood dishes that are both delicious and environmentally responsible. The restaurant’s mission is to make sustainable seafood accessible to everyone, and this includes those with dietary restrictions like gluten intolerance.

How Slapfish Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Slapfish takes this seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Slapfish uses dedicated preparation areas for gluten-free items to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Gluten-Free Ingredients: The restaurant sources gluten-free ingredients, including buns, tortillas, and sauces, from trusted suppliers.
  • Staff Training: Slapfish staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all precautions are taken during preparation and serving.
  • Clear Labeling: The menu clearly labels gluten-free options, making it easy for customers to identify safe choices.

FAQs About Slapfish Gluten-Free Options

3. Does Slapfish offer gluten-free buns and tortillas?

Yes, Slapfish offers gluten-free buns and corn tortillas for many of their dishes, including sandwiches and tacos.

4. Can I find nutritional information for Slapfish’s gluten-free options?

Yes, Slapfish provides nutritional information on their website, including details on gluten-free options. Customers can also inquire in-store for more information.

5. Are Slapfish’s sauces and dressings gluten-free?

Many of Slapfish’s sauces and dressings are gluten-free, but it’s always best to check with the staff to confirm, as recipes may vary by location.
