How Chuy's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Chuy's takes the safety of their gluten-free diners seriously. The restaurant has implemented several measures to prevent cross-contamination and ensure that gluten-free meals are prepared safely:

  • Dedicated Prep Areas: Chuy's uses separate prep areas and utensils for gluten-free dishes to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: The staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all precautions are taken during preparation.
  • Gluten-Free Menu: The gluten-free menu is clearly labeled, making it easy for diners to identify safe options.
  • Communication: Chuy's encourages diners to inform their server about any dietary restrictions so that the kitchen can take extra precautions.

FAQs About Chuy's Gluten-Free Options

1. Is Chuy's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Chuy's takes several precautions to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for those with celiac disease. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary needs so they can take extra care.

2. Are the corn tortillas at Chuy's gluten-free?

Yes, the corn tortillas at Chuy's are gluten-free. They are made from 100% corn and do not contain any wheat or gluten ingredients.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Chuy's is very accommodating when it comes to customizing orders. Just let your server know about your dietary restrictions, and they will help you create a gluten-free meal.

4. Are there gluten-free dessert options at Chuy's?

While Chuy's does not have a dedicated gluten-free dessert menu, some options may be available upon request. It's best to ask your server for the most up-to-date information.
