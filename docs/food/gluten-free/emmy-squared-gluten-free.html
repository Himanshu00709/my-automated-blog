  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Kitchen: Emmy Squared takes cross-contamination seriously. They have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen space to ensure that their gluten-free pizzas are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

The Gluten-Free Dough: A Closer Look

One of the standout features of Emmy Squared's gluten-free pizzas is the dough. Unlike many gluten-free crusts that can be dry or crumbly, Emmy Squared's dough is soft, chewy, and flavorful. Here’s what goes into making their gluten-free dough:

  • Rice Flour: A common base for gluten-free dough, rice flour provides a light and airy texture.
  • Tapioca Starch: This ingredient adds elasticity, helping the dough stretch without breaking.
  • Xanthan Gum: A natural binder that helps hold the dough together, mimicking the properties of gluten.
  • Olive Oil: Adds moisture and richness to the dough, enhancing its flavor.

FAQs About Emmy Squared's Gluten-Free Options

1. Is Emmy Squared's gluten-free pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Emmy Squared takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, including using a dedicated gluten-free kitchen space. However, it's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions.

2. What ingredients are used in the gluten-free dough?

The gluten-free dough is made from rice flour, tapioca starch, xanthan gum, and olive oil, among other ingredients. It's designed to mimic the texture and flavor of traditional pizza crust.

3. Are there vegan options available for gluten-free pizzas?

Yes, Emmy Squared offers vegan cheese and a variety of vegetable toppings that can be added to their gluten-free pizzas.

4. Can I order gluten-free pizzas for delivery or takeout?

Absolutely! Emmy Squared offers both delivery and takeout options for their gluten-free pizzas. Just make sure to specify your dietary needs when placing your order.
