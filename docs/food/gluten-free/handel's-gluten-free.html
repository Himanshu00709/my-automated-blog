In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, driven by an increasing awareness of celiac disease, gluten intolerance, and the desire for healthier eating habits. The History of Handel's Homemade Ice Cream

Handel's Homemade Ice Cream & Yogurt was founded in 1945 by Alice Handel in Youngstown, Ohio. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into a beloved brand with over 40 locations across the United States. Known for their commitment to using fresh, high-quality ingredients, Handel's has built a reputation for creating some of the creamiest and most flavorful ice creams available. Over the years, they have expanded their menu to include a variety of gluten-free options, catering to the growing number of customers with dietary restrictions.

Handel's Gluten-Free Ice Cream: What Makes It Special?

Handel's gluten-free ice cream is crafted with the same dedication to quality and flavor as their traditional offerings. The brand ensures that their gluten-free products are made in a dedicated environment to avoid cross-contamination, making them a safe choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Here are some key features that set Handel's gluten-free ice cream apart:

  • Wide Variety of Flavors: Handel's offers a diverse range of gluten-free flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to more unique options like mint chocolate chip and butter pecan.
  • Fresh Ingredients: All Handel's ice creams are made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring a rich and authentic taste.
  • No Artificial Additives: Handel's prides itself on using natural ingredients, free from artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives.
  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Preparation: To ensure safety, Handel's prepares their gluten-free ice creams in a separate area, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.
