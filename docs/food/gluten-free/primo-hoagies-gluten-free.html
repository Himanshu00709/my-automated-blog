Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Before we delve into Primo Hoagies' gluten-free options, it’s important to understand what a gluten-free diet entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people must adhere to a strict gluten-free diet to maintain their health and well-being.

When dining out, it’s crucial to ensure that the food you’re consuming is not only free from gluten-containing ingredients but also prepared in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination. This is where Primo Hoagies shines, as they have taken steps to accommodate gluten-free customers.

Cross-Contamination Precautions

One of the biggest concerns for individuals with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Even if a menu item is gluten-free, it can still come into contact with gluten during preparation. Primo Hoagies takes this issue seriously and has implemented several precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Primo Hoagies uses separate preparation areas for gluten-free orders to reduce the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Staff use dedicated utensils for gluten-free orders to ensure that gluten-free ingredients do not come into contact with gluten-containing items.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that they understand the importance of avoiding cross-contamination.

While these precautions are in place, it’s always a good idea to communicate your dietary needs clearly with the staff when placing your order. This will help ensure that your meal is prepared safely and to your satisfaction.
