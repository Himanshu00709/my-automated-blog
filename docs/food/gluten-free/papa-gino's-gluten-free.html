Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from digestive discomfort to long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, restaurants like Papa Gino's have recognized the importance of providing safe and delicious gluten-free options.

Cross-Contamination Precautions

One of the biggest concerns for those with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Papa Gino's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe for consumption. These precautions include:

  • Using separate preparation areas for gluten-free items.
  • Training staff on the importance of avoiding cross-contamination.
  • Using dedicated utensils and equipment for gluten-free orders.
  • Clearly labeling gluten-free items on the menu and in the kitchen.

While these measures significantly reduce the risk of cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff of your dietary restrictions when placing your order.
