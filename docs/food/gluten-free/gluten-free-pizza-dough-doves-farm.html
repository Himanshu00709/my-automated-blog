How to Make Gluten-Free Pizza Dough with Doves Farm Mix

One of the standout features of Doves Farm’s gluten-free pizza dough mix is its simplicity. Even if you’re new to gluten-free baking, you’ll find the process straightforward and rewarding. Here’s a step-by-step guide to making your own gluten-free pizza dough:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: You’ll need the Doves Farm gluten-free pizza dough mix, water, and olive oil. Some recipes also recommend adding an egg for extra richness.
  2. Mix the Dough: Combine the dry mix with water and olive oil in a bowl. Stir until a dough forms. If the dough feels too sticky, you can add a little more flour.
  3. Knead the Dough: Unlike traditional dough, gluten-free dough doesn’t require extensive kneading. Simply shape it into a ball and let it rest for a few minutes.
  4. Roll Out the Dough: Place the dough on a floured surface and roll it out to your desired thickness. For a crispier crust, roll it thinner; for a thicker, chewier crust, leave it a bit thicker.
  5. Add Toppings: Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking tray or pizza stone. Add your favorite toppings, such as tomato sauce, cheese, and vegetables.
  6. Bake: Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F) and bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the toppings are cooked through.

Tips for the Perfect Gluten-Free Pizza

Making gluten-free pizza can be a bit different from traditional pizza, but with a few tips, you can achieve excellent results:

  • Use a Pizza Stone: A pizza stone helps distribute heat evenly, resulting in a crispier crust.
  • Don’t Overload with Toppings: Gluten-free dough can be more delicate, so avoid adding too many heavy toppings.
  • Experiment with Flavors: Gluten-free pizza dough is versatile. Try adding herbs or spices to the dough for extra flavor.
  • Let the Dough Rest: Allowing the dough to rest before rolling it out can improve its texture and make it easier to handle.
