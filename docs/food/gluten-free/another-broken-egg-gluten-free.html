How Another Broken Egg Cafe Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Another Broken Egg Cafe takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free dishes. These measures include:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: The cafe has designated areas in the kitchen specifically for preparing gluten-free meals, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Separate Utensils and Cookware: Gluten-free dishes are prepared using separate utensils and cookware to avoid any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: The staff at Another Broken Egg Cafe are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care and attention to detail, ensuring that each dish meets the highest standards of safety.
