Giordano's Gluten-Free: A Delicious and Safe Dining Experience

For those who follow a gluten-free diet, dining out can often feel like a challenge. However, Giordano's, the iconic Chicago-based pizzeria, has made it their mission to ensure that everyone can enjoy their famous deep-dish pizza, regardless of dietary restrictions. In this blog post, we’ll explore Giordano's gluten-free offerings, their commitment to safety, and why their gluten-free pizza is a must-try for anyone looking for a delicious and worry-free dining experience.

What Makes Giordano's Gluten-Free Pizza Special?

Giordano's is renowned for its deep-dish pizza, a culinary masterpiece that has been delighting pizza lovers for decades. Their gluten-free pizza is no exception. Made with a specially crafted gluten-free crust, Giordano's ensures that their gluten-free pizza retains the same rich, cheesy, and flavorful experience that their traditional pizzas are known for. The crust is crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, and perfectly complements the generous layers of sauce, cheese, and toppings.

What sets Giordano's apart is their dedication to quality and safety. The gluten-free crust is made in a dedicated facility to avoid cross-contamination, and the pizzeria follows strict protocols to ensure that every gluten-free pizza is safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. This attention to detail has earned Giordano's a loyal following among the gluten-free community.

How Giordano's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Giordano's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Preparation Area: Giordano's prepares their gluten-free pizzas in a separate area of the kitchen to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Separate Utensils and Equipment: The pizzeria uses dedicated utensils, pans, and cutting tools for gluten-free orders.
  • Staff Training: Giordano's staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, and staff are knowledgeable about which items are safe for gluten-free diners.

These measures provide peace of mind for gluten-free diners, allowing them to enjoy their meal without worry.

FAQs About Giordano's Gluten-Free Options

1. Is Giordano's gluten-free pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Giordano's takes extensive precautions to ensure that their gluten-free pizza is safe for individuals with celiac disease. They use a dedicated preparation area, separate utensils, and follow strict protocols to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free pizza with different toppings?

Absolutely! Giordano's offers a wide range of toppings that can be added to your gluten-free pizza. Just let the staff know your preferences, and they’ll be happy to accommodate.

3. Does Giordano's offer gluten-free desserts?

Yes, Giordano's has a selection of gluten-free desserts, including the Flourless Chocolate Cake and the Seasonal Fruit Cup. Be sure to ask your server for the current gluten-free dessert options.

4. Are the salads at Giordano's gluten-free?

Most of Giordano's salads can be made gluten-free by omitting croutons and choosing a gluten-free dressing. The Caesar Salad (without croutons) and the Garden Salad are popular gluten-free options.

Their commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction is evident in every bite of their gluten-free pizza.
