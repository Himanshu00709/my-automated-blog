Capriotti's Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Capriotti's Sandwich Shop has made significant strides in offering gluten-free options that cater to this growing demographic. Known for their legendary sandwiches, Capriotti's has expanded their menu to include gluten-free choices that don’t compromise on taste or quality. In this article, we’ll explore Capriotti's gluten-free offerings, how they ensure safety for gluten-sensitive customers, and what you can expect when dining at one of their locations.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Capriotti's

Capriotti's has recognized the importance of providing gluten-free options for their customers. Their commitment to quality and safety is evident in their menu, which features several gluten-free choices. From sandwiches to salads, Capriotti's ensures that those with gluten sensitivities can enjoy a delicious meal without worry.

One of the standout features of Capriotti's gluten-free menu is their use of gluten-free bread. This bread is specially sourced to meet gluten-free standards, ensuring that it is safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Additionally, Capriotti's takes extra precautions in their kitchen to prevent cross-contamination, which is crucial for maintaining the integrity of their gluten-free offerings.

How Capriotti's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Capriotti's takes the safety of their gluten-free customers seriously. They have implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free menu items are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. These measures include:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Capriotti's uses separate preparation areas for gluten-free items to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: They use dedicated utensils for gluten-free orders to prevent any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Capriotti's staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed to maintain safety.
  • Clear Labeling: All gluten-free menu items are clearly labeled, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.
