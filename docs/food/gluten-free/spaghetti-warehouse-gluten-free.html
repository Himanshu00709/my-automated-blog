Spaghetti Warehouse Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause serious health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Dining out requires careful consideration of ingredients and preparation methods to avoid cross-contamination. Spaghetti Warehouse has recognized this need and has taken steps to ensure that their gluten-free options are safe and delicious.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Spaghetti Warehouse

While Spaghetti Warehouse offers gluten-free options, it’s important to take extra precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you to the safest options and alert the kitchen staff.
  • Ask About Preparation: Ensure that your meal is prepared in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Check for Hidden Gluten: Some sauces and dressings may contain gluten. Always ask for a list of ingredients if you’re unsure.
  • Consider the Salad Bar: While the salad bar offers fresh vegetables, be cautious of cross-contamination from shared utensils.

1. Is Spaghetti Warehouse’s gluten-free pasta cooked separately?

Yes, Spaghetti Warehouse takes precautions to cook gluten-free pasta in separate water to avoid cross-contamination. However, it’s always a good idea to confirm with your server.

2. Are the sauces at Spaghetti Warehouse gluten-free?

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Spaghetti Warehouse is accommodating when it comes to customizing orders. Just let your server know about your dietary restrictions, and they will do their best to accommodate you.

4. Are there gluten-free dessert options?
