What is Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli?

Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli is a premium pasta product made without any gluten-containing ingredients. No, Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli contains cheese and other dairy products, making it unsuitable for a vegan diet. However, it is a great option for vegetarians.

Yes, you can freeze Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli. Simply store it in an airtight container or freezer bag, and it will keep for up to three months. When ready to eat, cook it directly from frozen, adding a couple of extra minutes to the cooking time.

Is Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli certified gluten-free?

Yes, Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli is certified gluten-free, ensuring that it meets strict standards for gluten-free products.

How long does it take to cook Celentano Gluten-Free Ravioli?
