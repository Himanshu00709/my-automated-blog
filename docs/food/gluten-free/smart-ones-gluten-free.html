What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe autoimmune reactions. Going gluten-free is not just a dietary choice for these individuals; it is a necessity. Additionally, some people choose a gluten-free diet for its perceived health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels.
