Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for these individuals. Even a small amount of gluten can trigger symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. For this reason, gluten-free options must be prepared with extreme care to avoid cross-contamination.

Village Inn understands the importance of catering to gluten-free customers. Their gluten-free pies are made with high-quality ingredients and are prepared in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination, ensuring a safe and enjoyable dining experience.

How Village Inn Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Village Inn takes several steps to ensure that their gluten-free pies are safe for consumption by individuals with gluten sensitivities. Here’s how they do it:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Village Inn uses separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free items to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Certified Gluten-Free Ingredients: All ingredients used in their gluten-free pies are certified gluten-free, ensuring that they meet strict standards.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and the steps needed to maintain a safe environment for gluten-sensitive customers.
