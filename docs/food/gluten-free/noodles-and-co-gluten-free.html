How Noodles and Co Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for individuals with gluten sensitivity or celiac disease is cross-contamination. Noodles and Co takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free dishes:

  • Dedicated Cooking Equipment: Noodles and Co uses separate pots, pans, and utensils for preparing gluten-free dishes to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Gluten-Free Pasta: Their gluten-free pasta is made from rice and corn, which are naturally gluten-free and safe for those with celiac disease.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that they follow strict protocols to prevent cross-contact.

While Noodles and Co takes these precautions, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions when placing your order. This will help them take extra care in preparing your meal.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Noodles and Co

To make your dining experience as enjoyable and safe as possible, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Check the Menu: Before visiting, review the gluten-free options on Noodles and Co’s website or app to familiarize yourself with the available choices.
  • Communicate with Staff: Let the staff know about your gluten-free needs when ordering. They can guide you to the safest options and ensure your meal is prepared correctly.
  • Customize Your Order: Don’t hesitate to ask for modifications to your dish. For example, you can request extra vegetables or a different protein to suit your preferences.
  • Be Mindful of Sauces: Some sauces may contain gluten, so always double-check with the staff before adding them to your dish.

Yes, the gluten-free noodles at Noodles and Co are made from a blend of rice and corn, which are naturally gluten-free and safe for those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.
