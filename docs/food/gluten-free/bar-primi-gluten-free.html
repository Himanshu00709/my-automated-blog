Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is not just a trend; it's a necessity for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for these individuals. Bar Primi recognizes this need and has crafted a menu that ensures safety and satisfaction for gluten-free diners.

This includes using separate cooking utensils, dedicated preparation areas, and thorough staff training.
