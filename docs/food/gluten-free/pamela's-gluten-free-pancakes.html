FAQs About Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancakes

Are Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancakes suitable for vegans?

Yes, Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancakes are vegan-friendly. The mix does not contain any animal products, making it a great option for those following a plant-based diet.

Can I make Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancakes without eggs?

Absolutely! If you prefer to avoid eggs, you can substitute them with a flax egg (1 tablespoon ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tablespoons water) or a commercial egg replacer. The pancakes will still turn out light and fluffy.

How should I store Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancake Mix?

To maintain freshness, store the pancake mix in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it's best to transfer the mix to an airtight container to prevent moisture from affecting the quality.

Are Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancakes safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Pamela's Gluten-Free Pancakes are certified gluten-free and are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The mix is produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.
