The Importance of Gluten-Free Certification

One of the reasons Taqueria Al Lado is so trusted by the gluten-free community is their commitment to safety. The restaurant is certified gluten-free, meaning that every ingredient and preparation method has been rigorously tested to ensure there is no cross-contamination. This is particularly important for those with celiac disease, where even trace amounts of gluten can cause severe reactions. The staff at Taqueria Al Lado are also well-trained in gluten-free protocols, ensuring that your meal is not only delicious but also safe.

FAQs About Taqueria Al Lado

1. Is Taqueria Al Lado completely gluten-free?

Yes, Taqueria Al Lado is a fully gluten-free restaurant. Every dish on the menu is made with gluten-free ingredients, and the kitchen is free from cross-contamination risks.

2. Are there vegan options available?

Absolutely! Taqueria Al Lado offers several vegan-friendly options, including veggie tacos, guacamole, and salads. Just let the staff know about your dietary preferences, and they’ll be happy to assist.

3. Can I make reservations?

While Taqueria Al Lado primarily operates on a walk-in basis, they do accept reservations for larger groups. It’s always a good idea to call ahead if you’re planning a special event or dining with a big party.

4. Do they offer catering services?

Yes, Taqueria Al Lado offers catering for events and gatherings. Their gluten-free menu is perfect for ensuring that all your guests can enjoy the meal without worry.
