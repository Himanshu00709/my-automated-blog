Gluten-Free Options at Chevys: A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Chevys Fresh Mex has emerged as a reliable destination for those seeking gluten-free options. Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many restaurants have started offering gluten-free options to accommodate these dietary restrictions.

Chevys Fresh Mex has taken significant steps to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe and delicious. From tacos to salads, the restaurant provides a variety of dishes that cater to gluten-free diners without compromising on taste.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Chevys

While Chevys offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s essential to take precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and alert the kitchen staff to take extra precautions.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing ingredients. Ask the staff about their procedures to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Stick to the Gluten-Free Menu: Chevys provides a dedicated gluten-free menu, which is your safest bet. Avoid ordering items that are not explicitly marked as gluten-free.
  • Check Ingredients: If you’re unsure about a particular dish, don’t hesitate to ask for a list of ingredients. Chevys staff are usually well-informed and can provide detailed information.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Chevys

1. Is Chevys’ gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Chevys takes precautions to ensure that their gluten-free menu items are safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, it’s always a good idea to communicate your dietary needs to the staff and ask about their cross-contamination prevention measures.

2. Are the corn tortillas at Chevys gluten-free?

Yes, Chevys uses 100% corn tortillas, which are naturally gluten-free. However, always confirm with your server to ensure that there is no cross-contamination during preparation.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While Chevys offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, some dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. Discuss your options with your server, who can guide you on safe modifications.

4. Does Chevys offer gluten-free desserts?

Chevys’ dessert options are limited for gluten-free diners. However, you can always enjoy fresh fruit or ask your server for any available gluten-free dessert options.
