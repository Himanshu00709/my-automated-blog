Erbert & Gerbert's Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

Erbert & Gerbert's Sandwich Shop has long been a favorite for sandwich lovers across the Midwest. Known for their creative and hearty sandwiches, the brand has also made significant strides in catering to dietary restrictions, particularly gluten-free diets. For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding safe and delicious dining options can be a challenge. Fortunately, Erbert & Gerbert's has embraced the gluten-free movement, offering a variety of options that don’t compromise on taste or quality. In this article, we’ll explore their gluten-free offerings, how they ensure safety, and what makes them stand out in the crowded sandwich market.

Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. Even small amounts of gluten can cause severe health issues for those with this condition. Additionally, many people without celiac disease choose to avoid gluten due to gluten sensitivity or other health reasons. For these individuals, finding restaurants that offer safe and tasty gluten-free options is crucial. Erbert & Gerbert's has recognized this need and has taken steps to accommodate gluten-free customers without sacrificing flavor or variety.

It’s important to note that while these items are gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in shared kitchen spaces. Always inform the staff of your dietary restrictions to ensure proper handling of your food.

How Erbert & Gerbert's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Erbert & Gerbert's takes several steps to minimize this risk:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Many locations have designated areas for preparing gluten-free orders to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free protocols and how to handle gluten-free orders safely.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly marked on the menu, making it easier for customers to identify safe options.

While these measures are in place, it’s always a good idea to communicate your needs with the staff to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

Erbert & Gerbert's gluten-free offerings stand out for several reasons:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is Erbert & Gerbert's gluten-free bread certified?

Yes, Erbert & Gerbert's gluten-free bread is certified gluten-free, ensuring it meets strict standards for gluten-free products.

2. Are all Erbert & Gerbert's locations gluten-free friendly?

While most locations offer gluten-free options, it’s always best to check with your local shop to confirm their specific offerings and protocols.

3. Can I customize my sandwich to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Many of their sandwiches can be made gluten-free by substituting regular bread with gluten-free bread. Just let the staff know your preference.

4. Are there gluten-free desserts available?

Currently, Erbert & Gerbert's does not offer gluten-free desserts, but their sides like potato salad and coleslaw are gluten-free and make great accompaniments to your meal.
