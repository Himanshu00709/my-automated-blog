Bertucci's has long been celebrated for its authentic Italian cuisine, but in recent years, the restaurant has expanded its menu to include a variety of gluten-free options. Whether you're celiac, gluten-sensitive, or simply exploring a gluten-free lifestyle, Bertucci's offers a range of dishes that don't compromise on taste or quality.

Precautions Against Cross-Contamination

One of the biggest concerns for those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Bertucci's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free dishes are safe for consumption. These precautions include:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in designated areas to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils are used exclusively for gluten-free dishes to prevent any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Bertucci's staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed meticulously.
