Red Robin Gluten-Free French Fries: A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Red Robin has emerged as a beacon of hope for those seeking gluten-free options, particularly with their gluten-free French fries. In this article, we’ll delve into everything you need to know about Red Robin’s gluten-free French fries, from their preparation to their nutritional value, and why they’ve become a favorite among gluten-free diners.

What Makes Red Robin’s French Fries Gluten-Free?

Red Robin’s gluten-free French fries are a standout option for those avoiding gluten. The key to their gluten-free status lies in their preparation. Unlike many other restaurants that use shared fryers, Red Robin takes extra precautions to ensure their fries are safe for gluten-sensitive individuals. They use a dedicated fryer for their French fries, which means there’s no risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing foods.

Additionally, Red Robin’s French fries are made from whole, fresh potatoes that are cut in-house. This ensures that there are no hidden gluten-containing ingredients or additives. The fries are then cooked in 100% pure canola oil, which is naturally gluten-free. This meticulous attention to detail is what sets Red Robin apart and makes their gluten-free French fries a reliable choice for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.
