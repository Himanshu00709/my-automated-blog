Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. As a result, many people are opting for gluten-free diets to improve their overall health and well-being.

Rubio's has recognized the importance of catering to this growing demographic by offering a range of gluten-free menu items. However, it's crucial to understand that while Rubio's takes steps to minimize cross-contamination, the risk is not entirely eliminated. Therefore, individuals with severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease should exercise caution and communicate their dietary needs clearly when ordering.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Rubio's

Dining out gluten-free can be challenging, but with a little preparation and communication, you can enjoy a safe and delicious meal at Rubio's. Here are some tips to help you navigate the menu:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you to the safest options and take extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Check Ingredients: While Rubio's provides a list of gluten-free items, it's always a good idea to double-check the ingredients, especially for sauces and dressings.
  • Avoid Cross-Contamination: Be aware that cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. If you have severe gluten intolerance, consider dining during off-peak hours when the kitchen is less busy.
  • Use the Gluten-Free Menu: Rubio's offers a gluten-free menu online and in-store. Use this as a guide to make informed choices.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Rubios

1. Are Rubio's corn tortillas gluten-free?

Yes, Rubio's corn tortillas are gluten-free. However, always confirm with the staff to ensure there is no cross-contamination.

2. Can I get a gluten-free burrito at Rubio's?

Rubio's does not offer gluten-free flour tortillas, so traditional burritos are not gluten-free. However, you can enjoy a burrito bowl with gluten-free ingredients.

3. Are Rubio's salsas and dressings gluten-free?

Most of Rubio's salsas and dressings are gluten-free, but it's always best to check the ingredients list or ask the staff for confirmation.

4. How does Rubio's prevent cross-contamination?

Rubio's takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, such as using separate utensils and preparation areas. However, the risk cannot be entirely eliminated, so those with severe gluten intolerance should be cautious.
