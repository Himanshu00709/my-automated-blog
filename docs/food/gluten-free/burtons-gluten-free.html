The History of Burton's

Burton's Foods Ltd. has a rich history dating back to 1935 when it was founded by Joseph Burton in Blackpool, England. Initially, the company focused on producing high-quality biscuits, and over the decades, it has grown into one of the UK's most beloved snack brands. Burton's is best known for iconic products like Jammie Dodgers, Wagon Wheels, and Maryland Cookies. However, as the demand for gluten-free options grew, Burton's recognized the need to cater to this market segment. In response, they launched a dedicated gluten-free range, ensuring that everyone could enjoy their favorite treats without compromising on taste or quality.
