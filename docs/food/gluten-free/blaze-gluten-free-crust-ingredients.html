Blaze Pizza's gluten-free crust is crafted with care to ensure it meets the needs of those avoiding gluten. The secret lies in the carefully selected ingredients that mimic the texture and taste of traditional pizza crust while remaining entirely gluten-free.

FAQs About Blaze Gluten-Free Crust

Is Blaze's gluten-free crust safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Blaze's gluten-free crust is designed to be safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen, so it's important to inform the staff about your dietary needs to minimize the risk.

Does Blaze's gluten-free crust contain any allergens?

Blaze's gluten-free crust contains egg whites, which are a common allergen. If you have an egg allergy, you may want to consider other options or consult with the staff before ordering.

How does the gluten-free crust compare in taste to the regular crust?

Many customers find that Blaze's gluten-free crust is just as delicious as the regular crust. It has a slightly different texture, but the flavor is well-balanced and satisfying.

Can I customize my pizza with gluten-free crust?

Absolutely! Blaze Pizza offers a wide range of toppings that you can customize to create your perfect gluten-free pizza.
