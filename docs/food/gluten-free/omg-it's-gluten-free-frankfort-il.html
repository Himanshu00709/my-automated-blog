Why Gluten-Free Matters

For those with celiac disease, consuming gluten—a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye—can lead to severe health issues, including damage to the small intestine, malnutrition, and a host of other complications. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause uncomfortable symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. This is why dedicated gluten-free establishments like OMG It's Gluten Free are so important. They provide a safe space where individuals can enjoy food without fear of adverse reactions.

Moreover, the rise of gluten-free dining options reflects a broader shift toward inclusivity in the food industry. Restaurants and bakeries are increasingly recognizing the need to accommodate diverse dietary needs, and OMG It's Gluten Free is at the forefront of this movement in Frankfort, IL.
