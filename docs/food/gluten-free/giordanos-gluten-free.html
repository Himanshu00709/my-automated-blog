Giordano's Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

Giordano's, a name synonymous with deep-dish pizza, has long been a favorite among pizza enthusiasts. Known for its rich, cheesy, and hearty pizzas, Giordano's has been a staple in Chicago and beyond. However, for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, enjoying a slice of pizza can often be a challenge. Fortunately, Giordano's has stepped up to the plate by offering gluten-free options that allow everyone to indulge in their famous pies. In this article, we’ll explore Giordano's gluten-free offerings, their preparation process, and what you need to know before ordering.

Understanding Gluten-Free at Giordano's

Giordano's gluten-free pizza is made with a specially formulated crust that is free from gluten-containing ingredients. The crust is designed to mimic the texture and flavor of their traditional deep-dish crust, ensuring that gluten-free diners don’t miss out on the authentic Giordano's experience. However, it’s important to note that while the crust is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Giordano's makes every effort to minimize this risk, but those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease should exercise caution.

Gluten-Free Crust Ingredients

The gluten-free crust at Giordano's is made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch. These ingredients are carefully selected to create a crust that is both flavorful and structurally sound, capable of holding up to the generous toppings and cheese that Giordano's is known for. The crust is also free from artificial flavors and preservatives, making it a healthier option for those looking to avoid processed foods.

Gluten-Free Menu Options

Giordano's offers a variety of gluten-free options, including their famous deep-dish pizza, thin-crust pizza, and even some appetizers. Here’s a breakdown of what you can expect:

How Giordano's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Giordano's takes several precautions to ensure that their gluten-free options are safe for those with gluten sensitivities. The gluten-free crust is prepared separately from regular dough, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care. However, it’s important to note that Giordano's kitchens are not entirely gluten-free environments. Cross-contamination can occur, especially during busy periods. If you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s recommended to inform the staff when placing your order so they can take extra precautions.

Customer Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free

When ordering gluten-free at Giordano's, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Always inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. This will help them take extra care when preparing your meal.
  • Check the Menu: While Giordano's offers gluten-free crust, not all toppings and sauces are gluten-free. Be sure to check the menu or ask the staff for guidance.
  • Be Mindful of Cross-Contamination: If you have a severe allergy, consider dining during off-peak hours when the kitchen is less busy, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.

FAQs About Giordano's Gluten-Free Options

1. Is Giordano's gluten-free crust safe for celiacs?

While Giordano's gluten-free crust is made without gluten-containing ingredients, the kitchen is not entirely gluten-free. Cross-contamination is possible, so those with celiac disease should proceed with caution and inform the staff of their condition.

2. Can I order gluten-free pizza for delivery?

Yes, Giordano's offers gluten-free pizza for both dine-in and delivery. However, be aware that the risk of cross-contamination may be higher with delivery due to the handling process.

3. Are all toppings gluten-free?

Not all toppings at Giordano's are gluten-free. Some sauces and processed meats may contain gluten. Always check with the staff or refer to the menu for gluten-free options.

4. Does Giordano's offer gluten-free desserts?

Currently, Giordano's does not offer gluten-free desserts. However, their salads and some appetizers are gluten-free, providing alternative options for those with dietary restrictions.

Conclusion

Giordano's has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diners, offering a range of options that allow everyone to enjoy their famous deep-dish and thin-crust pizzas. While the gluten-free crust is a great option for those with mild gluten sensitivities, those with celiac disease should be cautious due to the risk of cross-contamination. By communicating your needs and being mindful of the menu, you can enjoy a delicious and safe meal at Giordano's. Whether you're a long-time fan or a first-time visitor, Giordano's gluten-free options are sure to satisfy your pizza cravings.
