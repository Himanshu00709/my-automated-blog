The History of French Onion Soup

French onion soup has a long and storied history, dating back to ancient Rome. Originally a humble peasant dish, it has evolved over the centuries into a gourmet delicacy. The soup is traditionally made with caramelized onions, beef broth, and a topping of toasted bread and melted cheese. Dean's French Onion Gluten-Free soup stays true to these traditional flavors while ensuring that it is accessible to those who need to avoid gluten.
