Ensuring a Safe Gluten-Free Experience

Bobby's Burgers takes several precautions to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Here are some of the steps they take:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Bobby's Burgers has designated areas in their kitchen specifically for preparing gluten-free meals. This helps to prevent cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Separate Cooking Utensils: They use separate utensils, cutting boards, and grills for gluten-free items to avoid any risk of cross-contact.
  • Staff Training: All staff members are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and the potential risks of cross-contamination. This ensures that every team member is aware of the protocols in place.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, and staff are always available to answer any questions about ingredients or preparation methods.
