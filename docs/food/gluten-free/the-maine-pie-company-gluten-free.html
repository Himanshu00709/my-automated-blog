How The Maine Pie Company Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a serious concern. The Maine Pie Company takes this issue seriously and has implemented strict protocols to ensure their gluten-free pies are safe for consumption. Here’s how they do it:

  • Dedicated Equipment: The Maine Pie Company uses separate equipment for their gluten-free products to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Certified Ingredients: All ingredients used in their gluten-free pies are certified gluten-free, providing an extra layer of safety.
  • Staff Training: Their staff is trained in gluten-free handling procedures to ensure that every pie meets the highest standards of safety.

How should I store my gluten-free pie?

Gluten-free pies from The Maine Pie Company should be stored in the refrigerator if not consumed immediately. They can also be frozen for longer storage, ensuring you can enjoy them whenever the craving strikes.
