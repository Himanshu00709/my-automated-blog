The Importance of Gluten-Free Dining

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can be a daunting experience. Cross-contamination and hidden gluten in ingredients are constant concerns. Fire on the Mountain addresses these challenges by offering a dedicated gluten-free menu and implementing strict kitchen protocols to prevent cross-contamination. This level of care and attention to detail ensures that gluten-free diners can enjoy their meal with peace of mind.

FAQs About Fire on the Mountain Gluten-Free Options

1. Are all gluten-free dishes at Fire on the Mountain safe for celiacs?

Yes, Fire on the Mountain takes great care to prevent cross-contamination. However, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure extra precautions are taken.

2. What gluten-free sauces are available for the wings?

Fire on the Mountain offers a variety of gluten-free sauces, including Buffalo, BBQ, and Garlic Parmesan. Be sure to check with the staff for the most up-to-date options.

3. Can I order gluten-free items for delivery or takeout?

Absolutely! Fire on the Mountain offers gluten-free options for both delivery and takeout. Just specify your dietary needs when placing your order.
