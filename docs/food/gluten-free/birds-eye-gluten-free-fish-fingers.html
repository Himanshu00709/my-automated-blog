Are Bird's Eye Gluten-Free Fish Fingers suitable for vegans?

No, Bird's Eye Gluten-Free Fish Fingers are made from fish fillets and are not suitable for vegans. However, they are a great option for pescatarians and those who follow a gluten-free diet.

Can I cook Bird's Eye Gluten-Free Fish Fingers in an air fryer?

Yes, you can cook Bird's Eye Gluten-Free Fish Fingers in an air fryer. Simply preheat the air fryer to 200°C (400°F), place the fish fingers in the basket, and cook for 8-10 minutes, turning halfway through, until golden and crispy.
