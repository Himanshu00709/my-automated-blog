Schar Gluten-Free Rolls: A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those simply choosing to avoid gluten, finding high-quality gluten-free products can be a challenge. Schar, a leading brand in the gluten-free market, has been a game-changer for many. Among their wide range of products, Schar Gluten-Free Rolls stand out as a versatile and delicious option. In this article, we’ll explore everything you need to know about Schar Gluten-Free Rolls, from their nutritional profile to how they can fit into your daily diet.

What Are Schar Gluten-Free Rolls?

Schar Gluten-Free Rolls are a bakery product specifically designed for those who need or prefer to avoid gluten. Made with a blend of gluten-free flours and starches, these rolls are soft, fluffy, and have a texture that closely resembles traditional wheat-based rolls. They are perfect for sandwiches, burgers, or simply enjoyed with a spread of butter or jam.

Ingredients and Allergen Information

Schar Gluten-Free Rolls are made with a carefully selected blend of ingredients to ensure they are safe for those with gluten intolerance. The primary ingredients include:

  • Water
  • Corn starch
  • Potato starch
  • Rice flour
  • Vegetable oil (sunflower)
  • Psyllium seed husk
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar

It’s important to note that Schar Gluten-Free Rolls are free from gluten, wheat, and lactose. However, they may contain traces of soy and eggs, so individuals with allergies to these ingredients should exercise caution.

How to Incorporate Schar Gluten-Free Rolls into Your Diet

Schar Gluten-Free Rolls are incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of ways. Here are some ideas:
