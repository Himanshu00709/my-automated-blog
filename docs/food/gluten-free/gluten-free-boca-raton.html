Why Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including damage to the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause discomfort, bloating, and other digestive problems. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to improve their overall health and well-being.

FAQs

1. What is celiac disease?

Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. It affects about 1% of the population and requires a strict gluten-free diet to manage symptoms and prevent complications.

2. Are gluten-free diets healthier?

Gluten-free diets are essential for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, for individuals without these conditions, a gluten-free diet is not necessarily healthier and may lack certain nutrients if not properly balanced.

3. Can I find gluten-free options at regular restaurants?

Yes, many regular restaurants in Boca Raton offer gluten-free options. It's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure that your meal is prepared safely.

4. Are gluten-free products more expensive?

Gluten-free products can be more expensive due to the cost of alternative ingredients and specialized production processes. However, prices have been decreasing as gluten-free diets become more popular.
