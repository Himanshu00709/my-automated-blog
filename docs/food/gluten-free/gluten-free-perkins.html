Gluten-Free Options at Perkins: A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Perkins Restaurant & Bakery has made strides in accommodating gluten-free diets, offering a variety of options that cater to those who need to avoid gluten. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Perkins, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions about gluten-free dining at this popular chain.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many restaurants, including Perkins, have started to offer gluten-free menu items to cater to this growing demographic.

It’s important to note that while Perkins offers gluten-free options, cross-contamination can still occur in the kitchen. Therefore, it’s crucial to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Perkins

While Perkins offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s essential to take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Communicate Clearly: Inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. Ask them to notify the kitchen staff to take extra precautions.
  • Ask About Preparation: Inquire about how your food will be prepared. For example, ask if the gluten-free pancakes are cooked on the same griddle as regular pancakes.
  • Check Ingredients: Even if a dish is labeled gluten-free, it’s a good idea to double-check the ingredients, especially in dressings, sauces, and condiments.
  • Consider Cross-Contamination: Be aware that cross-contamination can occur in shared fryers, grills, and prep areas. If you’re highly sensitive, you may want to avoid items that are fried or grilled.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Perkins

1. Does Perkins have a dedicated gluten-free menu?

Yes, Perkins offers a dedicated gluten-free menu with a variety of options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. However, it’s always a good idea to check with your local Perkins location, as menu offerings may vary.

2. Are Perkins’ gluten-free pancakes safe for celiacs?

Perkins’ gluten-free pancakes are made with gluten-free flour, but they may be cooked on the same griddle as regular pancakes. If you have celiac disease, be sure to inform your server and ask about cross-contamination risks.

3. Can I get a gluten-free bun for my burger at Perkins?

Yes, Perkins offers gluten-free buns for their burgers. Be sure to request it when placing your order.

4. Are there gluten-free dessert options at Perkins?

Yes, Perkins offers a gluten-free brownie as a dessert option. However, always double-check with your server to ensure it’s safe for your dietary needs.

5. How does Perkins prevent cross-contamination?

While Perkins takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, it’s not a gluten-free facility. Cross-contamination can occur in shared cooking areas. Always communicate your dietary needs to the staff to minimize risks.
