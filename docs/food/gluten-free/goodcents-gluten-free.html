Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to improve their health and well-being.

Goodcents recognizes the importance of catering to this growing demographic. Their gluten-free options are carefully crafted to ensure they meet the dietary needs of their customers without compromising on taste or quality.

How Goodcents Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Goodcents takes several precautions to ensure their gluten-free options are safe:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in designated areas to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils are used for gluten-free orders to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care and attention to detail.

These measures help ensure that customers can enjoy their meals with confidence, knowing that their dietary needs are being respected.
