Key Ingredients

The ingredients used in Mendocino Farms gluten-free bread are carefully selected to ensure both nutritional value and great taste. Some of the key ingredients include:

  • Brown Rice Flour: A staple in gluten-free baking, brown rice flour provides a mild, nutty flavor and a soft texture.
  • Tapioca Starch: This ingredient helps to bind the bread together, giving it a chewy texture similar to traditional bread.
  • Potato Starch: Known for its ability to retain moisture, potato starch ensures that the bread stays fresh and soft.
  • Xanthan Gum: A common gluten substitute, xanthan gum helps to mimic the elasticity and structure that gluten provides in traditional bread.
  • Flaxseed: Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, flaxseed adds a nutritional boost to the bread.
