The Origins of Mildreds Gluten-Free

Mildreds Gluten-Free was born out of a necessity to provide delicious, high-quality gluten-free options for those who cannot tolerate gluten. The brand was founded by a team of passionate food enthusiasts who recognized the gap in the market for gluten-free products that didn't compromise on taste or texture. Named after the founder's grandmother, Mildred, who had celiac disease, the brand is a tribute to her struggle and a commitment to making gluten-free living easier and more enjoyable for everyone.
