FAQs About Schar Gluten-Free Chocolate Croissants

Are Schar gluten-free chocolate croissants suitable for vegans?

No, Schar’s gluten-free chocolate croissants contain dairy products, so they are not suitable for vegans. However, they are gluten-free and safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

Where can I buy Schar gluten-free chocolate croissants?

Schar products are widely available in grocery stores, health food stores, and online retailers. You can check the Schar website for a store locator to find a retailer near you.

How should I store Schar gluten-free chocolate croissants?

It’s best to store Schar gluten-free chocolate croissants in a cool, dry place. Once opened, they should be consumed within a few days or stored in an airtight container to maintain freshness.

Can I freeze Schar gluten-free chocolate croissants?

Yes, you can freeze Schar gluten-free chocolate croissants. Simply place them in an airtight container or freezer bag and store them in the freezer for up to three months. When you’re ready to enjoy them, let them thaw at room temperature or warm them in the oven for a few minutes.
