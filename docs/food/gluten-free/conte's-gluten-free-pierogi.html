How to Enjoy Conte's Gluten-Free Pierogi

Conte's Gluten-Free Pierogi are incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. Here are some popular methods to prepare and serve them:

Boiling

Boiling is the most traditional method of cooking pierogi. Simply bring a pot of water to a boil, add the pierogi, and cook for about 5-7 minutes until they float to the surface. Serve with a dollop of sour cream or a drizzle of melted butter for a classic experience.

Pan-Frying

For a crispy texture, pan-frying is an excellent option. After boiling, heat some butter or oil in a skillet and fry the pierogi until they are golden brown on both sides. This method adds a delightful crunch to the soft dumplings.

Baking

Baking is a healthier alternative that still yields delicious results. Arrange the pierogi on a baking sheet, brush with a little oil or melted butter, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through. This method is perfect for those who prefer a lighter option.
