Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many individuals are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health and well-being.

Sizzler has recognized the importance of catering to this growing demographic by offering a variety of gluten-free options. Whether you're dining out with friends or enjoying a family meal, Sizzler's gluten-free menu ensures that everyone can enjoy a delicious and safe meal.

Cross-Contamination: What You Need to Know

One of the biggest concerns for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity is cross-contamination. Even a small amount of gluten can cause a reaction, so it's crucial that restaurants take steps to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchens.

Sizzler takes this issue seriously. The restaurant has implemented strict protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. This includes using separate cooking utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free dishes. Additionally, staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that your meal is safe to eat.
