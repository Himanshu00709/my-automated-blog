Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause discomfort and digestive problems. As a result, many people are opting for gluten-free diets to improve their overall health and well-being.

Rubio's recognizes the importance of catering to this growing demographic. By offering gluten-free options, they ensure that everyone can enjoy their delicious coastal-inspired dishes without compromising their dietary needs.

How Rubio's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Rubio's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to minimize the risk of cross-contamination in their kitchens:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Rubio's uses dedicated preparation areas for gluten-free items to prevent cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that they understand the importance of avoiding cross-contamination.
  • Clear Labeling: Menu items that are gluten-free or can be made gluten-free are clearly labeled, making it easier for customers to make informed choices.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Rubio's

While Rubio's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it's essential to take a few extra steps to ensure a safe dining experience:

  1. Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff about your gluten-free requirements when placing your order. They can guide you through the menu and make necessary adjustments.
  2. Double-Check Ingredients: Even if an item is labeled as gluten-free, it's always a good idea to double-check the ingredients and preparation methods to ensure there are no hidden sources of gluten.
  3. Be Mindful of Cross-Contamination: If you have celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, remind the staff to take extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Are Rubio's corn tortillas gluten-free?

Yes, Rubio's corn tortillas are gluten-free. However, it's always a good idea to confirm with the staff to ensure there is no cross-contamination during preparation.

2. Can I get a gluten-free burrito at Rubio's?

While Rubio's does not offer gluten-free flour tortillas, you can enjoy a "naked" burrito bowl, which includes all the burrito fillings without the tortilla.

3. Are Rubio's sauces and dressings gluten-free?

Many of Rubio's sauces and dressings are gluten-free, but it's essential to check with the staff or refer to the allergen menu to confirm.

4. Does Rubio's have a dedicated gluten-free fryer?

Rubio's does not have a dedicated gluten-free fryer, so items that are typically fried may not be safe for those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance.
