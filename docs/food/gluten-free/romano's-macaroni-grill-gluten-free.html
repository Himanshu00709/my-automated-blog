Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into the specifics of Romano's Macaroni Grill's gluten-free menu, it's essential to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from mild discomfort to severe health complications. Therefore, it's crucial for restaurants to offer gluten-free options that are prepared in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Romano's Macaroni Grill

While Romano's Macaroni Grill offers a variety of gluten-free options, it's essential to take precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Inform your server about your gluten sensitivity or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and alert the kitchen staff to take extra precautions.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Inquire about the restaurant's procedures for preventing cross-contamination. This includes using separate cooking utensils, pots, and pans for gluten-free dishes.
  • Double-Check Ingredients: Even if a dish is listed as gluten-free, it's always a good idea to double-check the ingredients, especially if you have severe gluten sensitivity.
  • Consider Dining During Off-Peak Hours: Dining during less busy times can reduce the risk of cross-contamination, as the kitchen staff may have more time to focus on preparing your meal safely.
