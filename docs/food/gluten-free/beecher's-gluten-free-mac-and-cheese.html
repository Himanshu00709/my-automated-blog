What Makes Beecher’s Gluten-Free Mac and Cheese Special?

Beecher’s Handmade Cheese, a Seattle-based company, has long been celebrated for its artisanal cheeses and commitment to quality. Their gluten-free mac and cheese is no exception. The pasta is crafted from brown rice, ensuring a satisfying texture that holds up well under the creamy cheese sauce.

How to Prepare Beecher’s Gluten-Free Mac and Cheese

Preparing Beecher’s Gluten-Free Mac and Cheese is a straightforward process that can be done in just a few simple steps:

  1. Boil Water: Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt if desired.
  2. Cook the Pasta: Add the brown rice pasta to the boiling water and cook for about 8-10 minutes, or until al dente.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Sauce: While the pasta is cooking, heat the cheese sauce in a separate saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally until it’s warm and smooth.
  4. Combine and Serve: Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and mix it with the cheese sauce. Serve immediately and enjoy!
