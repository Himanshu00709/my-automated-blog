What Makes Freddy's Gluten-Free Bun Special?

Freddy's gluten-free bun is not just another run-of-the-mill gluten-free product. It’s a carefully crafted bun designed to meet the needs of gluten-sensitive individuals without compromising on taste or texture. Unlike many gluten-free buns that can be dry, crumbly, or flavorless, Freddy's has managed to create a bun that is soft, fluffy, and holds up well to the juiciness of their signature steakburgers.

The bun is made from a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, which provide a light and airy texture. It’s also enriched with xanthan gum, a common ingredient in gluten-free baking that helps mimic the elasticity and structure typically provided by gluten. The result is a bun that not only tastes great but also feels satisfying to eat.
