Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it's a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Postino understands this and has crafted a menu that caters to these dietary needs without sacrificing flavor or variety.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Postino

Dining out gluten-free can be challenging, but with a little preparation, you can enjoy a stress-free meal at Postino. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Always inform your server about your gluten-free requirements. Postino staff are trained to accommodate dietary restrictions and can guide you through the menu.
  • Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to ask about ingredients or preparation methods. Postino is committed to transparency and will provide the information you need.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Ensure that your meal is prepared in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Explore the Menu: Postino's gluten-free menu is extensive, so take the time to explore all the options available to you.
