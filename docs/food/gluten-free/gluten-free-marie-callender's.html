How Marie Callender's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Marie Callender's takes several steps to ensure their gluten-free products are safe:

  • Dedicated Facilities: The brand uses dedicated facilities to prepare their gluten-free products, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Rigorous Testing: Each batch of gluten-free products undergoes rigorous testing to ensure they meet the FDA's gluten-free standards.
  • Clear Labeling: All gluten-free products are clearly labeled, making it easy for consumers to identify safe options.
