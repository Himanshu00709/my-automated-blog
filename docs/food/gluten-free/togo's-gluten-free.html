Togo's Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free options has skyrocketed, driven by an increasing awareness of celiac disease, gluten intolerance, and the desire for healthier eating habits. Togo's, a popular sandwich chain, has responded to this demand by offering a variety of gluten-free options. This article delves into Togo's gluten-free offerings, providing a detailed guide for those who need or prefer to avoid gluten.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including damage to the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause discomfort and digestive problems. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to improve their health and well-being.

Togo's Commitment to Gluten-Free Dining

Togo's has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free customers. The chain offers a range of gluten-free options, from sandwiches to salads, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a delicious meal without worrying about gluten. Below, we explore the various gluten-free options available at Togo's.

Gluten-Free Bread Options

One of the standout features of Togo's gluten-free menu is their gluten-free bread. Made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and other gluten-free ingredients, this bread is soft, flavorful, and perfect for sandwiches. It's important to note that while the bread itself is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Togo's takes precautions to minimize this risk, but customers with severe gluten allergies should exercise caution.

FAQs About Togo's Gluten-Free Options

1. Is Togo's gluten-free bread safe for people with celiac disease?

While Togo's gluten-free bread is made without gluten-containing ingredients, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Togo's takes precautions to minimize this risk, but individuals with celiac disease should be aware of the potential for cross-contamination.

2. Can I customize my sandwich to be gluten-free?

Yes, most of Togo's sandwiches can be made gluten-free by substituting the regular bread with gluten-free bread. Be sure to inform the staff of your dietary needs when placing your order.

3. Are Togo's salads gluten-free?

Many of Togo's salads are naturally gluten-free, but it's always a good idea to check the ingredients and ask the staff if you have any concerns.

4. Does Togo's offer gluten-free dressings?

Yes, Togo's offers a variety of gluten-free dressings, including Caesar, vinaigrette, and ranch. Be sure to ask for gluten-free dressing when ordering your salad.
