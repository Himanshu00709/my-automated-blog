Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into Pick Up Stix’s gluten-free options, it’s essential to understand what “gluten-free” means. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, gluten-free dining isn’t just a dietary preference for many—it’s a necessity.

When dining out, gluten-free individuals must be vigilant about cross-contamination, which occurs when gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing ingredients or surfaces. This is especially important in restaurants that serve a mix of gluten-free and gluten-containing dishes, like Pick Up Stix.

Pick Up Stix’s Commitment to Gluten-Free Dining

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Pick Up Stix

To ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience at Pick Up Stix, consider the following tips:

  • Communicate Clearly: Inform the staff about your gluten-free needs when placing your order. Be specific about your dietary restrictions and ask about the preparation process.
  • Ask About Sauces: Many Asian sauces contain gluten. Always ask if the sauce used in your dish is gluten-free or if a gluten-free alternative is available.
  • Choose Simple Dishes: Opt for dishes with fewer ingredients, as they are less likely to contain hidden sources of gluten.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Inquire about the restaurant’s practices to prevent cross-contamination, such as using separate utensils and cooking surfaces for gluten-free dishes.
