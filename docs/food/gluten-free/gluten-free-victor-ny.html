Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. Even for those without a medical condition, many people choose to go gluten-free for its potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels.

While there are no dedicated gluten-free bakeries in Victor, NY, many local bakeries offer gluten-free options. Be sure to ask about cross-contamination if you have celiac disease or a severe gluten sensitivity.

3. Can I find gluten-free products at regular grocery stores?

Yes, most major grocery stores in Victor, NY, carry a variety of gluten-free products. Look for dedicated gluten-free sections or check the labels on individual items.
