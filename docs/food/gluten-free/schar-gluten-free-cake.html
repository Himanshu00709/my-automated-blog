What Makes Schär Gluten-Free Cakes Special?

Schär has been a pioneer in the gluten-free industry for over 35 years, and their expertise shines through in their cake products. Unlike many gluten-free baked goods that can be dry or crumbly, Schär cakes are moist, flavorful, and have a texture that rivals traditional wheat-based cakes. This is achieved through a combination of carefully selected ingredients and advanced baking techniques.

One of the key ingredients in Schär cakes is a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour, corn starch, and potato starch. These flours are combined with other natural ingredients like eggs, sugar, and vegetable oils to create a cake that is not only gluten-free but also rich in flavor. Additionally, Schär cakes are free from artificial colors and preservatives, making them a healthier choice for those with dietary restrictions.

FAQs About Schär Gluten-Free Cakes

1. Are Schär gluten-free cakes suitable for vegans?

Most Schär gluten-free cakes contain eggs and dairy, so they are not suitable for vegans. However, Schär does offer some vegan-friendly products, so be sure to check the ingredient list before purchasing.

2. Can I freeze Schär gluten-free cakes?

Yes, Schär gluten-free cakes can be frozen to extend their shelf life. Simply wrap the cake tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and store it in the freezer for up to three months. Thaw the cake in the refrigerator before serving.

3. Are Schär gluten-free cakes safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Schär gluten-free cakes are certified gluten-free and are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. They are produced in dedicated gluten-free facilities to prevent cross-contamination.

4. How do Schär gluten-free cakes compare to regular cakes in terms of taste?

Schär gluten-free cakes are known for their excellent taste and texture, which are comparable to regular cakes. Many people find it difficult to tell the difference between Schär cakes and traditional wheat-based cakes.
