Mountain Mike's Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

For those who follow a gluten-free diet, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Mountain Mike's Pizza has emerged as a haven for gluten-sensitive individuals, offering a variety of delicious and safe options. Why Choose Mountain Mike's for Gluten-Free Pizza?

Mountain Mike's Pizza has built a reputation for quality and taste, and their gluten-free options are no exception. The brand understands the importance of catering to dietary restrictions without compromising on flavor. Their gluten-free pizza crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, ensuring a crispy and satisfying base that rivals traditional crusts.

Moreover, Mountain Mike's takes cross-contamination seriously. They have implemented strict protocols to ensure that gluten-free pizzas are prepared separately from their regular offerings. This includes using dedicated utensils, pans, and preparation areas, giving customers peace of mind when ordering.

How Mountain Mike's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Mountain Mike's has taken several steps to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Gluten-free pizzas are prepared in a separate area to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Specialized Utensils: The kitchen uses dedicated utensils and pans for gluten-free orders.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed.

These measures make Mountain Mike's a trusted choice for gluten-free dining.
