What Makes Blaze Gluten-Free Crust Special?

Blaze Pizza's gluten-free crust is not just another alternative; it's a carefully crafted product designed to meet the needs of those who avoid gluten. Unlike many gluten-free crusts that can be dry, crumbly, or lack flavor, Blaze's version is known for its texture and taste. How Does Blaze Gluten-Free Crust Compare to Traditional Crusts?

When comparing Blaze's gluten-free crust to traditional pizza crusts, several factors come into play. Traditional crusts are typically made from wheat flour, which contains gluten. While gluten gives traditional crusts their characteristic chewiness, it can be problematic for those with gluten-related disorders.

Blaze's gluten-free crust, on the other hand, achieves a similar texture without the use of gluten. The combination of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch creates a crust that is both light and chewy, closely mimicking the texture of traditional pizza crusts. Additionally, Blaze's crust is lower in calories and fat, making it a healthier option overall.
