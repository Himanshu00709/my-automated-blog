The Science Behind Gluten-Free Cooking

Creating gluten-free dishes that are both delicious and safe for those with gluten sensitivities is no small feat. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, is responsible for the elasticity and texture of traditional baked goods. Removing gluten requires a deep understanding of alternative ingredients and cooking techniques. Biaggi's chefs have mastered this art, using a combination of rice flour, almond flour, and other gluten-free substitutes to replicate the texture and flavor of traditional Italian dishes.

FAQs About Biaggi's Gluten-Free Options

1. Is Biaggi's gluten-free menu safe for those with celiac disease?

Yes, Biaggi's takes great care to prevent cross-contamination. Their gluten-free dishes are prepared in a separate area of the kitchen, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with the utmost care.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

While Biaggi's gluten-free menu is primarily focused on accommodating gluten sensitivities, there are several vegan-friendly options available. Be sure to inform your server of any dietary restrictions.

3. Can I order gluten-free dishes for takeout or delivery?

Absolutely! Biaggi's gluten-free menu is available for both dine-in and takeout. Just specify your dietary needs when placing your order.
