Why These Ingredients Work Together

The combination of brown rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch creates a balanced texture that is both crispy and chewy. Brown rice flour provides a sturdy base, while tapioca and potato starches add moisture and elasticity. Olive oil and egg whites contribute to the richness and structure, ensuring the crust doesn’t crumble. Xanthan gum acts as a binding agent, mimicking the properties of gluten, and yeast gives the crust its signature rise and flavor.
