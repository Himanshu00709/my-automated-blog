Tips for Enjoying a Gluten-Free Diet at Chicken Salad Chick

While Chicken Salad Chick offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take a few precautions to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience. Here are some tips:

  • Communicate with Staff: Always inform the staff about your gluten-free needs. They are trained to handle such requests and can guide you to the safest options.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Even though Chicken Salad Chick takes precautions, it’s always a good idea to ask about their preparation methods to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Explore the Menu: Don’t hesitate to try different gluten-free options. With so many flavors and combinations, you’re sure to find something you love.
  • Pair with Gluten-Free Sides: Enhance your meal by pairing your chicken salad with gluten-free sides like fresh fruit or a side salad.
