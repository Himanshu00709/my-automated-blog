How to Use Sweet Lorens Gluten-Free Puff Pastry

Using Sweet Lorens Gluten-Free Puff Pastry is incredibly easy, even for those who are new to gluten-free baking. Here’s a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Thaw the Dough: Remove the puff pastry from the freezer and let it thaw in the refrigerator for about 4-6 hours, or overnight. Avoid thawing at room temperature to prevent the dough from becoming too soft.
  2. Roll It Out: Once thawed, lightly flour your work surface with gluten-free flour and roll out the dough to your desired thickness. Be gentle to avoid compressing the layers.
  3. Cut and Shape: Use a sharp knife or pastry cutter to cut the dough into your desired shapes. Whether you're making croissants, turnovers, or tart shells, the possibilities are endless.
  4. Bake: Preheat your oven to the temperature specified in your recipe. Place the shaped dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake until golden brown and puffed.
