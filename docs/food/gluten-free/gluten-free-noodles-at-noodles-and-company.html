Why Choose Gluten-Free Noodles?

Gluten-free noodles are made from alternative flours such as rice, corn, quinoa, or legumes. These noodles are not only suitable for those with gluten-related health issues but also offer a different texture and flavor profile that many people enjoy. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health complications, making gluten-free options essential. Even for those without gluten intolerance, gluten-free noodles can be a healthier choice, as they often contain more fiber and protein than traditional wheat-based noodles.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Noodles at Noodles & Company

1. Are all gluten-free noodles at Noodles & Company safe for celiacs?

While Noodles & Company offers gluten-free noodle options, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. It’s important to inform the staff of your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free dish?

Yes, Noodles & Company allows you to customize your dish. You can choose from a variety of gluten-free noodles and add your favorite proteins and vegetables.

3. Are the sauces gluten-free?

Most of the sauces at Noodles & Company are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to double-check with the staff to ensure they meet your dietary needs.

4. Are there vegan gluten-free options?

Yes, Noodles & Company offers several vegan and gluten-free options, such as the Zoodles with Marinara Sauce or the Rice Noodles with Tofu.
