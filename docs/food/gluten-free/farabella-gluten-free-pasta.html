How to Cook Farabella Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking Farabella Gluten-Free Pasta is simple and straightforward, but there are a few tips to ensure the best results:

  1. Boil Water: Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil.
  2. Add Pasta: Add the Farabella pasta to the boiling water and stir gently to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook Time: Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, usually between 8-10 minutes, depending on the variety.
  4. Drain and Serve: Once the pasta is al dente, drain it and serve immediately with your favorite sauce or toppings.
