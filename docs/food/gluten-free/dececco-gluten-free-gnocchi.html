How to Cook DeCecco Gluten-Free Gnocchi

Cooking DeCecco Gluten-Free Gnocchi is a breeze, and it can be prepared in a variety of ways to suit your taste preferences. Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to cooking perfect gnocchi:

  1. Boil Water: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add Gnocchi: Gently add the gnocchi to the boiling water, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook: Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the gnocchi floats to the surface.
  4. Drain: Drain the gnocchi using a colander, but reserve a small amount of the cooking water.
  5. Serve: Toss the gnocchi with your favorite sauce, adding a bit of the reserved cooking water to achieve the desired consistency.

For a more indulgent experience, you can pan-fry the gnocchi in a bit of olive oil or butter until golden brown. This method adds a delightful crispiness to the gnocchi, enhancing its texture and flavor.
