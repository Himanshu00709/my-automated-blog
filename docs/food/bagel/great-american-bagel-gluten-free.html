Great American Bagel Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding delicious and safe food options can be a challenge. Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As awareness of gluten-related disorders has grown, so has the demand for gluten-free food options. Great American Bagel recognizes this need and has worked to create a menu that caters to those who must avoid gluten.

How Great American Bagel Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for those with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Great American Bagel takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in a separate area of the kitchen to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Separate Utensils: Utensils used for gluten-free items are kept separate from those used for gluten-containing products.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free safety and how to handle gluten-free items properly.
  • Third-Party Certification: Great American Bagel’s gluten-free products are certified by a third-party organization, ensuring they meet strict gluten-free standards.
