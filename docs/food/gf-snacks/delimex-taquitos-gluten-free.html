How to Prepare Delimex Taquitos Gluten-Free

One of the best things about Delimex Taquitos is how easy they are to prepare. Whether you’re in a hurry or just want a quick snack, these taquitos can be ready in minutes. Here’s a simple guide to preparing them:

  1. Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the taquitos on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until they are crispy and golden brown.
  2. Microwave: For a quicker option, you can microwave the taquitos on high for 1-2 minutes. However, this method may result in a slightly less crispy texture.
  3. Air Fryer: If you have an air fryer, you can cook the taquitos at 400°F (200°C) for 6-8 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through to ensure even cooking.

Once cooked, you can enjoy the taquitos as they are or pair them with your favorite dipping sauces, such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.
