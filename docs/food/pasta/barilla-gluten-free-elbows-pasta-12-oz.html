Cooking Tips for Perfect Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking gluten-free pasta can be a bit different from cooking traditional pasta, but with a few tips, you can achieve perfect results every time:

  • Use plenty of water: Gluten-free pasta tends to absorb more water, so make sure to use a large pot with plenty of boiling water.
  • Stir frequently: To prevent the pasta from sticking together, stir it frequently during cooking.
  • Follow the package instructions: Barilla provides specific cooking times on the package, so be sure to follow them for the best texture.
  • Rinse with caution: Some people prefer to rinse gluten-free pasta after cooking to remove excess starch, but this can also remove some of the flavor. If you do rinse, do so quickly and with cold water.
