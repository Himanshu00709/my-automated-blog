How to Cook Garofalo Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking Garofalo Gluten-Free Pasta is simple and straightforward, but there are a few tips to ensure the best results:

  1. Use Plenty of Water: Use a large pot of boiling water to cook the pasta. This prevents the pasta from sticking together and ensures even cooking.
  2. Add Salt: Add a generous amount of salt to the boiling water. This enhances the flavor of the pasta.
  3. Stir Occasionally: Stir the pasta occasionally to prevent it from clumping together.
  4. Check for Doneness: Follow the recommended cooking time on the package, but start checking the pasta a minute or two before the time is up. The pasta should be al dente, with a slight bite to it.
  5. Drain and Serve: Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and immediately toss it with your favorite sauce or toppings.
