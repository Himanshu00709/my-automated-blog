Cooking Tips for Perfect Gluten-Free Fettuccine

Cooking gluten-free pasta can be slightly different from cooking traditional wheat-based pasta. Here are some tips to ensure your Barilla Gluten-Free Fettuccine turns out perfectly every time:

  • Use Plenty of Water: Always cook your pasta in a large pot of boiling water. This prevents the pasta from sticking together and ensures even cooking.
  • Stir Frequently: Gluten-free pasta can be more delicate, so stirring frequently helps prevent clumping.
  • Follow the Package Instructions: Barilla provides specific cooking times on the package. Overcooking can make the pasta mushy, so keep an eye on the clock.
  • Rinse with Caution: Unlike traditional pasta, gluten-free pasta should not be rinsed after cooking. Rinsing can remove the starch that helps the sauce adhere to the pasta.
