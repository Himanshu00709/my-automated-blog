Why Choose a Gluten-Free Bakery?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Cross-contamination is a significant concern, as even a tiny amount of gluten can trigger symptoms. This is why dedicated gluten-free bakeries, like the one in Maple Grove, are essential. They provide a safe environment where every ingredient and process is carefully monitored to ensure gluten-free integrity.
