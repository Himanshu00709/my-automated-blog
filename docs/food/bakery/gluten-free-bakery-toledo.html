Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to severe digestive issues, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term health complications. Additionally, many people without celiac disease experience non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can cause symptoms like bloating, fatigue, and headaches. By choosing gluten-free options, these individuals can enjoy their favorite foods without compromising their health.
