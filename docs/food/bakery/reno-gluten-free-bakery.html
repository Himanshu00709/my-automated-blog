The Science Behind Gluten-Free Baking

Gluten-free baking is a science in itself. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, provides elasticity and structure to baked goods. When removed, bakers must find alternative ways to achieve the same texture and consistency. Reno Gluten-Free Bakery excels in this area by using a combination of gluten-free flours such as almond flour, coconut flour, and tapioca starch. These ingredients not only mimic the texture of gluten but also add unique flavors and nutritional benefits.

Nutritional Benefits

Many of the ingredients used in gluten-free baking are naturally nutrient-dense. For example, almond flour is rich in healthy fats, protein, and vitamin E, while coconut flour is high in fiber and low in carbohydrates. By incorporating these ingredients, Reno Gluten-Free Bakery not only caters to those with dietary restrictions but also offers healthier options for everyone.
