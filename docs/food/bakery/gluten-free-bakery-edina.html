Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. For those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. Even for those without these conditions, many people choose gluten-free diets for their potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and reduced inflammation.

Edina's gluten-free bakeries understand the importance of providing safe, delicious options for their customers. They go above and beyond to ensure that their products are free from cross-contamination, using dedicated equipment and certified gluten-free ingredients. This commitment to quality and safety makes Edina a standout destination for gluten-free baked goods.

The Importance of Gluten-Free Certification

When it comes to gluten-free products, certification is key. Gluten-free certification ensures that products meet strict standards for gluten content, typically less than 20 parts per million (ppm). This is crucial for individuals with celiac disease, as even trace amounts of gluten can trigger symptoms and cause intestinal damage.

Edina's gluten-free bakeries take this seriously, with many of them offering certified gluten-free products. This commitment to safety and quality gives customers peace of mind, knowing that they can enjoy their favorite baked goods without worrying about gluten contamination.
