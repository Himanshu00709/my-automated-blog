Why Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten consumption. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe digestive issues, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term health complications for those with celiac disease. Additionally, many people experience non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can lead to similar symptoms without the autoimmune response. For these individuals, gluten-free bakeries like the one in Des Moines provide a safe and delicious alternative to traditional baked goods.
