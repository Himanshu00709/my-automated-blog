Why Choose a Gluten-Free Bakery?

Gluten-free bakeries specialize in creating baked goods that are free from gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Gluten-free bakeries ensure that their products are safe for consumption by using alternative flours like almond, coconut, rice, and tapioca flour. These bakeries also take extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination, making them a trusted choice for gluten-free consumers.

Most gluten-free bakeries take extensive measures to ensure their products are safe for celiac patients, including using dedicated facilities and avoiding cross-contamination. However, it's always a good idea to check with the bakery about their specific practices.

Yes, many gluten-free bakeries in Eagan also offer vegan options, catering to those who follow a plant-based diet in addition to being gluten-free.

4. Are gluten-free baked goods more expensive?

Gluten-free baked goods can be slightly more expensive due to the cost of alternative ingredients and the specialized processes involved. However, many customers find the quality and health benefits well worth the price.
