FAQs About Gluten-Free Bakeries in Beaverton

1. Are all products in gluten-free bakeries completely gluten-free?

Yes, most gluten-free bakeries in Beaverton are dedicated gluten-free facilities, meaning that all products are made without gluten-containing ingredients. However, it’s always a good idea to check with the bakery if you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

2. Do gluten-free bakeries offer vegan options?

Many gluten-free bakeries in Beaverton also offer vegan options. Be sure to ask the staff or check the menu for vegan-friendly items.

3. Can I order custom cakes or pastries from these bakeries?

Absolutely! Most gluten-free bakeries in Beaverton offer custom orders for cakes, cupcakes, and other pastries. It’s best to place your order in advance, especially for special occasions.

4. Are gluten-free baked goods more expensive?

Gluten-free baked goods can sometimes be more expensive due to the cost of specialty ingredients. However, many people find the quality and taste to be well worth the price.
