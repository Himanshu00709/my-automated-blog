Why Choose a Gluten-Free Bakery?

For many, gluten-free living is not a choice but a necessity. Celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten, affects approximately 1 in 100 people worldwide. Additionally, non-celiac gluten sensitivity and wheat allergies have become increasingly common. For these individuals, finding safe and delicious baked goods can be a challenge. Traditional bakeries often lack the facilities and knowledge to prevent cross-contamination, making gluten-free options risky. This is where the Savannah Gluten-Free Bakery shines, offering a completely gluten-free environment where customers can indulge without worry.

FAQs About Savannah Gluten-Free Bakery

1. Is the bakery completely gluten-free?

Yes, the Savannah Gluten-Free Bakery is a 100% gluten-free facility. This ensures that there is no risk of cross-contamination, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance.

2. Do they offer vegan options?

Absolutely! The bakery has a variety of vegan options, including pastries, desserts, and breads. These items are made without any animal products, making them suitable for vegans and those with dairy allergies.

3. Can I place custom orders for special occasions?
