Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Additionally, many people experience non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. For these individuals, avoiding gluten is not just a dietary preference but a necessity for maintaining good health.

Gluten-free bakeries in Des Moines, IA, understand the importance of providing safe, delicious options for their customers. These bakeries go above and beyond to ensure that their products are free from cross-contamination, offering peace of mind to those with gluten-related health concerns.
