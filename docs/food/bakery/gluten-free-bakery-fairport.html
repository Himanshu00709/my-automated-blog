Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from digestive discomfort to more serious long-term health complications. As a result, the need for gluten-free options has become more than just a dietary preference—it's a necessity for many.

Fairport's gluten-free bakeries have risen to the occasion, offering a wide range of products that are not only safe but also delicious. From bread and pastries to cakes and cookies, these bakeries ensure that no one has to miss out on the joy of baked goods.
