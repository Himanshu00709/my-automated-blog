Why Gluten-Free Baking Matters

Gluten-free baking has become increasingly popular, not just for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, but also for people looking to improve their overall health. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause inflammation and digestive issues in some individuals. For those with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health complications. Gluten-free bakeries in Palm Springs are stepping up to provide safe, delicious alternatives that don’t compromise on taste or texture.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Bakeries in Palm Springs

1. Are gluten-free baked goods healthier?

Not necessarily. While gluten-free baked goods are essential for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, they can still be high in sugar and calories. It’s important to read labels and choose options made with whole, nutritious ingredients.

2. Can I find vegan options at gluten-free bakeries?

Yes, many gluten-free bakeries in Palm Springs also offer vegan options, using plant-based ingredients like almond milk, flax eggs, and coconut oil.

3. How do I know if a bakery is truly gluten-free?

Look for bakeries that are certified gluten-free or have strict protocols in place to avoid cross-contamination. Many bakeries will also have their ingredients and processes clearly listed on their website or in-store.

4. Can I order custom cakes and pastries?

Absolutely! Many gluten-free bakeries in Palm Springs offer custom orders for special occasions, ensuring that you can enjoy a delicious, gluten-free treat for any event.
