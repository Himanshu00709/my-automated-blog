Why Choose a Gluten-Free Bakery?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to severe health complications, including digestive issues, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Even for those without a medical condition, many people choose gluten-free diets to improve digestion, reduce inflammation, or simply explore healthier eating habits. Gluten-free bakeries, like those in Lititz, PA, provide a safe and delicious alternative to traditional baked goods, ensuring that everyone can enjoy treats without worry.
