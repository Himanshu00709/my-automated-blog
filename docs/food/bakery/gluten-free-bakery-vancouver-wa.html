Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have become increasingly popular for various reasons. For some, it's a medical necessity due to conditions like celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For others, it's a lifestyle choice aimed at improving overall health and well-being. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause inflammation and digestive issues in sensitive individuals. By opting for gluten-free products, you can enjoy your favorite baked goods without the adverse effects.
