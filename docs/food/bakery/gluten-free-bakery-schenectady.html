Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to severe digestive issues, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term health complications. Additionally, many people without celiac disease experience gluten sensitivity, which can cause symptoms like bloating, fatigue, and headaches. Choosing gluten-free options ensures that these individuals can enjoy baked goods without compromising their health.

1. Are all products in gluten-free bakeries safe for celiacs?

Most gluten-free bakeries in Schenectady take strict measures to avoid cross-contamination. However, it's always a good idea to ask the staff about their preparation processes to ensure that the products are safe for celiacs.

2. Can I find vegan options at gluten-free bakeries?

Yes, many gluten-free bakeries in Schenectady also offer vegan options. Be sure to check the menu or ask the staff for recommendations.

3. Do gluten-free bakeries offer custom orders?

4. Are gluten-free baked goods more expensive?
