Graeter's Gluten-Free: A Delicious and Safe Indulgence

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding delicious and safe desserts can be a challenge. Enter Graeter's, a brand renowned for its premium ice cream and commitment to quality. Graeter's gluten-free offerings provide a safe and indulgent option for those who need to avoid gluten without compromising on taste. In this article, we’ll explore the world of Graeter's gluten-free products, their ingredients, and why they stand out in the crowded market of gluten-free desserts.

What Makes Graeter's Gluten-Free Ice Cream Special?

Graeter's has been crafting ice cream since 1870, and their dedication to quality is evident in every scoop. Their gluten-free ice cream is no exception. Made with the same care and attention to detail as their traditional flavors, Graeter's gluten-free options are crafted to ensure they meet the highest standards of safety and taste. Here’s what sets them apart:

  • High-Quality Ingredients: Graeter's uses only the finest ingredients, including fresh cream, pure cane sugar, and real fruit. Their gluten-free flavors are made without any artificial additives or preservatives.
  • French Pot Process: Graeter's ice cream is made in small batches using the French Pot process, which results in a denser, creamier texture. This method ensures that each flavor is rich and indulgent.
  • Gluten-Free Certification: Graeter's gluten-free ice cream is certified by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO), ensuring that it meets strict gluten-free standards.

FAQs About Graeter's Gluten-Free Ice Cream

Is Graeter's gluten-free ice cream safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Graeter's gluten-free ice cream is certified by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO), ensuring that it meets strict gluten-free standards and is safe for those with celiac disease.

What flavors of Graeter's ice cream are gluten-free?

Graeter's offers a variety of gluten-free flavors, including Black Raspberry Chocolate Chip, Mint Chocolate Chip, Vanilla, Chocolate, and Strawberry. Always check the label for the most up-to-date information.

Where can I buy Graeter's gluten-free ice cream?

Graeter's gluten-free ice cream is available at select grocery stores, Graeter's scoop shops, and online through their website. Use their store locator to find a retailer near you.

Does Graeter's offer dairy-free or vegan options?

While Graeter's is known for its traditional dairy-based ice cream, they also offer a selection of sorbets that are dairy-free and vegan-friendly. However, these options may not be gluten-free, so always check the label.
