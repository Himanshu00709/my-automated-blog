Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms like digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Beyond medical necessity, many people choose gluten-free diets for perceived health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels. Regardless of the reason, finding a bakery that caters to these needs is a game-changer.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Baking

1. What is gluten, and why do some people avoid it?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. People with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity must avoid gluten to prevent adverse health effects. Others choose gluten-free diets for personal health reasons.

2. Is gluten-free baking more expensive?

Gluten-free baking can be more expensive due to the cost of alternative flours and ingredients. However, many people find the health benefits and peace of mind worth the investment.

3. Can gluten-free baked goods taste as good as traditional ones?

Absolutely! With the right techniques and ingredients, gluten-free baked goods can be just as delicious as their gluten-containing counterparts. The bakery in St. Peters, MO, is proof of this.

4. How do I know if a bakery is truly gluten-free?

Look for bakeries that are certified gluten-free or operate in dedicated gluten-free facilities. This ensures that there is no risk of cross-contamination.
