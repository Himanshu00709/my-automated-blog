Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. For others, gluten sensitivity can cause a range of symptoms, from bloating and fatigue to joint pain and headaches. The rise in awareness of these conditions has led to an increased demand for gluten-free options, and Saratoga Springs has risen to the occasion with its exceptional gluten-free bakery.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Baking

1. What is gluten, and why do some people avoid it?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. People with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity must avoid gluten to prevent adverse health effects.

2. Are all gluten-free products healthy?

Not necessarily. While gluten-free products are essential for those with gluten-related disorders, they can still be high in sugar, fat, and calories. It’s important to read labels and choose whole, nutrient-dense options.

3. Can I bake gluten-free at home?

Absolutely! With the right ingredients and recipes, you can create delicious gluten-free baked goods at home. Look for gluten-free flours like almond, coconut, or rice flour.

4. Is the bakery’s kitchen completely gluten-free?

Yes, the bakery’s kitchen is 100% gluten-free, ensuring that there is no risk of cross-contamination.
