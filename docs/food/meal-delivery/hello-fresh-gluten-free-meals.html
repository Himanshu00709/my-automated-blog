How HelloFresh Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. HelloFresh takes several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free meals:

  • Dedicated Facilities: HelloFresh works with suppliers who have dedicated gluten-free facilities to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Clear Labeling: All gluten-free meals are clearly labeled, making it easy for customers to identify suitable options.
  • Rigorous Testing: Ingredients are rigorously tested to ensure they meet gluten-free standards.
  • Customer Support: HelloFresh offers robust customer support to address any concerns or questions regarding gluten-free meals.

FAQs About HelloFresh Gluten-Free Meals

1. Are all HelloFresh meals gluten-free?

No, not all HelloFresh meals are gluten-free. However, HelloFresh offers a dedicated selection of gluten-free meals that are clearly labeled on their menu.

2. Can I customize my HelloFresh box to be entirely gluten-free?

Yes, you can customize your HelloFresh box to include only gluten-free meals. Simply select the gluten-free options available on the menu each week.

3. Are the gluten-free meals suitable for people with celiac disease?

HelloFresh takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, but individuals with celiac disease should consult with their healthcare provider before subscribing. Always check the labels and ingredient lists for any potential allergens.

4. How do I know if a meal is gluten-free?

Gluten-free meals are clearly marked on the HelloFresh menu. You can also find detailed ingredient lists and nutritional information for each meal on the HelloFresh website.

5. Are there vegetarian or vegan gluten-free options?

Yes, HelloFresh offers a variety of vegetarian and vegan gluten-free meals. You can filter the menu to display only vegetarian or vegan options that are also gluten-free.
