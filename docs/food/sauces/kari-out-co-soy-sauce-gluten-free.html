Why Choose Gluten-Free Soy Sauce?

Traditional soy sauce is made from fermented soybeans and wheat, which means it contains gluten. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health problems. Gluten-free soy sauce, like the one offered by Kari-Out Co., is made without wheat, using alternative ingredients such as rice or other gluten-free grains. This makes it a safe and delicious option for those who need to avoid gluten.
