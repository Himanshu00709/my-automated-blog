Key Ingredients

The sauce is crafted with a meticulous selection of ingredients to ensure both flavor and safety for those with gluten intolerance. The primary components include:

  • Orange Juice Concentrate: Provides a natural citrusy tang.
  • Gluten-Free Soy Sauce: Made from soybeans, rice, and salt, ensuring a rich umami flavor.
  • Sugar: Adds a subtle sweetness to balance the tanginess.
  • Vinegar: Enhances the overall flavor profile.

Culinary Uses of Kikkoman Gluten-Free Orange Sauce

The versatility of Kikkoman Gluten-Free Orange Sauce is one of its most appealing attributes. Here are some ways you can incorporate it into your cooking:

  • Marinades: Use it as a marinade for chicken, beef, or tofu. The citrusy flavor tenderizes the protein while infusing it with a delightful taste.
  • Stir-Fries: Add a splash of the sauce to your stir-fry dishes for an instant flavor boost.
  • Dipping Sauce: Serve it as a dipping sauce for spring rolls, dumplings, or tempura.
  • Salad Dressing: Mix it with a bit of olive oil and sesame oil to create a unique salad dressing.
  • Glazes: Brush it over grilled or roasted vegetables for a glossy, flavorful finish.

FAQs About Kikkoman Gluten-Free Orange Sauce

Is Kikkoman Gluten-Free Orange Sauce suitable for vegans?

Yes, the sauce is vegan-friendly as it contains no animal-derived ingredients.

Can I use this sauce if I have a soy allergy?

Unfortunately, no. The sauce contains soy sauce, which is derived from soybeans. If you have a soy allergy, it’s best to avoid this product.

How should I store the sauce after opening?

Once opened, store the sauce in the refrigerator to maintain its freshness and flavor. It should be consumed within a few weeks.

Is this sauce available internationally?

Kikkoman products are widely available in many countries. However, availability may vary, so it’s best to check with local retailers or online stores.
