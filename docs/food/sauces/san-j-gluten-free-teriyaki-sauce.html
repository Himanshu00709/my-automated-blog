What Makes San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce Special?

San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce stands out in the crowded market of teriyaki sauces for several reasons. The sauce is made with tamari, a type of soy sauce that is naturally gluten-free. Tamari is richer and less salty than regular soy sauce, giving the teriyaki sauce a deeper, more complex flavor. Combined with a blend of spices, vinegar, and a touch of sweetness, San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce delivers a balanced taste that is both bold and nuanced.

How to Use San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce in Your Cooking

One of the best things about San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce is its versatility. It can be used in a variety of dishes, from traditional Japanese cuisine to fusion recipes. Here are some creative ways to incorporate it into your meals:

1. Marinade for Meats and Seafood

San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce makes an excellent marinade for chicken, beef, pork, or seafood. Simply coat your protein of choice with the sauce and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for deeper flavor) before grilling, baking, or sautéing.

2. Stir-Fry Sauce

Add a splash of San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce to your stir-fry for an instant flavor boost. It pairs beautifully with vegetables, tofu, and noodles, creating a dish that is both satisfying and nutritious.

3. Dipping Sauce

Use San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce as a dipping sauce for sushi, spring rolls, or tempura. Its rich, savory flavor complements the delicate taste of these dishes perfectly.

4. Salad Dressing

Mix San-J Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce with a bit of olive oil, rice vinegar, and sesame oil to create a unique salad dressing. It’s especially delicious on Asian-inspired salads with ingredients like cabbage, carrots, and edamame.
