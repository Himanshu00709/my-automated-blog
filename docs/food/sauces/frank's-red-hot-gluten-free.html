What Makes Frank's RedHot Gluten-Free?

Frank's RedHot is made from a simple yet flavorful blend of aged cayenne peppers, vinegar, water, salt, and garlic powder. The absence of gluten-containing ingredients is what makes this hot sauce a safe choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The brand takes pride in ensuring that their product meets the strict standards required for gluten-free certification, giving consumers peace of mind when enjoying their favorite spicy condiment.

FAQs About Frank's RedHot Gluten-Free

Is Frank's RedHot certified gluten-free?

Yes, Frank's RedHot is certified gluten-free, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Does Frank's RedHot contain any allergens?

Frank's RedHot is free from common allergens like dairy, nuts, and soy. However, it does contain vinegar, which is derived from grains, but the distillation process removes gluten proteins.

Can I use Frank's RedHot on a low-carb diet?

Absolutely! Frank's RedHot is zero-carb, making it an excellent choice for those following a low-carb or keto diet.

How long does Frank's RedHot last after opening?

When stored in a cool, dry place, Frank's RedHot can last up to a year after opening. However, for the best flavor, it’s recommended to use it within six months.
