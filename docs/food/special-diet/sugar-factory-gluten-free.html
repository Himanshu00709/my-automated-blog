Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe allergic reactions. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health. The Sugar Factory has recognized this need and has made significant efforts to cater to this growing demographic.

How Sugar Factory Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Ensuring that gluten-free items are truly free from gluten contamination is crucial for the safety of customers with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The Sugar Factory takes several steps to maintain the integrity of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: The kitchen has designated areas for preparing gluten-free items to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Gluten-free dishes are prepared using separate utensils and cookware to prevent any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to understand the importance of gluten-free preparation and are knowledgeable about the menu's gluten-free options.
  • Ingredient Sourcing: The Sugar Factory sources high-quality, certified gluten-free ingredients to ensure the safety and satisfaction of their customers.
