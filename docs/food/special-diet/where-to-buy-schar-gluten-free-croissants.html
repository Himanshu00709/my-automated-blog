FAQs About Schar Gluten-Free Croissants

1. Are Schar gluten-free croissants dairy-free?

No, Schar gluten-free croissants contain dairy. If you’re looking for a dairy-free option, you may need to explore other brands or recipes.

2. Can I freeze Schar gluten-free croissants?

Yes, you can freeze Schar gluten-free croissants. Simply place them in an airtight container or freezer bag and store them for up to three months. Thaw at room temperature or in the microwave when ready to eat.

3. Are Schar gluten-free croissants vegan?

No, Schar gluten-free croissants are not vegan as they contain dairy and eggs. However, Schar offers other vegan-friendly products that you might enjoy.

4. How do Schar gluten-free croissants taste compared to regular croissants?

Schar gluten-free croissants are known for their light, flaky texture and buttery flavor, making them very similar to traditional croissants. Many people find them to be a satisfying alternative.
