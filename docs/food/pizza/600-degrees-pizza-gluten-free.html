The Art of Gluten-Free Pizza at 600 Degrees

At 600 Degrees Pizza, the process of creating a gluten-free pizza is both an art and a science. The restaurant uses a specialized gluten-free dough that is carefully crafted to mimic the texture and flavor of traditional pizza crust. This dough is made from a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, which are combined to create a light, airy, and crispy crust.

This intense heat ensures that the crust is perfectly cooked, with a crispy exterior and a soft, chewy interior.
