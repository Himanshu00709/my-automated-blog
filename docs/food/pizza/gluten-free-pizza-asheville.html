FAQs About Gluten-Free Pizza in Asheville

1. Is gluten-free pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, many pizzerias in Asheville take extra precautions to ensure that their gluten-free pizzas are safe for those with celiac disease. This includes using separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Can I find vegan gluten-free pizza in Asheville?

Absolutely! Many pizzerias in Asheville offer vegan and gluten-free options, allowing you to enjoy a pizza that meets both dietary preferences.

3. Are gluten-free pizzas more expensive?

While gluten-free pizzas may be slightly more expensive due to the cost of specialty ingredients, many diners find the quality and taste to be well worth the price.

4. Do I need to make a reservation for gluten-free pizza?

It's always a good idea to call ahead, especially if you're dining during peak hours. Some pizzerias may require advance notice to prepare gluten-free crusts.
