What Makes Mellow Mushroom Gluten-Free Pizza Special?

Mellow Mushroom's gluten-free pizza is crafted with care to ensure that it meets the needs of those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. The crust is made from a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour, tapioca flour, and potato starch, which gives it a light and airy texture that is surprisingly close to traditional pizza crust. The pizza is then topped with fresh, high-quality ingredients, ensuring that every bite is packed with flavor.

One of the standout features of Mellow Mushroom's gluten-free pizza is its commitment to avoiding cross-contamination. The pizzeria takes extra precautions to ensure that gluten-free pizzas are prepared separately from their gluten-containing counterparts. This includes using dedicated utensils, pans, and preparation areas, making it a safe option for those with severe gluten allergies.
