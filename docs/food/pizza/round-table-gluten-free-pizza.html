What Makes Round Table Gluten-Free Pizza Special?

Round Table Pizza has been a staple in the pizza industry for decades, and their gluten-free pizza is no exception to their high standards. The gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, creating a crispy yet tender base that rivals traditional pizza crusts. One of the standout features of Round Table's gluten-free pizza is the variety of options available. Nutritional Benefits of Gluten-Free Pizza

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, from digestive discomfort to more severe autoimmune reactions. By opting for a gluten-free pizza, individuals can avoid these problems and enjoy a meal that is both safe and satisfying. Additionally, gluten-free diets have been associated with other health benefits, such as improved digestion, increased energy levels, and reduced inflammation.

Round Table's gluten-free pizza is not only safe for those with gluten-related health concerns but also offers a balanced nutritional profile. The crust is lower in calories and carbohydrates compared to traditional pizza crusts, making it a suitable option for those looking to manage their weight or blood sugar levels. The toppings, which include fresh vegetables, lean proteins, and high-quality cheeses, provide essential nutrients that contribute to overall health and well-being.

How Round Table Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity is the risk of cross-contamination. Even a small amount of gluten can trigger symptoms, so it's crucial that gluten-free foods are prepared in a controlled environment. Round Table Pizza takes this responsibility seriously and has implemented strict protocols to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings.

From the moment the gluten-free crust is received at the restaurant, it is stored separately from other ingredients to prevent any contact with gluten-containing products. The pizza is prepared on a dedicated surface, and staff are trained to follow specific procedures to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. Additionally, Round Table uses separate utensils and equipment for gluten-free pizzas, further reducing the likelihood of gluten exposure.
