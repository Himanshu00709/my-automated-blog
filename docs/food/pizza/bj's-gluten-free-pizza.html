What Makes BJ's Gluten-Free Pizza Special?

BJ's gluten-free pizza is crafted with care, ensuring that it meets the needs of those who must avoid gluten. The crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, which provides a light and crispy texture that rivals traditional pizza crusts. The pizza is then topped with high-quality ingredients, including fresh vegetables, premium meats, and a rich tomato sauce, all of which are gluten-free.

Ingredients and Preparation

BJ's takes great care in preparing their gluten-free pizza to avoid cross-contamination. The pizza is prepared in a dedicated gluten-free area of the kitchen, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with the utmost care. This attention to detail ensures that customers with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities can enjoy their meal without worry.
