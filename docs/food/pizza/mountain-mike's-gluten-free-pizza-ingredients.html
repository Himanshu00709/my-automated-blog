FAQs About Mountain Mike's Gluten-Free Pizza

Is Mountain Mike's gluten-free pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Mountain Mike's gluten-free pizza is made with ingredients that are free from gluten, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen, so it's important to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions.

Can I customize my gluten-free pizza at Mountain Mike's?

Absolutely! Mountain Mike's offers a wide range of toppings and sauces that are gluten-free, allowing you to create a pizza that suits your taste preferences.

How does the gluten-free crust compare to the regular crust?

While the gluten-free crust has a slightly different texture due to the absence of gluten, it is still delicious and satisfying. It's slightly thinner and crispier, providing a unique eating experience.

Are there any vegan options for gluten-free pizza at Mountain Mike's?

Yes, Mountain Mike's offers vegan cheese and a variety of vegetable toppings that can be added to your gluten-free pizza, making it a great option for vegans.
