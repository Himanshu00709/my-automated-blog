Blaze Gluten-Free Pizza: A Delicious and Healthy Choice

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free options has skyrocketed, and for good reason. What Makes Blaze Gluten-Free Pizza Special?

Blaze Pizza has carved out a niche in the fast-casual dining scene by offering a unique, build-your-own pizza experience. What sets their gluten-free pizza apart is not just the absence of gluten, but the care and attention to detail that goes into every step of the process. From the dough to the toppings, Blaze ensures that their gluten-free pizzas are safe, delicious, and satisfying.

Gluten-Free Dough: The Foundation of a Great Pizza

The cornerstone of any pizza is its dough, and Blaze’s gluten-free dough is no exception. Made with a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, this dough is carefully crafted to mimic the texture and flavor of traditional pizza crust. It’s also free from artificial preservatives and flavors, making it a healthier choice for those with dietary restrictions. The dough is rolled out thin and cooked in a high-temperature oven, resulting in a crispy, golden crust that’s both flavorful and satisfying.

How Blaze Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a major concern. Blaze Pizza takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free pizzas are safe for consumption. Here’s how they do it:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Blaze uses dedicated preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free pizzas to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Gluten-Free Certification: Their gluten-free dough is certified by the Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG), ensuring that it meets strict gluten-free standards.
  • Staff Training: All Blaze employees undergo thorough training on gluten-free protocols to ensure that every pizza is prepared safely.
