Why Gluten-Free Pizza?

Gluten-free pizza is more than just a trend; it's a necessity for many people. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Symptoms can range from digestive problems to skin rashes and even neurological issues. For these individuals, consuming gluten-free food is not a choice but a requirement for maintaining their health. Fortunately, the demand for gluten-free options has led to a surge in restaurants offering gluten-free pizzas that are both safe and delicious.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Pizza in St. Paul, MN

1. Is gluten-free pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, as long as the restaurant takes proper precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Always inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure they take the necessary steps.

2. Can I find vegan gluten-free pizza in St. Paul?

Absolutely! Many restaurants, like Black Sheep Pizza, offer both gluten-free crusts and vegan cheese options, making it easy to enjoy a pizza that meets both dietary needs.

3. Are gluten-free pizzas more expensive?

Gluten-free pizzas can be slightly more expensive due to the cost of gluten-free ingredients and the additional precautions taken during preparation. However, the price difference is usually minimal.

4. Can I order gluten-free pizza for delivery?

Yes, many of the restaurants listed above offer delivery services. Just make sure to specify that you need a gluten-free pizza when placing your order.
