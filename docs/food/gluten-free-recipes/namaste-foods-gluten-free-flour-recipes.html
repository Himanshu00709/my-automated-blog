1. Gluten-Free Banana Bread

Banana bread is a classic treat that’s even better when made gluten-free. Here’s how to make it:

Ingredients Quantity
Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour 2 cups
Ripe bananas (mashed) 3
Eggs 2
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Butter (melted) 1/2 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the mashed bananas, melted butter, sugar, and eggs.
  3. Add the Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Let the bread cool before slicing and serving.

2. Gluten-Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

Who doesn’t love a good chocolate chip cookie? This gluten-free version is just as delicious as the original.

Ingredients Quantity
Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour 2 1/4 cups
Butter (softened) 1 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 9-11 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

FAQs About Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour

1. Can I use Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour for all types of baking?

Yes, Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour is versatile and can be used for baking bread, cakes, cookies, and even thickening sauces. It’s a 1:1 substitute for wheat flour in most recipes.

2. Is Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour safe for people with celiac disease?

Absolutely. Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour is certified gluten-free and produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

3. How should I store Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour?

Store the flour in a cool, dry place, preferably in an airtight container. It can also be stored in the refrigerator or freezer to extend its shelf life.

4. Can I use Namaste Foods Gluten-Free Flour for yeast-based recipes?

Yes, you can use it for yeast-based recipes like bread and pizza dough. However, you may need to add xanthan gum or another binding agent to achieve the desired texture.
