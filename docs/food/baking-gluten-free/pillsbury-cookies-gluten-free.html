Baking Tips for Perfect Gluten-Free Cookies

Baking gluten-free cookies can be a bit different from baking traditional cookies, but with a few tips, you can achieve perfect results every time:

  • Follow the Instructions: Always follow the baking instructions on the package for the best results.
  • Use Parchment Paper: To prevent sticking, line your baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Don’t Overbake: Gluten-free cookies can dry out quickly, so keep a close eye on them as they bake.
  • Let Them Cool: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack.

FAQs About Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies

Are Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies are certified gluten-free and are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities.

Can I find Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies at my local grocery store?

Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies are widely available at most major grocery stores. You can usually find them in the refrigerated dough section.

Do Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies contain dairy?

Some varieties of Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookies may contain dairy. Always check the ingredient list if you have a dairy allergy or intolerance.

Can I freeze Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookie dough?

Yes, you can freeze the dough for up to 2 months. Just make sure to wrap it tightly in plastic wrap or place it in an airtight container before freezing.
