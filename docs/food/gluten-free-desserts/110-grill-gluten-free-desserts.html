The Importance of Gluten-Free Desserts

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Gluten-free desserts, like those offered at 110 Grill, provide a safe and enjoyable way for these individuals to indulge in sweet treats without compromising their health. Moreover, gluten-free desserts are often made with alternative flours and ingredients that can offer additional nutritional benefits, such as higher fiber content or lower glycemic index.
