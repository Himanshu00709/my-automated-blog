FAQs About David's Gluten-Free Brownies

Are David's Gluten-Free Brownies suitable for vegans?

While the base recipe for David's Gluten-Free Brownies is vegan-friendly, it's always best to check the specific ingredients list, as some variations may include dairy or other non-vegan ingredients.

Can I freeze David's Gluten-Free Brownies?

Yes, these brownies freeze exceptionally well. Simply wrap them tightly in plastic wrap or place them in an airtight container before freezing. They can be thawed at room temperature or warmed in the oven when you're ready to enjoy them.

Do David's Gluten-Free Brownies contain nuts?

Yes, the primary flour used in these brownies is almond flour, which is made from almonds. If you have a nut allergy, it's important to be cautious and consider alternative gluten-free brownie options.

How do David's Gluten-Free Brownies compare to traditional brownies?

David's Gluten-Free Brownies are just as rich and fudgy as traditional brownies, but with the added benefit of being gluten-free and made with healthier ingredients. Many people find them to be even more satisfying due to their nutrient-dense profile.
