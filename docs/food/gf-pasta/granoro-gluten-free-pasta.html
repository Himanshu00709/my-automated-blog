Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta suitable for celiacs?

Yes, Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta is specifically designed for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. It is made in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

Where can I buy Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta?

Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta is available at most major supermarkets, health food stores, and online retailers. Check the Granoro website for a list of retailers near you.

Can I use Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta in any recipe that calls for regular pasta?

Absolutely! Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta can be used in any recipe that calls for traditional pasta. Its texture and taste are very similar, making it an excellent substitute in all your favorite dishes.

How should I store Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta?

Store Granoro Gluten-Free Pasta in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. Once opened, transfer any unused pasta to an airtight container to maintain freshness.
