What is Amy's Kitchen?

Amy's Kitchen is a family-owned company that has been producing organic and natural frozen meals since 1987. Founded by Andy and Rachel Berliner, the company was inspired by their daughter Amy, who was born with a sensitivity to certain foods. Amy's Kitchen has since grown into a trusted brand, offering a wide range of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Their commitment to quality, sustainability, and health has made them a favorite among consumers seeking convenient yet nutritious meal solutions.

The Importance of Gluten-Free Diets

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it is commonly present in many processed foods. A gluten-free diet eliminates these grains and focuses on naturally gluten-free foods like fruits, vegetables, meats, and certain grains like rice and quinoa. Amy's Kitchen has made it easier for those on a gluten-free diet to enjoy a variety of meals without compromising on taste or nutrition.

No, not all Amy's products are gluten-free. However, they have a dedicated line of gluten-free products that are clearly labeled. Always check the packaging to ensure the product meets your dietary needs.

2. Are Amy's gluten-free products also vegan?

Some of Amy's gluten-free products are vegan, but not all. For example, their Gluten-Free Mac & Cheese is dairy-free and vegan, while their Gluten-Free Margherita Pizza contains cheese. Be sure to read the labels if you're following a vegan diet.

3. Where can I buy Amy's gluten-free products?

4. Are Amy's gluten-free products suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Amy's gluten-free products are made in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease.
