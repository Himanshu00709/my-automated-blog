The History of Stonewall Kitchen

Stonewall Kitchen was founded in 1991 by Jonathan King and Jim Stott, who started by selling homemade jams and jellies at local farmers' markets. Their passion for creating high-quality, flavorful products quickly gained them a loyal following. Over the years, the company has grown exponentially, offering a wide range of gourmet foods, including sauces, condiments, baking mixes, and more. Their commitment to quality and innovation has made them a household name, and their foray into gluten-free products is a testament to their dedication to meeting the evolving needs of their customers.
