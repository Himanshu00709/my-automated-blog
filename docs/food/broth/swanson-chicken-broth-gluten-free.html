What Makes Swanson Chicken Broth Gluten-Free?

Swanson Chicken Broth is made from a blend of high-quality ingredients, including chicken stock, salt, and natural flavors. The brand takes pride in ensuring that its products meet strict gluten-free standards. This means that the broth is free from wheat, barley, rye, and other gluten-containing grains. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, this is a crucial factor when selecting food products.

Swanson’s commitment to quality is evident in its manufacturing processes. The company adheres to rigorous testing protocols to ensure that its chicken broth is free from gluten cross-contamination. This makes Swanson Chicken Broth a safe and reliable choice for those who need to avoid gluten in their diet.

FAQs About Swanson Chicken Broth

Is Swanson Chicken Broth certified gluten-free?

While Swanson Chicken Broth is not certified gluten-free, the brand follows strict manufacturing processes to ensure that its products are free from gluten and cross-contamination. Always check the label for the most up-to-date information.

Can Swanson Chicken Broth be used in a vegetarian diet?

No, Swanson Chicken Broth is made from chicken stock and is not suitable for vegetarians. However, Swanson offers a variety of vegetable broths that are vegetarian-friendly.

How should Swanson Chicken Broth be stored?

Swanson Chicken Broth should be stored in a cool, dry place before opening. Once opened, it should be refrigerated and used within a few days. Alternatively, you can freeze the broth for longer storage.
