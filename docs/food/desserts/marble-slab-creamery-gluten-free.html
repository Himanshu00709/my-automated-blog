Understanding Gluten-Free Needs

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, which can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to digestive issues, fatigue, and other health problems. Therefore, it’s crucial to identify safe food options that are free from gluten contamination.

Marble Slab Creamery recognizes the importance of catering to dietary restrictions and has taken steps to provide gluten-free options. However, it’s essential to understand that while some products are gluten-free, cross-contamination can still occur in a shared kitchen environment. Always communicate your dietary needs to the staff to ensure your safety.

How Marble Slab Creamery Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Marble Slab Creamery takes several steps to minimize the risk of cross-contamination and ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings. These measures include:

  • Separate Utensils: Staff use separate scoops and utensils for gluten-free products to prevent cross-contact.
  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Whenever possible, gluten-free items are prepared in designated areas to avoid contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free requests and understand the importance of avoiding cross-contamination.

Despite these precautions, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff about your gluten-free needs so they can take extra care when preparing your order.
