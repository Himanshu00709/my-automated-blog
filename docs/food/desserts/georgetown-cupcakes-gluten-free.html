The Baking Process: Ensuring Gluten-Free Quality

One of the most important aspects of gluten-free baking is ensuring that there is no cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients. Georgetown Cupcakes takes this responsibility seriously. Their gluten-free cupcakes are baked in a dedicated gluten-free facility, separate from their regular baking operations. This ensures that there is no risk of gluten particles contaminating the gluten-free products.

In addition to using a separate facility, Georgetown Cupcakes also sources their ingredients from trusted suppliers who provide gluten-free certified products. Each batch of gluten-free cupcakes is tested to ensure that it meets the strict gluten-free standards set by the FDA. This level of care and attention to detail is what makes Georgetown Cupcakes a trusted name in the gluten-free community.
