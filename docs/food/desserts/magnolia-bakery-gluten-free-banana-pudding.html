The History of Magnolia Bakery’s Banana Pudding

Magnolia Bakery first opened its doors in New York City in 1996, and it didn’t take long for their banana pudding to become a fan favorite. The classic recipe, which features layers of vanilla wafers, fresh bananas, and creamy custard, quickly became a staple for dessert lovers. Over the years, the bakery has expanded its menu to cater to various dietary needs, including gluten-free options. The gluten-free banana pudding is a testament to Magnolia Bakery’s commitment to inclusivity, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a slice of their dessert heaven.

FAQs About Magnolia Bakery’s Gluten-Free Banana Pudding

Is the gluten-free banana pudding available at all Magnolia Bakery locations?

Yes, the gluten-free banana pudding is available at all Magnolia Bakery locations. However, it’s always a good idea to call ahead and confirm availability, as some locations may have limited quantities.

Can I order the gluten-free banana pudding online?

Absolutely! Magnolia Bakery offers online ordering for their gluten-free banana pudding, allowing you to enjoy this delicious treat from the comfort of your home.

Is the gluten-free banana pudding suitable for vegans?

Unfortunately, the gluten-free banana pudding is not vegan-friendly as it contains dairy and eggs. However, Magnolia Bakery is continually expanding its menu to include more dietary options, so keep an eye out for future vegan offerings.

How should I store the gluten-free banana pudding?

The gluten-free banana pudding should be stored in the refrigerator and consumed within 2-3 days for optimal freshness. Be sure to keep it covered to prevent it from absorbing other odors in the fridge.
