For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding a delicious, gluten-free treat can be a challenge. Many ice cream bars and desserts contain gluten either in the form of additives or cross-contamination during production. Magnum has taken great care to ensure that their Double Caramel Gluten-Free ice cream bar is safe for those who need to avoid gluten.

The ingredients used in the Magnum Double Caramel Gluten-Free are carefully selected to ensure they meet strict gluten-free standards. Additionally, the production process is designed to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, giving you peace of mind with every bite. This attention to detail makes the Magnum Double Caramel Gluten-Free not just a treat, but a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.
