What Makes Blue Bunny Ice Cream Gluten-Free?

Blue Bunny Ice Cream is crafted with care, ensuring that many of its products are free from gluten-containing ingredients. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Blue Bunny takes this into account by carefully selecting ingredients and maintaining strict manufacturing processes to avoid cross-contamination.

Some of the popular gluten-free flavors from Blue Bunny include:

  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate
  • Strawberry
  • Butter Pecan
  • Cookies & Cream (made with gluten-free cookies)

It’s important to note that while these flavors are gluten-free, always check the packaging for the most up-to-date information, as formulations can change.
