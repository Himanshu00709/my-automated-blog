What Makes Rita's Italian Ice Gluten-Free?

Rita's Italian Ice is made from a simple blend of water, sugar, and natural fruit flavors. The absence of wheat, barley, rye, or any other gluten-containing ingredients makes it a safe option for those who need to avoid gluten. The company takes great care to ensure that their products are free from cross-contamination, making it a reliable choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

It's important to note that while Rita's Italian Ice is gluten-free, some of their other products, such as custards and certain toppings, may contain gluten. Always check the ingredient list or ask a staff member if you're unsure about a particular item.
