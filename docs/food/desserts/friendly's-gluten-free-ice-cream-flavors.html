Why Choose Gluten-Free Ice Cream?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause serious health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Even trace amounts of gluten can lead to symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. For those who must avoid gluten, it’s crucial to choose products that are certified gluten-free or made in a gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination. Friendly's has recognized this need and offers a range of gluten-free ice cream flavors that are both safe and delicious.
