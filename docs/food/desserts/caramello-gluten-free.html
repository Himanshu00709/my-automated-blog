FAQs About Caramello Gluten-Free

Is Caramello Gluten-Free suitable for vegans?

No, Caramello Gluten-Free contains milk and milk products, making it unsuitable for vegans. However, it is safe for those following a gluten-free diet.

Can I eat Caramello Gluten-Free if I have celiac disease?

Yes, Caramello Gluten-Free is made without gluten-containing ingredients and is processed in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease.

How should I store Caramello Gluten-Free?

It’s best to store Caramello Gluten-Free in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposing it to direct sunlight or high temperatures, as this can cause the chocolate to melt and the caramel to become too soft.

Are there any allergens in Caramello Gluten-Free?

Caramello Gluten-Free contains milk and soy. It is free from gluten, nuts, and other common allergens, but always check the label for the most up-to-date information.
