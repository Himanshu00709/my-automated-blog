FAQs About Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free

1. Is Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free chocolates are certified gluten-free, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities.

2. Are there any allergens in Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free chocolates?

Yes, the product contains milk and soy, which are common allergens. Always check the label if you have other food allergies.

3. Can I use Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free chocolates in baking?

Absolutely! These chocolates can be melted and used in a variety of baking recipes, from cookies to cakes, adding a rich caramel flavor to your desserts.

4. Where can I buy Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free chocolates?

Ghirardelli products are widely available in grocery stores, online retailers like Amazon, and specialty food shops. You can also purchase them directly from the Ghirardelli website.

5. How should I store Ghirardelli Caramel Gluten-Free chocolates?

To maintain their freshness and quality, store the chocolates in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid storing them in the refrigerator, as this can cause the chocolate to develop a white film known as "bloom."
