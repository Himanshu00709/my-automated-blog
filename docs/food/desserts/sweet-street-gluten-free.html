FAQs About Sweet Street Gluten-Free

1. Are Sweet Street Gluten-Free products safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Sweet Street Gluten-Free products are crafted to be safe for individuals with celiac disease. They are made in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

2. Where can I buy Sweet Street Gluten-Free desserts?

Sweet Street Gluten-Free desserts are available at select grocery stores, specialty food shops, and online retailers. Check their official website for a list of retailers near you.

3. Do Sweet Street Gluten-Free desserts contain dairy or nuts?

Some Sweet Street Gluten-Free desserts may contain dairy or nuts. Always check the product label or contact the manufacturer for specific allergen information.

4. How should I store Sweet Street Gluten-Free desserts?

Most Sweet Street Gluten-Free desserts should be stored in a cool, dry place. Some items may require refrigeration, so be sure to check the packaging for storage instructions.
