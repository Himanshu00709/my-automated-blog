FAQs About Georgetown Cupcake Gluten-Free Options

Are Georgetown Cupcake’s gluten-free cupcakes safe for celiacs?

Yes, Georgetown Cupcake takes extensive measures to ensure that their gluten-free cupcakes are safe for those with celiac disease. They use dedicated equipment and follow strict protocols to avoid cross-contamination.

Do they offer dairy-free options as well?

Yes, some of their gluten-free cupcakes come with dairy-free frosting options. Be sure to check the menu or ask the staff for details.

Can I order gluten-free cupcakes for special occasions?

Absolutely! Georgetown Cupcake offers custom orders for special occasions, including weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Just make sure to specify your dietary requirements when placing the order.

How long do gluten-free cupcakes stay fresh?

Gluten-free cupcakes from Georgetown Cupcake are best enjoyed within 2-3 days of purchase. Store them in an airtight container to maintain freshness.
