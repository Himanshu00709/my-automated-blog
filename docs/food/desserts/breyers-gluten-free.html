Yes, Breyers gluten-free ice cream is made without gluten-containing ingredients and is safe for those with celiac disease. However, always check the packaging for the most up-to-date information and certifications.

Not all Breyers ice cream flavors are gluten-free. The brand clearly labels their gluten-free options, so be sure to check the packaging before purchasing.

Where can I buy Breyers gluten-free ice cream?

Breyers gluten-free ice cream is widely available in grocery stores, supermarkets, and online retailers. Look for it in the frozen dessert section.
