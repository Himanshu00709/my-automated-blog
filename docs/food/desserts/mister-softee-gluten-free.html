What is Mister Softee Gluten-Free?

Mister Softee Gluten-Free is a line of soft-serve ice cream and frozen desserts that are specifically formulated to be free from gluten. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Mister Softee has taken great care to ensure that their gluten-free products are safe for consumption by these individuals, without compromising on taste or texture.

The gluten-free options are made with high-quality ingredients and are free from cross-contamination, making them a reliable choice for those who need to avoid gluten. Whether you’re craving a classic vanilla cone or a decadent chocolate sundae, Mister Softee Gluten-Free has you covered.
