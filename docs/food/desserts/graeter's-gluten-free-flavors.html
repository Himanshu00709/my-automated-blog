2. Is Graeter's gluten-free ice cream safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Graeter's gluten-free ice cream is produced in a dedicated facility to avoid cross-contamination, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease.

3. Where can I buy Graeter's gluten-free ice cream?

Graeter's gluten-free ice cream is available at their scoop shops, as well as in select grocery stores across the United States. You can also order online through their website for home delivery.
