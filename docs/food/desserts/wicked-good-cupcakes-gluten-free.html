The Science Behind Gluten-Free Baking

Gluten-free baking is a science, and Wicked Good Cupcakes has mastered it. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, gives traditional baked goods their structure and elasticity. When removed, bakers must find alternative ways to achieve the same texture and consistency. Wicked Good Cupcakes achieves this by:

Ingredient Role in Gluten-Free Baking
Almond Flour Provides a rich, nutty flavor and moist texture.
Coconut Flour Adds a light, airy texture and subtle sweetness.
Xanthan Gum Acts as a binding agent, replacing the elasticity of gluten.
Eggs Helps to bind ingredients and add moisture.

By carefully balancing these ingredients, Wicked Good Cupcakes creates a product that rivals traditional cupcakes in both taste and texture.
