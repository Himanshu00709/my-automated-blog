Carvel ice cream cakes have been a beloved dessert for decades, known for their creamy layers of ice cream, crunchy chocolate crunches, and delightful frosting. What Makes Carvel Ice Cream Cakes Gluten-Free?

Carvel’s gluten-free ice cream cakes are crafted with the same care and attention to detail as their traditional cakes, but with a focus on avoiding gluten-containing ingredients. The key components of these cakes, such as the ice cream layers and the chocolate crunches, are made without gluten. However, it’s important to note that while the cakes themselves are gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in the production facility. Therefore, individuals with severe gluten allergies should exercise caution.
