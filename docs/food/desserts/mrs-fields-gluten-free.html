Mrs. Fields Gluten-Free: A Delicious and Safe Indulgence

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding delicious and safe treats can often feel like an impossible task. Enter Mrs. Fields, a brand synonymous with decadent cookies and baked goods, now offering a range of gluten-free options. In this blog post, we’ll explore the world of Mrs. Fields gluten-free products, their ingredients, nutritional benefits, and why they’re a game-changer for anyone avoiding gluten.

Why Choose Mrs. Fields Gluten-Free?

Mrs. Fields has long been a household name, known for its rich, buttery cookies and indulgent desserts. With the rise in gluten-free diets, the brand has stepped up to meet the demand, offering a variety of gluten-free options that don’t compromise on taste or quality. Whether you’re celiac, gluten-sensitive, or simply choosing to avoid gluten, Mrs. Fields gluten-free products provide a safe and delicious way to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Nutritional Benefits of Mrs. Fields Gluten-Free Products

While indulging in cookies and brownies may not seem like a health-conscious choice, Mrs. Fields gluten-free products offer some nutritional benefits, especially for those with dietary restrictions. Here are a few key points to consider:

  • Safe for Celiac Disease: Mrs. Fields gluten-free products are made in a dedicated gluten-free facility, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Rich in Flavor: Despite being gluten-free, these products are packed with flavor, thanks to high-quality ingredients like real butter and premium chocolate.
  • Variety: With a wide range of options, there’s something for everyone, from chocolate lovers to fans of classic oatmeal raisin cookies.

How Mrs. Fields Ensures Gluten-Free Quality

Mrs. Fields takes gluten-free certification seriously. Their products are tested and certified by third-party organizations to ensure they meet strict gluten-free standards. This includes:

  • Dedicated Facilities: All gluten-free products are made in facilities that do not process gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Rigorous Testing: Each batch of gluten-free products is tested to ensure it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, the FDA standard for gluten-free labeling.
  • Transparent Labeling: Mrs. Fields clearly labels all gluten-free products, making it easy for consumers to identify safe options.

FAQs About Mrs. Fields Gluten-Free Products

Yes, Mrs. Fields gluten-free products are made in dedicated gluten-free facilities and are rigorously tested to ensure they meet the FDA’s gluten-free standards, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease.

What ingredients are used in Mrs. Fields gluten-free cookies?

Mrs. Fields gluten-free cookies are made with a blend of gluten-free flours, real butter, sugar, eggs, and high-quality chocolate or other flavorings, depending on the variety.

Where can I buy Mrs. Fields gluten-free products?

Mrs. Fields gluten-free products are available online through their official website and in select retail locations. Check their website for a store locator.

Do Mrs. Fields gluten-free products taste different from their regular products?

While the texture may be slightly different due to the absence of gluten, Mrs. Fields gluten-free products are designed to be just as delicious and satisfying as their regular counterparts.
