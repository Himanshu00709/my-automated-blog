What Makes Talenti Gelato Gluten-Free?

Talenti Gelato is crafted with a commitment to quality and inclusivity. The brand ensures that its gluten-free products are made without any gluten-containing ingredients and are processed in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination. This is particularly important for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, as even trace amounts of gluten can cause adverse reactions.

Talenti’s gluten-free gelato is made with simple, natural ingredients, including fresh milk, cream, and sugar. The absence of gluten-containing grains like wheat, barley, and rye makes it a safe choice for those avoiding gluten. Additionally, Talenti is transparent about its ingredients, listing them clearly on the packaging so consumers can make informed choices.
