Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Goat Hill Pizza’s gluten-free crust vegan?

No, Goat Hill Pizza’s gluten-free crust contains dairy. However, they offer a variety of vegan toppings that can be paired with their gluten-free crust for a vegan-friendly option.

Can I order gluten-free pizza for delivery?

Yes, Goat Hill Pizza offers gluten-free pizza for both dine-in and delivery. Be sure to specify that you’d like the gluten-free crust when placing your order.

Is there a risk of cross-contamination?

Goat Hill Pizza takes precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. However, as with any restaurant that serves both gluten-free and gluten-containing items, there is always a slight risk. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s best to inform the staff when placing your order.

Are there other gluten-free options besides pizza?

Yes, Goat Hill Pizza offers gluten-free salads and appetizers. Their menu is clearly labeled, making it easy to identify gluten-free options.
