Upper Crust Pizza: A Gluten-Free Delight

Pizza is a universally loved dish, but for those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease, enjoying a slice can be a challenge. Enter Upper Crust Pizza, a brand that has mastered the art of gluten-free pizza without compromising on taste or quality. In this blog post, we’ll explore what makes Upper Crust Pizza a standout choice for gluten-free pizza lovers, from its ingredients to its preparation methods, and why it’s a must-try for anyone seeking a delicious, gluten-free alternative.

What Makes Upper Crust Pizza Gluten-Free?

Upper Crust Pizza has taken great care to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are not only safe for those with gluten sensitivities but also delicious. The key to their success lies in their meticulous selection of ingredients and their commitment to maintaining a gluten-free environment during preparation.

Ingredients

The foundation of any good pizza is its crust, and Upper Crust Pizza’s gluten-free crust is made from a blend of high-quality, gluten-free flours such as rice flour, tapioca flour, and potato starch. These ingredients are carefully chosen to mimic the texture and flavor of traditional wheat-based crusts while remaining completely gluten-free. Additionally, the toppings are sourced from trusted suppliers who guarantee that their products are free from gluten contamination.

Preparation

Upper Crust Pizza takes extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Their gluten-free pizzas are prepared in a dedicated area of the kitchen, using separate utensils and equipment. This ensures that there is no risk of gluten particles coming into contact with the gluten-free pizzas, making them safe for even the most sensitive individuals.

FAQs About Upper Crust Pizza Gluten-Free Options

Is Upper Crust Pizza’s gluten-free crust vegan?

While the gluten-free crust is free from animal products, some toppings may contain dairy or meat. Be sure to check the menu or ask the staff for vegan options.

Can I order Upper Crust Pizza’s gluten-free pizzas online?

Yes, Upper Crust Pizza offers online ordering for their gluten-free pizzas. Simply select the gluten-free option when placing your order.

Are there any additional charges for gluten-free pizzas?

Some locations may charge a small fee for gluten-free crusts due to the higher cost of ingredients. It’s best to check with your local Upper Crust Pizza for specific pricing.

How does Upper Crust Pizza ensure their gluten-free pizzas are safe?

Upper Crust Pizza uses a dedicated preparation area and separate utensils to avoid cross-contamination. They also source ingredients from trusted suppliers who guarantee their products are gluten-free.
