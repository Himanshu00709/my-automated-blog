Hideaway Pizza Gluten-Free: A Delicious and Safe Option

For those who follow a gluten-free diet, finding a pizza place that offers both delicious and safe options can be a challenge. Hideaway Pizza, a beloved pizza chain with a rich history, has stepped up to the plate by offering a gluten-free pizza option that doesn’t compromise on taste or quality. In this blog post, we’ll explore everything you need to know about Hideaway Pizza’s gluten-free offerings, from their ingredients to their preparation process, and why it’s a great choice for anyone with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

What Makes Hideaway Pizza’s Gluten-Free Option Stand Out?

Hideaway Pizza has been a staple in the pizza industry since 1957, known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Their gluten-free pizza is no exception. Here’s what sets it apart:

  • High-Quality Ingredients: Hideaway Pizza uses only the finest ingredients, ensuring that their gluten-free crust is both tasty and safe for those with gluten sensitivities.
  • Dedicated Preparation: To avoid cross-contamination, Hideaway Pizza takes extra precautions in their kitchen, including using separate utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free orders.
  • Variety of Toppings: Just like their regular pizzas, Hideaway’s gluten-free option allows you to customize your pizza with a wide range of toppings, from classic pepperoni to fresh vegetables.

The Gluten-Free Crust: A Closer Look

The foundation of any great pizza is its crust, and Hideaway Pizza’s gluten-free crust is a game-changer. Made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and other gluten-free ingredients, the crust is both crispy and chewy, providing the perfect base for your favorite toppings. Here’s a breakdown of the key ingredients:

Ingredient Purpose
Rice Flour Provides structure and a light texture.
Tapioca Starch Adds chewiness and elasticity.
Xanthan Gum Helps bind the dough and improve texture.
Olive Oil Enhances flavor and adds moisture.

This combination of ingredients ensures that the gluten-free crust is not only safe for those with gluten sensitivities but also delicious enough to satisfy any pizza lover.

FAQs About Hideaway Pizza’s Gluten-Free Option

Is Hideaway Pizza’s gluten-free crust safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Hideaway Pizza takes extra precautions to ensure that their gluten-free crust is safe for those with celiac disease. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

Can I order a gluten-free pizza online?

Absolutely! Hideaway Pizza offers online ordering for all their pizzas, including the gluten-free option. Simply select the gluten-free crust when customizing your pizza.

Are there any additional charges for the gluten-free crust?

Yes, there is typically a small additional charge for the gluten-free crust due to the higher cost of gluten-free ingredients. However, many customers find it well worth the price for the quality and safety it provides.

What other gluten-free options does Hideaway Pizza offer?

In addition to their gluten-free pizza, Hideaway Pizza also offers gluten-free salads and appetizers, making it easy to enjoy a complete meal without worrying about gluten.
