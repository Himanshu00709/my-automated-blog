How Mountain Mike's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for individuals with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Mountain Mike's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free pizzas are safe for consumption. The gluten-free crust is stored separately from other ingredients, and dedicated utensils and preparation areas are used to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. Additionally, staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that the pizza is prepared in a safe environment.
