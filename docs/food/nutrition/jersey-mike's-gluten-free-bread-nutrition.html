What is Jersey Mike's Gluten-Free Bread Made Of?

Jersey Mike's gluten-free bread is crafted to provide a safe and enjoyable option for those who need to avoid gluten. The bread is made from a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch. These ingredients are commonly used in gluten-free baking to mimic the texture and taste of traditional wheat bread. Additionally, the bread contains xanthan gum, which acts as a binding agent to give the bread its structure and elasticity.

It's important to note that while Jersey Mike's takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, their kitchens are not entirely gluten-free environments. Therefore, individuals with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease should exercise caution when consuming their gluten-free bread.
