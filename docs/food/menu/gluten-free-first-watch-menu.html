Exploring the Gluten-Free First Watch Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

First Watch, known for its fresh and healthy breakfast, brunch, and lunch options, has become a favorite among health-conscious diners. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating restaurant menus can be challenging. Fortunately, First Watch offers a variety of gluten-free options that cater to these dietary needs. In this article, we’ll dive deep into the gluten-free offerings at First Watch, providing you with a detailed guide to help you make informed choices.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at First Watch

First Watch takes dietary restrictions seriously, offering a range of gluten-free options that don’t compromise on taste or quality. The restaurant is committed to providing fresh, made-to-order meals, which makes it easier to accommodate gluten-free requests. However, it’s important to note that while First Watch offers gluten-free items, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. If you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, it’s always best to inform your server and discuss your dietary needs.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at First Watch

1. Is First Watch a gluten-free restaurant?

First Watch is not a dedicated gluten-free restaurant, but it offers a variety of gluten-free options. Cross-contamination is possible, so it’s important to communicate your dietary needs to your server.

2. Does First Watch offer gluten-free bread?

Yes, First Watch offers gluten-free bread for certain menu items, such as sandwiches and avocado toast. Be sure to request it when ordering.

3. Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

First Watch does not always clearly mark gluten-free items on the menu, so it’s best to ask your server for recommendations or check the allergen menu available at the restaurant.

4. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many items on the menu can be customized to be gluten-free. For example, you can request gluten-free bread for sandwiches or ask for no croutons on salads.

Conclusion

First Watch is a fantastic option for those seeking gluten-free dining without sacrificing flavor or variety. With a wide range of breakfast, lunch, and side options, there’s something for everyone. While the restaurant is not entirely gluten-free, the staff is accommodating and willing to help you navigate the menu to find safe and delicious options. Whether you’re starting your day with a hearty breakfast or enjoying a light lunch, First Watch’s gluten-free offerings are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Remember to always communicate your dietary needs to your server to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience. Happy eating!
