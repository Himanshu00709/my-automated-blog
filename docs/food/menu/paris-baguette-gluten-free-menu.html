Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Before diving into the menu, it's essential to understand what a gluten-free diet entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues. As a result, many people opt for gluten-free diets to avoid these complications. Paris Baguette has taken steps to accommodate these dietary needs, offering a selection of gluten-free items that do not compromise on taste or quality.
