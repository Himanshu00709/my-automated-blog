What Makes Duncan Hines Frosting Gluten-Free?

Duncan Hines has been a trusted name in baking for decades, and their gluten-free frosting is no exception. The company ensures that their gluten-free products are made without any gluten-containing ingredients and are processed in a way to avoid cross-contamination. This makes their frosting a safe choice for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

The primary ingredients in Duncan Hines Gluten-Free Frosting include sugar, water, vegetable oil, and cornstarch. These ingredients are naturally gluten-free, but Duncan Hines takes extra precautions to ensure that their products meet strict gluten-free standards. The frosting is also free from artificial flavors and colors, making it a healthier option for those who are mindful of what they consume.
