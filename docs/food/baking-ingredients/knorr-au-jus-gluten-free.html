What is Knorr Au Jus?

Knorr Au Jus is a savory sauce mix that is traditionally used to create a rich, flavorful jus or gravy. It is particularly popular for serving with roast beef, but its versatility extends to a variety of dishes. The gluten-free version of Knorr Au Jus is specially formulated to cater to those who need or prefer to avoid gluten in their diet.

Ingredients and Nutritional Information

The gluten-free version of Knorr Au Jus is made with a blend of natural flavors, salt, and other seasonings. It is free from wheat, barley, and rye, which are the primary sources of gluten. Below is a table summarizing the key ingredients and nutritional information:

Salt 1g
