Absolutely! Carbonaut Gluten-Free Bagels are low in carbs and high in fiber, making them an excellent choice for those following a ketogenic diet. They can help you stay within your daily carb limit while still enjoying a delicious and satisfying meal.

Where can I buy Carbonaut Gluten-Free Bagels?

Carbonaut Gluten-Free Bagels are available at many health food stores, as well as online retailers. You can also check the Carbonaut website for a store locator to find a retailer near you.

How should I store Carbonaut Gluten-Free Bagels?
