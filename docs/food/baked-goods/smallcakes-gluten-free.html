Ingredients and Nutritional Benefits

Smallcakes takes great care in selecting their ingredients to ensure that their gluten-free cupcakes are not only safe but also nutritious. Here’s a breakdown of some key ingredients used in their gluten-free cupcakes:

Ingredient Benefits
Almond Flour Rich in healthy fats, protein, and fiber, almond flour is a great gluten-free alternative to traditional wheat flour.
Coconut Flour Low in carbohydrates and high in fiber, coconut flour is another excellent gluten-free option that adds a subtle sweetness to the cupcakes.
Xanthan Gum Used as a binding agent, xanthan gum helps to mimic the texture of gluten, ensuring that the cupcakes are soft and moist.
Natural Sweeteners Smallcakes uses natural sweeteners like honey and maple syrup, which are lower on the glycemic index compared to refined sugars.

These ingredients not only make Smallcakes gluten-free cupcakes safe for those with gluten intolerance but also provide a range of nutritional benefits, making them a healthier option for everyone.
