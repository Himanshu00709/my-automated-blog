FAQs About Antonina's Gluten-Free Cupcakes

Are Antonina's cupcakes suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, all of Antonina's cupcakes are certified gluten-free and are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Do Antonina's cupcakes contain dairy or nuts?

Some of Antonina's cupcakes contain dairy and nuts. However, they offer a selection of dairy-free and nut-free options. Be sure to check the product description or ask the staff for recommendations.

Can I order custom cupcakes for a special event?

Absolutely! Antonina's offers customizable cupcakes for special occasions. You can choose the flavors, frosting, and decorations to create the perfect treat for your event.

How should I store Antonina's cupcakes?

Antonina's cupcakes are best enjoyed fresh. If you need to store them, keep them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to two days or in the refrigerator for up to five days.
