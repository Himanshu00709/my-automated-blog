FAQs About Rocky Mountain Chocolate Gluten-Free Products

Are all Rocky Mountain Chocolate products gluten-free?

No, not all Rocky Mountain Chocolate products are gluten-free. However, the company clearly labels their gluten-free options, making it easy for consumers to identify which products are safe for them.

How does Rocky Mountain Chocolate prevent cross-contamination?

Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory uses dedicated facilities and equipment for their gluten-free products. They also follow strict cleaning protocols to ensure that there is no cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.

Yes, many of Rocky Mountain Chocolate’s gluten-free products are available for purchase online through their official website and other retailers. This makes it convenient for consumers to enjoy their favorite gluten-free treats from the comfort of their homes.
