What Makes Elmer Chocolate Gluten-Free?

Elmer Chocolate’s gluten-free products are crafted with meticulous attention to detail to ensure they meet the dietary needs of those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease. The company uses only the finest ingredients, and their gluten-free chocolates are produced in facilities that adhere to strict gluten-free protocols to prevent cross-contamination.

Here’s a breakdown of what makes Elmer Chocolate gluten-free:

  • Certified Gluten-Free Ingredients: All ingredients used in Elmer Chocolate’s gluten-free products are certified gluten-free, ensuring that there is no trace of gluten in the final product.
  • Dedicated Production Lines: Elmer Chocolate uses dedicated production lines for their gluten-free products to avoid any risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Rigorous Testing: Each batch of gluten-free chocolate is rigorously tested to ensure it meets the gluten-free standards set by regulatory bodies.
