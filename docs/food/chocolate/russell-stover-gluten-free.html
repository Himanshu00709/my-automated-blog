Ingredients and Safety

Russell Stover takes the safety of their gluten-free products seriously. All gluten-free items are produced in facilities that are free from cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients. The company also adheres to strict manufacturing processes to ensure that their gluten-free chocolates meet the highest standards of quality and safety.

Key ingredients in Russell Stover’s gluten-free chocolates include:

  • Milk Chocolate: Made with real milk and cocoa butter for a rich, creamy texture.
  • Dark Chocolate: Contains a higher percentage of cocoa for a more intense flavor.
  • Natural Flavors: Used to enhance the taste without adding gluten or artificial additives.
  • Soy Lecithin: Acts as an emulsifier to keep the chocolate smooth and consistent.

FAQs About Russell Stover Gluten-Free

1. Are all Russell Stover products gluten-free?

No, not all Russell Stover products are gluten-free. However, the company clearly labels their gluten-free items, making it easy for consumers to identify safe options.

2. Are Russell Stover’s gluten-free products safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Russell Stover’s gluten-free products are produced in facilities that are free from gluten cross-contamination, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease.

3. Where can I buy Russell Stover gluten-free chocolates?

Russell Stover gluten-free chocolates are widely available in grocery stores, drugstores, and online retailers. You can also purchase them directly from the Russell Stover website.

4. Do Russell Stover’s gluten-free chocolates contain nuts?

Some Russell Stover products may contain nuts or be processed in facilities that handle nuts. Always check the label for allergen information.
