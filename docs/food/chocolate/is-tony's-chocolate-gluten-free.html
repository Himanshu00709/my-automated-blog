Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, it’s crucial to ensure that the foods we consume are free from gluten contamination.
