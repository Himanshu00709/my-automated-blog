The History of Ghirardelli Chocolate

Ghirardelli’s story began in 1852 when Domenico Ghirardelli, an Italian chocolatier, founded the Ghirardelli Chocolate Company in San Francisco. Over the years, the company has become synonymous with high-quality chocolate, using only the finest ingredients and traditional methods. Today, Ghirardelli is one of the oldest chocolate companies in the United States, and its products are enjoyed by millions around the world.
