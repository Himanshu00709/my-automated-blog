FAQs About Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate

Is Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate really gluten-free?

Yes, Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate is gluten-free. Ghirardelli takes great care to ensure that their products are free from gluten-containing ingredients and follow strict manufacturing processes to prevent cross-contamination.

What are the main ingredients in Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate?

The main ingredients include sugar, cocoa butter, milk, chocolate, natural flavors, and spices such as ginger, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Always check the packaging for the most accurate and up-to-date ingredient list.

Can I find Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate year-round?

Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate is a seasonal product, typically available during the holiday season. However, availability may vary depending on your location and the retailer.

Is Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate suitable for vegans?

No, Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate contains milk and is not suitable for vegans. However, Ghirardelli offers a range of other chocolate products that may be suitable for those following a vegan diet.

How should I store Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate?

To maintain its quality and flavor, store Ghirardelli Gingerbread Chocolate in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid storing it in the refrigerator, as this can cause the chocolate to develop a whitish coating known as "bloom," which affects its texture.
