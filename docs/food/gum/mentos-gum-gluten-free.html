What Makes Mentos Gum Gluten-Free?

Mentos Gum is crafted with a focus on quality and safety, ensuring that it is free from gluten-containing ingredients. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Mentos Gum avoids these ingredients entirely, making it a safe option for those who need to follow a gluten-free diet.

The manufacturing process of Mentos Gum also plays a crucial role in maintaining its gluten-free status. The facilities where Mentos Gum is produced are carefully monitored to prevent cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. This rigorous attention to detail ensures that every piece of gum is safe for consumption by individuals with gluten-related health concerns.
