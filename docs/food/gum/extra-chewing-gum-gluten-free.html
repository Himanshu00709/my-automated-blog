Is Extra Chewing Gum Safe for Everyone?

While Extra Chewing Gum is gluten-free and safe for most people, there are a few considerations to keep in mind:

1. Artificial Sweeteners

Extra Chewing Gum contains artificial sweeteners like aspartame and acesulfame K. While these are generally recognized as safe, some individuals may prefer to avoid them due to personal preferences or health concerns.

2. Sorbitol Sensitivity

Sorbitol, a sugar alcohol used in Extra Chewing Gum, can cause digestive issues in some people, especially when consumed in large quantities. If you’re sensitive to sorbitol, it’s best to chew gum in moderation.

3. Allergies

Although Extra Chewing Gum is gluten-free, it’s always a good idea to check the ingredient list if you have other allergies or sensitivities. The gum contains natural and artificial flavors, which could potentially trigger allergic reactions in rare cases.
