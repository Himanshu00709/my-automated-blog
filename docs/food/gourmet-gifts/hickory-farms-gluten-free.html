What Does Gluten-Free Mean?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the intestines. A gluten-free diet eliminates these proteins, allowing individuals to manage their symptoms and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Hickory Farms recognizes the importance of catering to those with dietary restrictions, and their gluten-free products are carefully crafted to ensure they meet the needs of gluten-sensitive consumers. Let's take a closer look at what they offer.
