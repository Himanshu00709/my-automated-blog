Heinz 57 Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

Heinz 57 sauce has been a staple in kitchens around the world for over a century. Known for its unique blend of flavors, this versatile condiment has found its way into countless recipes, from marinades to dips. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the question remains: Is Heinz 57 gluten-free? In this article, we’ll dive deep into the ingredients, manufacturing processes, and certifications that make Heinz 57 a safe choice for those avoiding gluten.

What is Heinz 57 Sauce?

Heinz 57 sauce is a tangy, slightly sweet, and savory condiment that has been a favorite among food enthusiasts since its creation in the late 19th century. The sauce is a blend of tomatoes, vinegar, and a secret mix of spices and seasonings, which gives it its distinctive flavor. It’s often used as a steak sauce, but its versatility makes it a great addition to a variety of dishes, including burgers, sandwiches, and even salads.

Is Heinz 57 Gluten-Free?

Yes, Heinz 57 sauce is gluten-free. According to Heinz, the sauce does not contain any gluten-containing ingredients, and the company has taken steps to ensure that the product is safe for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. However, it’s always important to check the label for any changes in ingredients or manufacturing processes, as formulations can change over time.

Gluten-Free Testing Results

Heinz regularly tests their products to ensure that they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold set by the FDA for a product to be considered gluten-free. The results of these tests have consistently shown that Heinz 57 sauce meets this standard, making it safe for most people with gluten sensitivities.
