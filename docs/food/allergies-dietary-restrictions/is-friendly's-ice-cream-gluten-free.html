Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are on the lookout for gluten-free options when it comes to their favorite foods, including ice cream.

When it comes to ice cream, gluten can sometimes be hidden in additives, flavorings, or mix-ins. Therefore, it's essential to scrutinize the ingredients and manufacturing processes to ensure that the product is safe for those avoiding gluten.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

Even if a product is made without gluten-containing ingredients, cross-contamination can occur during manufacturing. This is especially important for individuals with celiac disease, who must avoid even trace amounts of gluten. Friendly's has stated that they take precautions to prevent cross-contamination, but they also acknowledge that their facilities handle gluten-containing products. Therefore, if you have a severe gluten allergy or celiac disease, it's crucial to contact Friendly's directly for the most accurate and up-to-date information.
