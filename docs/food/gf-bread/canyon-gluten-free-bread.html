What is Canyon Gluten-Free Bread?

Canyon Gluten-Free Bread is a line of gluten-free bread products made by Canyon Bakehouse, a company dedicated to providing high-quality gluten-free baked goods. Founded in 2009 by a family with a personal connection to celiac disease, Canyon Bakehouse has become a trusted name in the gluten-free community. Their bread is made with whole grains, free from artificial preservatives, and certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO).
