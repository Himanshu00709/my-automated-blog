What Makes Herdez Guacamole Salsa Gluten-Free?

Herdez Guacamole Salsa is crafted with high-quality ingredients that are naturally gluten-free. The primary ingredients include ripe avocados, tomatillos, onions, cilantro, and a blend of spices. None of these ingredients contain gluten, making the salsa a safe and delicious option for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. Additionally, Herdez ensures that their production facilities follow strict gluten-free protocols to prevent cross-contamination, giving consumers peace of mind.
