Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Before diving into Blue Koi’s gluten-free offerings, it’s essential to understand what gluten is and why it matters. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to improve their health and well-being.

How Blue Koi Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Blue Koi takes this seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free dishes:

  • Dedicated Cooking Utensils: Blue Koi uses separate utensils and cookware for gluten-free dishes to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Gluten-Free Ingredients: They source high-quality gluten-free ingredients, including gluten-free soy sauce and tamari, to ensure that their dishes are safe for consumption.
  • Staff Training: The staff at Blue Koi are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all precautions are taken during preparation and serving.
