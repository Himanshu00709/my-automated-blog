What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe autoimmune reactions. Going gluten-free is not just a dietary choice for these individuals; it's a necessity for maintaining their health and well-being.

Checkers has recognized this need and has developed a range of gluten-free products that allow individuals to enjoy their favorite foods without the worry of gluten-related health issues. From snacks to ready-to-eat meals, Checkers has made it easier than ever to maintain a gluten-free lifestyle.

How Checkers Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Checkers takes this issue seriously and has implemented strict manufacturing processes to ensure that their gluten-free products are safe for consumption. This includes using dedicated production lines, regular testing for gluten traces, and adhering to stringent quality control measures.

Checkers also clearly labels their gluten-free products, making it easy for consumers to identify which items are safe for them to eat. This transparency is crucial for building trust with their customers and ensuring that they can confidently enjoy Checkers products without fear of adverse reactions.
