How Dinnerly Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Dinnerly takes several steps to ensure that their gluten-free meals are safe for consumption:

  • Dedicated Facilities: Dinnerly uses dedicated facilities for preparing and packaging gluten-free meals to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Certified Ingredients: All ingredients used in Dinnerly's gluten-free meals are certified gluten-free by reputable organizations.
  • Clear Labeling: Dinnerly clearly labels all gluten-free meals, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.
  • Regular Audits: Dinnerly conducts regular audits of their facilities and suppliers to ensure compliance with gluten-free standards.
