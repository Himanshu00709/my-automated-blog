Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergy. Consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. Even for those without these conditions, a gluten-free diet can sometimes improve overall well-being. Georgetown's restaurants and cafes have recognized this growing need and have adapted their menus to include gluten-free options.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free in Georgetown

Dining out gluten-free can be challenging, but with a few tips, you can enjoy a safe and delicious meal in Georgetown:

  • Research Ahead: Check restaurant menus online or call ahead to confirm gluten-free options.
  • Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to ask your server about gluten-free preparation methods.
  • Be Cautious of Cross-Contamination: Ensure that the kitchen uses separate utensils and cooking surfaces for gluten-free dishes.
  • Carry Snacks: Always have gluten-free snacks on hand in case you can't find suitable options.
