Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it’s crucial for those on a gluten-free diet to carefully scrutinize food labels and understand the potential sources of gluten in their food.
