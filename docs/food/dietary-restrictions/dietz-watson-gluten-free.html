Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Before diving into Dietz & Watson's gluten-free products, it's essential to understand what a gluten-free diet entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including damage to the small intestine. Others may have non-celiac gluten sensitivity, experiencing symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue after consuming gluten. A gluten-free diet eliminates all sources of gluten, requiring careful selection of foods and ingredients.

FAQs About Dietz & Watson Gluten-Free Products

1. Are all Dietz & Watson products gluten-free?

No, not all Dietz & Watson products are gluten-free. However, the company offers a specific range of gluten-free deli meats and cheeses that are clearly labeled as such. Always check the packaging for the gluten-free certification.

2. Where can I buy Dietz & Watson gluten-free products?

Dietz & Watson gluten-free products are available at most major grocery stores and delis. You can also find them online through various retailers. Check the company's website for a store locator tool.

3. Are Dietz & Watson gluten-free products suitable for vegans?

No, Dietz & Watson's gluten-free products are made from animal-derived ingredients, such as meat and dairy. They are not suitable for a vegan diet.

4. How should I store Dietz & Watson gluten-free products?

Dietz & Watson gluten-free deli meats and cheeses should be stored in the refrigerator at or below 40°F (4°C). Once opened, they should be consumed within a few days for optimal freshness.

5. Can I freeze Dietz & Watson gluten-free products?

Yes, you can freeze Dietz & Watson gluten-free products to extend their shelf life. However, it's best to consume them within one to two months of freezing for the best quality.
