On The Border Queso: A Gluten-Free Delight

When it comes to Mexican cuisine, few dishes are as universally loved as queso. This creamy, cheesy dip is a staple at parties, family gatherings, and casual dining experiences. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding a safe and delicious queso can be a challenge. Enter On The Border Queso—a gluten-free option that doesn’t compromise on flavor. In this article, we’ll dive deep into what makes On The Border Queso a standout choice for gluten-free eaters, its ingredients, nutritional information, and more.

What Makes On The Border Queso Gluten-Free?

On The Border Queso is crafted with a focus on quality ingredients, ensuring that it remains gluten-free without sacrificing taste. The primary ingredients include melted cheese, tomatoes, green chilies, and a blend of spices. Importantly, the recipe avoids any gluten-containing additives or thickeners, making it a safe choice for those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

Key Ingredients

  • Cheese: A blend of cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses provides the creamy base.
  • Tomatoes: Diced tomatoes add a fresh, tangy flavor.
  • Green Chilies: These give the queso a mild, spicy kick.
  • Spices: A mix of garlic, onion, and other spices enhances the overall taste.

How to Enjoy On The Border Queso

On The Border Queso is incredibly versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. Here are some popular serving suggestions:

As a Dip

Pair the queso with gluten-free tortilla chips for a classic snack. The creamy texture and rich flavor make it a crowd-pleaser at any gathering.

As a Topping

Use the queso as a topping for gluten-free nachos, baked potatoes, or even grilled vegetables. It adds a delicious cheesy layer to any dish.

In Recipes

Incorporate the queso into your favorite gluten-free recipes. It can be used as a base for casseroles, a filling for stuffed peppers, or a sauce for gluten-free pasta.

FAQs About On The Border Queso

Is On The Border Queso certified gluten-free?

While On The Border Queso is made with gluten-free ingredients, it’s always a good idea to check the packaging for any certification or consult with the manufacturer if you have severe gluten sensitivities.

Can I find On The Border Queso in stores?

Yes, On The Border Queso is available in many grocery stores, typically in the refrigerated section. You can also find it at On The Border restaurants.

How should I store On The Border Queso?

Store the queso in the refrigerator and consume it by the expiration date on the packaging. Once opened, it’s best to consume it within a few days for optimal freshness.

Are there any allergens in On The Border Queso?

On The Border Queso contains dairy, so it’s not suitable for those with lactose intolerance or a dairy allergy. Always check the ingredient list for any potential allergens.
