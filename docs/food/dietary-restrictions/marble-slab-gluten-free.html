Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health. Fortunately, Marble Slab Creamery has taken steps to accommodate these dietary needs, offering a variety of gluten-free options that don’t compromise on taste or quality.

FAQs About Marble Slab Gluten-Free Options

1. Are all Marble Slab ice cream flavors gluten-free?

While many of Marble Slab’s ice cream flavors are naturally gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur. Always inform the staff of your dietary restrictions to ensure your ice cream is prepared safely.

2. Can I trust the gluten-free cones at Marble Slab?

Marble Slab takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination by storing gluten-free cones separately. However, it’s always a good idea to confirm with the staff when placing your order.

3. Are there any gluten-free dairy-free options at Marble Slab?

Marble Slab primarily offers dairy-based ice cream, but they may have sorbet options that are both gluten-free and dairy-free. Check with your local store for availability.

4. How can I ensure my ice cream is gluten-free?

To ensure your ice cream is gluten-free, communicate your dietary needs to the staff, choose gluten-free flavors and mix-ins, and opt for a gluten-free cone or cup.
