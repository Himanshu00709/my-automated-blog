What Does Gluten-Free Mean?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. A gluten-free diet eliminates all sources of gluten, requiring careful selection of foods and personal care products.

Dove, a brand known for its skincare and personal care products, has recognized the need for gluten-free options. While Dove is primarily associated with beauty and hygiene, it’s essential to understand how their products cater to those avoiding gluten.
