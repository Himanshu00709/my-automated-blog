How to Use Krusteaz Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix

Using the Krusteaz Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix is incredibly straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C). This ensures that your cookies bake evenly.
  2. Prepare the Mix: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cookie mix with softened butter, an egg, and a teaspoon of vanilla extract. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Scoop the Dough: Using a cookie scoop or a tablespoon, drop rounded balls of dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Make sure to space them about 2 inches apart to allow for spreading.
  4. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  5. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Then, enjoy your delicious gluten-free cookies!

FAQs About Krusteaz Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix

1. Is Krusteaz Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix really gluten-free?

Yes, Krusteaz Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix is certified gluten-free, making it safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

2. Can I make the cookies without eggs?

Yes, you can substitute eggs with alternatives like flax eggs or applesauce, though the texture may vary slightly.

3. How should I store the cookies?

Store the cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. You can also freeze the baked cookies for up to 3 months.

4. Can I add other ingredients to the mix?

Absolutely! Feel free to add nuts, dried fruit, or even extra chocolate chips to customize your cookies.

5. Is the mix suitable for vegans?

The mix itself is vegan, but the recipe on the package calls for butter and eggs. You can use plant-based substitutes to make vegan cookies.
