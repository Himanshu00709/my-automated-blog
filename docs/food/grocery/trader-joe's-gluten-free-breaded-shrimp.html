Preparation Tips

One of the best things about Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Breaded Shrimp is how easy they are to prepare. Here are a few methods to cook them to perfection:

Oven Baking

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Arrange the shrimp in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the shrimp are golden brown and crispy.

Air Frying

For an even crispier texture, try air frying. Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C). Place the shrimp in the basket in a single layer and cook for 8-10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through.

Pan Frying

If you prefer a quicker method, pan frying is a great option. Heat a small amount of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden and crispy.
