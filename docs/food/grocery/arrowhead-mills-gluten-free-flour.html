How to Use Arrowhead Mills Gluten-Free Flour

Using Arrowhead Mills Gluten-Free Flour is straightforward, but there are a few tips to keep in mind to achieve the best results:

  • 1:1 Substitution: In most recipes, you can substitute Arrowhead Mills Gluten-Free Flour for wheat flour in a 1:1 ratio. This makes it easy to convert your favorite recipes to gluten-free versions.
  • Add Binders: Gluten-free flours often require additional binders to mimic the elasticity of gluten. Consider adding xanthan gum or guar gum to your recipes to improve texture and structure.
  • Adjust Liquid Content: Gluten-free flours can absorb more liquid than wheat flour. You may need to adjust the amount of liquid in your recipe to achieve the desired consistency.
  • Experiment: Baking with gluten-free flour can require some experimentation. Don't be afraid to tweak recipes to find the perfect balance of ingredients.
