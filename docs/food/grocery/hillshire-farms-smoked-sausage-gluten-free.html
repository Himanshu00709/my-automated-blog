What Makes Hillshire Farms Smoked Sausage Gluten-Free?

Hillshire Farms has taken great care to ensure that their smoked sausages meet the needs of those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. The gluten-free status of their sausages is achieved by carefully selecting ingredients that do not contain gluten and by maintaining strict manufacturing processes to avoid cross-contamination. This means that you can enjoy the rich, smoky flavors of Hillshire Farms sausages without worrying about gluten.

How to Incorporate Hillshire Farms Smoked Sausage into Your Diet

One of the best things about Hillshire Farms smoked sausage is its versatility. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a hearty addition to your breakfast, this sausage can do it all. Here are some creative ways to incorporate it into your meals:

1. Breakfast Skillet

Start your day with a hearty breakfast skillet. Simply slice the smoked sausage and sauté it with diced potatoes, bell peppers, and onions. Top it off with a fried egg for a complete and satisfying meal.

2. Grilled Sausage and Veggie Kabobs

For a fun and healthy dinner option, try making kabobs with Hillshire Farms smoked sausage, cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and bell peppers. Grill them to perfection and serve with a side of quinoa or rice.

3. Sausage and Bean Soup

On a chilly day, nothing beats a warm bowl of sausage and bean soup. Combine sliced smoked sausage with white beans, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth. Add some spinach or kale for an extra boost of nutrients.

4. Sausage and Rice Casserole

For a comforting casserole, mix cooked rice with sliced smoked sausage, cream of mushroom soup, and shredded cheese. Bake until bubbly and golden brown for a crowd-pleasing dish.

Are there any artificial flavors or preservatives in Hillshire Farms smoked sausage?

Hillshire Farms is committed to using high-quality ingredients. Their smoked sausages are made with natural flavorings and do not contain artificial flavors or preservatives.

What is the shelf life of Hillshire Farms smoked sausage?
