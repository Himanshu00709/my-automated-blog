How to Cook Costco Gluten-Free Meatballs

Cooking Costco's gluten-free meatballs is straightforward, and there are several methods you can use depending on your preference and the dish you're preparing. Here are some popular cooking methods:

1. Oven Baking

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the meatballs on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway through, until they are heated through and slightly browned.

2. Stovetop Cooking

Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of oil. Place the meatballs in the skillet and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are heated through and browned on all sides.

3. Microwave

For a quick option, place the meatballs on a microwave-safe plate and cover with a microwave-safe lid or paper towel. Microwave on high for 2-3 minutes, or until heated through.

FAQs About Costco Gluten-Free Meatballs

1. Are Costco gluten-free meatballs suitable for people with celiac disease?

2. Can I use Costco gluten-free meatballs in a slow cooker?
