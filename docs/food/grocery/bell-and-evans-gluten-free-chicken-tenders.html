Cooking Methods

One of the best things about Bell & Evans Gluten-Free Chicken Tenders is their versatility. They can be prepared in a variety of ways to suit your taste preferences. Here are some popular cooking methods:

Baking

Baking is one of the healthiest ways to prepare Bell & Evans Gluten-Free Chicken Tenders. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and place the tenders on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they are golden brown and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Air Frying

Air frying is another excellent option for achieving a crispy texture without the need for excessive oil. Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C) and cook the tenders for 10-12 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through to ensure even cooking.

Pan-Frying

For those who prefer a more traditional approach, pan-frying is a quick and easy method. Heat a small amount of oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook the tenders for 4-5 minutes on each side until they are crispy and fully cooked.
