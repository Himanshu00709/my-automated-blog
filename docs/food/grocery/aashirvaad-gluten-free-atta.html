What is Aashirvaad Gluten-Free Atta?

Aashirvaad Gluten-Free Atta is a specially formulated flour blend designed to cater to the dietary needs of individuals who cannot consume gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Aashirvaad's Gluten-Free Atta is made from a combination of gluten-free grains and flours, ensuring that it is safe for consumption by those who need to avoid gluten.

The primary ingredients in Aashirvaad Gluten-Free Atta include:

  • Rice Flour: A staple in gluten-free diets, rice flour is easily digestible and provides a good source of carbohydrates.
  • Chickpea Flour (Besan): Rich in protein and fiber, chickpea flour adds nutritional value and helps in binding the dough.
  • Maize Flour: Adds a slightly sweet flavor and is a good source of essential nutrients like vitamin B6 and magnesium.
  • Psyllium Husk: Known for its high fiber content, psyllium husk helps in improving digestion and provides a soft texture to the dough.
