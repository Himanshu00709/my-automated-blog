Yes, Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chicken Nuggets are certified gluten-free and are suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, always check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings if you have severe allergies.

These nuggets can be cooked in the oven, air fryer, or microwave. For the best results, we recommend baking them in the oven or using an air fryer to achieve a crispy texture. Follow the cooking instructions on the packaging for optimal results.

Can I freeze Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chicken Nuggets?

Yes, these nuggets can be frozen. If you purchase them frozen, simply store them in your freezer until you’re ready to cook them. If you have leftovers, you can also freeze them for later use.
