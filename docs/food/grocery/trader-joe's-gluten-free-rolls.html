FAQs About Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Rolls

Are Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Rolls suitable for vegans?

No, Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Rolls contain egg whites, which makes them unsuitable for vegans. However, they are suitable for vegetarians.

Can I freeze Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Rolls?

Yes, you can freeze these rolls. Simply place them in an airtight container or freezer bag and store them in the freezer for up to three months. When you're ready to eat them, just thaw and toast as usual.

Are these rolls certified gluten-free?

Yes, Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Rolls are certified gluten-free, meaning they have been tested and confirmed to contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.

How do I store Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Rolls?

It is recommended to store these rolls in a cool, dry place. Once opened, they should be kept in an airtight container to maintain freshness. If you don't plan to consume them within a few days, freezing is a good option.
