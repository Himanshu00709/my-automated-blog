What Are Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Nuggets?

Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Nuggets are a plant-based, gluten-free alternative to traditional chicken nuggets. Made primarily from a blend of vegetables, grains, and legumes, these nuggets are designed to mimic the texture and flavor of chicken while being entirely free from gluten. They are an excellent option for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those simply looking to reduce their gluten intake.
