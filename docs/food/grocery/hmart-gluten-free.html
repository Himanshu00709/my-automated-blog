How to Identify Gluten-Free Products at H Mart

When shopping for gluten-free products at H Mart, it's essential to know how to identify them. Here are some tips to help you make informed choices:

  • Check Labels: Look for products that are explicitly labeled "gluten-free." This ensures that the product meets the necessary standards for gluten-free certification.
  • Read Ingredient Lists: Even if a product isn't labeled gluten-free, you can often determine its gluten content by reading the ingredient list. Avoid products that contain wheat, barley, rye, or malt.
  • Ask for Assistance: H Mart staff are usually knowledgeable about their products and can help you find gluten-free options.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Shopping at H Mart

1. Are all rice-based products at H Mart gluten-free?

While many rice-based products are naturally gluten-free, it's essential to check the labels, as some may contain gluten-containing additives or be processed in facilities that handle gluten.

2. Does H Mart offer gluten-free baked goods?

Yes, H Mart carries a selection of gluten-free baked goods, including bread, cakes, and pastries. Be sure to check the labels to confirm they are gluten-free.

3. Can I find gluten-free sauces and condiments at H Mart?

Absolutely! H Mart offers a variety of gluten-free sauces and condiments, such as gluten-free soy sauce, tamari, and hoisin sauce. Always check the labels to ensure they are safe for your dietary needs.

4. Are there gluten-free options in H Mart's prepared food section?

H Mart's prepared food section may have gluten-free options, but cross-contamination can be a concern. It's best to ask the staff for guidance or stick to packaged gluten-free products.
