Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Are all Trader Joe's gluten-free products certified gluten-free?

Not all Trader Joe's gluten-free products are certified gluten-free, but they are labeled as gluten-free if they meet the FDA's standard of containing less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten. Always check the packaging for specific certifications if needed.

2. Does Trader Joe's offer gluten-free bread?

Yes, Trader Joe's offers a variety of gluten-free bread options, including sliced bread, bagels, and rolls. These products are typically found in the frozen section.

3. Are Trader Joe's gluten-free products safe for people with celiac disease?

While Trader Joe's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it's important to note that their facilities are not dedicated gluten-free. Individuals with celiac disease should exercise caution and consult with their healthcare provider if they have concerns.

4. Can I find gluten-free desserts at Trader Joe's?

Absolutely! Trader Joe's offers a range of gluten-free desserts, including cookies, brownies, and cakes. These are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without compromising your dietary needs.
