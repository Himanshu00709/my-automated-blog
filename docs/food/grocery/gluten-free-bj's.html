Gluten-Free BJ's: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free options has skyrocketed, and BJ's Restaurant & Brewhouse has risen to the occasion. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health. BJ's Restaurant & Brewhouse recognizes this need and has made significant efforts to accommodate gluten-free diners.

Tips for a Safe Gluten-Free Dining Experience

While BJ's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it's important to take precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Inform your server about your gluten-free needs and ask about the preparation methods for your chosen dishes.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Ensure that the kitchen staff is aware of the importance of avoiding cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Review the Gluten-Free Menu: BJ's provides a dedicated gluten-free menu, but it's always a good idea to double-check the ingredients and preparation methods.
  • Ask About Sauces and Dressings: Some sauces and dressings may contain gluten, so it's important to ask about these before ordering.
