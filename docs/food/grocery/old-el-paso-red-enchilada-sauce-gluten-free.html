What Makes Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce Gluten-Free?

Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce is crafted with a blend of traditional Mexican spices, tomatoes, and other natural ingredients. The key to its gluten-free status lies in the careful selection of ingredients and the manufacturing process. The sauce is made without any wheat, barley, or rye, which are the primary sources of gluten. Additionally, Old El Paso ensures that their production facilities are free from cross-contamination, making it a safe choice for those who need to avoid gluten.

How to Use Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce

Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce is incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes beyond just enchiladas. Here are some creative ways to incorporate this gluten-free sauce into your meals:

  • Classic Enchiladas: The most obvious use, of course, is in enchiladas. Simply fill tortillas with your choice of protein (chicken, beef, or beans), roll them up, and smother them with the sauce. Top with cheese and bake until bubbly.
  • Enchilada Casserole: Layer tortillas, sauce, cheese, and your favorite fillings in a baking dish for a quick and easy casserole.
  • Mexican Rice: Stir the sauce into cooked rice for a flavorful side dish that pairs perfectly with grilled meats or vegetables.
  • Taco Night: Use the sauce as a topping for tacos or as a dipping sauce for nachos.
  • Soup Base: Add the sauce to chicken or vegetable broth for a quick and easy Mexican-inspired soup.

Yes, Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce is certified gluten-free. It is made without any gluten-containing ingredients and is produced in a facility that avoids cross-contamination.

Absolutely. The sauce is safe for individuals with celiac disease, as it meets the strict standards for gluten-free certification.

How long does Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce last after opening?

Once opened, the sauce should be stored in the refrigerator and used within 7-10 days for optimal freshness.

Are there any artificial flavors or preservatives in the sauce?

No, Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce is made with natural ingredients and does not contain any artificial flavors or preservatives.

Can I freeze Old El Paso Red Enchilada Sauce?

Yes, you can freeze the sauce for up to 3 months. Just make sure to store it in an airtight container to maintain its quality.
