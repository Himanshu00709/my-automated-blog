Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Before diving into Publix’s gluten-free cake options, it’s important to understand what a gluten-free diet entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many people must adhere to a strict gluten-free diet, which means avoiding foods that contain gluten or are cross-contaminated with it.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While Publix offers gluten-free cakes, it’s important to be aware of the potential for cross-contamination. Publix takes precautions to minimize this risk, but it’s always a good idea to discuss your concerns with the bakery staff.
