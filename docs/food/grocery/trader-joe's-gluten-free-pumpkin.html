Nutritional Benefits of Pumpkin

Pumpkin is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. It's rich in vitamins A and C, which are essential for immune function and skin health. Pumpkin is also a good source of fiber, which aids in digestion and helps keep you feeling full longer. Additionally, pumpkin seeds are high in magnesium, which is important for muscle and nerve function.
