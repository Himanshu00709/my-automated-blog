How to Use Ortega Gluten-Free Taco Seasoning

Using Ortega Gluten-Free Taco Seasoning is incredibly easy and can be done in just a few simple steps:

  1. Prepare Your Protein: Start by browning your choice of protein—be it beef, chicken, turkey, or even tofu—in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the Seasoning: Once the protein is cooked, sprinkle the desired amount of Ortega Gluten-Free Taco Seasoning over the meat or vegetables. For a standard packet, this is typically enough for one pound of protein.
  3. Mix and Simmer: Add a small amount of water to the skillet, mix well, and let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Serve: Your taco filling is now ready! Serve it in taco shells, over a salad, or as a topping for nachos.

Recipes to Try with Ortega Gluten-Free Taco Seasoning

While tacos are the obvious choice, Ortega Gluten-Free Taco Seasoning can be used in a variety of dishes. Here are a few recipe ideas to get you started:

1. Taco-Stuffed Bell Peppers

This recipe is a healthy twist on traditional tacos. Simply hollow out bell peppers, fill them with your seasoned taco meat, top with cheese, and bake until the peppers are tender and the cheese is melted.

2. Taco Salad

For a lighter option, create a taco salad by layering lettuce, seasoned meat, beans, corn, tomatoes, and avocado. Top with a dollop of sour cream or guacamole for added flavor.

3. Taco Soup

Combine your seasoned meat with broth, beans, corn, and diced tomatoes to create a hearty taco soup. Serve with tortilla chips and a sprinkle of cheese for a comforting meal.
