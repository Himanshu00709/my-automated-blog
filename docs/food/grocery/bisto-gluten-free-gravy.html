Bisto Gluten Free Gravy: A Comprehensive Guide

Gravy is a staple in many households, especially during festive seasons or family dinners. However, for those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease, finding a suitable gravy can be a challenge. Enter Bisto Gluten Free Gravy, a product that promises the same rich, savory taste without the gluten. In this article, we’ll delve into everything you need to know about Bisto Gluten Free Gravy, from its ingredients and nutritional profile to how it compares to traditional gravy options.

What is Bisto Gluten Free Gravy?

Bisto Gluten Free Gravy is a specially formulated gravy powder designed for individuals who need to avoid gluten in their diet. Made by the renowned brand Bisto, this product aims to deliver the same delicious flavor and texture as their traditional gravy, but without the gluten. It’s perfect for those who suffer from celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or simply prefer a gluten-free lifestyle.

How to Use Bisto Gluten Free Gravy

Using Bisto Gluten Free Gravy is straightforward and similar to using traditional gravy powders. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Measure the Powder: Start by measuring out the desired amount of gravy powder. Typically, one heaped tablespoon is sufficient for one serving.
  2. Add Water: Gradually add cold water to the powder, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Heat and Stir: Transfer the mixture to a saucepan and heat it gently, stirring constantly until it thickens.
  4. Serve: Once the gravy reaches your desired consistency, it’s ready to serve. Pour it over your favorite dishes like roast meats, mashed potatoes, or vegetables.

FAQs About Bisto Gluten Free Gravy

Yes, Bisto Gluten Free Gravy is suitable for vegans as it contains no animal-derived ingredients.

Can I use Bisto Gluten Free Gravy in recipes?

Absolutely! Bisto Gluten Free Gravy can be used in a variety of recipes, from casseroles to pies, adding a rich, savory flavor to your dishes.

How should I store Bisto Gluten Free Gravy?
