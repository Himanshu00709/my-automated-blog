Hillshire Farms Polska Kielbasa Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

What is Hillshire Farms Polska Kielbasa?

Hillshire Farms Polska Kielbasa is a type of smoked sausage that originates from Poland. It is made from a blend of pork and beef, seasoned with garlic and other spices, and then smoked to perfection. The result is a savory, smoky flavor that pairs well with a variety of dishes. What sets this kielbasa apart is its gluten-free status, making it a safe option for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Ingredients and Gluten-Free Certification

One of the key reasons Hillshire Farms Polska Kielbasa is favored by those on a gluten-free diet is its ingredient list. The primary ingredients include pork, beef, water, salt, corn syrup, dextrose, spices, garlic, and sodium erythorbate. Importantly, it does not contain any gluten-containing grains such as wheat, barley, or rye.

Hillshire Farms takes extra precautions to ensure that their Polska Kielbasa is gluten-free. The product is manufactured in facilities that adhere to strict gluten-free protocols, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. Additionally, the kielbasa is certified gluten-free by recognized organizations, providing an extra layer of assurance for consumers.

How long does Hillshire Farms Polska Kielbasa last in the refrigerator?
