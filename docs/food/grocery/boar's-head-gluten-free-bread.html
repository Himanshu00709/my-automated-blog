What is Boar's Head Gluten-Free Bread?

Boar's Head Gluten-Free Bread is a product designed to cater to individuals who need or prefer to avoid gluten in their diet. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Boar's Head has crafted a bread that not only meets the dietary needs of these individuals but also delivers on taste and texture, which are often compromised in gluten-free products.

The bread is made with a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, which provide a soft and chewy texture similar to traditional bread. It is also enriched with vitamins and minerals to ensure that it is not only safe for those with gluten intolerance but also nutritious.
