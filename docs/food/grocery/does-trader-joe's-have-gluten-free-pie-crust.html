Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Before diving into Trader Joe's offerings, it's essential to understand what it means for a product to be gluten-free. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, gluten-free products must be free from these grains and any cross-contamination during processing.
