How to Use Barilla Gluten-Free Lasagna Noodles in Your Recipes

Barilla gluten-free lasagna noodles can be used in a variety of recipes, from traditional lasagna to creative new dishes. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Classic Lasagna: Layer the noodles with your favorite marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and ground beef or turkey for a comforting and hearty meal.
  • Vegetable Lasagna: Swap out the meat for a mix of sautéed vegetables like zucchini, spinach, and mushrooms for a lighter, vegetarian option.
  • White Lasagna: Use a creamy béchamel sauce instead of marinara, and add in ingredients like chicken, spinach, and artichokes for a rich and indulgent dish.
  • Lasagna Roll-Ups: Spread a layer of filling (such as ricotta and spinach) on each noodle, roll them up, and bake with sauce and cheese on top.

When cooking with Barilla gluten-free lasagna noodles, it’s important to follow the package instructions carefully. Unlike traditional lasagna noodles, gluten-free noodles do not need to be boiled before baking. Simply layer them in your dish, and they will cook perfectly in the oven.
