Old El Paso Gluten-Free Tortillas: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity. Among the many brands that have risen to the occasion, Old El Paso has made a significant impact with its gluten-free tortillas. These tortillas offer a delicious and convenient option for those who need to avoid gluten but still want to enjoy the flavors of Mexican cuisine. In this article, we’ll dive deep into what makes Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas a standout choice, their nutritional benefits, and how you can incorporate them into your meals.

What Are Old El Paso Gluten-Free Tortillas?

Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas are a product line designed specifically for individuals who need to avoid gluten in their diet. Made with a blend of gluten-free flours, these tortillas are soft, pliable, and perfect for wrapping around your favorite fillings. Whether you’re making tacos, burritos, or quesadillas, these tortillas provide a gluten-free alternative without compromising on taste or texture.

How to Use Old El Paso Gluten-Free Tortillas

Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas are incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes. Here are some creative ways to incorporate them into your meals:

1. Tacos

Fill your gluten-free tortillas with seasoned ground beef, chicken, or tofu, and top with fresh vegetables, cheese, and salsa for a quick and easy taco night.

2. Burritos

Wrap up your favorite fillings—such as rice, beans, meat, and cheese—in a gluten-free tortilla for a hearty and satisfying burrito.

3. Quesadillas

Layer cheese and your choice of fillings between two tortillas and cook on a skillet until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are golden brown.

4. Wraps

Use the tortillas as a wrap for sandwiches, salads, or even breakfast burritos. The possibilities are endless!

FAQs About Old El Paso Gluten-Free Tortillas

Are Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas certified gluten-free?

Yes, Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas are certified gluten-free, meaning they meet strict standards to ensure they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is considered safe for most people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Where can I buy Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas?

Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas are widely available in most grocery stores, both in the gluten-free section and the international foods aisle. You can also purchase them online through various retailers.

Can I freeze Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas?

Yes, you can freeze Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas. Simply place them in a resealable plastic bag and store them in the freezer for up to 3 months. When you’re ready to use them, thaw them at room temperature or warm them in the microwave.

Are Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas vegan?

While Old El Paso gluten-free tortillas do not contain any animal-derived ingredients, they may be processed in facilities that handle dairy products. If you’re following a strict vegan diet, it’s best to check the packaging for any allergen information.
