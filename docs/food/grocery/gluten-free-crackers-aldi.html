Why Choose Gluten-Free Crackers?

Gluten-free crackers are not just for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Many people choose gluten-free options because they believe it leads to a healthier diet. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and while it’s harmless for most, it can cause serious health issues for those with gluten-related disorders. Gluten-free crackers are made from alternative flours like rice, almond, or chickpea, which can offer different nutritional benefits and flavors.
