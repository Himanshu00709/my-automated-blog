Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. While it is harmless for most people, those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity must avoid it to prevent adverse health effects. Additionally, some people choose a gluten-free diet for its potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and reduced inflammation. Good & Gather's gluten-free products are designed to meet these needs without compromising on taste or quality.
