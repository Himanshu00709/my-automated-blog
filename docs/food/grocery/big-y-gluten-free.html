What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues. Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Symptoms can range from digestive issues to fatigue, skin rashes, and even neurological problems. Gluten sensitivity, while not as severe as celiac disease, can still cause discomfort and health issues for those affected.

Going gluten-free is not just a trend; for many, it's a medical necessity. However, even for those without gluten-related health issues, a gluten-free diet can offer benefits such as improved digestion, increased energy levels, and better overall health. Big Y has recognized the importance of catering to this growing demographic by offering a wide array of gluten-free products.
