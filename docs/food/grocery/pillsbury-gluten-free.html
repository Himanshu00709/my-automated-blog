What is Pillsbury Gluten-Free?

Pillsbury Gluten-Free is a line of baking products specifically designed for individuals who need to avoid gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Pillsbury's gluten-free products are made with alternative flours and ingredients that do not contain gluten, ensuring that they are safe for those with gluten-related disorders.

The Pillsbury Gluten-Free line includes a variety of products such as flour blends, cookie dough, pizza crusts, and more. These products are crafted to deliver the same great taste and texture that Pillsbury is known for, making it easier for individuals on a gluten-free diet to enjoy their favorite baked goods.
