Frequently Asked Questions

Are all Trader Joe's products labeled gluten-free safe for celiacs?

While Trader Joe's does a great job of labeling their gluten-free products, it's always important to read the labels carefully. Some products may be processed in facilities that also handle gluten-containing ingredients, which could pose a risk for those with celiac disease. If you're unsure, it's best to contact Trader Joe's customer service for more information.

Does Trader Joe's offer gluten-free bread?

Yes, Trader Joe's offers several gluten-free bread options, including their Gluten-Free Whole Grain Bread and Gluten-Free White Bread. These breads are perfect for sandwiches, toast, or any other bread-based dish.

Can I find gluten-free desserts at Trader Joe's?

Absolutely! Trader Joe's has a variety of gluten-free desserts, including cookies, brownies, and even gluten-free ice cream cones. These treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without the worry of gluten.
