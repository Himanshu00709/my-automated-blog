How to Cook Buitoni Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking Buitoni Gluten-Free Pasta is simple and straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to preparing the perfect gluten-free pasta dish:

  1. Boil Water: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Use about 4-6 quarts of water for every 8 ounces of pasta.
  2. Add Pasta: Once the water is boiling, add the Buitoni Gluten-Free Pasta. Stir gently to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook: Cook the pasta for 7-9 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of tenderness. Be sure to stir occasionally.
  4. Drain: Drain the pasta in a colander, but do not rinse. Rinsing can remove the starch that helps sauces adhere to the pasta.
  5. Serve: Toss the pasta with your favorite sauce, vegetables, or protein, and enjoy!
