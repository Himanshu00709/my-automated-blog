Yes, Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Dough is specifically formulated to be safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. It is made with gluten-free ingredients and is processed in a facility that takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

Absolutely! While it’s perfect for sweet pies, Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Dough can also be used for savory dishes like quiches, pot pies, and more. Its versatile flavor profile makes it suitable for a wide range of recipes.
