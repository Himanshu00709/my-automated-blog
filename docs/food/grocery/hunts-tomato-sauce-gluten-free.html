How to Incorporate Hunt's Tomato Sauce into Your Gluten-Free Diet

One of the best things about Hunt's Tomato Sauce is its versatility. Whether you're cooking for yourself or your family, there are countless ways to incorporate this gluten-free sauce into your meals. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Pasta Dishes: Use Hunt's Tomato Sauce as a base for your favorite gluten-free pasta. Add sautéed vegetables, grilled chicken, or plant-based protein for a complete meal.
  • Pizza: Spread a layer of Hunt's Tomato Sauce on a gluten-free pizza crust, then top with your favorite cheese and toppings for a delicious homemade pizza.
  • Stews and Soups: Add Hunt's Tomato Sauce to your stews and soups for a rich, tomatoey flavor. It pairs well with beans, lentils, and hearty vegetables.
  • Casseroles: Use Hunt's Tomato Sauce as a binder in gluten-free casseroles. It adds moisture and flavor to dishes like lasagna or baked ziti.
  • Dipping Sauce: Serve Hunt's Tomato Sauce as a dipping sauce for gluten-free breadsticks, mozzarella sticks, or vegetable sticks.

FAQs About Hunt's Tomato Sauce and Gluten-Free Diets

Is Hunt's Tomato Sauce certified gluten-free?

While Hunt's Tomato Sauce does not carry a certified gluten-free label, the brand states that their tomato sauce is made without gluten-containing ingredients. However, it's always a good idea to check the label for any updates or changes in ingredients.

Can I use Hunt's Tomato Sauce if I have celiac disease?

Yes, Hunt's Tomato Sauce is considered safe for individuals with celiac disease, as it does not contain gluten ingredients. However, if you have severe gluten sensitivity, it's recommended to contact the manufacturer to confirm that the product is processed in a gluten-free facility.

Are there any other gluten-free products from Hunt's?

Yes, Hunt's offers a variety of gluten-free products, including their diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and some of their specialty sauces. Always check the label to ensure the product is gluten-free.

How should I store Hunt's Tomato Sauce?

Unopened Hunt's Tomato Sauce can be stored in a cool, dry place, such as a pantry. Once opened, it should be refrigerated and used within 5-7 days.
