What Are Kellogg's Corn Flakes Gluten-Free?

Kellogg's Corn Flakes Gluten-Free is a variation of the classic Corn Flakes cereal that has been specially formulated to be free from gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The gluten-free version of Corn Flakes is made with corn as the primary ingredient, ensuring that it is safe for those who need to avoid gluten.

Kellogg's has taken great care to ensure that their gluten-free Corn Flakes are produced in a facility that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. This makes it a reliable option for individuals who need to adhere to a strict gluten-free diet.
