What Are Madeleines?

Madeleines are small, shell-shaped sponge cakes that originated in the Lorraine region of France. They are known for their light, airy texture and slightly crispy edges, often flavored with a hint of lemon or vanilla. Traditionally, madeleines are made with wheat flour, butter, sugar, and eggs, which gives them their signature texture and flavor. However, Trader Joe's has reimagined this classic treat by creating a gluten-free version that retains all the deliciousness of the original.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Madeleines?

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding baked goods that are both safe to eat and delicious can be a challenge. Gluten-free madeleines from Trader Joe's offer a perfect solution. Made with a blend of gluten-free flours, these madeleines are soft, moist, and have a delicate crumb that rivals their traditional counterparts. They are also free from artificial flavors and preservatives, making them a healthier option for anyone looking to indulge without compromising on quality.

FAQs About Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Madeleines

Are Trader Joe's gluten-free madeleines suitable for vegans?

No, Trader Joe's gluten-free madeleines contain eggs and butter, making them unsuitable for a vegan diet.

Do these madeleines contain any nuts?

Trader Joe's gluten-free madeleines are made in a facility that processes nuts, so there is a risk of cross-contamination. If you have a severe nut allergy, it's best to avoid them.

How should I store these madeleines?

To maintain their freshness, store the madeleines in an airtight container at room temperature. They can also be frozen for longer storage.

Are these madeleines keto-friendly?

No, Trader Joe's gluten-free madeleines are not keto-friendly due to their high carbohydrate content.
