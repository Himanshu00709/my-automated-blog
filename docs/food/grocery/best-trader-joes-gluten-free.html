FAQs

Are all Trader Joe's gluten-free products certified gluten-free?

Not all Trader Joe's gluten-free products are certified gluten-free, but they are labeled as gluten-free if they meet the FDA's standards for gluten-free labeling. Always check the packaging for specific information.

Can I find gluten-free options in every Trader Joe's store?

Yes, Trader Joe's stores typically carry a wide range of gluten-free products. However, availability may vary by location, so it’s a good idea to check with your local store if you’re looking for a specific item.

Are Trader Joe's gluten-free products safe for people with celiac disease?

Trader Joe's gluten-free products are generally safe for people with celiac disease, but it’s important to read labels carefully and look for any potential cross-contamination warnings. If you have severe gluten sensitivity, you may want to contact Trader Joe's customer service for more detailed information.
