How to Prepare Gluten-Free Perdue Chicken Tenders

One of the best things about Perdue Chicken Tenders is their versatility. They can be prepared in a variety of ways to suit your taste preferences. Here are some popular methods:

1. Baking

Baking is one of the healthiest ways to prepare gluten-free Perdue Chicken Tenders. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C), place the tenders on a baking sheet, and bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through. This method ensures a crispy exterior while keeping the inside moist and tender.

2. Air Frying

Air frying is another excellent option for those looking to reduce oil consumption. Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C), place the tenders in the basket, and cook for 10-12 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through. The result is a crispy, golden-brown tender with minimal oil.

3. Pan-Frying

For a quicker option, you can pan-fry the tenders. Heat a small amount of oil in a skillet over medium heat, add the tenders, and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through. This method is perfect for those who want a quick and easy meal.
