What Does Gluten-Free Mean?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. A gluten-free diet eliminates all sources of gluten, requiring careful selection of foods and ingredients.

Heinz has recognized the importance of providing safe and delicious options for those on a gluten-free diet. Their gluten-free products are rigorously tested to ensure they meet the strict standards required for gluten-free certification.
