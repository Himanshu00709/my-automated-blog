How to Prepare Perdue Gluten-Free Chicken Nuggets

One of the key advantages of Perdue Gluten-Free Chicken Nuggets is their convenience. They can be prepared in a variety of ways, making them a versatile option for quick meals or snacks. Here are some popular methods for preparing these nuggets:

1. Oven Baking

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 12-14 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the nuggets are golden brown and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

2. Air Frying

For a crispier texture, you can use an air fryer. Preheat the air fryer to 400°F (200°C). Place the nuggets in the air fryer basket in a single layer, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Cook for 8-10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through, until the nuggets are crispy and fully cooked.

3. Pan Frying

If you prefer a more traditional method, you can pan-fry the nuggets. Heat a small amount of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the nuggets and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until they are golden brown and reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
