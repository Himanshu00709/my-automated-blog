Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Even for those without a medical condition, many people choose gluten-free diets for perceived health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels. BJ's Wholesale Club recognizes the importance of catering to these dietary needs and has made significant strides in offering gluten-free products.

Tips for Shopping Gluten-Free at BJ's

Navigating the gluten-free aisle can be overwhelming, especially if you're new to the diet. Here are some tips to make your shopping experience at BJ's more efficient and enjoyable:

  • Read Labels Carefully: Always check the ingredient list and look for the "gluten-free" label. Even if a product seems safe, it's important to verify that it hasn't been processed in a facility that handles gluten-containing products.
  • Plan Ahead: Make a list of the gluten-free items you need before heading to the store. This will help you stay focused and avoid impulse buys that may not be gluten-free.
  • Take Advantage of Bulk Savings: One of the perks of shopping at BJ's is the ability to buy in bulk. Stock up on your favorite gluten-free staples to save money in the long run.
  • Ask for Assistance: If you're unsure about a product, don't hesitate to ask a store associate for help. They can guide you to the gluten-free section or provide additional information about specific products.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Food at BJ's

1. Does BJ's offer a dedicated gluten-free section?

Yes, many BJ's locations have a dedicated gluten-free section, making it easier for shoppers to find what they need. However, gluten-free products can also be found throughout the store, so it's always a good idea to check labels.

2. Are BJ's private label products gluten-free?

BJ's offers a range of private label products, including some that are gluten-free. Look for the "gluten-free" label on the packaging to ensure the product meets your dietary needs.

3. Can I find gluten-free fresh produce at BJ's?

Fresh produce is naturally gluten-free, so you can safely purchase fruits and vegetables at BJ's without worrying about gluten. However, be cautious with pre-packaged or processed produce items, as they may contain additives or be processed in facilities that handle gluten.

4. Does BJ's offer gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, BJ's carries a variety of gluten-free snacks and meals that are kid-friendly, such as gluten-free mac and cheese, chicken nuggets, and cookies. These products are perfect for packing school lunches or serving as quick snacks.

5. Are BJ's gluten-free products affordable?
