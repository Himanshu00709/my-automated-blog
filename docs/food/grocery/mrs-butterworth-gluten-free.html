Enter Mrs. Butterworth's Gluten-Free Syrup—a classic favorite that has been a staple in American households for decades. What Makes Mrs. Butterworth's Syrup Gluten-Free?

Mrs. Butterworth's syrup has long been a beloved brand, known for its rich, buttery flavor and smooth texture. The good news for gluten-free consumers is that many of Mrs. Butterworth's products are naturally gluten-free. The primary ingredients in Mrs. Butterworth's syrup include corn syrup, high fructose corn syrup, water, and natural and artificial flavors. None of these ingredients contain gluten, making the syrup safe for those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

However, it’s always important to check the label, as formulations can change. Mrs. Butterworth's has made efforts to clearly label their gluten-free products, ensuring that consumers can make informed choices. If you’re unsure, look for the "gluten-free" label on the packaging or consult the manufacturer’s website for the most up-to-date information.
