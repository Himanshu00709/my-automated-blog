What Makes Carando Spiral Ham Gluten-Free?

Carando Spiral Ham is crafted with care, ensuring that it meets the dietary needs of those who are gluten-sensitive or have celiac disease. The ham is made from high-quality pork, and the seasoning and glaze used are free from gluten-containing ingredients. This makes it a safe and delicious option for anyone avoiding gluten.

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with gluten intolerance or celiac disease. By choosing Carando Spiral Ham, you can enjoy a flavorful meal without worrying about gluten contamination.

FAQs About Carando Spiral Ham

Is Carando Spiral Ham fully cooked?

Yes, Carando Spiral Ham is fully cooked and ready to eat. However, it is recommended to heat it before serving to enhance its flavor and texture.

Can I freeze Carando Spiral Ham?

Yes, you can freeze Carando Spiral Ham. Make sure to wrap it tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and place it in an airtight container to maintain its quality.

How long does Carando Spiral Ham last in the refrigerator?

Carando Spiral Ham can last up to 5-7 days in the refrigerator when stored properly. Make sure to keep it in its original packaging or wrap it tightly in plastic wrap.

Is Carando Spiral Ham suitable for a low-sodium diet?

Carando Spiral Ham contains 700mg of sodium per serving, which may be high for those on a strict low-sodium diet. It’s best to consult with your healthcare provider if you have specific dietary restrictions.
