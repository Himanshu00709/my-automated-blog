Is Pepperidge Farm Gluten-Free Bread suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Pepperidge Farm’s gluten-free bread is specifically designed for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. It is made in a facility that adheres to strict gluten-free standards to prevent cross-contamination.

Does Pepperidge Farm Gluten-Free Bread contain any allergens?

Can I freeze Pepperidge Farm Gluten-Free Bread?

Yes, you can freeze Pepperidge Farm’s gluten-free bread to extend its shelf life. Simply place the bread in an airtight container or freezer bag and store it in the freezer. When you’re ready to use it, thaw it at room temperature or toast it directly from the freezer.
