What is Zatarain's Fish Fry Gluten-Free?

Zatarain's Fish Fry Gluten-Free is a specially formulated coating mix designed to give your fish that perfect crispy texture without the use of gluten-containing ingredients. Made with a blend of corn flour, rice flour, and a unique mix of spices, this product delivers the same great taste and crunch that Zatarain's is known for, but in a gluten-free format. Whether you're frying catfish, tilapia, or any other type of fish, this mix ensures a delicious result every time.

  • Versatility: While it’s designed for fish, this mix can also be used to fry shrimp, chicken, or even vegetables, making it a versatile addition to your pantry.

How to Use Zatarain's Fish Fry Gluten-Free

Using Zatarain's Fish Fry Gluten-Free is incredibly simple. Here’s a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Prepare Your Fish: Start by rinsing your fish fillets and patting them dry with a paper towel. This helps the coating adhere better.
  2. Coat the Fish: Place the Zatarain's Fish Fry Gluten-Free mix in a shallow dish. Dredge each fillet in the mix, ensuring an even coating on all sides.
  3. Heat the Oil: In a deep skillet or fryer, heat your oil to 375°F (190°C). The ideal oil temperature ensures a crispy exterior without absorbing too much oil.
  4. Fry the Fish: Carefully place the coated fish fillets into the hot oil. Fry for 3-5 minutes on each side, or until the fish is golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Drain and Serve: Remove the fish from the oil and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil. Serve immediately with your favorite sides.
