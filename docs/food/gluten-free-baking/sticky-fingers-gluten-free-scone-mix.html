What Makes Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix Special?

Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix is more than just a baking mix—it's a carefully crafted blend of high-quality ingredients designed to replicate the texture and taste of traditional scones. Unlike many gluten-free products that can be dense or dry, this mix produces scones that are tender, moist, and perfectly crumbly. How to Use Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix

Using Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix is incredibly straightforward, making it accessible to bakers of all skill levels. Here's a step-by-step guide to creating perfect scones every time:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Start by preheating your oven to the temperature specified on the package, usually around 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Mix: In a large mixing bowl, combine the scone mix with the required wet ingredients, typically cold butter and milk or a dairy-free alternative. Mix until the dough just comes together—be careful not to overmix.
  3. Add Your Mix-Ins: If you're adding any extras, such as berries or cheese, gently fold them into the dough at this stage.
  4. Shape the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference. Cut the dough into wedges or squares.
  5. Bake: Place the scones on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for the recommended time, usually 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  6. Enjoy: Allow the scones to cool slightly before serving. They're best enjoyed fresh out of the oven with a cup of tea or coffee.

FAQs About Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix

1. Is Sticky Fingers Gluten-Free Scone Mix suitable for vegans?

While the base mix is gluten-free, it does contain dairy ingredients. However, you can easily make it vegan by substituting the butter and milk with plant-based alternatives such as coconut oil and almond milk.

2. Can I freeze the scones after baking?

Absolutely! Once baked, allow the scones to cool completely, then store them in an airtight container or freezer bag. They can be frozen for up to three months. To reheat, simply pop them in the oven or microwave until warmed through.

3. Are there any allergens in the mix besides gluten?

Yes, the mix contains dairy and soy. If you have allergies to these ingredients, be sure to check the label carefully or consider alternative products.

4. How long do the scones stay fresh?

Freshly baked scones are best enjoyed within 1-2 days. To maintain their texture, store them in an airtight container at room temperature. If you need to keep them longer, freezing is your best option.
