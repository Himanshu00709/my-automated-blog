What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe autoimmune reactions. Going gluten-free is not just a trend; for many, it’s a medical necessity. Gluten-free diets have also been adopted by individuals seeking to improve their overall health, reduce inflammation, or manage conditions like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
