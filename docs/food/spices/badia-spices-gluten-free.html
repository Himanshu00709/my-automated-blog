Why Choose Gluten-Free Spices?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. While spices themselves are naturally gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during processing, packaging, or transportation. This is why it's crucial to choose spices from brands that prioritize gluten-free practices. Badia Spices has taken this responsibility seriously, ensuring that their products are safe for those who need to avoid gluten.
