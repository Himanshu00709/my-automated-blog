What is Prego Alfredo Gluten-Free?

Prego Alfredo Gluten-Free is a creamy, rich pasta sauce made with high-quality ingredients, free from gluten. It’s a perfect alternative for those who are gluten-sensitive or have celiac disease but still want to enjoy the classic Alfredo flavor. The sauce is made with real cream, Parmesan cheese, and a blend of spices, offering a smooth and indulgent taste that pairs well with a variety of gluten-free pasta options.

  • Versatility: Prego Alfredo Gluten-Free can be used in a variety of dishes, from classic fettuccine Alfredo to casseroles and baked pasta dishes.

How to Use Prego Alfredo Gluten-Free in Your Cooking

Prego Alfredo Gluten-Free is incredibly versatile and can be used in a variety of recipes. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Classic Fettuccine Alfredo

Pair the sauce with gluten-free fettuccine noodles for a classic and comforting dish. Add grilled chicken or sautéed vegetables for extra flavor and nutrition.

2. Alfredo Pizza

Use the sauce as a base for a gluten-free pizza. Top with mozzarella cheese, spinach, and grilled chicken for a delicious twist on traditional pizza.

3. Baked Ziti

Combine the sauce with gluten-free ziti noodles, ricotta cheese, and marinara sauce for a hearty baked ziti dish. Top with mozzarella and bake until bubbly.

4. Alfredo Casserole

Layer the sauce with gluten-free pasta, cooked vegetables, and shredded chicken in a casserole dish. Bake until golden and serve as a complete meal.
