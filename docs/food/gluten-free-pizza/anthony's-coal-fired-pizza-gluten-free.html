The Gluten-Free Revolution at Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza

Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza has always been about quality and authenticity. Their pizzas are cooked in coal-fired ovens, which impart a unique flavor and texture that's hard to replicate. However, with the rise of gluten-free diets, Anthony's recognized the need to adapt. They now offer a gluten-free pizza crust that doesn't compromise on taste or quality. This crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, ensuring a crispy yet tender base that holds up well under the weight of toppings.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza takes this seriously. They have implemented strict protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. This includes using separate preparation areas, utensils, and even ovens for their gluten-free pizzas. While they cannot guarantee a 100% gluten-free environment, their efforts go a long way in ensuring the safety of their gluten-free customers.
