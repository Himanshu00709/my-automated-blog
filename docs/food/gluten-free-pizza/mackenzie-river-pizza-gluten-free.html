Mackenzie River Pizza has built a reputation for crafting delicious, high-quality pizzas that cater to a wide range of dietary needs. Their gluten-free options are no exception. Additionally, their gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, resulting in a crust that is both crispy and flavorful.

The Gluten-Free Crust: A Closer Look

The foundation of any great pizza is its crust, and Mackenzie River Pizza’s gluten-free crust is a standout. Made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, this crust is designed to mimic the texture and taste of traditional pizza dough. It’s crispy on the outside, yet soft and chewy on the inside, providing a satisfying bite that gluten-free diners often miss. The crust is also free from artificial flavors and preservatives, making it a healthier option for those looking to enjoy a guilt-free meal.

Cross-Contamination: How Mackenzie River Pizza Ensures Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a major concern. Mackenzie River Pizza takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings. The gluten-free crust is prepared separately from regular dough, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care. Additionally, the restaurant uses separate utensils and preparation areas to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. While no restaurant can guarantee a 100% gluten-free environment, Mackenzie River Pizza goes above and beyond to provide a safe dining experience for gluten-sensitive customers.

FAQs About Mackenzie River Pizza Gluten-Free Options

1. Is the gluten-free crust vegan?

No, the gluten-free crust at Mackenzie River Pizza contains dairy and eggs, so it is not suitable for vegans.

2. Can I order gluten-free pizza for delivery?

Yes, Mackenzie River Pizza offers gluten-free pizza for both dine-in and delivery. However, it’s always a good idea to inform the restaurant of your gluten-free needs when placing your order.

3. Are there any gluten-free desserts available?

While Mackenzie River Pizza primarily focuses on pizzas, they do offer a few gluten-free dessert options, such as gluten-free brownies. Be sure to ask your server for the latest gluten-free dessert offerings.

4. How does Mackenzie River Pizza prevent cross-contamination?

The restaurant uses separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free orders. Staff are also trained to handle gluten-free orders with care to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
