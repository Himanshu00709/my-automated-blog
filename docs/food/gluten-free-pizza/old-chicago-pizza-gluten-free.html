Old Chicago Pizza: A Gluten-Free Delight

For pizza lovers with gluten sensitivities, finding a place that offers delicious, gluten-free options can be a challenge. Old Chicago Pizza, a well-known chain with a rich history, has stepped up to the plate by offering a variety of gluten-free pizzas that don’t compromise on taste or quality. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Old Chicago Pizza, their ingredients, and what makes them stand out in the crowded pizza market.

The History of Old Chicago Pizza

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom was founded in 1976 in Boulder, Colorado. Known for its deep-dish pizzas and extensive beer selection, the restaurant quickly gained a loyal following. Over the years, Old Chicago has expanded its menu to cater to a variety of dietary needs, including gluten-free options. This commitment to inclusivity has made it a favorite among those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

What Makes Old Chicago’s Gluten-Free Crust Special?

Old Chicago’s gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, which gives it a light and crispy texture. The crust is also free from artificial preservatives and flavors, making it a healthier option for those with gluten sensitivities. Additionally, the restaurant takes extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination, ensuring that their gluten-free pizzas are safe for those with celiac disease.

FAQs About Old Chicago’s Gluten-Free Pizza

1. Is Old Chicago’s gluten-free pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Old Chicago takes extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination, making their gluten-free pizzas safe for those with celiac disease. However, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure the highest level of safety.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free pizza?

Absolutely! Old Chicago allows you to customize your gluten-free pizza with a variety of toppings, including vegetables, meats, and cheeses. Just make sure to specify that you need a gluten-free crust.

3. Does Old Chicago offer gluten-free desserts?

While Old Chicago’s primary focus is on gluten-free pizzas, they do offer a limited selection of gluten-free desserts. Be sure to ask your server for the latest options.

4. How does the gluten-free crust compare to the regular crust?

The gluten-free crust is slightly thinner and crispier than the regular crust, but it still delivers the same great taste. Many customers find it to be a satisfying alternative.

Conclusion

Old Chicago Pizza has successfully carved out a niche in the gluten-free pizza market by offering a variety of delicious options that cater to those with gluten sensitivities. Their commitment to quality, safety, and taste makes them a top choice for anyone looking to enjoy a gluten-free pizza without compromising on flavor. Whether you’re a fan of classic cheese, pepperoni, or loaded veggie pizzas, Old Chicago has something for everyone. So, the next time you’re craving pizza, don’t hesitate to visit Old Chicago and indulge in their gluten-free offerings.
