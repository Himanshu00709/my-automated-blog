Gluten-Free Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza: A Delicious and Safe Dining Experience

For those who follow a gluten-free diet, finding a restaurant that offers both delicious and safe dining options can be a challenge. Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza has risen to the occasion, offering a gluten-free menu that doesn't compromise on taste or quality. Known for their authentic coal-fired pizzas, Anthony's has become a go-to spot for gluten-free diners who crave the flavors of traditional Italian cuisine without the worry of gluten contamination.

What Makes Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza Stand Out?

Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza is renowned for its commitment to quality and authenticity. The restaurant uses coal-fired ovens, which reach temperatures of up to 800 degrees Fahrenheit, to create pizzas with a perfectly crisp crust and a smoky flavor that is hard to replicate. This method of cooking not only enhances the taste but also ensures that the pizzas are cooked quickly, retaining the freshness of the ingredients.

For gluten-free diners, Anthony's offers a dedicated gluten-free menu that includes a variety of options, from pizzas to salads and appetizers. The gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, providing a texture that is both crispy and chewy. The restaurant takes extra precautions to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free dishes are prepared separately from those containing gluten.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, making it a safe option for people with celiac disease. However, it's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure that extra care is taken during preparation.

What ingredients are used in the gluten-free crust?

The gluten-free crust at Anthony's is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour. This combination creates a crust that is both crispy and chewy, closely resembling the texture of traditional pizza crust.

Can I customize my gluten-free pizza?

Absolutely! Anthony's offers a variety of toppings that can be added to your gluten-free pizza. Whether you prefer classic toppings like pepperoni and mushrooms or more unique options like roasted peppers and Italian sausage, you can create a pizza that suits your taste.

Are there gluten-free dessert options available?

While Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza primarily focuses on savory dishes, they do offer a few dessert options that can be made gluten-free upon request. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.
