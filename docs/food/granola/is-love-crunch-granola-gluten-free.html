Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to skin rashes and fatigue. Therefore, it’s crucial for individuals with these conditions to ensure that their food is free from gluten contamination.

When it comes to granola, oats are a primary ingredient. While oats are naturally gluten-free, they are often processed in facilities that also handle wheat, barley, or rye, leading to cross-contamination. This makes it essential to look for granola that is certified gluten-free, ensuring that it meets strict standards for gluten content.
