FAQs About Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chocolate Muffins

Are Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chocolate Muffins vegan?

No, Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chocolate Muffins are not vegan. They contain eggs, which are an animal product. However, they are dairy-free and soy-free, making them suitable for those with those specific dietary restrictions.

Can I freeze Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chocolate Muffins?

Yes, you can freeze these muffins! Simply wrap them individually in plastic wrap or place them in an airtight container before freezing. When you're ready to enjoy one, let it thaw at room temperature or warm it up in the microwave for a few seconds.

Are these muffins suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Chocolate Muffins are made in a facility that processes gluten-free products, and they are certified gluten-free. However, if you have severe celiac disease or a high sensitivity to gluten, it's always a good idea to check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings.

How long do these muffins stay fresh?

These muffins typically stay fresh for about 5-7 days when stored in an airtight container at room temperature. If you want to extend their shelf life, you can store them in the refrigerator for up to two weeks or freeze them for several months.
