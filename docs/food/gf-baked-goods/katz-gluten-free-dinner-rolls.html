What Are Katz Gluten-Free Dinner Rolls?

Katz Gluten-Free Dinner Rolls are soft, fluffy, and delicious rolls that are entirely free from gluten, making them suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. These rolls are made with a blend of gluten-free flours and other high-quality ingredients to mimic the texture and taste of traditional dinner rolls. They are pre-baked and frozen, allowing you to enjoy fresh, warm rolls at any time by simply heating them in the oven or microwave.
