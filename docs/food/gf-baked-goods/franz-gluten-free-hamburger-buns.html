What Are Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns?

Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns are specially designed to cater to individuals who need or prefer a gluten-free diet. Made with a blend of gluten-free flours and starches, these buns aim to replicate the texture and flavor of traditional hamburger buns. They are free from wheat, barley, and rye, making them safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

Ingredients and Nutritional Profile

Understanding the ingredients and nutritional content of any food product is crucial, especially for those with dietary restrictions. Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns are made with a combination of gluten-free flours such as rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch. Taste and Texture

No, Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns contain eggs, making them unsuitable for a vegan diet. However, they are free from dairy, which may be suitable for some vegetarians.

Can I freeze Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns?

Yes, Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns can be frozen to extend their shelf life. Simply place them in an airtight container or freezer bag and store them in the freezer for up to three months. Thaw at room temperature or toast directly from frozen.

Are these buns certified gluten-free?

Yes, Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns are certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO), ensuring they meet strict gluten-free standards.

Do these buns contain any artificial preservatives?

No, Franz Gluten-Free Hamburger Buns are free from artificial preservatives, making them a cleaner option for those who prefer natural ingredients.
