Understanding Gluten-Free Certification

When it comes to gluten-free products, certification is crucial. Russell Stover ensures that its gluten-free chocolates meet strict standards. The brand's gluten-free products are certified by recognized organizations, which means they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten. This level is considered safe for most people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

It's important to note that while Russell Stover takes great care to avoid cross-contamination, their facilities also process products that contain gluten. However, the company follows rigorous cleaning protocols to minimize the risk. Always check the packaging for the most up-to-date information regarding gluten-free status.

3. Where can I buy Russell Stover gluten-free chocolates?

Russell Stover chocolates are widely available in grocery stores, drugstores, and online retailers. You can also purchase them directly from the Russell Stover website.

4. Are Russell Stover's sugar-free chocolates also gluten-free?

Yes, many of Russell Stover's sugar-free chocolates are also gluten-free. Always check the packaging for gluten-free labeling to be sure.
