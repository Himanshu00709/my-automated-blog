What is Colby Ridge Popcorn?

Colby Ridge Popcorn is a family-owned company that has been producing high-quality popcorn since 1986. Based in Iowa, the company prides itself on using only the finest ingredients, including non-GMO corn, to create a product that is not only delicious but also safe for those with gluten sensitivities. Their popcorn is gluten-free, making it a perfect snack for anyone looking to avoid gluten without sacrificing taste.

How Colby Ridge Popcorn is Made

Colby Ridge Popcorn is made using a traditional popping method that ensures each kernel is perfectly popped. The process begins with high-quality, non-GMO corn kernels that are carefully selected for their size and moisture content. These kernels are then popped using a small amount of oil, which helps to create a light and fluffy texture. After popping, the popcorn is seasoned with a variety of flavors, ranging from classic butter to more adventurous options like Jalapeño Cheddar.

One of the key aspects of Colby Ridge's production process is their commitment to gluten-free standards. The company takes extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination, ensuring that their popcorn is safe for those with gluten sensitivities. This includes using dedicated equipment and facilities that are free from gluten-containing ingredients.
