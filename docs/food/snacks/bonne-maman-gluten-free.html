FAQs About Bonne Maman Gluten-Free Products

Are all Bonne Maman products gluten-free?

No, not all Bonne Maman products are gluten-free. However, the brand offers a specific range of gluten-free products that are clearly labeled and certified gluten-free.

Where can I buy Bonne Maman gluten-free products?

Bonne Maman gluten-free products are available in most major grocery stores, as well as online retailers. Check the product label to ensure it is gluten-free.

Are Bonne Maman gluten-free products suitable for vegans?

Some Bonne Maman gluten-free products, such as their jams and preserves, are suitable for vegans. However, products like the lemon curd and chocolate spread contain dairy and eggs, so they are not vegan-friendly.

How should I store Bonne Maman gluten-free products?

Bonne Maman gluten-free products should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, they should be refrigerated and consumed within a few weeks.
