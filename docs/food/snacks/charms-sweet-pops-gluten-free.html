The History of Charms Sweet Pops

Charms Sweet Pops have been a staple in the candy world since their introduction in the early 20th century. Today, Charms Sweet Pops are produced by Tootsie Roll Industries, which has maintained the quality and tradition of this beloved candy.

Why Charms Sweet Pops Are Gluten-Free

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding gluten-free candies can be a daunting task. Many candies contain hidden sources of gluten, such as malt flavoring or wheat-based ingredients. However, Charms Sweet Pops are proudly gluten-free. The ingredients used in these lollipops are carefully selected to ensure they meet gluten-free standards. The primary ingredients include sugar, corn syrup, and natural and artificial flavors, all of which are free from gluten. This makes Charms Sweet Pops a safe and enjoyable treat for those who need to avoid gluten.
