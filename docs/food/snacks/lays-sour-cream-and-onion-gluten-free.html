What Makes Lay's Sour Cream and Onion Gluten-Free?

Lay's Sour Cream and Onion chips are made from simple ingredients: potatoes, vegetable oil, and a seasoning blend that includes sour cream and onion flavors. The key to their gluten-free status lies in the absence of wheat, barley, rye, or any other gluten-containing grains in the ingredients list. Additionally, Lay's follows strict manufacturing processes to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that their gluten-free products remain safe for those with gluten sensitivities.
