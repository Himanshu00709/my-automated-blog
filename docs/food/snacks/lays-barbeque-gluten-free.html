What Makes Lays Barbecue Gluten-Free?

Lays Barbecue Gluten-Free chips are made with the same high-quality potatoes and signature barbecue seasoning that fans love, but with one crucial difference: they are free from gluten-containing ingredients. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Lays ensures that their barbecue chips are safe for these individuals by using gluten-free ingredients and adhering to strict manufacturing processes to avoid cross-contamination.

The barbecue flavor is a perfect blend of smoky, sweet, and tangy notes, making it a crowd-pleaser.
