What Are Lance Cheese Crackers?

Lance Cheese Crackers are a classic snack that has been enjoyed by many for decades. These crackers are known for their rich, cheesy flavor and satisfying crunch. Traditionally, Lance Cheese Crackers are made with wheat flour, which contains gluten. However, in response to the growing demand for gluten-free options, Lance has introduced a gluten-free version of their beloved cheese crackers.

The gluten-free Lance Cheese Crackers are made with alternative flours such as rice flour and potato starch, ensuring that they are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Despite the absence of gluten, these crackers retain the same delicious taste and texture that Lance is known for.
