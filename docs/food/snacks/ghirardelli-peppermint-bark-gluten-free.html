Why Gluten-Free Matters

For individuals with gluten intolerance or celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can often be hidden in unexpected places, including some chocolates and candies. Ghirardelli's Gluten-Free Peppermint Bark is carefully formulated to exclude gluten-containing ingredients, making it a safe and enjoyable option for those with dietary restrictions.

FAQs About Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark Gluten-Free

Is Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark truly gluten-free?

Yes, Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark is certified gluten-free, meaning it has been tested and confirmed to contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.

Can I find Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark year-round?

Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark is a seasonal product, typically available during the holiday season. However, some specialty stores or online retailers may carry it outside of the traditional holiday period.

Is Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark suitable for vegans?

No, Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark contains dairy products, making it unsuitable for a vegan diet. However, it is gluten-free and safe for those with gluten sensitivities.

How should I store Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark?

To maintain its freshness and texture, store Ghirardelli Peppermint Bark in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. If stored properly, it can last for several weeks.
