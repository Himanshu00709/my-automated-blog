Is Snickers Peanut Butter Gluten-Free safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Snickers Peanut Butter Gluten-Free is safe for people with celiac disease. It is certified gluten-free, meaning it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for those with celiac disease.

Does Snickers Peanut Butter Gluten-Free contain nuts?

Yes, Snickers Peanut Butter Gluten-Free contains peanuts, which are a type of nut. If you have a nut allergy, this product may not be suitable for you.
