What Are Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares?

Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares are bite-sized chocolate treats that are specifically crafted to be free from gluten. Made by the renowned Ghirardelli Chocolate Company, these squares are a testament to the brand’s commitment to quality and innovation. Each square is made with premium ingredients, ensuring a rich and smooth chocolate experience that rivals traditional chocolate bars. Whether you’re a chocolate lover or someone with dietary restrictions, these squares are a must-try.

Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares stand out for several reasons. Here are some of their key features:

FAQs About Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares

Are Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares are certified gluten-free, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

Do Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares contain dairy?

Most Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares contain dairy, so they may not be suitable for those with dairy allergies or lactose intolerance. Always check the label for specific ingredients.

Can I use Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares in baking?

Absolutely! These squares can be melted and used in a variety of gluten-free baking recipes, from brownies to cookies.

How should I store Ghirardelli Gluten-Free Squares?

Store the squares in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For longer storage, you can keep them in the refrigerator.
