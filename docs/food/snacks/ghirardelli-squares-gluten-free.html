What Are Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free?

Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free are bite-sized chocolate squares that come in a variety of flavors, all crafted to be free from gluten. Each square is individually wrapped, making them perfect for on-the-go snacking or sharing with friends and family.

FAQs About Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free

Are Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free are certified gluten-free, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, always check the packaging for the most up-to-date information.

Where can I buy Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free?

These chocolates are widely available at grocery stores, specialty food stores, and online retailers. You can also purchase them directly from the Ghirardelli website.

Do Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free contain nuts?

Some varieties may contain nuts or be processed in facilities that handle nuts. Always read the label to ensure the product meets your dietary needs.

How should I store Ghirardelli Squares Gluten-Free?

Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For best results, keep them at room temperature to maintain their texture and flavor.
