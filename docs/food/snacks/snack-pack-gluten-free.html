What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of health issues, from digestive problems to severe autoimmune reactions. Going gluten-free is essential for these individuals, and it has also become a popular dietary choice for others seeking to improve their overall health.

Gluten-free diets require careful attention to food labels and ingredients, as gluten can be hidden in many processed foods. This is where Snack Pack gluten-free products come into play, offering a convenient and safe option for those avoiding gluten.
