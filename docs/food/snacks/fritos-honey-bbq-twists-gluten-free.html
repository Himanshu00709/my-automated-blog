Fritos Honey BBQ Twists are made with corn as the primary ingredient, which is naturally gluten-free. Unlike many other snacks that use wheat or barley-based ingredients, Fritos ensures that their Honey BBQ Twists are free from gluten-containing grains. This makes them a safe and enjoyable option for anyone avoiding gluten.
