The Fine Cheese Company has long been synonymous with premium quality and artisanal craftsmanship. What Makes These Crackers Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free products are made without wheat, barley, rye, or any other gluten-containing grains. The Fine Cheese Company Gluten-Free Crackers are crafted using alternative flours such as rice flour, corn flour, and potato starch. These ingredients not only make the crackers safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity but also contribute to their unique texture and flavor.

FAQs About Fine Cheese Company Gluten-Free Crackers

Are these crackers suitable for vegans?

Yes, the Fine Cheese Company Gluten-Free Crackers are vegan-friendly. They do not contain any animal-derived ingredients, making them suitable for those following a vegan diet.

Do these crackers contain any allergens?

While these crackers are gluten-free, they may contain traces of nuts and soy due to cross-contamination during manufacturing. Always check the packaging for the most up-to-date allergen information.

Where can I purchase these crackers?

Fine Cheese Company Gluten-Free Crackers are available at select gourmet food stores, specialty cheese shops, and online retailers. You can also purchase them directly from the Fine Cheese Company’s website.

How should I store these crackers?

To maintain their freshness and crispness, store the crackers in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposing them to moisture, as this can cause them to become soft.
