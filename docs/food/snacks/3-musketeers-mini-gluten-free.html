What Makes 3 Musketeers Mini Gluten-Free?

3 Musketeers Mini bars are made with simple ingredients that do not include gluten-containing grains like wheat, barley, or rye. The primary ingredients are sugar, corn syrup, hydrogenated palm kernel oil, cocoa powder, and skim milk. These ingredients are carefully selected to ensure that the final product is safe for those who need to avoid gluten. However, it's always a good idea to check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings, especially if you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease.
