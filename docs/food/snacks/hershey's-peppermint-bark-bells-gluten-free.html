Why Choose Gluten-Free Treats?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten intolerance, or wheat allergies. Consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues for these individuals, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Even for those without gluten sensitivities, opting for gluten-free treats can sometimes be a healthier choice, as they often contain fewer processed ingredients.

Hershey's Peppermint Bark Bells are certified gluten-free, meaning they meet strict standards to ensure they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten. This certification provides peace of mind for consumers who need to avoid gluten in their diet.
