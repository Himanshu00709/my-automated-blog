FAQs About Blue Diamond Honey Roasted Almonds

Are Blue Diamond Honey Roasted Almonds really gluten-free?

Yes, Blue Diamond Honey Roasted Almonds are certified gluten-free, making them safe for individuals with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

How many almonds are in a serving?

A standard serving size is about 28 grams, which is roughly 23 almonds.

Can I eat these almonds if I’m on a low-carb diet?

While almonds are relatively low in carbs, the honey-roasted coating does add some sugar. If you're on a strict low-carb diet, you may want to opt for plain almonds instead.

Are these almonds suitable for vegans?

Yes, Blue Diamond Honey Roasted Almonds are vegan-friendly as they contain no animal-derived ingredients.

How should I store these almonds?

To maintain freshness, store the almonds in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it’s best to keep them in an airtight container.
