What Are Crème Savers?

Crème Savers are a type of hard candy that combines a creamy center with a fruity outer layer. Originally introduced by Nestlé in the 1990s, these candies quickly gained popularity for their unique texture and flavor combinations. The most famous flavors include Strawberries & Crème and Oranges & Crème, which offer a perfect balance of sweet and tangy notes. The creamy center is what sets Crème Savers apart from other candies, providing a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth experience that’s hard to resist.

Are Crème Savers Gluten-Free?

Yes, Crème Savers are gluten-free. This is great news for individuals who need to avoid gluten due to celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The ingredients used in Crème Savers do not include any gluten-containing grains such as wheat, barley, or rye. However, it’s always a good idea to check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings, especially if you have severe gluten intolerance.

FAQs About Crème Savers

Are Crème Savers vegan?

No, Crème Savers are not vegan. They contain dairy products, which are used to create their creamy center.

Can I find Crème Savers in stores?

While Crème Savers were discontinued by Nestlé in the early 2000s, they have since been reintroduced by other manufacturers. You can often find them in specialty candy stores or online retailers.

Are there any sugar-free versions of Crème Savers?

As of now, there are no sugar-free versions of Crème Savers available. However, you can check with specialty candy makers for alternative options.
