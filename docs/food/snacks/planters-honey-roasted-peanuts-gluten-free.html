What Makes Planter's Honey Roasted Peanuts Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, from digestive discomfort to more severe autoimmune reactions. Therefore, finding gluten-free snacks is crucial for maintaining a healthy and enjoyable diet.

Planter's Honey Roasted Peanuts are made with simple, natural ingredients that do not include any gluten-containing grains. The primary ingredients are peanuts, sugar, honey, and salt. The roasting process enhances the natural flavors of the peanuts while the honey adds a touch of sweetness, creating a snack that is both satisfying and safe for those avoiding gluten.

How should I store Planter's Honey Roasted Peanuts?
