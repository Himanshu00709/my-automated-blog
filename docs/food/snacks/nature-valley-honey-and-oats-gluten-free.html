Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity in recent years, not just among those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance but also among individuals looking to improve their overall health. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause digestive issues for some people. By choosing gluten-free snacks like Nature Valley Honey and Oats bars, you can enjoy a delicious treat without worrying about adverse effects on your digestive system.
