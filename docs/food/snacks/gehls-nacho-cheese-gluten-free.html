What Makes Gehl's Nacho Cheese Gluten-Free?

Gehl's Nacho Cheese is crafted with a careful selection of ingredients to ensure it meets the gluten-free standard. The primary ingredients include cheddar cheese, water, vegetable oil, and a blend of spices. Importantly, the product is free from wheat, barley, rye, and other gluten-containing grains. This makes it a safe choice for individuals who need to avoid gluten due to health reasons or personal preference.

Moreover, Gehl's Nacho Cheese is produced in facilities that adhere to strict gluten-free protocols, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. This commitment to quality and safety has earned Gehl's a loyal following among gluten-free consumers.

No, Gehl's Nacho Cheese contains dairy products, specifically cheddar cheese, making it unsuitable for a vegan diet.

Can I freeze Gehl's Nacho Cheese?

It is not recommended to freeze Gehl's Nacho Cheese as it may alter the texture and consistency of the product. It is best stored in the refrigerator and consumed within the recommended time frame.

Is Gehl's Nacho Cheese safe for individuals with celiac disease?

Yes, Gehl's Nacho Cheese is gluten-free and produced in facilities that follow strict gluten-free protocols, making it safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities.

How long does Gehl's Nacho Cheese last after opening?

Once opened, Gehl's Nacho Cheese should be consumed within 7-10 days if stored properly in the refrigerator. Always check the product label for specific storage instructions.
