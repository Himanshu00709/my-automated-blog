What Are Snack Factory Pretzel Crisps Gluten-Free?

Snack Factory Pretzel Crisps Gluten-Free are a unique twist on traditional pretzels. Unlike regular pretzels, which are typically made from wheat flour, these crisps are crafted from a blend of gluten-free ingredients, including rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca starch. The result is a light, crispy snack that retains the classic pretzel flavor without the gluten. These crisps are baked, not fried, which further enhances their health profile by reducing the fat content.

One of the standout features of Snack Factory Pretzel Crisps Gluten-Free is their versatility. They can be enjoyed on their own as a snack, paired with dips, or even used as a base for appetizers. Their thin, crunchy texture makes them a favorite among snack enthusiasts who are looking for something different from the usual chips or crackers.
