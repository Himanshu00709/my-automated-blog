What Makes Kettle Brand Chips Gluten-Free?

Kettle Brand Chips are made from simple, natural ingredients, which is one of the reasons they are gluten-free. The primary ingredients include potatoes, oil, and salt. Unlike many other snack brands, Kettle Brand does not use any gluten-containing additives or flavorings in their chips. This makes them a safe choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Moreover, Kettle Brand takes extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Their manufacturing facilities are designed to prevent any contact between gluten-free products and those that contain gluten. This level of care ensures that their chips remain safe for gluten-free consumers.
