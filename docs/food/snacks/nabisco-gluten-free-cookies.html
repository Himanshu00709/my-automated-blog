What Are Gluten-Free Cookies?

Gluten-free cookies are baked goods made without gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. These cookies are specifically designed for individuals who have celiac disease, gluten intolerance, or those who choose to follow a gluten-free diet for other health reasons. Nabisco has stepped up to the plate by offering a variety of gluten-free cookies that are not only safe but also incredibly delicious.

Health Benefits of Gluten-Free Cookies

While gluten-free cookies are essential for those with gluten-related disorders, they also offer several health benefits for anyone looking to improve their diet:

  • Digestive Health: For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, gluten-free cookies can help prevent digestive issues such as bloating, gas, and diarrhea.
  • Nutrient-Rich Ingredients: Many gluten-free cookies are made with nutrient-dense ingredients like almond flour, coconut flour, and oats, which provide essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Lower in Calories: Some gluten-free cookies are lower in calories compared to their traditional counterparts, making them a healthier option for those watching their calorie intake.
  • Allergen-Friendly: Gluten-free cookies are often free from other common allergens like dairy and nuts, making them suitable for a wider range of dietary needs.
