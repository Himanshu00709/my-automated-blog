Why Choose Gluten-Free Fries?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding gluten-free options can be a challenge. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause severe digestive issues and other health problems for those who are intolerant. Alexia Seasoned Waffle Fries are certified gluten-free, meaning they have been rigorously tested to ensure they contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, the threshold considered safe for most people with gluten-related disorders.

But even if you don’t have a gluten intolerance, choosing gluten-free products like Alexia Seasoned Waffle Fries can still be beneficial. Many people report feeling less bloated and more energetic when they reduce their gluten intake. Plus, gluten-free products often contain fewer processed ingredients, making them a healthier choice overall.
