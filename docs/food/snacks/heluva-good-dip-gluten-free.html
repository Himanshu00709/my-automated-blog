Ingredients to Watch Out For

While many Heluva Good Dips are gluten-free, it's essential to be aware of potential sources of gluten in dips. Some common ingredients that may contain gluten include:

  • Modified Food Starch: This can sometimes be derived from wheat, which contains gluten.
  • Natural Flavors: While often gluten-free, natural flavors can sometimes contain gluten, so it's best to verify with the manufacturer.
  • Thickeners: Some thickeners used in dips may contain gluten, so always check the label.

Heluva Good! is transparent about their ingredients, and their gluten-free dips are clearly labeled, making it easier for consumers to make informed choices.
