What Are Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Crackers?

Gluten-free peanut butter crackers are a type of snack that combines the rich, creamy flavor of peanut butter with the satisfying crunch of crackers, all without containing any gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Lance's gluten-free peanut butter crackers are made with alternative flours and ingredients that do not contain gluten, making them safe for those who need to avoid it.
