What Are Milton Gluten-Free Crackers?

Milton Gluten-Free Crackers are a type of snack cracker made without gluten-containing ingredients. They are designed to cater to individuals who have celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those who simply prefer to avoid gluten in their diet. These crackers are crafted with a blend of gluten-free flours and natural ingredients, ensuring a satisfying crunch and a delightful taste.

Ingredients and Nutritional Profile

One of the key factors that set Milton Gluten-Free Crackers apart is their carefully selected ingredients. The crackers are made from a combination of gluten-free flours such as rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch. These ingredients not only provide a gluten-free base but also contribute to the crackers' light and crispy texture.

In addition to the flours, Milton Gluten-Free Crackers often include a variety of seeds, such as sesame seeds, flaxseeds, and sunflower seeds. These seeds not only enhance the flavor but also add a nutritional boost, providing essential fatty acids, fiber, and protein.
