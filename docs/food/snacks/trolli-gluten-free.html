What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many individuals have adopted a gluten-free diet to manage their symptoms and improve their overall health.
