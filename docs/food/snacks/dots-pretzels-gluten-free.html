What Makes Dot's Pretzels Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for these individuals. Dot's Pretzels recognized the need for a gluten-free option and developed a recipe that eliminates gluten without sacrificing the taste and texture that made their original pretzels so popular.

The gluten-free version of Dot's Pretzels is made with a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour and tapioca starch. These ingredients provide the same satisfying crunch as traditional pretzels, while the unique seasoning blend ensures that every bite is packed with flavor. The result is a snack that is not only safe for those with gluten sensitivities but also delicious enough to appeal to everyone.
