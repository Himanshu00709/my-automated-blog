Are Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles Safe for Celiac Disease?

For individuals with celiac disease, even trace amounts of gluten can cause severe reactions. The good news is that Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles are certified gluten-free, meaning they meet strict standards for gluten content. This certification ensures that the product contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the threshold considered safe for most people with celiac disease.

FAQs About Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles

1. Are Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles vegan?

No, Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles are not vegan. They contain dairy-derived ingredients like cheddar cheese and whey.

2. Can I find Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles in different sizes?

Yes, Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles are available in various sizes, including single-serving bags and family-sized packages.

3. Are there any allergens in Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles?

Yes, Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles contain milk, which is a common allergen. They do not contain wheat, soy, or nuts, but always check the label for the most accurate information.

4. How should I store Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles?

To maintain their freshness and crunch, store Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles in a cool, dry place. Once opened, reseal the bag tightly to prevent them from going stale.

5. Can I eat Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles on a low-carb diet?

While Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles are gluten-free, they are not low in carbohydrates. If you’re following a strict low-carb diet, you may want to limit your intake of these chips.

Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles are a delicious and gluten-free snack option that can be enjoyed by many, including those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. With their bold flavor and satisfying crunch, they make a great addition to any snack lineup. However, like all snacks, they should be consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet. Whether you’re enjoying them at a party, on a road trip, or just as a treat at home, you can feel confident knowing that Cheddar Sour Cream Ruffles are a safe and tasty choice.
