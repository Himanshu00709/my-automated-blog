What Makes Old Trapper Jerky Gluten-Free?

Old Trapper Jerky is crafted with a commitment to quality and simplicity. The brand ensures that its jerky is free from gluten-containing ingredients, making it a safe choice for those who need to avoid gluten. The primary ingredients in Old Trapper Jerky include high-quality beef, water, sugar, salt, and natural flavors. The absence of wheat, barley, rye, or any gluten-containing additives ensures that the jerky is gluten-free.

Moreover, Old Trapper Jerky is produced in facilities that adhere to strict gluten-free protocols. This means that the risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing products is minimized, providing an extra layer of safety for consumers with gluten sensitivities.
