What is Gluten and Why is Gluten-Free Important?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless and even beneficial, providing elasticity to dough and helping it rise. However, for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient malabsorption, and even long-term damage to the intestines.

The demand for gluten-free products has skyrocketed in recent years, not only among those with medical conditions but also among health-conscious consumers who believe that a gluten-free diet can improve their overall well-being. This growing demand has led many food companies, including Nabisco, to develop gluten-free versions of their popular products.

The Journey to Chips Ahoy Gluten-Free

Creating a gluten-free version of a classic cookie like Chips Ahoy was no small feat. The challenge was to replicate the same chewy texture and rich chocolatey flavor that fans have come to love, while ensuring that the product is completely free of gluten. Nabisco’s team of food scientists and product developers worked tirelessly to achieve this balance, experimenting with various gluten-free flours and ingredients.

After years of research and development, Nabisco finally perfected the recipe. The new Chips Ahoy Gluten-Free cookies are made with a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, which provide the same satisfying texture as the original cookies. The chocolate chips used in the cookies are also gluten-free, ensuring that every bite is safe for those with gluten sensitivities.
