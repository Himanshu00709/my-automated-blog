Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health issues, from digestive problems to more severe autoimmune reactions. As a result, finding gluten-free snacks that are both safe and enjoyable can be a challenge.

Universal Yums recognizes the importance of providing gluten-free options for its subscribers. By including gluten-free snacks in their boxes, they ensure that everyone can participate in the joy of discovering new flavors, regardless of dietary restrictions.

How Universal Yums Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Universal Yums takes this issue seriously and works closely with their suppliers to ensure that the gluten-free snacks they include in their boxes are produced in facilities that avoid cross-contamination. Additionally, each snack is clearly labeled, making it easy for subscribers to identify which items are safe for them to eat.

Universal Yums also provides detailed information about each snack in their guidebook, including ingredients and allergen information. This transparency is crucial for those who need to carefully monitor their diet.
