What Are Gluten-Free Pretzels?

Gluten-free pretzels are a type of snack made without gluten-containing ingredients such as wheat, barley, or rye. Instead, they are typically made from alternative flours like rice flour, corn flour, or almond flour. Good Health Gluten-Free Pretzels are crafted with care to ensure they are not only free from gluten but also packed with nutrients that support a healthy lifestyle.
