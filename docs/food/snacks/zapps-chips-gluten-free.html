What Are Zapp's Chips?

Zapp's Chips are a brand of kettle-cooked potato chips that originated in Louisiana. Known for their thick-cut, crunchy texture and unique flavor profiles, Zapp's Chips have gained a loyal following over the years. The brand prides itself on using high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create a snack that is both satisfying and flavorful. But what truly sets Zapp's Chips apart is their commitment to catering to a wide range of dietary needs, including those who require gluten-free options.

Why Zapp's Chips Are Gluten-Free

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding snacks that are safe to eat can be a daunting task. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for those who are intolerant. Zapp's Chips have recognized this need and have made sure that their products are gluten-free. The chips are made from simple ingredients like potatoes, oil, and seasonings, all of which are naturally gluten-free. Additionally, Zapp's Chips are produced in facilities that take precautions to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing products, ensuring that their chips are safe for those with gluten sensitivities.
