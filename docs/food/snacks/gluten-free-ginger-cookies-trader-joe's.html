FAQs About Trader Joe's Gluten-Free Ginger Cookies

Are Trader Joe's gluten-free ginger cookies vegan?

No, these cookies contain butter, which is a dairy product, so they are not vegan. However, they are suitable for those following a gluten-free diet.

Do these cookies contain nuts?

Trader Joe's gluten-free ginger cookies do not contain nuts, but they are produced in a facility that processes nuts, so there may be a risk of cross-contamination.

How long do these cookies stay fresh?

When stored in an airtight container, these cookies can stay fresh for up to two weeks. For longer storage, you can freeze them for up to three months.

Can I find these cookies year-round?

While Trader Joe's often stocks these cookies year-round, they are especially popular during the holiday season. It's always a good idea to check with your local store for availability.
