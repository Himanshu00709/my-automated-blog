For decades, Bit-O-Honey has been a beloved candy, known for its unique combination of honey-flavored taffy and almond bits. What is Bit-O-Honey?

Bit-O-Honey is a classic American candy that has been around since 1924. It’s made from a chewy honey-flavored taffy that’s filled with small pieces of almond. The candy is individually wrapped, making it a convenient and nostalgic treat for people of all ages. Its unique texture and flavor have made it a staple in candy aisles for nearly a century.

Is Bit-O-Honey Gluten-Free?

Yes, Bit-O-Honey is gluten-free! According to the manufacturer, Bit-O-Honey does not contain any gluten-containing ingredients. This makes it a safe option for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, it’s always a good idea to check the packaging for any updates or changes in ingredients, as formulations can vary over time.

FAQs About Bit-O-Honey

1. Is Bit-O-Honey safe for people with nut allergies?

No, Bit-O-Honey contains almonds, which are tree nuts. If you have a nut allergy, it’s best to avoid this candy.

2. Can Bit-O-Honey be part of a vegan diet?

No, Bit-O-Honey contains nonfat milk, which is an animal-derived ingredient. Therefore, it is not suitable for vegans.

3. How should I store Bit-O-Honey?

Bit-O-Honey should be stored in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposing it to high temperatures, as this can cause the candy to melt or become too sticky.

4. Are there any artificial colors in Bit-O-Honey?

No, Bit-O-Honey does not contain any artificial colors. Its natural color comes from the ingredients used.
