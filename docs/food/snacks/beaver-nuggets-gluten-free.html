What Are Beaver Nuggets?

Beaver Nuggets are a signature snack from Buc-ee's, known for their unique texture and flavor. Are Beaver Nuggets Gluten-Free?

Yes, Beaver Nuggets are gluten-free! This is great news for anyone who needs to avoid gluten due to celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or personal dietary preferences. The primary ingredients in Beaver Nuggets are cornmeal, sugar, and oil, all of which are naturally gluten-free. Additionally, Buc-ee's takes care to ensure that their production process minimizes the risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. However, it's always a good idea to check the packaging for the most up-to-date ingredient and allergen information, as formulations can change.
