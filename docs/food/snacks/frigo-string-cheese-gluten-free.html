What is Frigo String Cheese?

Frigo String Cheese is a type of mozzarella cheese that is known for its unique stringy texture, making it a fun and interactive snack for both kids and adults. Why Choose Gluten-Free Snacks?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free snacks to avoid these adverse effects. Frigo String Cheese is an excellent choice for those looking to maintain a gluten-free diet without sacrificing taste or convenience.
