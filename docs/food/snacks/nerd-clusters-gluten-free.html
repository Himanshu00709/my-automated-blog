Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, consuming gluten is not an issue. However, for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies, consuming gluten can lead to a range of health problems, including digestive issues, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine.

As awareness of these conditions has grown, so has the demand for gluten-free products. The gluten-free market has expanded significantly, with a wide variety of snacks, baked goods, and other food items now available to cater to this need. Nerd Clusters are part of this trend, offering a delicious and convenient snack option that is safe for those who need to avoid gluten.

How Nerd Clusters Are Made Gluten-Free

The process of making Nerd Clusters gluten-free involves careful selection of ingredients and rigorous testing to ensure that no gluten-containing substances are present. Here’s a step-by-step overview of how Nerd Clusters are made gluten-free:

  1. Ingredient Selection: The primary ingredients in Nerd Clusters include sugar, corn syrup, chocolate, and Nerds candy. All of these ingredients are naturally gluten-free.   2. Manufacturing Process: The production facilities where Nerd Clusters are made must adhere to strict gluten-free protocols. This includes thorough cleaning of equipment and production lines to prevent any cross-contamination with gluten-containing products.
  3. Testing and Certification: To ensure that Nerd Clusters meet gluten-free standards, they undergo rigorous testing. This testing is often conducted by third-party organizations that specialize in gluten-free certification. Once the product passes these tests, it can be labeled as gluten-free.

By following these steps, Nerd Clusters are able to maintain their gluten-free status, providing a safe and enjoyable snack option for those who need to avoid gluten.
