What is Chester's Puffcorn?

Chester's Puffcorn is a gluten-free snack made from cornmeal, which gives it a light and airy texture. Unlike traditional popcorn, Puffcorn is not made from whole kernels but rather from a cornmeal base that is extruded and puffed to create its unique shape. This process results in a snack that is not only gluten-free but also free from the hard, unpopped kernels that can sometimes be found in regular popcorn. Chester's Puffcorn comes in a variety of flavors, including Butter, Cheese, and White Cheddar, making it a versatile snack for any occasion.
