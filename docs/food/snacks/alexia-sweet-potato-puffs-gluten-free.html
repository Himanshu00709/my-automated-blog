FAQs About Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs

Are Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs really gluten-free?

Yes, Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs are certified gluten-free, making them safe for individuals with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

How do I cook Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs?

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Spread the puffs in a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. For best results, flip them halfway through the cooking time.

Can I air fry Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs?

Yes, you can air fry Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs. Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C) and cook for 8-10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through to ensure even cooking.

Are Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs vegan?

Yes, Alexia Sweet Potato Puffs are vegan, as they contain no animal-derived ingredients.
