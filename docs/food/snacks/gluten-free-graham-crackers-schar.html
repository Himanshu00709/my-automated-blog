What Are Gluten-Free Graham Crackers?

Graham crackers are a classic snack, often used in desserts like s'mores, pie crusts, or simply enjoyed on their own. Traditionally, graham crackers are made with graham flour, which contains gluten. However, Schär has reimagined this beloved snack by creating a gluten-free version that maintains the same great taste and texture without the gluten.

Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers are made with a blend of gluten-free flours, including rice flour and cornstarch, ensuring that they are safe for those with gluten-related disorders. They are also free from artificial flavors and preservatives, making them a healthier option for everyone.

How to Incorporate Schär’s Gluten-Free Graham Crackers into Your Diet

One of the best things about Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers is their versatility. Here are some creative ways to enjoy them:

1. Classic S'mores

No campfire? No problem! You can make s'mores at home using Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate. Simply layer the ingredients and microwave for a few seconds until the marshmallow is gooey and the chocolate is melted.

2. Pie Crusts

Crush the graham crackers and mix them with melted butter and a bit of sugar to create a delicious gluten-free pie crust. This works perfectly for cheesecakes, key lime pies, or any other dessert that calls for a graham cracker crust.

3. Snack Time

Sometimes, the simplest way is the best. Enjoy Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers on their own or paired with your favorite spread, such as peanut butter, almond butter, or cream cheese.

4. Yogurt Parfait

Break the graham crackers into smaller pieces and layer them with yogurt and fresh fruit for a quick and easy parfait. This makes for a nutritious and satisfying breakfast or snack.

1. Are Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers are certified gluten-free and are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. They are produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

3. Where can I buy Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers?

Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers are widely available in most grocery stores, health food stores, and online retailers. You can also purchase them directly from Schär’s website.

4. How do Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers compare to traditional graham crackers?

Schär’s gluten-free graham crackers are designed to mimic the taste and texture of traditional graham crackers as closely as possible. While there may be slight differences due to the absence of gluten, most people find them to be a satisfying alternative.
