What Makes Wiley Wallaby Gluten-Free?

Wiley Wallaby's commitment to producing gluten-free products is evident in their meticulous ingredient selection and manufacturing processes. Unlike traditional licorice, which often contains wheat flour as a primary ingredient, Wiley Wallaby uses a combination of tapioca flour and potato starch to achieve the perfect texture and consistency. This substitution not only makes their licorice gluten-free but also ensures that it remains soft, chewy, and delicious.

Moreover, Wiley Wallaby's manufacturing facilities are dedicated gluten-free environments, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. This is crucial for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivities, as even trace amounts of gluten can cause adverse reactions. By adhering to strict gluten-free standards, Wiley Wallaby provides a safe and enjoyable snacking option for everyone.
