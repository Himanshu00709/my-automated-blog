Snack Factory Gluten-Free Pretzel Crisps are a modern twist on the classic pretzel. The result is a light, airy, and crispy snack that retains all the savory goodness of a pretzel without the gluten. Available in a variety of flavors, these crisps are perfect for dipping, pairing with cheese, or enjoying on their own.
