What is Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free?

Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free is a variation of the classic Mr. Goodbar candy bar, which has been a beloved treat since its introduction in 1925. The original Mr. Goodbar is known for its combination of creamy milk chocolate and crunchy peanuts. The gluten-free version maintains the same delicious taste but is made without any gluten-containing ingredients, making it safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

FAQs About Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free

Is Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free suitable for vegans?

No, Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free contains milk chocolate and is not suitable for vegans. However, it is a great option for those who follow a gluten-free diet.

Can children eat Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free?

Yes, Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free is safe for children, provided they do not have any allergies to peanuts or dairy. Always check with a healthcare provider if you have concerns about introducing new foods to your child's diet.

Where can I buy Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free?

Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free is available at most major grocery stores, as well as online retailers. Check the candy aisle or the gluten-free section of your local store.

How should I store Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free?

To maintain freshness, store Mr. Goodbar Gluten-Free in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight or high temperatures, as this can cause the chocolate to melt.
