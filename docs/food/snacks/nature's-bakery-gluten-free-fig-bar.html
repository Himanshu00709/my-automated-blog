Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity over the years, not just among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, but also among individuals looking to improve their overall health. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and while it is harmless for most people, it can cause digestive issues and other health problems for those with gluten-related disorders.

Nature's Bakery Gluten-Free Fig Bar is an excellent choice for anyone following a gluten-free diet. It is certified gluten-free, meaning it has been rigorously tested to ensure it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, the threshold considered safe for most people with celiac disease. This certification provides peace of mind for those who need to avoid gluten strictly.
