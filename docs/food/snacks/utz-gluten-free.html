What is Gluten and Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergy, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues. Symptoms can range from digestive problems and fatigue to more severe complications like nutrient deficiencies and intestinal damage. As a result, many individuals are opting for gluten-free diets to manage their health and well-being.

UTZ recognizes the importance of providing safe and delicious options for those who need to avoid gluten. Their gluten-free snacks are carefully crafted to ensure they meet the dietary needs of their consumers while maintaining the great taste that UTZ is known for.
