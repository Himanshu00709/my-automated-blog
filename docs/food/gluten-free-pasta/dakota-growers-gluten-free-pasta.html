How to Cook Dakota Growers Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking Dakota Growers Gluten-Free Pasta is simple and straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to ensure you get the best results:

  1. Boil Water: Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor of the pasta.
  2. Add Pasta: Gently add the pasta to the boiling water. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook: Follow the cooking time indicated on the package, usually around 7-9 minutes. Taste the pasta to ensure it’s cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  4. Drain: Once cooked, drain the pasta in a colander. Avoid rinsing the pasta, as this can remove the starch that helps sauces adhere to it.
  5. Serve: Toss the pasta with your favorite sauce or toppings and enjoy!
