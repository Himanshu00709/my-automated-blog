How to Cook Farabella Gluten-Free Pasta

Cooking Farabella Gluten-Free Pasta is simple and straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to ensure you get the perfect texture every time:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the Farabella Gluten-Free Pasta and stir gently to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente. Be sure to check the package instructions for specific cooking times.
  4. Drain the pasta and rinse briefly with cold water to stop the cooking process.
  5. Toss with your favorite sauce and serve immediately.
