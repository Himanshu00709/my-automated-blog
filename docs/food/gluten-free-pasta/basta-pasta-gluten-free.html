How to Cook Basta Pasta Gluten-Free

Cooking Basta Pasta Gluten-Free is just as easy as cooking traditional pasta. Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Boil Water: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Use about 4-6 quarts of water for every 8 ounces of pasta.
  2. Add Pasta: Once the water is boiling, add the Basta Pasta Gluten-Free. Stir gently to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook: Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente. Be sure to check the package instructions for specific cooking times.
  4. Drain: Drain the pasta in a colander, but do not rinse. Rinsing can remove the starch that helps sauces adhere to the pasta.
  5. Serve: Toss the pasta with your favorite sauce and toppings, and enjoy!

One tip to keep in mind is that gluten-free pasta can sometimes cook faster than traditional pasta, so it's important to keep an eye on it to avoid overcooking. Overcooked gluten-free pasta can become mushy, so aim for that perfect al dente texture.
