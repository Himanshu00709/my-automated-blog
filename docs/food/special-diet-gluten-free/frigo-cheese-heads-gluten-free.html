What Are Frigo Cheese Heads?

Frigo Cheese Heads are a brand of string cheese that has been a favorite among cheese lovers for decades. Are Frigo Cheese Heads Gluten-Free?

Yes, Frigo Cheese Heads are gluten-free. The primary ingredient in Frigo Cheese Heads is pasteurized part-skim milk, which is naturally gluten-free. Additionally, the cheese-making process does not involve any gluten-containing ingredients, ensuring that the final product is safe for those who need to avoid gluten. However, it's always a good idea to check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings, especially if you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease.
