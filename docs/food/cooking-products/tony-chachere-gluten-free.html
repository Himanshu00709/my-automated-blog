The Legacy of Tony Chachere's

Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning was first introduced in 1972 by Tony Chachere himself, a Louisiana native with a passion for cooking. His blend of spices quickly became a favorite among home cooks and professional chefs alike. The original seasoning is a mix of salt, red pepper, garlic, and other spices, creating a versatile blend that can be used on everything from meats to vegetables.

Over the years, the brand has expanded its product line to include a variety of seasonings, marinades, and even rice mixes. However, the gluten-free version of their classic Creole Seasoning has become particularly popular among those with dietary restrictions. This product maintains the same great taste while ensuring it’s safe for those who need to avoid gluten.
