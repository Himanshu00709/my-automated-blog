Understanding Gluten-Free Certification

When it comes to gluten-free products, certification is key. Tillamook ensures that their gluten-free ice cream flavors meet strict standards to avoid cross-contamination. They use dedicated equipment and facilities to produce these flavors, giving consumers peace of mind. Always look for the gluten-free label on the packaging to ensure the product meets your dietary needs.

Tips for Enjoying Gluten-Free Ice Cream

Here are some tips to make the most of your gluten-free Tillamook ice cream experience:

  • Check Labels: Always read the ingredient list and look for the gluten-free certification on the packaging.
  • Store Properly: Keep your ice cream in the coldest part of your freezer to maintain its texture and flavor.
  • Pair with Gluten-Free Toppings: Enhance your ice cream with gluten-free toppings like fresh fruit, nuts, or gluten-free cookies.
  • Experiment with Recipes: Use Tillamook gluten-free ice cream as a base for milkshakes, sundaes, or even ice cream sandwiches using gluten-free cookies.
