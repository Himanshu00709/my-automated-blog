Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a host of health issues, including digestive problems, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. This is why gluten-free options are not just a trend but a necessity for many. Big Apple Bagels recognizes this need and has made significant strides in offering gluten-free bagels that don’t compromise on taste or texture.

FAQs About Big Apple Bagels Gluten-Free Options

1. Are Big Apple Bagels’ gluten-free bagels certified gluten-free?

Yes, Big Apple Bagels’ gluten-free bagels are certified gluten-free, meaning they meet strict standards for gluten content and are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

2. Can I get gluten-free bagels at any Big Apple Bagels location?

Most Big Apple Bagels locations offer gluten-free bagels, but it’s always a good idea to call ahead and confirm availability at your local store.

3. Are there any cross-contamination risks?

Big Apple Bagels takes cross-contamination seriously and follows strict protocols to minimize the risk. However, if you have severe gluten sensitivity, it’s always best to inform the staff so they can take extra precautions.

4. What are the most popular gluten-free bagel flavors?

The plain and cinnamon raisin gluten-free bagels are among the most popular, but the everything and blueberry flavors also have a strong following.

5. Can I order gluten-free bagels online?

Yes, many Big Apple Bagels locations offer online ordering, allowing you to customize your gluten-free bagel and have it ready for pickup.
