Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it’s crucial for these individuals to carefully scrutinize the ingredients of any food product they consume.

Cross-Contamination Risks

While the ingredients themselves are gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during the manufacturing process. This happens when gluten-free products come into contact with gluten-containing ingredients or equipment. For individuals with celiac disease, even trace amounts of gluten can cause a reaction.

If you’re highly sensitive to gluten, it’s advisable to look for candies that are certified gluten-free. These products have undergone rigorous testing to ensure they meet strict gluten-free standards.
