What Makes Halo Top Gluten-Free?

Halo Top's gluten-free ice cream is crafted with care to ensure that it meets the dietary needs of those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. The brand uses high-quality ingredients that are naturally gluten-free, such as milk, cream, and sugar, and avoids any additives or flavorings that contain gluten. Additionally, Halo Top's production facilities are designed to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that their gluten-free products remain safe for consumption.
