The Importance of Gluten-Free Options

Gluten-free diets have become increasingly popular over the years, not just among those with celiac disease but also among individuals who are sensitive to gluten or simply choose to avoid it for health reasons. The Capital Grille recognizes the importance of catering to these dietary needs and has curated a selection of desserts that are both gluten-free and delicious. This commitment to inclusivity ensures that everyone can enjoy a sweet ending to their meal, regardless of their dietary restrictions.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Desserts at The Capital Grille?

The Capital Grille’s gluten-free desserts are not just about meeting dietary needs; they are about delivering an exceptional culinary experience. FAQs About Capital Grille Gluten-Free Desserts

1. Are all gluten-free desserts at The Capital Grille also dairy-free?

Not all gluten-free desserts at The Capital Grille are dairy-free. While some options, like the Seasonal Sorbet, are free from dairy, others, such as the Flourless Chocolate Espresso Cake and Cheesecake with Fresh Berries, contain dairy. It’s always best to inform your server of any dietary restrictions so they can guide you to the most suitable options.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free dessert?

While The Capital Grille offers a curated selection of gluten-free desserts, customization options may be limited due to the complexity of preparing gluten-free items. However, the staff is always willing to accommodate special requests whenever possible, so don’t hesitate to ask.

3. How does The Capital Grille ensure there is no cross-contamination?

The Capital Grille takes cross-contamination very seriously. The kitchen staff follows strict protocols to prevent cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients. Separate utensils, cookware, and preparation areas are used for gluten-free items to ensure the safety of guests with gluten intolerance.

4. Are the gluten-free desserts available at all locations?

Most Capital Grille locations offer a selection of gluten-free desserts, but the specific options may vary depending on the location and seasonal availability. It’s recommended to check with your local restaurant or view their online menu for the most up-to-date information.
