How to Bake Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookie Dough

Baking Pillsbury Gluten-Free Cookie Dough is as simple as it gets. Here’s a step-by-step guide to ensure your cookies come out perfectly every time:

  1. Preheat Your Oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C) and allow it to preheat while you prepare the dough.
  2. Prepare the Dough: Remove the dough from the packaging and place the pre-portioned pieces onto a baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  3. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  4. Cool: Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  5. Enjoy: Once cooled, your gluten-free cookies are ready to be enjoyed!
