How to Use Schar Puff Pastry Dough

One of the best things about Schar Puff Pastry Dough is its ease of use. Here are some tips and ideas for incorporating it into your recipes:

1. Sweet Treats

Use the dough to make gluten-free versions of classic pastries like croissants, danishes, or fruit tarts. Simply roll out the dough, add your favorite fillings, and bake according to the recipe instructions.

2. Savory Dishes

Schar Puff Pastry Dough is also perfect for savory dishes. Try making gluten-free quiches, sausage rolls, or cheese straws. The dough's light and flaky texture pairs well with a variety of savory ingredients.

3. Custom Creations

Don't be afraid to get creative! Use the dough as a base for gluten-free pizza, or cut it into shapes for appetizers. The possibilities are endless.

FAQs About Schar Puff Pastry Dough

Is Schar Puff Pastry Dough suitable for vegans?

No, Schar Puff Pastry Dough contains butter, making it unsuitable for a vegan diet. However, it is gluten-free and suitable for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Can I freeze Schar Puff Pastry Dough?

Yes, Schar Puff Pastry Dough can be frozen. Simply wrap it tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and store it in the freezer for up to three months. Thaw in the refrigerator before use.

Where can I buy Schar Puff Pastry Dough?

Schar products are widely available in grocery stores, health food stores, and online retailers. Check the Schar website for a store locator or to purchase directly from their online shop.

Does Schar Puff Pastry Dough contain any allergens?

In addition to containing dairy (butter), Schar Puff Pastry Dough is processed in a facility that handles nuts and soy. Always check the packaging for the most up-to-date allergen information.
