What Are Candy Cane Kisses?

Candy Cane Kisses are a seasonal variation of the classic Hershey’s Kisses. These festive candies are shaped like the traditional Kisses but are infused with the refreshing flavor of peppermint and adorned with red and white stripes, reminiscent of candy canes. They are a popular holiday treat, often used in baking, as toppings, or simply enjoyed on their own.

What makes Candy Cane Kisses particularly special is that they are gluten-free. This means that individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity can indulge in these sweet treats without worry. Hershey’s has confirmed that Candy Cane Kisses do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients, making them a safe option for those on a gluten-free diet.

FAQs About Candy Cane Kisses

Are Candy Cane Kisses really gluten-free?

Yes, Candy Cane Kisses are gluten-free. Hershey’s has confirmed that they do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients, making them safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Can I use Candy Cane Kisses in baking?

Absolutely! Candy Cane Kisses are a popular choice for holiday baking. They can be used in cookies, brownies, and other desserts to add a festive touch.

How should I store Candy Cane Kisses?

Candy Cane Kisses should be stored in a cool, dry place. The resealable bag helps maintain freshness, but you can also transfer them to an airtight container for longer storage.

Are there any allergens in Candy Cane Kisses?

Candy Cane Kisses contain milk and soy, which are common allergens. They are also manufactured in facilities that process tree nuts, so cross-contamination is possible.
