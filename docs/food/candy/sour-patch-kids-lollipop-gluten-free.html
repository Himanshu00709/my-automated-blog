What Makes Sour Patch Kids Lollipops Gluten-Free?

Sour Patch Kids Lollipops are made with a careful selection of ingredients that do not contain gluten. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The manufacturers of Sour Patch Kids have ensured that their lollipops are free from these grains, making them safe for gluten-free diets.
