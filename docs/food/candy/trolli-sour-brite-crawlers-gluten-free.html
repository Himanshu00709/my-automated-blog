What Are Trolli Sour Brite Crawlers?

Are Trolli Sour Brite Crawlers Gluten-Free?

Yes, Trolli Sour Brite Crawlers are gluten-free. The ingredients listed above do not include any gluten-containing substances such as wheat, barley, or rye. Additionally, Trolli has confirmed that their Sour Brite Crawlers are produced in a facility that takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. This makes them a safe choice for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.
