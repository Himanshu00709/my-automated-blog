What Makes Jolly Rancher Jelly Beans Gluten-Free?

Jolly Rancher Jelly Beans are made without any gluten-containing ingredients, which is great news for those who need to avoid gluten. The primary ingredients in these jelly beans include sugar, corn syrup, and modified food starch, none of which contain gluten. Additionally, the manufacturing process for Jolly Rancher Jelly Beans is designed to prevent cross-contamination with gluten-containing products, ensuring that the final product is safe for those with gluten sensitivities.

It’s important to note that while Jolly Rancher Jelly Beans are gluten-free, they are produced in facilities that also handle other products that may contain gluten. However, the company takes extensive measures to prevent cross-contamination, making these jelly beans a reliable choice for those on a gluten-free diet.
