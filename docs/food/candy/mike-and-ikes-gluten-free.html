Mike and Ikes Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding delicious and safe snacks can be a challenge. Mike and Ikes, the beloved chewy fruit candies, have been a favorite for generations. But are they gluten-free? In this article, we’ll dive deep into the gluten-free status of Mike and Ikes, explore their ingredients, and provide you with all the information you need to enjoy these treats without worry.

What Are Mike and Ikes?

Mike and Ikes are chewy, fruit-flavored candies that have been around since 1940. Produced by Just Born, the same company that makes Peeps and Hot Tamales, these candies come in a variety of flavors and are known for their vibrant colors and sweet taste. They are a popular choice for moviegoers, party favors, and everyday snacking.

Are Mike and Ikes Gluten-Free?

The good news is that Mike and Ikes are generally considered gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Just Born, Mike and Ikes do not contain any gluten ingredients. However, it’s important to note that they are not certified gluten-free, which means there could be a risk of cross-contamination during the manufacturing process.

For those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease, it’s always best to check the packaging for any allergen warnings or contact the manufacturer directly for the most up-to-date information.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While the ingredients in Mike and Ikes are gluten-free, cross-contamination is always a potential risk in facilities that process gluten-containing products. Just Born does not specifically label Mike and Ikes as gluten-free, which means there could be a risk of cross-contamination during manufacturing.
