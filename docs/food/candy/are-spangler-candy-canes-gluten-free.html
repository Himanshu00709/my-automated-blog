Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For people with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to long-term health complications. Therefore, it’s crucial to scrutinize food labels and understand the manufacturing practices of food companies.
