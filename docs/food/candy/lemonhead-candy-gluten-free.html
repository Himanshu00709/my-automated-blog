What Makes Lemonhead Candy Gluten-Free?

Lemonhead candies are made with simple ingredients that are naturally free from gluten. The primary ingredients include sugar, corn syrup, citric acid, and natural flavors. These ingredients are carefully selected to ensure that the final product is safe for those with gluten intolerance. Additionally, the manufacturing process for Lemonhead candies is designed to prevent cross-contamination with gluten-containing products, further ensuring their safety.

It's important to note that while Lemonhead candies are gluten-free, they are produced in facilities that may also process other products containing gluten. However, the company takes strict measures to avoid cross-contamination, making Lemonhead candies a reliable option for those with gluten sensitivities.
