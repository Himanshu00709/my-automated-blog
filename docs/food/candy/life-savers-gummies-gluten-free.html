What Makes Life Savers Gummies Gluten-Free?

Life Savers Gummies are made without any gluten-containing ingredients, which means they are safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. The primary ingredients in these gummies include sugar, corn syrup, gelatin, and natural flavors. None of these ingredients contain gluten, ensuring that the gummies are free from this common allergen.

It’s important to note that while Life Savers Gummies are gluten-free, they are produced in facilities that may also process gluten-containing products. However, the manufacturer takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, making them a reliable choice for those avoiding gluten.
