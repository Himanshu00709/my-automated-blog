Lifesaver Mints: A Gluten-Free Delight

For those who love mints but need to adhere to a gluten-free diet, Lifesaver mints have become a go-to choice. These iconic, ring-shaped candies are not only refreshing but also safe for individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. What Makes Lifesaver Mints Gluten-Free?

Lifesaver mints are naturally gluten-free, meaning they do not contain any gluten-containing ingredients such as wheat, barley, or rye. The primary ingredients in Lifesaver mints include sugar, corn syrup, natural and artificial flavors, and coloring agents. These ingredients are carefully selected to ensure they meet the standards for gluten-free products.

Additionally, Lifesaver mints are manufactured in facilities that take precautions to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing products. This makes them a safe choice for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.
