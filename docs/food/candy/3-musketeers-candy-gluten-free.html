What Makes 3 Musketeers Candy Gluten-Free?

3 Musketeers candy bars are known for their light, fluffy nougat center covered in rich milk chocolate. The primary ingredients in 3 Musketeers include sugar, corn syrup, hydrogenated palm kernel oil, cocoa powder, and skim milk. Importantly, none of these ingredients contain gluten, making the candy a safe option for those avoiding gluten.

However, it’s always essential to check the packaging for any potential cross-contamination warnings, as manufacturing processes can vary. Mars, the company behind 3 Musketeers, has stated that they take precautions to prevent cross-contamination, but it’s always best to verify the label for the most accurate information.
