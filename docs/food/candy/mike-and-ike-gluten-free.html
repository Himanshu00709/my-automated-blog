Are Mike and Ike Candies Gluten-Free?

Yes, Mike and Ike candies are gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Just Born, Inc., Mike and Ike products do not contain gluten ingredients. This makes them a safe option for individuals who need to avoid gluten due to celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. However, it’s always important to check the packaging for any updates or changes in ingredients, as formulations can change over time.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even small amounts of gluten can cause symptoms such as bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. For those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, gluten can cause similar symptoms, though without the intestinal damage. Therefore, it’s crucial for these individuals to carefully select foods that are certified gluten-free.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While Mike and Ike candies do not contain gluten ingredients, there is always a risk of cross-contamination during the manufacturing process. Cross-contamination can occur if gluten-containing products are processed on the same equipment as gluten-free products. Just Born, Inc. states that they have strict cleaning procedures in place to prevent cross-contamination. However, if you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s advisable to contact the manufacturer directly for the most current information on their production practices.
