Why Choose Gluten-Free Candy Corn?

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding safe and delicious treats can be a challenge. Many candies contain hidden sources of gluten, such as malt flavoring or cross-contamination during manufacturing. Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn is specifically formulated to avoid these issues, making it a safe choice for anyone avoiding gluten. Additionally, it’s a great option for those who are simply looking to reduce their gluten intake without sacrificing flavor.

FAQs About Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn

Is Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn certified gluten-free?

While Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn is made without gluten-containing ingredients, it is not certified gluten-free. However, it is manufactured in a facility that takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

Can I find Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn year-round?

Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn is primarily available during the Halloween season. However, some retailers may carry it year-round, so it’s worth checking your local stores or online retailers.

Is Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn suitable for vegans?

Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn contains confectioner’s glaze, which is derived from shellac, a resin secreted by the lac bug. As such, it is not considered vegan-friendly.

How should I store Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn?

To maintain its freshness and texture, store Brach's Gluten-Free Candy Corn in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposure to heat or moisture, as this can cause the candy to become sticky or lose its shape.
