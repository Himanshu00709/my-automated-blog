What Makes Jolly Rancher Gummies Gluten-Free?

Jolly Rancher gummies are made without any gluten-containing ingredients. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The ingredients used in Jolly Rancher gummies, such as sugar, corn syrup, and gelatin, are naturally gluten-free. Additionally, the manufacturing process ensures that there is no cross-contamination with gluten-containing products, making these gummies a safe choice for those who need to avoid gluten.
