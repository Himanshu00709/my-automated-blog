When it comes to salad dressings, Ken's Steak House is a name that resonates with quality and flavor. What Makes Ken's Caesar Dressing Gluten-Free?

Ken's Caesar Dressing Gluten-Free is crafted with the same attention to flavor and quality as their traditional Caesar dressing, but without any gluten-containing ingredients. This makes it a safe and delicious option for those who need to avoid gluten. The dressing is made with a blend of high-quality ingredients, including:

  • Canola Oil
  • Water
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg Yolks
  • Distilled Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Anchovies
  • Spices

These ingredients are carefully selected to ensure that the dressing is not only gluten-free but also rich in flavor. The absence of gluten-containing grains like wheat, barley, or rye makes this dressing a safe choice for those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.
