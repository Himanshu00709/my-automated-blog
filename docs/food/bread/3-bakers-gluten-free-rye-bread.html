What is 3 Bakers Gluten-Free Rye Bread?

3 Bakers Gluten-Free Rye Bread is a specially formulated bread designed for individuals who need to avoid gluten due to celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or personal dietary preferences. Unlike traditional rye bread, which contains gluten, this version uses a blend of gluten-free flours and ingredients to mimic the taste and texture of rye bread without compromising on dietary needs.

Ingredients and Nutritional Profile

The ingredients in 3 Bakers Gluten-Free Rye Bread are carefully selected to ensure they are free from gluten while providing a rich, satisfying flavor. The primary ingredients include:

  • Water: The base of the bread, providing moisture.
  • Brown Rice Flour: A gluten-free flour that adds structure and a mild, nutty flavor.
  • Tapioca Starch: Helps to create a light and airy texture.
  • Potato Starch: Adds softness and helps retain moisture.
  • Flaxseed: Provides fiber and a slight nutty flavor.
  • Yeast: For leavening and that classic bread aroma.
  • Molasses: Adds a touch of sweetness and depth of flavor.
  • Caraway Seeds: The hallmark of rye bread, providing that distinctive taste.
