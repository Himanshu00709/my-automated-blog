What is Gluten-Free Bread?

Gluten-free bread is a type of bread made without gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. This makes it suitable for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those who choose to follow a gluten-free diet for other health reasons. Gluten-free bread is typically made from alternative flours such as rice flour, almond flour, or tapioca starch, which provide a similar texture and taste to traditional bread.
