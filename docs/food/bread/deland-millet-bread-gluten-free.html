What is Deland Millet Bread?

Deland Millet Bread is a type of bread made primarily from millet flour, which is naturally gluten-free. Millet is a small-seeded grass that has been cultivated for thousands of years and is a staple food in many parts of the world. The bread is known for its slightly nutty flavor and dense texture, making it a satisfying alternative to traditional wheat bread. Deland Millet Bread is often enriched with other gluten-free flours and ingredients to enhance its texture and nutritional value.
