FAQs About Freddy's Gluten-Free Bun

1. Is Freddy's gluten-free bun safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Freddy's gluten-free bun is made with gluten-free ingredients and is safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen, so it’s important to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure proper handling.

2. Can I order any menu item with a gluten-free bun?

Most of Freddy's burger and hot dog menu items can be customized with a gluten-free bun. However, some items may contain gluten in other components, so it’s best to check with the staff or consult the allergen menu before ordering.

3. Does the gluten-free bun cost extra?

Yes, there is typically an additional charge for substituting a regular bun with a gluten-free bun. The exact cost may vary by location, so it’s a good idea to check with your local Freddy's for pricing details.

4. How should I store leftover gluten-free buns?

If you have leftover gluten-free buns, it’s best to store them in an airtight container or resealable plastic bag at room temperature. They can also be frozen for longer storage. To reheat, simply toast the bun in a toaster or oven until warm.
