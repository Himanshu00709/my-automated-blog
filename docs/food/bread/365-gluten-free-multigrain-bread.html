Ingredients and Allergens

The ingredient list of 365 Gluten-Free Multigrain Bread is another aspect that sets it apart from other gluten-free bread options. FAQs

Is 365 Gluten-Free Multigrain Bread suitable for vegans?

Yes, 365 Gluten-Free Multigrain Bread is vegan-friendly as it does not contain any animal-derived ingredients.

How should I store 365 Gluten-Free Multigrain Bread?

To maintain freshness, it is recommended to store the bread in a cool, dry place. Once opened, you can keep it in the refrigerator to extend its shelf life.

Can I freeze 365 Gluten-Free Multigrain Bread?

Yes, you can freeze the bread to keep it fresh for a longer period. Simply slice it before freezing, and you can toast individual slices directly from the freezer.

Is 365 Gluten-Free Multigrain Bread certified gluten-free?

Yes, this bread is certified gluten-free, meaning it has been tested and confirmed to contain less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, which is the standard for gluten-free certification.
