What is Hilltop Gluten-Free Bread?

Hilltop Gluten-Free Bread is a specially crafted bread designed for individuals who need or choose to avoid gluten. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Hilltop's bread is made from a blend of gluten-free flours, such as rice flour, almond flour, and tapioca starch, ensuring that it is safe for those with gluten-related disorders.

What sets Hilltop apart is its commitment to quality and taste. Unlike many gluten-free breads that can be dry or crumbly, Hilltop Gluten-Free Bread is moist, flavorful, and has a texture that closely resembles traditional bread. This makes it an excellent option for sandwiches, toast, or even as a side to your favorite meals.
