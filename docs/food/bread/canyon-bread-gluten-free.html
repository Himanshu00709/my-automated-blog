What is Canyon Bakehouse Gluten-Free Bread?

Canyon Bakehouse is a brand dedicated to producing gluten-free baked goods that are not only safe for those with gluten sensitivities but also delicious and satisfying. Founded in 2009, the company has grown to become one of the leading names in the gluten-free market. Their bread is made with whole grains, free from artificial preservatives, and certified gluten-free by the Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO).

What sets Canyon Bakehouse apart is their commitment to creating bread that mimics the taste and texture of traditional wheat bread. This is no small feat, as many gluten-free breads are often dry, crumbly, or lack the satisfying chewiness of regular bread. Canyon Bakehouse achieves this by using a blend of gluten-free flours, including brown rice flour, millet, and sorghum, along with other natural ingredients.
