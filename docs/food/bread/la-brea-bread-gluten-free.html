The Rise of Gluten-Free Products

The gluten-free movement has gained significant momentum over the past decade. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, the need for gluten-free alternatives has become more pressing, and food manufacturers have responded by developing a wide array of gluten-free products.

La Brea Bakery, a brand synonymous with high-quality artisanal bread, recognized this need and expanded its product line to include gluten-free options. By leveraging its expertise in baking, La Brea Bakery has managed to create gluten-free bread that not only meets dietary requirements but also delivers on taste and texture, which are often compromised in gluten-free products.
