What is Bakehouse Gluten-Free Bread?

Bakehouse Gluten-Free Bread is a specially crafted bread designed for individuals who need or choose to avoid gluten in their diet. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergy. Bakehouse Gluten-Free Bread is made with alternative flours and ingredients that do not contain gluten, making it a safe and tasty option for those with dietary restrictions.
