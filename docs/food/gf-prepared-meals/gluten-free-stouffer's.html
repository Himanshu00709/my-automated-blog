Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For people with celiac disease, consuming gluten triggers an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even for those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, gluten can cause symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health.

Stouffer's has recognized the growing demand for gluten-free options and has introduced a range of products that are safe for those avoiding gluten. These products are not only convenient but also maintain the brand's reputation for hearty, satisfying meals.
