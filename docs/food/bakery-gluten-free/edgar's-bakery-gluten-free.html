The Rise of Gluten-Free Baking

Gluten-free baking is not just a trend; it's a necessity for millions of people worldwide. Celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten, affects approximately 1% of the global population. Additionally, many individuals experience non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can cause similar symptoms without the autoimmune response. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, making gluten-free options essential.

Edgar's Bakery recognized this need early on and has since become a pioneer in the gluten-free baking industry. Their dedication to quality, taste, and safety has set them apart from other bakeries, making them a trusted name in the gluten-free community.
