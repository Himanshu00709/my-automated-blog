FAQs About Carlo's Bakery Gluten-Free Products

1. Are Carlo's Bakery gluten-free products safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Carlo's Bakery takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free products are safe for individuals with celiac disease. They use separate equipment and dedicated preparation areas to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Do Carlo's Bakery gluten-free products taste different from their regular products?

While there may be slight differences in texture due to the absence of gluten, Carlo's Bakery has worked hard to ensure that their gluten-free products are just as delicious as their regular offerings. Many customers can't even tell the difference!

3. Can I order gluten-free cakes for special occasions?

Absolutely! Carlo's Bakery offers custom gluten-free cakes for birthdays, weddings, and other special occasions. You can choose from a variety of flavors and designs to create the perfect cake for your event.

4. Are Carlo's Bakery gluten-free products more expensive?

Gluten-free products can sometimes be more expensive due to the cost of specialty ingredients. However, Carlo's Bakery strives to keep their prices competitive while maintaining the highest quality standards.
