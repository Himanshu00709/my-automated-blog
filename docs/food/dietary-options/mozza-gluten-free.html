What is Mozza Gluten-Free?

Mozza Gluten-Free is a type of cheese that is specifically crafted to be free from gluten, making it a safe and enjoyable option for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those who simply prefer a gluten-free diet. Mozzarella, traditionally made from cow's milk, is naturally gluten-free. However, Mozza Gluten-Free ensures that no cross-contamination occurs during the production process, providing peace of mind for consumers.
