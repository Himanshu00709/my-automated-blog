Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine, leading to a host of health issues. Even for those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can cause discomfort, bloating, and other digestive problems. As awareness of these conditions has grown, so has the demand for gluten-free options in restaurants and food establishments.

DP Dough has recognized this need and has taken steps to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are not only safe but also delicious. Their gluten-free calzones are made with high-quality ingredients and are prepared with care to avoid cross-contamination, making them a reliable choice for those who need to avoid gluten.

How DP Dough Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity is cross-contamination. Even a small amount of gluten can cause a reaction, so it’s crucial that gluten-free foods are prepared in a separate area with dedicated utensils and equipment.

DP Dough takes this issue seriously. They have implemented strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchens. This includes using separate preparation areas, utensils, and cooking surfaces for gluten-free orders. Additionally, staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that your meal is safe to eat.

Here’s a breakdown of the steps DP Dough takes to ensure gluten-free safety:

Step Description
Separate Preparation Area Gluten-free orders are prepared in a designated area to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
Dedicated Utensils Special utensils are used exclusively for gluten-free orders to prevent cross-contamination.
Staff Training Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free safety and how to handle gluten-free orders properly.
Clear Labeling Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, and staff are knowledgeable about which items are safe for gluten-sensitive customers.
