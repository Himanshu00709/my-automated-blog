What is Gluten-Free Bread?

Gluten-free bread is made without gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Gluten-free bread is crafted using alternative flours such as rice flour, almond flour, or tapioca starch, which do not contain gluten.

Can I freeze Jason's Deli gluten-free bread?
