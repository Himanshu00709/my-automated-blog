What Makes Champs Chicken Gluten-Free?

Champs Chicken has taken significant steps to ensure that their gluten-free options are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. How Champs Chicken Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a major concern. Champs Chicken has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe:

  • Dedicated Fryers: Champs Chicken uses separate fryers for their gluten-free items, preventing any cross-contamination with gluten-containing foods.
  • Gluten-Free Ingredients: All gluten-free menu items are made with certified gluten-free ingredients, ensuring that they meet strict safety standards.
  • Staff Training: Champs Chicken staff are trained in gluten-free preparation techniques, reducing the risk of accidental contamination.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.
