Why Choose Gluten-Free Bread?

Gluten-free bread is essential for individuals with celiac disease, a condition where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. However, gluten-free diets have also gained popularity among those without celiac disease, as many people report feeling better when they eliminate gluten from their diets. Gluten-free bread is made without wheat, barley, or rye, which are the primary sources of gluten. Instead, these breads are typically made from alternative flours such as rice, almond, coconut, or tapioca flour. Harvest Bread Company has mastered the art of creating gluten-free bread that not only meets dietary needs but also satisfies the taste buds.
