The Rise of Gluten-Free Diets

Gluten-free diets have become more than just a trend; they are now a necessity for millions of people worldwide. Celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten, affects approximately 1% of the global population. Additionally, many individuals suffer from non-celiac gluten sensitivity, which can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. For these individuals, avoiding gluten is not a choice but a requirement for maintaining their health.

Great Harvest Bread recognized this growing need and responded by developing a line of gluten-free bread that caters to those with dietary restrictions. But what sets Great Harvest apart from other gluten-free brands? Let’s delve deeper into their offerings.
