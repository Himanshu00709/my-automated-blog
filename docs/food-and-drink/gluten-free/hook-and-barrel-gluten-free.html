Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Hook and Barrel

For those new to gluten-free dining, it's important to understand what it entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. At Hook and Barrel, the kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that cross-contamination is minimized.

Cross-Contamination Prevention

Cross-contamination is a significant concern for those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance. Hook and Barrel takes this issue seriously, implementing strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination. These include:

  • Using separate cooking utensils and equipment for gluten-free dishes.
  • Designating specific areas of the kitchen for gluten-free meal preparation.
  • Training staff on the importance of avoiding cross-contamination and how to handle gluten-free orders safely.
