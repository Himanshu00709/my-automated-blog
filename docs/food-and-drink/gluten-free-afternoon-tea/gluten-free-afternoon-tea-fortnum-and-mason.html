Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea at Fortnum & Mason: A Culinary Delight

Afternoon tea is a quintessentially British tradition, and Fortnum & Mason is one of the most iconic establishments to experience it. Known for its luxurious setting and exquisite offerings, Fortnum & Mason has long been a destination for those seeking a refined tea experience. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the prospect of enjoying afternoon tea can be daunting. However, Fortnum & Mason has risen to the occasion, offering a gluten-free afternoon tea that is every bit as delightful as its traditional counterpart. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free afternoon tea experience at Fortnum & Mason, from the menu to the ambiance, and why it’s a must-try for anyone looking to indulge in a safe and sumptuous treat.

The Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea Menu

Fortnum & Mason’s gluten-free afternoon tea menu is a carefully curated selection of savory and sweet treats, all crafted to meet the needs of gluten-sensitive guests. The menu is designed to ensure that no one misses out on the full afternoon tea experience, with a variety of options that cater to different tastes and dietary requirements.

Savory Selection

The savory selection includes a range of finger sandwiches, all made with gluten-free bread. The fillings are as luxurious as you’d expect from Fortnum & Mason, with options such as smoked salmon with cream cheese, cucumber with mint butter, and coronation chicken. Each sandwich is carefully prepared to ensure that it is free from cross-contamination, giving guests peace of mind as they enjoy their meal.

Sweet Treats

The sweet treats are the highlight of any afternoon tea, and Fortnum & Mason’s gluten-free offerings do not disappoint. The selection includes a variety of pastries, cakes, and scones, all made with gluten-free flour. The scones are served with clotted cream and strawberry jam, and are a particular favorite among guests. Other sweet treats include macarons, chocolate éclairs, and fruit tarts, all of which are beautifully presented and deliciously indulgent.

Tea Selection

No afternoon tea would be complete without a selection of fine teas, and Fortnum & Mason offers an extensive range to choose from. Whether you prefer a classic English Breakfast tea, a fragrant Earl Grey, or something more exotic like a white tea or herbal infusion, there’s something to suit every palate. The teas are served in elegant china teapots, adding to the overall sense of occasion.

The Ambiance

Fortnum & Mason’s Diamond Jubilee Tea Salon is the perfect setting for a gluten-free afternoon tea. The salon is light and airy, with large windows that let in plenty of natural light. The decor is elegant and understated, with a color palette of soft pastels and gold accents. The atmosphere is relaxed yet refined, making it the ideal place to unwind and enjoy a leisurely afternoon tea.

The service at Fortnum & Mason is second to none, with attentive staff who are knowledgeable about the gluten-free menu and happy to accommodate any special requests. The attention to detail is evident in every aspect of the experience, from the presentation of the food to the way the tea is served.

Why Choose Fortnum & Mason for Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea?

There are several reasons why Fortnum & Mason stands out as a top choice for gluten-free afternoon tea:

  • Quality Ingredients: Fortnum & Mason is known for its commitment to quality, and this is evident in the ingredients used in their gluten-free menu. Everything is made with the finest ingredients, ensuring a delicious and safe dining experience.
  • Attention to Detail: The staff at Fortnum & Mason take great care to ensure that the gluten-free menu is prepared and served with the utmost attention to detail, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Variety: The gluten-free menu offers a wide variety of options, ensuring that there’s something for everyone to enjoy.
  • Ambiance: The Diamond Jubilee Tea Salon provides a luxurious and relaxing setting for afternoon tea, making it a truly special experience.

FAQs

Is the gluten-free afternoon tea menu available every day?

Yes, the gluten-free afternoon tea menu is available daily at Fortnum & Mason. However, it’s always a good idea to book in advance to ensure availability.

Can I make special dietary requests?

Absolutely. Fortnum & Mason is happy to accommodate special dietary requests, including dairy-free or nut-free options. Be sure to inform the staff of any dietary restrictions when making your reservation.

Is the gluten-free menu suitable for celiacs?

Yes, the gluten-free menu is suitable for those with celiac disease. The kitchen takes great care to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always a good idea to inform the staff of your dietary needs when booking.

How much does the gluten-free afternoon tea cost?

The price of the gluten-free afternoon tea varies depending on the time of day and any additional extras you may choose. It’s best to check the current pricing on Fortnum & Mason’s website or by contacting them directly.

With a carefully curated menu, luxurious setting, and impeccable service, it’s an experience that everyone can enjoy, regardless of dietary restrictions. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a delightful afternoon, Fortnum & Mason’s gluten-free afternoon tea is a must-try. So, book your table, sit back, and savor every moment of this exquisite culinary journey.
