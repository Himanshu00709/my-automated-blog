Is Rao's Pizza Sauce Gluten-Free? A Detailed Guide

When it comes to gluten-free diets, every ingredient matters. For those who suffer from celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even the smallest trace of gluten can cause significant health issues. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to damage in the small intestine, resulting in a range of symptoms from digestive issues to nutrient deficiencies. Even for those with non-celiac gluten sensitivity, avoiding gluten is crucial to maintaining good health. Therefore, understanding whether a product like Rao's Pizza Sauce is gluten-free is essential for those on a gluten-free diet.

Rao's Pizza Sauce is produced in facilities that may also process gluten-containing products, which increases the risk of cross-contamination.
