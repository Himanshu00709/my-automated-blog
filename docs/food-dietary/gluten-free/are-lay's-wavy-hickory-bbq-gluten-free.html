When it comes to snacking, Lay's Wavy Hickory BBQ chips are a popular choice for many. Their bold flavor and satisfying crunch make them a go-to snack for parties, movie nights, or just a quick bite. Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues. Symptoms can range from digestive problems to skin rashes, fatigue, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, it’s essential for individuals with these conditions to carefully scrutinize the ingredients of their food products.

Manufacturing Processes and Cross-Contamination

Even if the ingredients are gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during the manufacturing process. This happens when gluten-free products come into contact with gluten-containing products or surfaces. Lay's, like many snack manufacturers, produces a variety of products, some of which may contain gluten. Therefore, it’s important to consider the manufacturing environment.

Lay's has stated that they take steps to minimize cross-contamination, but they do not guarantee that their products are entirely gluten-free. This means that while Lay's Wavy Hickory BBQ chips may not contain gluten ingredients, there is still a risk of trace amounts of gluten due to shared equipment or facilities.

Certifications and Labeling
