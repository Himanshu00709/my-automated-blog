Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into Newk's specific offerings, it's essential to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Therefore, finding restaurants that offer safe, gluten-free options is crucial for these individuals.

Cross-Contamination Prevention

One of the biggest concerns for individuals with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Newk's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchens. These measures include using separate preparation areas, utensils, and cooking surfaces for gluten-free items. Additionally, staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that your meal is safe to eat.
