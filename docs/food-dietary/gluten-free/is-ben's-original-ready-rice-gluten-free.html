Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it's crucial for these individuals to carefully scrutinize food labels and understand the gluten content of the products they consume.

Ingredients in Ben's Original Ready Rice

To determine whether Ben's Original Ready Rice is gluten-free, it's essential to examine the ingredients list. The primary ingredient in most varieties of Ben's Original Ready Rice is rice, which is naturally gluten-free. Manufacturing Processes and Cross-Contamination

Even if a product's ingredients are gluten-free, cross-contamination during manufacturing can pose a risk. Cross-contamination occurs when gluten-free products come into contact with gluten-containing products or surfaces during production. This can happen in facilities that process both gluten-free and gluten-containing foods.

Ben's Original Ready Rice is produced in facilities that may also process gluten-containing products. While the company takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, there is always a risk. Therefore, individuals with severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease should exercise caution and consider contacting the manufacturer for more detailed information.

Gluten-Free Certification
