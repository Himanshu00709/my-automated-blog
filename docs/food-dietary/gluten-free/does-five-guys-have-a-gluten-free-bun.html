Does Five Guys Have a Gluten-Free Bun? A Comprehensive Guide

Five Guys is a popular fast-food chain known for its customizable burgers, fries, and hot dogs. With the increasing demand for gluten-free options, many people wonder if Five Guys offers a gluten-free bun. This article will explore whether Five Guys has a gluten-free bun, the options available for those with gluten sensitivities, and how to navigate the menu safely.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. As a result, many people seek gluten-free alternatives when dining out.

Five Guys has recognized the growing need for gluten-free options and has made efforts to accommodate customers with dietary restrictions. However, it's essential to understand the limitations and potential risks associated with cross-contamination in a fast-food environment.

Does Five Guys Offer a Gluten-Free Bun?

As of the latest information available, Five Guys does not offer a gluten-free bun. The standard buns used for their burgers and hot dogs contain gluten. However, Five Guys does provide an alternative for those who need to avoid gluten: the option to order your burger or hot dog "protein style," which means it will be served in a lettuce wrap instead of a bun.

While this is not a traditional gluten-free bun, it is a viable option for those who need to avoid gluten. The lettuce wrap is naturally gluten-free and can be a refreshing and healthy alternative to a traditional bun.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Five Guys

If you have celiac disease or a severe gluten sensitivity, here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience at Five Guys:

  • Communicate Clearly: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can take extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Choose the Lettuce Wrap: Opt for the lettuce wrap instead of the standard bun to avoid gluten.
  • Be Cautious with Toppings: While most toppings are gluten-free, some sauces and condiments may contain gluten. Always ask for a list of ingredients if you're unsure.
  • Avoid Shared Fryers: If you're concerned about cross-contamination, it's best to avoid the fries, even though they are cooked in peanut oil.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys

1. Does Five Guys have a gluten-free bun?

No, Five Guys does not offer a gluten-free bun. However, you can order your burger or hot dog in a lettuce wrap, which is gluten-free.

2. Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free as they are cooked in peanut oil. However, there is a risk of cross-contamination if other gluten-containing items are cooked in the same fryer.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free at Five Guys?

Yes, you can customize your order by choosing a lettuce wrap and selecting gluten-free toppings. Always inform the staff of your dietary restrictions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.

4. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Five Guys milkshakes are generally gluten-free, but you should check the ingredients of any add-ins, such as cookies or brownies, which may contain gluten.

5. Is Five Guys a safe option for people with celiac disease?

While Five Guys offers some gluten-free options, the risk of cross-contamination is present. People with celiac disease should exercise caution and communicate their dietary needs clearly to the staff.

Conclusion

While Five Guys does not offer a gluten-free bun, they do provide a lettuce wrap option for those who need to avoid gluten. The fries are also gluten-free, but there is a risk of cross-contamination in the fryer. By communicating your dietary needs clearly and making informed choices, you can enjoy a meal at Five Guys while adhering to a gluten-free diet.
