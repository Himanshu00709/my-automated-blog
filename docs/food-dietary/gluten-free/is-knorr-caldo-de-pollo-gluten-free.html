Is Knorr Caldo de Pollo Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

Knorr Caldo de Pollo is a popular bouillon product used in many households to enhance the flavor of soups, stews, and other dishes. Understanding Gluten and Its Implications

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and triticale. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and long-term health complications. Therefore, it is essential to scrutinize food labels and understand the manufacturing processes of products to ensure they are gluten-free.

Manufacturing and Cross-Contamination Risks

Even if a product's ingredients are gluten-free, cross-contamination during manufacturing can pose a risk. Knorr, like many food manufacturers, produces a variety of products, some of which may contain gluten. Shared equipment or facilities can lead to trace amounts of gluten ending up in otherwise gluten-free products.

Knorr does not explicitly label their Caldo de Pollo as gluten-free, which suggests that there may be a risk of cross-contamination. For individuals with severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, this lack of a gluten-free certification is a red flag.

Knorr's Stance on Gluten-Free Products
