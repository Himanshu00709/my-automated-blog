Does Five Guys Have Gluten-Free Options? A Detailed Guide

Five Guys is a popular fast-food chain known for its delicious burgers, fries, and milkshakes. However, for individuals with gluten intolerance or celiac disease, dining out can be a challenge. The question many people ask is: Does Five Guys have gluten-free options? In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Five Guys, discuss cross-contamination risks, and provide tips for safely enjoying a meal at this beloved burger joint.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, including digestive issues, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many individuals must adhere to a strict gluten-free diet to maintain their health and well-being.

When dining out, it’s crucial to identify restaurants that offer gluten-free options and understand how they handle food preparation to avoid cross-contamination. Let’s dive into what Five Guys has to offer for gluten-free diners.

Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys

Five Guys is known for its simple menu, which primarily consists of burgers, hot dogs, fries, and milkshakes. While the restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free menu, there are several items that can be customized to fit a gluten-free diet. Here’s a breakdown of the gluten-free options available:

Burgers

Five Guys’ burgers are made with 100% fresh beef and are free from fillers, additives, and preservatives. The burgers themselves are gluten-free, but the buns are not. However, you can order your burger “lettuce-wrapped” or “in a bowl” to avoid the gluten-containing bun. This allows you to enjoy the juicy patty and your favorite toppings without worrying about gluten.

Hot Dogs

Similar to the burgers, Five Guys’ hot dogs are gluten-free, but the buns are not. You can request your hot dog to be served without the bun or wrapped in lettuce. Be sure to check with the staff to ensure that the hot dog itself is free from any gluten-containing ingredients.

Fries

Five Guys’ fries are a fan favorite, and the good news is that they are gluten-free! The fries are made from fresh potatoes and cooked in 100% peanut oil, which is naturally gluten-free. However, it’s important to note that the fries are cooked in the same oil as other items that may contain gluten, so there is a risk of cross-contamination.

Milkshakes

Five Guys’ milkshakes are made with real ice cream and are generally gluten-free. However, some mix-ins, such as Oreo cookies, contain gluten. Be sure to check the ingredients of any mix-ins before adding them to your milkshake.

Cross-Contamination Risks

While Five Guys offers several gluten-free options, it’s important to be aware of the risk of cross-contamination. Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing foods or surfaces. At Five Guys, the following factors may contribute to cross-contamination:

  • Shared Cooking Surfaces: The same grill is used to cook both burgers and buns, which may contain gluten.
  • Shared Fryers: The fries are cooked in the same oil as other items that may contain gluten.
  • Toppings and Condiments: Toppings and condiments may come into contact with gluten-containing items during preparation.

If you have celiac disease or a severe gluten intolerance, it’s essential to communicate your dietary needs to the staff and ask about their food preparation practices. While Five Guys does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, they may be able to take extra precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Five Guys

Here are some tips to help you safely enjoy a gluten-free meal at Five Guys:

  • Order a Lettuce-Wrapped Burger: Skip the bun and opt for a lettuce-wrapped burger or a burger in a bowl.
  • Check the Toppings: Some toppings, such as the malt vinegar used for fries, may contain gluten. Be sure to ask about the ingredients of any toppings or condiments.
  • Communicate with the Staff: Let the staff know about your dietary restrictions and ask about their food preparation practices.
  • Be Mindful of Cross-Contamination: Understand that there is a risk of cross-contamination, especially if you have celiac disease.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys

1. Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free as they are made from fresh potatoes and cooked in 100% peanut oil. However, they are cooked in the same oil as other items that may contain gluten, so there is a risk of cross-contamination.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Five Guys?

No, Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can order your burger or hot dog lettuce-wrapped or in a bowl to avoid the gluten-containing bun.

3. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Five Guys milkshakes are generally gluten-free, but some mix-ins, such as Oreo cookies, contain gluten. Be sure to check the ingredients of any mix-ins before adding them to your milkshake.

4. Is there a risk of cross-contamination at Five Guys?

Yes, there is a risk of cross-contamination at Five Guys, as the same cooking surfaces and fryers are used for both gluten-free and gluten-containing items. If you have celiac disease or a severe gluten intolerance, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff.

Conclusion

Five Guys offers several gluten-free options, including lettuce-wrapped burgers, hot dogs without buns, and gluten-free fries. However, it’s important to be aware of the risk of cross-contamination, especially if you have celiac disease or a severe gluten intolerance. By communicating your dietary needs to the staff and being mindful of the ingredients and preparation practices, you can enjoy a safe and delicious meal at Five Guys. Always remember to double-check with the staff and make informed choices to ensure a gluten-free dining experience.
