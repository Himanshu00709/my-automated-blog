Many restaurants, including Village Inn, are becoming more accommodating to dietary restrictions, and there may be ways to enjoy a gluten-free meal.

Customizing Your Order

While Village Inn may not have gluten-free pancakes listed on their menu, it’s always worth asking your server about possible modifications. Some locations may be able to accommodate special requests, such as using gluten-free ingredients or preparing your meal in a way that minimizes cross-contamination. However, it’s important to note that cross-contamination can still occur in a kitchen that handles gluten-containing ingredients, so those with severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease should proceed with caution.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Village Inn

Dining out gluten-free requires a bit of preparation and communication. Here are some tips to help you enjoy your meal at Village Inn while staying gluten-free:

  • Communicate Clearly: Inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. Be specific about your needs and ask questions about how food is prepared.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Even if a dish is naturally gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Ask if the restaurant has procedures in place to prevent this.
  • Check the Menu Online: Before visiting, review the menu online to identify potential gluten-free options. This can save time and help you plan your meal.
  • Bring Your Own Gluten-Free Bread or Pancake Mix: Some restaurants may allow you to bring your own gluten-free items for them to prepare. Call ahead to confirm if this is an option.
