Does First Watch Have Gluten-Free Pancakes? A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. The question of whether a restaurant offers gluten-free options is crucial, especially when it comes to breakfast favorites like pancakes. First Watch, a popular breakfast and brunch chain, is known for its fresh and healthy menu. But does First Watch have gluten-free pancakes? In this article, we’ll explore everything you need to know about gluten-free options at First Watch, with a particular focus on their pancakes.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at First Watch

First Watch has built a reputation for offering a variety of healthy and fresh breakfast options. Their menu is designed to cater to a wide range of dietary preferences, including gluten-free diets. However, it’s important to note that while First Watch does offer gluten-free options, cross-contamination can still be a concern for those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease.

First Watch’s gluten-free menu items are clearly marked, making it easier for customers to identify safe options. The restaurant also takes steps to minimize cross-contamination, but they do not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. Therefore, if you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s essential to communicate your needs clearly to the staff.

Gluten-Free Pancakes at First Watch

Now, let’s get to the main question: Does First Watch have gluten-free pancakes? The answer is yes! First Watch offers gluten-free pancakes as part of their menu. These pancakes are made with a gluten-free batter, allowing those with gluten sensitivities to enjoy a classic breakfast favorite without worry.

The gluten-free pancakes at First Watch are typically served with a variety of toppings, including fresh fruit, whipped cream, and syrup. They are a popular choice among customers who are looking for a delicious and safe breakfast option. However, it’s important to note that while the pancakes themselves are gluten-free, the toppings and syrups may contain gluten or be subject to cross-contamination. Always double-check with your server to ensure that all components of your meal are safe for your dietary needs.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at First Watch

1. Are First Watch’s gluten-free pancakes safe for people with celiac disease?

While First Watch does offer gluten-free pancakes, they are prepared in a kitchen that also handles gluten-containing ingredients. Cross-contamination is possible, so individuals with celiac disease should exercise caution and communicate their dietary needs to the staff.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free pancakes at First Watch?

Yes, you can customize your gluten-free pancakes with a variety of toppings. However, be sure to ask your server about the gluten-free status of any additional toppings or syrups you choose.

3. Does First Watch have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen?

No, First Watch does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. While they take steps to minimize cross-contamination, it’s important to be aware that gluten-free items are prepared in the same kitchen as gluten-containing items.

4. Are there any other gluten-free breakfast options at First Watch?

Yes, First Watch offers a variety of gluten-free breakfast options, including gluten-free toast, power wraps, fresh fruit bowls, and more. Be sure to check the menu for the latest gluten-free offerings.
