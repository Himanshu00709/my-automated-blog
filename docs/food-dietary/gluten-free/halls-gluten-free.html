Understanding Gluten and Its Impact on Health

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, it can cause a range of health issues. Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Symptoms can vary widely and may include digestive issues, fatigue, skin rashes, and more. Gluten sensitivity, on the other hand, may cause similar symptoms but without the intestinal damage seen in celiac disease.

For individuals with these conditions, avoiding gluten is crucial. This is where gluten-free products like those from Halls come into play, offering a safe and effective way to manage symptoms and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
