For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding gluten-free alternatives for religious practices can be challenging. What is Cavanagh Altar Bread?

Cavanagh Altar Bread has been a staple in religious communities for decades, providing high-quality altar bread for Eucharistic celebrations. Known for their commitment to tradition and quality, Cavanagh has expanded its product line to include gluten-free options, ensuring that everyone can participate in the sacrament, regardless of dietary restrictions.

The Importance of Gluten-Free Altar Bread

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. The Catholic Church recognizes the importance of providing gluten-free options for those who cannot consume traditional wheat-based altar bread. Cavanagh Altar Bread's gluten-free products are made with the utmost care to ensure they meet the Church's requirements while being safe for those with gluten sensitivities.

How Cavanagh Altar Bread Ensures Gluten-Free Quality

Cavanagh Altar Bread takes several steps to ensure their gluten-free products are safe and of the highest quality:

  • Dedicated Facilities: The gluten-free altar bread is produced in a dedicated facility to prevent cross-contamination with gluten-containing products.
  • Rigorous Testing: Each batch of gluten-free altar bread undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it contains less than 20 parts per million (ppm) of gluten, the threshold set by the FDA for gluten-free products.
  • High-Quality Ingredients: Only the finest gluten-free ingredients are used, ensuring the bread meets both religious and dietary standards.
