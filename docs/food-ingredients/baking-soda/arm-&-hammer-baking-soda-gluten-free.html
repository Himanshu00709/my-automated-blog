Is Arm & Hammer Baking Soda Gluten-Free? A Comprehensive Guide

Baking soda is a staple in many kitchens, used for everything from baking to cleaning. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the question of whether a product is gluten-free is crucial. In this article, we’ll dive deep into whether Arm & Hammer Baking Soda is gluten-free, its uses, and what you need to know to make informed decisions in your kitchen.

What is Arm & Hammer Baking Soda?

Arm & Hammer Baking Soda is a well-known brand of sodium bicarbonate, a chemical compound with the formula NaHCO₃. It has been a household name for over 170 years, trusted for its versatility in baking, cleaning, and even personal care. But what makes it particularly interesting is its potential to be a gluten-free product, which is essential for those with gluten-related disorders.

Is Arm & Hammer Baking Soda Gluten-Free?

The short answer is yes, Arm & Hammer Baking Soda is gluten-free. According to the manufacturer, Arm & Hammer Baking Soda does not contain any gluten ingredients. However, it’s important to note that while the product itself is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during manufacturing or packaging. For those with severe gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, it’s always a good idea to check the packaging for any gluten-free certifications or contact the manufacturer directly for more information.
