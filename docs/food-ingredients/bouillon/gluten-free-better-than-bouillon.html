What is Better Than Bouillon?

Better Than Bouillon is a concentrated paste made from real meat, vegetables, and seasonings. Unlike traditional bouillon cubes or powders, which are often loaded with artificial flavors and preservatives, Better Than Bouillon offers a more natural and robust flavor profile. The gluten-free version of Better Than Bouillon is specifically formulated to cater to individuals who need to avoid gluten in their diet. This makes it an excellent choice for those with celiac disease, gluten intolerance, or anyone looking to reduce their gluten intake.

Why Choose Gluten-Free Better Than Bouillon?

There are several reasons why gluten-free Better Than Bouillon is a superior choice for both home cooks and professional chefs:

How to Use Gluten-Free Better Than Bouillon

Using gluten-free Better Than Bouillon is incredibly simple. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Measure: Use one teaspoon of the paste for every cup of water. Adjust the amount based on your desired flavor intensity.
  2. Mix: Stir the paste into the water until it is fully dissolved.
  3. Cook: Use the broth as a base for soups, stews, or any recipe that calls for broth or stock.

For those who love to experiment in the kitchen, gluten-free Better Than Bouillon can also be used as a seasoning. Add a small amount directly to your dishes to enhance the flavor without the need for additional salt or spices.
