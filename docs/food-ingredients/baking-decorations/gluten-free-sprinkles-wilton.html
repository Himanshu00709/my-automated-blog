What Are Gluten-Free Sprinkles?

Gluten-free sprinkles are decorative toppings used in baking and dessert decoration. Unlike traditional sprinkles, which may contain gluten-containing ingredients like wheat starch, gluten-free sprinkles are made without any gluten-containing components. Wilton, a well-known brand in the baking industry, offers a range of gluten-free sprinkles that are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance.

Why Choose Wilton's Gluten-Free Sprinkles?

Wilton has been a trusted name in the baking world for decades, known for its high-quality products and innovative designs. Their gluten-free sprinkles are no exception. Here are some reasons why you should consider Wilton's gluten-free sprinkles for your next baking project:

  • Certified Gluten-Free: Wilton's gluten-free sprinkles are certified by recognized gluten-free organizations, ensuring they meet strict gluten-free standards.
  • Wide Variety: Wilton offers a wide range of gluten-free sprinkles, including jimmies, nonpareils, sanding sugars, and more, in various colors and shapes.
  • High-Quality Ingredients: Wilton uses high-quality, food-grade ingredients to ensure that their sprinkles are not only safe but also delicious.
  • Versatility: These sprinkles can be used on a variety of baked goods, from cupcakes and cookies to cakes and donuts.

How to Use Wilton's Gluten-Free Sprinkles

Using Wilton's gluten-free sprinkles is as easy as using traditional sprinkles. Here are some tips to get the most out of your gluten-free sprinkles:

  1. Choose the Right Sprinkles: Depending on the type of dessert you're making, choose the appropriate sprinkle type. For example, jimmies are great for cupcakes, while nonpareils work well for cookies.
  2. Apply at the Right Time: For best results, apply the sprinkles while the frosting or glaze is still wet. This will ensure that the sprinkles stick properly.
  3. Experiment with Designs: Don't be afraid to get creative with your sprinkle designs. Use different colors and shapes to create unique patterns and themes.
  4. Store Properly: To keep your sprinkles fresh, store them in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight.
