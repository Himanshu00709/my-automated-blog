Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a group of proteins found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient malabsorption, and long-term complications. Therefore, identifying gluten-free products is essential for maintaining a healthy diet.

Potential Risks of Cross-Contamination

Even if a product does not contain gluten-containing ingredients, cross-contamination during manufacturing can pose a risk. Shared equipment or facilities that process gluten-containing products can lead to trace amounts of gluten in otherwise gluten-free items. Knorr does not explicitly state whether their Chicken Flavor Bouillon is produced in a gluten-free facility, so this remains a potential concern.
