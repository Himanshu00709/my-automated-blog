What is Knorr Vegetable Mix Gluten-Free?

Knorr Vegetable Mix Gluten-Free is a carefully crafted blend of dehydrated vegetables, herbs, and spices designed to enhance the flavor of your dishes without the use of gluten-containing ingredients. The mix typically includes a variety of vegetables such as carrots, onions, leeks, and celery, along with herbs like parsley and thyme. These ingredients are freeze-dried to preserve their flavor and nutritional value, ensuring that you get the best possible taste and health benefits.
