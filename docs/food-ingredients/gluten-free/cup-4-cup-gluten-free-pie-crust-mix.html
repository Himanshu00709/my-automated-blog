How to Use Cup4Cup Gluten-Free Pie Crust Mix

Using Cup4Cup Gluten-Free Pie Crust Mix is straightforward, even for those new to gluten-free baking. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Gather Your Ingredients: You’ll need the pie crust mix, cold butter, and ice water.
  2. Cut in the Butter: Cut the cold butter into small pieces and mix it into the pie crust mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add Water: Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, until the dough comes together.
  4. Chill the Dough: Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and chill it in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Roll and Bake: Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface, place it in your pie dish, and bake according to your recipe’s instructions.
