What Makes Cholula Taco Seasoning Gluten-Free?

Cholula Taco Seasoning is crafted with a blend of spices and natural flavors that are free from gluten-containing ingredients. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Cholula ensures that their seasoning is safe for these individuals by avoiding any gluten-containing additives or cross-contamination during the manufacturing process.

The primary ingredients in Cholula Taco Seasoning include:

  • Chili Pepper
  • Salt
  • Spices
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Natural Flavors

These ingredients are naturally gluten-free, and Cholula takes extra precautions to ensure that their products meet the strict standards for gluten-free certification. This makes Cholula Taco Seasoning a reliable choice for those who need to avoid gluten in their diet.
