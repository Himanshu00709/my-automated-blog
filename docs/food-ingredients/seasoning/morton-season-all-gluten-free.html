What is Morton Season All?

Morton Season All is a multi-purpose seasoning blend that combines salt, spices, and other flavorings to create a versatile seasoning that can be used on meats, vegetables, and more. It is designed to enhance the natural flavors of your food without overpowering them. The gluten-free version of Morton Season All is specifically formulated to meet the dietary needs of those who must avoid gluten.
