How to Use Steve's Gluten-Free Cake Flour

Using Steve's Gluten-Free Cake Flour is incredibly straightforward, making it an excellent choice for both experienced bakers and those new to gluten-free baking. Here are some tips to get the best results:

  • Measure Accurately: Always measure your flour using a kitchen scale for the most accurate results. If you don't have a scale, fluff the flour with a spoon before scooping it into your measuring cup.
  • Mix Thoroughly: Ensure that all your dry ingredients are well-mixed before adding the wet ingredients. This helps to distribute the xanthan gum evenly, which is essential for proper binding.
  • Adjust Liquid Ingredients: Gluten-free flours often require slightly more liquid than traditional flours. Be prepared to add a bit more milk, water, or other liquids to achieve the desired batter consistency.
  • Don't Overmix: Overmixing can lead to dense and tough baked goods. Mix just until the ingredients are combined to keep your cakes light and fluffy.
