Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Flour is a versatile blend designed to mimic the texture and taste of traditional wheat flour. How to Use Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Flour

One of the standout features of Bob's Red Mill Gluten-Free Flour is its versatility. It can be used in a variety of recipes, both sweet and savory. Here are some common uses:

  • Baking: This flour is ideal for baking bread, muffins, cookies, and cakes. It provides a texture similar to wheat flour, making it a great substitute in traditional recipes.
  • Thickening Agent: The flour can be used as a thickening agent in soups, sauces, and gravies. Its neutral flavor ensures that it won’t alter the taste of your dishes.
