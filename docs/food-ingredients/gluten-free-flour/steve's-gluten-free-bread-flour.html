How to Use Steve's Gluten-Free Bread Flour

Using Steve's Gluten-Free Bread Flour is straightforward, but there are a few tips and tricks to ensure the best results:

  1. Measure Accurately: Use a kitchen scale to measure the flour by weight for the most accurate results. If using measuring cups, spoon the flour into the cup and level it off with a knife.
  2. Adjust Liquid Ingredients: Gluten-free flours often absorb more liquid than wheat flour, so you may need to adjust the amount of liquid in your recipe. Start by adding a little less liquid than the recipe calls for, and add more as needed.
  3. Mix Thoroughly: Gluten-free doughs and batters may require more mixing than traditional recipes to ensure even distribution of ingredients.
  4. Allow for Resting Time: Letting the dough or batter rest for a few minutes before baking can help improve the texture of the final product.
