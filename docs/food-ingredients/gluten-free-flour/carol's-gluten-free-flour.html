FAQs About Carol's Gluten-Free Flour

1. Is Carol's Gluten-Free Flour suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Carol's Gluten-Free Flour is specifically formulated to be safe for individuals with celiac disease. It is made in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

2. Can I use Carol's Gluten-Free Flour in all my recipes?

Carol's Gluten-Free Flour is highly versatile and can be used as a 1:1 substitute for wheat flour in most recipes. However, for best results, it's recommended to follow recipes specifically designed for gluten-free baking.

3. Does Carol's Gluten-Free Flour contain any allergens?

Carol's Gluten-Free Flour is free from gluten, but it may contain other allergens such as nuts or soy, depending on the specific blend. Always check the label for allergen information.

4. How should I store Carol's Gluten-Free Flour?

Store Carol's Gluten-Free Flour in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. For extended shelf life, consider storing it in an airtight container in the refrigerator or freezer.
