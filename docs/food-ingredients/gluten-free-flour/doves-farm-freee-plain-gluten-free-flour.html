What is Doves Farm Freee Plain Gluten-Free Flour?

Doves Farm Freee Plain Gluten-Free Flour is a blend of naturally gluten-free flours and starches, designed to mimic the texture and functionality of traditional wheat flour. It is made from a combination of rice flour, potato starch, tapioca starch, maize flour, and buckwheat flour. This carefully crafted blend ensures that the flour is not only gluten-free but also suitable for a wide range of baking applications.

How to Use Doves Farm Freee Plain Gluten-Free Flour

One of the standout features of Doves Farm Freee Plain Gluten-Free Flour is its ease of use. It can be used as a direct substitute for wheat flour in most recipes, making it a convenient option for those transitioning to a gluten-free diet. Here are some tips for using this flour effectively:

  • Baking: When baking, it is often helpful to add a binding agent such as xanthan gum or psyllium husk to improve the texture and structure of the final product.
  • Mixing: Ensure that the flour is well-mixed with other dry ingredients before adding wet ingredients to achieve a uniform consistency.
  • Storage: Store the flour in a cool, dry place to maintain its freshness and prevent clumping.
