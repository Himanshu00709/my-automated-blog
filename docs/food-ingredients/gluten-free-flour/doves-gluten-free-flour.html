What is Dove's Gluten-Free Flour?

Dove's Gluten-Free Flour is a specially formulated flour blend designed to mimic the texture and functionality of traditional wheat flour. FAQs About Dove's Gluten-Free Flour

1. Is Dove's Gluten-Free Flour suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Dove's Gluten-Free Flour is specifically formulated to be safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. It is made in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

2. Can I use Dove's Gluten-Free Flour as a 1:1 substitute for wheat flour?

Yes, Dove's Gluten-Free Flour is designed to be a 1:1 substitute for traditional wheat flour in most recipes. However, for best results, it's recommended to follow recipes specifically developed for gluten-free baking.

3. Does Dove's Gluten-Free Flour contain any allergens?

Dove's Gluten-Free Flour is free from gluten, but it may contain traces of other allergens such as nuts or soy, depending on the manufacturing process. Always check the label for specific allergen information.

4. How should I store Dove's Gluten-Free Flour?

To maintain freshness, store Dove's Gluten-Free Flour in a cool, dry place, preferably in an airtight container. It can also be stored in the refrigerator or freezer for extended shelf life.

5. Can I use Dove's Gluten-Free Flour for yeast-based recipes?

Yes, Dove's Gluten-Free Flour can be used for yeast-based recipes such as bread and pizza dough. However, you may need to add xanthan gum or another binding agent to achieve the desired texture.
