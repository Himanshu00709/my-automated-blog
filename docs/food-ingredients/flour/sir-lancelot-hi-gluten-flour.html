What is Sir Lancelot Hi-Gluten Flour?

Sir Lancelot Hi-Gluten Flour is a high-protein flour specifically designed for bread baking. With a protein content of around 14.2%, it is one of the strongest flours available on the market. This high protein content translates to a higher gluten formation, which is essential for creating bread with a strong structure, chewy texture, and excellent oven spring. The flour is milled from hard red spring wheat, which is known for its robust gluten-forming capabilities.

One of the key features of Sir Lancelot Hi-Gluten Flour is its consistency. Bakers can rely on this flour to produce consistent results batch after batch, making it a trusted choice for both commercial bakeries and home bakers. How to Use Sir Lancelot Hi-Gluten Flour

Using Sir Lancelot Hi-Gluten Flour is straightforward, but there are a few tips to keep in mind to get the best results:

  1. Hydration: Due to its high protein content, Sir Lancelot Hi-Gluten Flour absorbs more water than lower-protein flours. Be prepared to adjust your hydration levels accordingly. A good rule of thumb is to start with a slightly higher hydration level than you would use with all-purpose flour.
  2. Kneading: Proper kneading is essential to develop the gluten fully. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic, which may take a bit longer than with lower-protein flours.
  3. Proofing: Allow the dough to proof adequately. The strong gluten structure will support a longer proofing time, which can enhance the flavor and texture of the bread.
  4. Baking: Bake at the recommended temperature for your recipe. The high gluten content will help the bread rise beautifully in the oven, resulting in a well-structured loaf with a crisp crust.
