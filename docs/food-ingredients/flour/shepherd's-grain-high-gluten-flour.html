Benefits of Using High Gluten Flour

High gluten flour is not just for professional bakers. Whether you're making a rustic loaf of sourdough or a chewy New York-style pizza, this flour offers several benefits:

  • Superior Dough Strength: The high protein content ensures that your dough has the strength to rise and hold its shape, even when filled with heavy ingredients like nuts or seeds.
  • Enhanced Texture: High gluten flour gives bread a chewy, satisfying texture that is hard to achieve with all-purpose flour.
  • Better Crust Development: The strong gluten network helps create a crisp, golden crust that is the hallmark of artisanal bread.
  • Versatility: While it’s perfect for bread, this flour can also be used in other baked goods like bagels, pretzels, and even some pastries that require a firmer structure.

How to Use Shepherd's Grain High Gluten Flour

Using high gluten flour is straightforward, but there are a few tips to keep in mind to get the best results:

  1. Hydration is Key: High gluten flour absorbs more water than all-purpose flour, so you may need to adjust the hydration levels in your recipe. Start by adding a little more water than usual and adjust as needed.
  2. Knead Thoroughly: The dough will require more kneading to develop the gluten fully. This can be done by hand or with a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook.
  3. Rest the Dough: Allow the dough to rest after kneading. This gives the gluten time to relax, making it easier to shape.
  4. Experiment: Don’t be afraid to experiment with different recipes. High gluten flour can be used in a variety of baked goods, so try it in your favorite bread or pizza dough recipe.

FAQs About Shepherd's Grain High Gluten Flour

1. Can I substitute high gluten flour for all-purpose flour?

While you can substitute high gluten flour for all-purpose flour, it’s important to note that the results will be different. High gluten flour will produce a chewier, denser texture, which is ideal for bread but may not be suitable for cakes or pastries.

2. Is Shepherd's Grain High Gluten Flour suitable for people with gluten intolerance?

No, high gluten flour contains a significant amount of gluten and is not suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. For those with gluten sensitivities, consider using a gluten-free flour blend instead.

3. Where can I buy Shepherd's Grain High Gluten Flour?

Shepherd's Grain High Gluten Flour is available at specialty baking stores, online retailers, and some grocery stores. Check the Shepherd's Grain website for a list of retailers near you.

4. How should I store high gluten flour?

To maintain its freshness, store high gluten flour in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. For long-term storage, you can keep it in the refrigerator or freezer.
