What is Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour?

Ardent Mills is a leading flour supplier in North America, known for its high-quality products. Their gluten-free flour is a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, designed to mimic the texture and performance of traditional wheat flour. This blend is carefully crafted to ensure that it can be used in a variety of recipes, from bread and cakes to cookies and pastries.

FAQs About Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour

Is Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour suitable for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour is certified gluten-free and is safe for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Can I use Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour for all types of baking?

Yes, this flour is versatile and can be used for a variety of baking needs, including bread, cakes, cookies, and pastries.

Does Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour contain any allergens?

While the flour itself is gluten-free, it is produced in a facility that also processes wheat, so there is a risk of cross-contamination. Always check the packaging for the most current allergen information.

How should I store Ardent Mills Gluten-Free Flour?

Store the flour in a cool, dry place, preferably in an airtight container to maintain its freshness and prevent moisture absorption.
