What Makes Wilton Sprinkles Gluten-Free?

Wilton Sprinkles Gluten-Free are specially formulated to meet the needs of individuals who avoid gluten. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Wilton ensures that their gluten-free sprinkles are made without any gluten-containing ingredients and are processed in a facility that minimizes cross-contamination risks.

Here’s a breakdown of what makes Wilton Sprinkles gluten-free:

Feature Description
Ingredients Wilton uses ingredients like sugar, cornstarch, and natural colors that are naturally gluten-free.
Certification Many Wilton Sprinkles are certified gluten-free by third-party organizations, ensuring compliance with strict standards.
Manufacturing Process Wilton follows rigorous protocols to prevent cross-contamination, including dedicated equipment and thorough cleaning procedures.
