Yes, Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Crust is typically available year-round, though it may be more prominently displayed during the holiday season when baking is more common.

Can I use Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Crust for savory dishes?

Absolutely! While it's often used for sweet pies, Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Crust is versatile and can be used for savory dishes like quiches or pot pies as well.

How should I store Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Crust?

Pillsbury Gluten-Free Pie Crust should be stored in the freezer until you're ready to use it. Once thawed, it should be used within a few days and not refrozen.
