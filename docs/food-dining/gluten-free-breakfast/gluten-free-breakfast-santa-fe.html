Gluten-Free Breakfast Recipes Inspired by Santa Fe

If you prefer to enjoy a gluten-free breakfast at home, here are a couple of recipes inspired by Santa Fe's culinary traditions:

Gluten-Free Blue Corn Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup blue cornmeal
  • 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the blue cornmeal, gluten-free flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, or honey.

Gluten-Free Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients:

  • 2 gluten-free tortillas
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced green chilies
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour the eggs into the skillet and scramble until cooked through.
  4. Warm the gluten-free tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  5. Divide the scrambled eggs, black beans, diced tomatoes, green chilies, and cheese (if using) between the two tortillas.
  6. Fold the tortillas into burritos and serve immediately.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Breakfast in Santa Fe

1. Are gluten-free options widely available in Santa Fe?

Yes, Santa Fe is known for its health-conscious dining scene, and many restaurants offer gluten-free options. It's always a good idea to check with the restaurant beforehand to ensure they can accommodate your dietary needs.

2. Can I find gluten-free baked goods in Santa Fe?

Absolutely! Many bakeries and cafes in Santa Fe offer gluten-free baked goods, including muffins, scones, and bread. Some even specialize in gluten-free baking.

3. Are there gluten-free options for traditional New Mexican breakfast dishes?

Yes, many restaurants in Santa Fe offer gluten-free versions of traditional New Mexican breakfast dishes, such as breakfast burritos and huevos rancheros. Be sure to ask about gluten-free tortillas and sauces.

4. How can I ensure my breakfast is truly gluten-free?

When dining out, always inform your server about your gluten-free dietary needs. Ask about cross-contamination and how the food is prepared. Many restaurants in Santa Fe are well-versed in gluten-free dining and take precautions to ensure your meal is safe.
