Best Gluten-Free Pizza in Denver: A Comprehensive Guide

Denver, known for its vibrant food scene, has become a haven for those seeking gluten-free options. Whether you're a local or just passing through, finding the best gluten-free pizza in Denver can be a game-changer for your dining experience. In this guide, we'll explore the top spots in the city that serve delicious, gluten-free pizzas, ensuring you don't have to compromise on taste or quality.

Why Gluten-Free Pizza?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity over the years, not just among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, but also among individuals looking to improve their overall health. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause digestive issues and other health problems for some people. Fortunately, Denver's culinary scene has embraced this dietary need, offering a variety of gluten-free pizza options that are both delicious and safe to eat.

Top Gluten-Free Pizza Spots in Denver

1. Hops & Pie

Located in the trendy Berkeley neighborhood, Hops & Pie is a must-visit for pizza lovers. Their gluten-free crust is made in-house and is available for any of their specialty pizzas. The crust is crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside, providing a satisfying texture that rivals traditional pizza. Pair your pizza with one of their many craft beers for a complete dining experience.

2. Blue Pan Pizza

Blue Pan Pizza, with locations in West Highland and Congress Park, is renowned for its Detroit-style pizza. They offer a gluten-free crust that is both thick and airy, providing a unique texture that sets it apart from other gluten-free options. Their extensive menu includes a variety of toppings, ensuring there's something for everyone.

3. Pizzeria Locale

Pizzeria Locale, a collaboration between the Frasca Food and Wine team and Chipotle, offers a fast-casual dining experience with a focus on Neapolitan-style pizza. Their gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice and tapioca flour, resulting in a light and crispy base. The simplicity of their menu allows the quality of their ingredients to shine through.

4. Proto's Pizza

With multiple locations across Denver, Proto's Pizza is a local favorite. Their gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, providing a chewy and flavorful base. They offer a wide range of toppings, including vegan and vegetarian options, making it a great choice for diverse dietary needs.

5. Marco's Coal-Fired Pizza

Marco's Coal-Fired Pizza, located in the Ballpark neighborhood, is known for its authentic Neapolitan-style pizza. Their gluten-free crust is made in-house and cooked in a coal-fired oven, giving it a unique flavor and texture. The menu features classic Italian toppings, ensuring a traditional pizza experience.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Pizza in Denver

1. What makes a pizza gluten-free?

A gluten-free pizza is made with a crust that does not contain any gluten-containing ingredients, such as wheat, barley, or rye. Instead, gluten-free flours like rice flour, almond flour, or tapioca flour are used to create the crust.

2. Are gluten-free pizzas safe for people with celiac disease?

Most gluten-free pizzas are safe for people with celiac disease, but it's important to ensure that the restaurant follows strict gluten-free protocols to avoid cross-contamination. Always inform the staff of your dietary needs.

3. Can I find vegan gluten-free pizza in Denver?

Yes, many restaurants in Denver offer vegan gluten-free pizza options. Proto's Pizza, for example, has a variety of vegan toppings that can be added to their gluten-free crust.

4. How does the taste of gluten-free pizza compare to regular pizza?

Gluten-free pizza can be just as delicious as regular pizza, especially when made with high-quality ingredients. The texture may differ slightly, but many people find gluten-free crusts to be equally satisfying.

5. Are there any gluten-free pizza delivery options in Denver?

Yes, many of the restaurants mentioned in this guide offer delivery services. You can also check popular food delivery apps for gluten-free pizza options in your area.

Conclusion

Denver's gluten-free pizza scene is thriving, offering a variety of options that cater to different tastes and dietary needs. Whether you're craving a classic Neapolitan-style pizza or something more unique like Detroit-style, there's a gluten-free pizza in Denver that's perfect for you. With so many excellent choices, you can enjoy a delicious, gluten-free meal without sacrificing flavor or quality. So, the next time you're in Denver, be sure to check out one of these top spots for a pizza experience that's both safe and satisfying.
