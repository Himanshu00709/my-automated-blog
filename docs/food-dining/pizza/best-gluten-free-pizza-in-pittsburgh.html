Best Gluten-Free Pizza in Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh, known for its vibrant food scene, has embraced the gluten-free movement with open arms. Whether you're a local or just visiting, finding a delicious gluten-free pizza in the Steel City is easier than ever. In this article, we'll explore some of the best gluten-free pizza options in Pittsburgh, highlighting their unique offerings, crust quality, and overall dining experience. So, let's dive into the world of gluten-free pizza and discover where you can enjoy a slice without compromising on taste or texture.

Top Gluten-Free Pizza Spots in Pittsburgh

1. Pizza Taglio

Pizza Taglio is a must-visit for anyone seeking a gluten-free pizza experience in Pittsburgh. Their gluten-free crust is made in-house and is known for its perfect balance of crispiness and chewiness. The restaurant offers a variety of toppings, from classic margherita to more adventurous options like prosciutto and arugula. The ambiance is modern and inviting, making it a great spot for both casual dinners and special occasions.

2. Mercurio's

Mercurio's is another top contender for the best gluten-free pizza in Pittsburgh. Their gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice and tapioca flour, resulting in a light and airy texture that's hard to resist. The menu features a wide range of toppings, including fresh mozzarella, basil, and San Marzano tomatoes. Mercurio's also offers a selection of gluten-free desserts, making it a one-stop shop for those with dietary restrictions.

3. Spak Brothers

Spak Brothers is a local favorite for gluten-free pizza. Their gluten-free crust is made from a proprietary blend of flours, ensuring a unique and satisfying texture. The menu is extensive, with options ranging from traditional pepperoni to vegan-friendly choices. Spak Brothers also offers gluten-free sandwiches and salads, making it a versatile dining option for those with gluten sensitivities.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Pizza in Pittsburgh

1. Are gluten-free pizzas in Pittsburgh safe for people with celiac disease?

Most restaurants offering gluten-free pizza in Pittsburgh take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. However, it's always best to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure they take extra care in preparing your meal.

2. Can I find vegan gluten-free pizza in Pittsburgh?

Yes, many restaurants in Pittsburgh offer vegan gluten-free pizza options. Spak Brothers, for example, has a variety of vegan toppings that can be paired with their gluten-free crust.

3. How does the taste of gluten-free pizza compare to regular pizza?

Gluten-free pizza has come a long way in terms of taste and texture. While it may differ slightly from traditional pizza, many people find it just as delicious, especially when made with high-quality ingredients.

4. Are there any gluten-free pizza delivery options in Pittsburgh?

Yes, several restaurants in Pittsburgh offer gluten-free pizza delivery. Pizza Taglio and Spak Brothers are two popular options that provide delivery services.

Conclusion

Pittsburgh's gluten-free pizza scene is thriving, offering a variety of options for those with dietary restrictions. Whether you're in the mood for a classic margherita or something more adventurous, you're sure to find a pizza that satisfies your cravings. From the modern ambiance of Pizza Taglio to the cozy vibe of Mercurio's and the casual friendliness of Spak Brothers, there's a gluten-free pizza spot for everyone in Pittsburgh. So, the next time you're in the Steel City, don't hesitate to explore these fantastic gluten-free pizza options and enjoy a slice of heaven without the gluten.
