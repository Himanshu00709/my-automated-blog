Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into Miller's gluten-free menu, it's essential to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. Therefore, gluten-free dining is not just a dietary preference but a necessity for many.

Miller's has recognized this need and has taken significant steps to ensure that their gluten-free menu is not only safe but also flavorful and diverse. The restaurant chain has implemented strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free dishes are prepared separately from gluten-containing items.

FAQs About Miller's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Miller's gluten-free menu safe for individuals with celiac disease?

Yes, Miller's takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. They have strict protocols in place to prevent cross-contamination, including separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free dishes.

2. Are there vegan options on Miller's gluten-free menu?

While Miller's gluten-free menu is primarily focused on accommodating gluten-free diets, there are some vegan options available. It's best to check with the restaurant staff to confirm which dishes are both gluten-free and vegan.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, Miller's offers the flexibility to customize your order to make it gluten-free. However, it's important to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure that your meal is prepared safely.

4. Does Miller's offer gluten-free bread or buns?

Yes, Miller's offers gluten-free bread and buns for sandwiches and burgers. Be sure to request them when placing your order.
