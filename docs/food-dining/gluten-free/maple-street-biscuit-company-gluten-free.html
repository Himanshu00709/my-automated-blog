Maple Street Biscuit Company: A Gluten-Free Haven

Maple Street Biscuit Company has become a beloved destination for biscuit lovers across the South. Known for their warm, flaky, and buttery biscuits, the company has also made significant strides in catering to those with dietary restrictions, particularly gluten-free diets. In this blog post, we’ll explore how Maple Street Biscuit Company has embraced the gluten-free movement, offering delicious options that don’t compromise on taste or quality.

The Rise of Gluten-Free Dining

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free options has skyrocketed. Whether due to celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or personal dietary choices, more people are seeking out restaurants that offer gluten-free alternatives. Maple Street Biscuit Company has recognized this trend and has worked diligently to ensure that their menu is inclusive and accommodating.

How Maple Street Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a major concern. Maple Street Biscuit Company takes this seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in a dedicated area to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Special Utensils: Separate utensils and equipment are used for gluten-free items.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free safety and how to handle gluten-free orders.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all items at Maple Street Biscuit Company gluten-free?

No, not all items are gluten-free. However, Maple Street offers a variety of gluten-free options, including biscuits, gravy, sandwiches, and bowls. Always check the menu or ask a staff member for the most up-to-date gluten-free offerings.

Is the gluten-free gravy vegetarian?

Yes, the gluten-free gravy at Maple Street Biscuit Company is vegetarian-friendly. It’s made with a rich, savory base that’s perfect for topping your favorite gluten-free biscuit or breakfast plate.

Can I customize my gluten-free order?

Absolutely! Maple Street Biscuit Company encourages customization. You can build your own gluten-free sandwich or bowl by choosing from a variety of proteins, toppings, and sauces.

How does Maple Street prevent cross-contamination?

Maple Street takes several precautions to prevent cross-contamination, including using separate preparation areas, utensils, and equipment for gluten-free items. Staff are also trained to handle gluten-free orders with care.
