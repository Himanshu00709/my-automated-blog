Ruth's Chris Gluten-Free Menu: A Detailed Guide

Ruth's Chris Steak House is renowned for its high-quality steaks and exceptional dining experience. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Ruth's Chris has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diets, offering a variety of options that ensure a safe and enjoyable meal. In this article, we will explore the gluten-free offerings at Ruth's Chris, providing you with a comprehensive guide to navigating their menu.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Ruth's Chris

Ruth's Chris Steak House takes dietary restrictions seriously, and their commitment to providing gluten-free options is evident in their menu. The restaurant has a dedicated gluten-free menu, which is carefully curated to avoid cross-contamination and ensure the safety of guests with gluten sensitivities. The staff is also well-trained to handle gluten-free requests, making it a reliable choice for those who need to avoid gluten.

It's important to note that while Ruth's Chris makes every effort to provide gluten-free options, cross-contamination can still occur in a shared kitchen environment. Therefore, it's always advisable to inform your server about your dietary restrictions and ask for their guidance in selecting the safest options.

FAQs About Ruth's Chris Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Ruth's Chris Steak House safe for people with celiac disease?

Ruth's Chris takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination and offers a dedicated gluten-free menu. However, as with any restaurant, there is always a risk of cross-contamination in a shared kitchen. It's important to inform your server about your dietary restrictions and ask for their guidance.

2. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. For example, you can request to have croutons removed from salads or ask for gluten-free sauces. Always communicate your needs to your server to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

3. Are there gluten-free bread options available?

Ruth's Chris does not currently offer gluten-free bread. However, you can enjoy a variety of gluten-free appetizers, entrees, and sides without the need for bread.

4. How can I be sure my meal is gluten-free?

Ruth's Chris staff are trained to handle gluten-free requests and will take extra care to ensure your meal is prepared safely. Always inform your server about your dietary restrictions and ask for their assistance in selecting gluten-free options.
