Why Be Our Guest Restaurant is a Must-Visit for Gluten-Free Diners

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Be Our Guest Restaurant has made it a priority to ensure that all guests, regardless of dietary restrictions, can enjoy a magical meal. The restaurant offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, and the staff is well-trained in handling food allergies and cross-contamination. This makes it a safe and enjoyable dining option for gluten-free diners.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Be Our Guest Restaurant

To ensure a seamless dining experience, here are some tips for gluten-free diners:

  • Notify the Staff: When making your reservation, inform the staff about your gluten-free dietary needs. This allows the kitchen to prepare in advance.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask your server about the ingredients and preparation methods to ensure your meal is safe.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: While the restaurant takes precautions, it’s always a good idea to double-check about cross-contamination risks.
  • Enjoy Dessert: Be Our Guest Restaurant offers gluten-free dessert options, so don’t miss out on the sweet finale to your meal.
