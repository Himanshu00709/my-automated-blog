Be Our Guest Restaurant: A Gluten-Free Dining Experience

Disney's Be Our Guest Restaurant, located in the heart of Magic Kingdom's Fantasyland, is a culinary destination that transports guests into the enchanting world of "Beauty and the Beast." Known for its immersive atmosphere and delectable French-inspired cuisine, Be Our Guest has become a must-visit for Disney enthusiasts. But what about those with dietary restrictions, particularly gluten-free diners? In this article, we’ll explore how Be Our Guest Restaurant caters to gluten-free guests, ensuring a magical dining experience for everyone.

Gluten-Free Dining at Be Our Guest: What to Expect

Be Our Guest Restaurant is renowned for its attention to detail, and this extends to its gluten-free offerings. The restaurant takes pride in accommodating guests with dietary restrictions, including those who require gluten-free meals. Whether you're visiting for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, the culinary team is well-equipped to provide safe and delicious gluten-free options.

Gluten-Free Menu Options

The gluten-free menu at Be Our Guest is thoughtfully curated to ensure that guests with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity can enjoy a variety of dishes without worry. Here’s a breakdown of the gluten-free options available:

Meal Gluten-Free Options
Breakfast Gluten-free pastries, scrambled eggs, bacon, sausage, and fresh fruit.
Lunch Grilled steak, roasted chicken, and seasonal vegetables. Gluten-free rolls are also available upon request.
Dinner Herb-crusted lamb, grilled shrimp, and ratatouille. Dessert options include a gluten-free version of the famous "Grey Stuff."

Cross-Contamination Prevention

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Be Our Guest Restaurant takes this seriously, implementing strict protocols to prevent cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients. The kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders separately, using dedicated utensils and cookware. Additionally, gluten-free meals are prepared in a designated area to minimize the risk of contamination.

Tips for Gluten-Free Diners

To make the most of your gluten-free dining experience at Be Our Guest, consider the following tips:

  • Notify the Staff: Inform your server about your gluten-free dietary needs when you arrive. They will guide you through the menu and ensure your meal is prepared safely.
  • Pre-Order Your Meal: If you’re dining during peak hours, consider pre-ordering your gluten-free meal to reduce wait times.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask questions about ingredients or preparation methods. The staff is knowledgeable and happy to assist.
  • Bring Backup Snacks: While Be Our Guest offers excellent gluten-free options, it’s always a good idea to carry gluten-free snacks, especially if you’re exploring the park afterward.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Be Our Guest

Is the "Grey Stuff" gluten-free?

Yes! The famous "Grey Stuff" dessert is available in a gluten-free version. Be sure to request it when ordering your meal.

Can I make reservations for a gluten-free meal?

While you can’t specifically reserve a gluten-free meal, you can note your dietary restrictions when making your reservation. This helps the kitchen prepare for your visit.

Are there gluten-free options for children?

Absolutely! The kids’ menu includes gluten-free options such as grilled chicken and vegetables. Gluten-free rolls are also available for children.

Conclusion

Be Our Guest Restaurant is a shining example of how Disney goes above and beyond to accommodate guests with dietary restrictions. With a dedicated gluten-free menu, strict cross-contamination protocols, and a knowledgeable staff, gluten-free diners can enjoy a magical and worry-free dining experience. Whether you’re indulging in the "Grey Stuff" or savoring a perfectly grilled steak, Be Our Guest ensures that every guest feels like royalty. So, the next time you’re at Magic Kingdom, don’t hesitate to step into the Beast’s castle and let them take care of your gluten-free needs. Bon appétit!
