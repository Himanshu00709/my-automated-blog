How Red Door Grill Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. The Red Door Grill takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Separate Cooking Surfaces: The kitchen uses separate grills and utensils for gluten-free dishes to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Fryers: Gluten-free items are fried in dedicated fryers to avoid contact with gluten-containing foods.
  • Staff Training: All staff members are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and are knowledgeable about the menu's gluten-free options.
  • Clear Labeling: The menu clearly labels gluten-free items, making it easy for diners to identify safe choices.
