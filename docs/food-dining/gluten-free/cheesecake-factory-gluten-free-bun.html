What Makes the Gluten-Free Bun Special?

The Cheesecake Factory’s gluten-free bun is a testament to the restaurant’s commitment to inclusivity. Made with a blend of gluten-free flours, the bun is designed to mimic the texture and taste of traditional bread without the gluten. The result is a soft, slightly chewy bun that holds up well to burgers and sandwiches. The bun is also free from common allergens like soy and dairy, making it a versatile option for those with multiple dietary restrictions.

One of the key features of the gluten-free bun is its ability to maintain its structure without crumbling, a common issue with many gluten-free bread products. This makes it an excellent choice for those who want to enjoy a hearty meal without compromising on texture or taste.

How to Order Gluten-Free at The Cheesecake Factory

Ordering gluten-free at The Cheesecake Factory is a straightforward process, thanks to their comprehensive gluten-free menu. FAQs About The Cheesecake Factory’s Gluten-Free Bun

No, the gluten-free bun at The Cheesecake Factory is not vegan. It contains eggs, which are used to help bind the ingredients together.

Can I buy the gluten-free bun separately?

Currently, The Cheesecake Factory does not offer the gluten-free bun for separate purchase. It is only available as part of their gluten-free menu items.

Is the gluten-free bun safe for people with celiac disease?

While The Cheesecake Factory takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, the kitchen is not entirely gluten-free. Those with celiac disease should consult with the staff and make an informed decision based on their level of sensitivity.
