Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Even a small amount of gluten can trigger symptoms like bloating, diarrhea, and fatigue. For these individuals, dining out can be a minefield, as cross-contamination is a common issue in kitchens that aren’t dedicated to gluten-free cooking. Melissa's Wells Fargo Center has taken this challenge head-on, offering a menu that is not only gluten-free but also crafted with the utmost care to ensure safety and satisfaction.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Melissa's

Is Melissa's Wells Fargo Center completely gluten-free?

While Melissa's offers a wide range of gluten-free options, the restaurant is not entirely gluten-free. However, they take extensive precautions to prevent cross-contamination, including using separate cooking utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free dishes.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Melissa's is known for its flexibility and willingness to accommodate dietary restrictions. Simply inform your server of your needs, and they will ensure your meal is prepared gluten-free.

Are there gluten-free dessert options?

Yes, Melissa's offers a selection of gluten-free desserts, including flourless chocolate cake and gluten-free cookies. Be sure to ask your server for the current gluten-free dessert options.

Is the gluten-free menu suitable for celiacs?

Melissa's takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server of your condition so they can take extra precautions.
