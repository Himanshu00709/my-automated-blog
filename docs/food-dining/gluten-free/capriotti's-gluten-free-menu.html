Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before we delve into Capriotti's specific offerings, it's important to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause serious health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, even trace amounts of gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from digestive discomfort to severe autoimmune reactions. As a result, restaurants offering gluten-free options must take extra care in food preparation to avoid cross-contamination.

Precautions Against Cross-Contamination

One of the biggest concerns for those with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Capriotti's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe for consumption. These measures include:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free sandwiches are prepared in a designated area to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils are used exclusively for gluten-free orders to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and how to handle gluten-free orders safely.
