Kona Grill Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Kona Grill has made it easier for gluten-free diners to enjoy a delicious and safe meal. Known for its diverse menu that blends American, sushi, and international flavors, Kona Grill offers a variety of gluten-free options that cater to different dietary needs. In this blog post, we’ll explore the Kona Grill gluten-free menu in detail, providing you with all the information you need to make an informed dining choice.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Kona Grill

Kona Grill takes gluten-free dining seriously. The restaurant has implemented protocols to minimize cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free dishes are prepared with care. While the menu is not entirely gluten-free, there are numerous options available that can be modified to meet gluten-free requirements. It’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can guide you through the menu and ensure your meal is prepared safely.

FAQs About Kona Grill’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Kona Grill’s gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

While Kona Grill takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s important to inform your server about your celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity. They can guide you on the safest options and ensure your meal is prepared with extra care.

2. Can I get gluten-free soy sauce at Kona Grill?

Yes, Kona Grill offers gluten-free tamari as a substitute for regular soy sauce. Be sure to request it when ordering sushi or any dish that typically comes with soy sauce.

3. Are there gluten-free options for kids at Kona Grill?

Yes, Kona Grill offers gluten-free options for kids, such as grilled chicken or steak with gluten-free sides. Be sure to inform your server about your child’s dietary needs.

4. Does Kona Grill have a dedicated gluten-free fryer?

Kona Grill does not have a dedicated gluten-free fryer, so it’s best to avoid fried items if you have a severe gluten allergy or celiac disease.

Always communicate your needs with your server to ensure your meal is prepared safely. Whether you’re craving sushi, grilled dishes, or a sweet treat, Kona Grill’s gluten-free menu has something to satisfy every palate.
