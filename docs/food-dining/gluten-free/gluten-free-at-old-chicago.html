Gluten-Free Dining at Old Chicago: A Comprehensive Guide

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom is a popular restaurant chain known for its wide variety of pizzas, pastas, and craft beers. However, for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. Fortunately, Old Chicago has made strides in accommodating gluten-free diets, offering a range of options that allow everyone to enjoy their meals without worry. In this blog post, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Old Chicago, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into the specifics of Old Chicago’s gluten-free menu, it’s important to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from mild discomfort to severe digestive issues. Therefore, it’s crucial for restaurants to offer gluten-free options that are prepared in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Old Chicago

While Old Chicago offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take certain precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and alert the kitchen staff to take extra precautions.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing foods. Ask the staff about their procedures for preventing cross-contamination in the kitchen.
  • Check Ingredients: Some sauces, dressings, and toppings may contain hidden gluten. Always ask for a list of ingredients or consult the allergen menu provided by the restaurant.
  • Opt for Simple Dishes: Dishes with fewer ingredients are less likely to contain hidden gluten. For example, a simple grilled chicken breast with steamed vegetables is a safe bet.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Old Chicago

1. Does Old Chicago offer gluten-free beer?

Yes, Old Chicago offers a selection of gluten-free beers. Be sure to ask your server for the current gluten-free beer options available at your location.

2. Is the gluten-free pizza crust at Old Chicago safe for celiacs?

Old Chicago’s gluten-free pizza crust is designed to be safe for those with celiac disease. However, cross-contamination can still occur in the kitchen. It’s important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff to ensure they take the necessary precautions.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free pizza at Old Chicago?

Absolutely! Old Chicago allows you to customize your gluten-free pizza with a variety of toppings. Just make sure to choose toppings that are gluten-free and inform your server about your dietary restrictions.

4. Are there gluten-free dessert options at Old Chicago?

Yes, Old Chicago offers gluten-free dessert options such as flourless chocolate cake and fresh fruit. These desserts are prepared to be gluten-free, but always double-check with your server to ensure they meet your dietary needs.
