Why Cafe 82 Stands Out for Gluten-Free Dining

Cafe 82 has earned a reputation for its commitment to inclusivity and quality. Unlike many establishments that offer a limited selection of gluten-free items, Cafe 82 has curated an entire menu dedicated to gluten-sensitive patrons. From breakfast classics to hearty dinners, every dish is crafted with care, ensuring that gluten-free diners can enjoy a full culinary experience. The kitchen staff is trained in cross-contamination prevention, making it a safe choice for those with celiac disease or severe gluten allergies.

FAQs About Cafe 82’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Cafe 82’s gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Cafe 82 takes cross-contamination seriously. The kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free ingredients separately, and dedicated equipment is used to prepare gluten-free dishes.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Cafe 82 offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the quinoa and roasted vegetable bowl and the avocado toast.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While the gluten-free menu is already extensive, Cafe 82 is happy to accommodate special requests whenever possible. Just inform your server of your dietary needs.

4. Are the gluten-free desserts made in-house?

Yes, all gluten-free desserts are made in-house using high-quality ingredients to ensure freshness and flavor.
