Why Choose Garcia's for Gluten-Free Dining?

Garcia's has built a reputation for its dedication to providing high-quality, flavorful meals that cater to diverse dietary needs. Their gluten-free menu is no exception. FAQs About Garcia's Gluten-Free Menu

Yes, Garcia's takes extensive measures to prevent cross-contamination, making their gluten-free menu safe for individuals with celiac disease. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure extra precautions are taken.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Garcia's offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the Vegan Gluten-Free Stir-Fry and the Quinoa Salad. Be sure to check with your server for the latest vegan options.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, Garcia's is happy to accommodate customizations to meet your dietary needs. Just let your server know, and they will work with the kitchen to prepare your meal accordingly.

4. Are the gluten-free desserts made in-house?

Yes, all of Garcia's gluten-free desserts are made in-house using high-quality, gluten-free ingredients. This ensures freshness and flavor in every bite.
