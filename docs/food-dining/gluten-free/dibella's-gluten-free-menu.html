FAQs About DiBella's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is DiBella's gluten-free bread safe for those with celiac disease?

Yes, DiBella's uses Udi's gluten-free bread, which is certified gluten-free and safe for those with celiac disease. However, cross-contamination is always a risk in any kitchen, so it's important to inform the staff of your dietary needs.

2. Are all DiBella's salads gluten-free?

Most of DiBella's salads are gluten-free, but it's important to check the ingredients and ask the staff if you have any concerns. Some dressings and toppings may contain gluten, so always double-check.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free sub or bowl?

Absolutely! DiBella's offers a wide range of toppings and condiments, so you can customize your gluten-free sub or bowl to your liking. Just make sure to inform the staff of your dietary restrictions.

4. Does DiBella's offer gluten-free desserts?

Currently, DiBella's does not offer gluten-free desserts. However, they are constantly updating their menu, so it's worth checking back for new additions.
