Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies. For others, it’s a lifestyle choice aimed at improving overall health. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, reducing gluten intake can lead to improved digestion, increased energy levels, and better overall well-being.
