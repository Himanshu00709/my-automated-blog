Nando's Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Nando's, the beloved South African restaurant chain known for its flame-grilled PERi-PERi chicken, has become a global favorite. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like a challenge. Fortunately, Nando's has embraced the need for gluten-free options, offering a variety of dishes that cater to gluten-free diets. In this blog post, we’ll explore Nando's gluten-free menu in detail, providing you with everything you need to know to enjoy a safe and delicious meal.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Nando's

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it’s a necessity for many people. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Nando's has recognized this and has made significant efforts to accommodate gluten-free diners. Their menu includes a range of gluten-free options, from starters to mains, sides, and even desserts.

However, it’s important to note that while Nando's offers gluten-free options, cross-contamination can still occur in the kitchen. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff and ask about their preparation methods to ensure your meal is as safe as possible.

FAQs About Nando's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Nando's chicken gluten-free?

Yes, Nando's chicken is gluten-free. However, it’s important to note that cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen, so if you have severe gluten sensitivity, inform the staff to take extra precautions.

2. Are Nando's sauces gluten-free?

Most of Nando's PERi-PERi sauces are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to check the label or ask the staff to be sure.

3. Can I get gluten-free bread at Nando's?

Nando's does not currently offer gluten-free bread, but you can enjoy their gluten-free options without bread.

4. Are Nando's chips gluten-free?

Yes, Nando's PERi-PERi chips are gluten-free and make a great side dish.

5. Does Nando's have a dedicated gluten-free fryer?

Nando's does not have a dedicated gluten-free fryer, so there is a risk of cross-contamination. If you have severe gluten sensitivity, it’s best to avoid fried items.

Conclusion

Nando's has made significant strides in offering gluten-free options, making it a great choice for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. From starters to mains, sides, and desserts, there’s something for everyone on the gluten-free menu. While cross-contamination is always a possibility, Nando's staff are generally well-informed and willing to accommodate special requests. So, the next time you’re craving some PERi-PERi chicken, rest assured that Nando's has you covered with a variety of gluten-free options to enjoy.
