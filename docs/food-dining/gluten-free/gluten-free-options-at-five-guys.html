Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys: A Comprehensive Guide

Five Guys is a popular fast-food chain known for its delicious burgers, fries, and milkshakes. However, for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can be a challenge. The good news is that Five Guys offers several gluten-free options that allow you to enjoy a meal without worrying about gluten. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free options available at Five Guys, how to navigate the menu, and tips for ensuring a safe dining experience.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Five Guys

Five Guys is committed to providing a safe dining experience for customers with dietary restrictions. While the restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. It’s important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff when ordering to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

Gluten-Free Menu Options

Five Guys offers a variety of gluten-free options, including burgers, hot dogs, and fries. Below is a detailed breakdown of the gluten-free items you can enjoy:

Item Description Gluten-Free Status
Burgers (No Bun) Five Guys burgers can be ordered without a bun and wrapped in lettuce. All burger toppings are gluten-free, including cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, grilled onions, grilled mushrooms, and jalapeños. Gluten-Free
Hot Dogs (No Bun) Hot dogs can also be ordered without a bun and wrapped in lettuce. Be sure to check with the staff about the hot dog brand used, as some may contain gluten. Gluten-Free (Check with Staff)
Fries Five Guys fries are cooked in 100% peanut oil and are gluten-free. However, they are cooked in the same fryer as other items, so there is a risk of cross-contamination. Gluten-Free (Risk of Cross-Contamination)
Milkshakes Five Guys milkshakes are gluten-free and come in a variety of flavors, including vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, and more. Be sure to check the ingredients of any mix-ins for gluten. Gluten-Free

Tips for a Safe Gluten-Free Experience

When dining at Five Guys, it’s important to take extra precautions to ensure your meal is gluten-free. Here are some tips to help you navigate the menu safely:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff that you have a gluten allergy or sensitivity. They can take extra care to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Order Without a Bun: Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns, so opt for a lettuce wrap instead.
  • Check Ingredients: Some toppings and condiments may contain gluten. Always double-check with the staff before adding them to your meal.
  • Be Aware of Cross-Contamination: While Five Guys takes precautions, cross-contamination is always a risk in a shared kitchen. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, consider the potential risks.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys

1. Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free as they are cooked in 100% peanut oil. However, they are cooked in the same fryer as other items, so there is a risk of cross-contamination.

2. Does Five Guys offer gluten-free buns?

No, Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns. You can order your burger or hot dog without a bun and have it wrapped in lettuce instead.

3. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys milkshakes are gluten-free. However, be sure to check the ingredients of any mix-ins for gluten.

4. Can I trust Five Guys to prepare a gluten-free meal?

While Five Guys takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, they do not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s important to communicate your needs to the staff and be aware of the potential risks.

Conclusion

Five Guys offers a variety of gluten-free options that allow you to enjoy a delicious meal without worrying about gluten. From burgers and hot dogs wrapped in lettuce to gluten-free fries and milkshakes, there’s something for everyone. However, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff and be aware of the potential risks of cross-contamination. With these tips in mind, you can safely enjoy a gluten-free meal at Five Guys.
