Yes, Wildflower takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. They follow strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination, and their staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with the utmost care.

Are there vegan options available on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Wildflower offers a variety of vegan and gluten-free options, such as the quinoa salad and the gluten-free pasta primavera. Be sure to inform your server of any dietary restrictions, and they will be happy to assist you.

Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While Wildflower already offers a wide range of gluten-free dishes, they are also happy to accommodate custom requests whenever possible. Just let your server know your preferences, and they will do their best to meet your needs.
