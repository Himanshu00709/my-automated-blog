Flaming Grill has earned a reputation for its commitment to providing safe and flavorful gluten-free options. FAQs About the Flaming Grill Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Flaming Grill gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Flaming Grill takes great care to prevent cross-contamination. Their kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders separately, ensuring a safe dining experience for those with celiac disease.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Flaming Grill offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the Vegetable Spring Rolls and the Berry Parfait.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While many dishes are already gluten-free, Flaming Grill is happy to accommodate customizations whenever possible. Just inform your server of your dietary needs.

4. Are the gluten-free options clearly labeled on the menu?

Yes, Flaming Grill clearly labels all gluten-free items on their menu, making it easy for diners to identify suitable options.
