How Carrabba's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for those with gluten sensitivities is cross-contamination. Carrabba's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free dishes are safe to eat. Here’s how they do it:

  • Separate Cooking Utensils: Carrabba's uses separate utensils, pans, and preparation areas for gluten-free dishes to avoid any cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: All staff members are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and how to handle gluten-free orders safely.
  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Menu: Carrabba's offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, making it easier for guests to identify safe options.
  • Regular Audits: The restaurant conducts regular audits to ensure that their gluten-free protocols are being followed correctly.

These measures help to create a safe dining environment for those with gluten sensitivities, allowing them to enjoy their meal without worry.
