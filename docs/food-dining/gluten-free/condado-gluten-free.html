How Condado Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Condado takes this seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Dedicated Prep Areas: Condado uses separate prep areas and utensils for gluten-free items to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed.
  • Clear Labeling: The menu clearly labels gluten-free options, making it easy for customers to identify safe choices.
  • Ingredient Transparency: Condado provides detailed ingredient lists, so customers can make informed decisions about their meals.
