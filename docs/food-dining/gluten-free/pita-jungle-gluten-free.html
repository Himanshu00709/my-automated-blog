How Pita Jungle Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Pita Jungle takes this issue seriously by implementing strict protocols in their kitchens. Here’s how they ensure a safe dining experience for gluten-free customers:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in separate areas to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Gluten-Free Ingredients: The restaurant sources high-quality, certified gluten-free ingredients for their dishes.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care and are knowledgeable about dietary restrictions.
