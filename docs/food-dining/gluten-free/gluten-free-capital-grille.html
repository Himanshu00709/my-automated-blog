Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Before diving into the specifics of Capital Grille's gluten-free offerings, it's essential to understand what gluten-free dining entails. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms ranging from mild discomfort to severe gastrointestinal issues.

Restaurants that cater to gluten-free diners must take extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free dishes are prepared separately from those containing gluten. Capital Grille has embraced this challenge, offering a variety of gluten-free options that do not compromise on flavor or quality.

Preparation and Cross-Contamination

One of the most critical aspects of gluten-free dining is ensuring that dishes are prepared in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination. Capital Grille takes this responsibility seriously, implementing strict protocols to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings.

When ordering a gluten-free meal at Capital Grille, it's essential to inform your server of your dietary restrictions. The kitchen staff will then take extra precautions, such as using separate utensils and cooking surfaces, to prevent cross-contamination. Additionally, Capital Grille's chefs are trained to handle gluten-free requests with care, ensuring that each dish meets the highest standards of safety and quality.
