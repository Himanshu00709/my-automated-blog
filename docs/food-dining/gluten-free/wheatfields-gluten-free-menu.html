The Importance of Gluten-Free Dining

Before we delve into the specifics of Wheatfields’ gluten-free menu, it’s essential to understand why gluten-free dining is so crucial for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Even trace amounts of gluten can trigger symptoms ranging from digestive discomfort to long-term damage to the small intestine. For these individuals, dining out can be a minefield, as cross-contamination is a constant concern. Wheatfields has addressed these concerns head-on, ensuring that their gluten-free menu is not only delicious but also safe for those with gluten-related disorders.
