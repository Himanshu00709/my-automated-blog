Cross-Contamination Concerns

While Applebee's has made significant efforts to provide gluten-free options, cross-contamination remains a concern for those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease. The restaurant has implemented several measures to minimize this risk, including:

  • Using separate cooking utensils and equipment for gluten-free dishes.
  • Training staff on the importance of avoiding cross-contamination.
  • Clearly labeling gluten-free items on the menu.

However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions and ask about the kitchen's practices to ensure your meal is prepared safely.
