Exploring Gluten-Free Options at bartaco

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free dining options has skyrocketed, driven by both medical necessity and lifestyle choices. For those with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those simply opting for a gluten-free diet, finding restaurants that cater to these needs can be a challenge. Enter bartaco, a trendy and vibrant restaurant chain known for its upscale street food and coastal vibe. But does bartaco live up to the gluten-free hype? Let’s dive into the details.

What Makes bartaco a Gluten-Free Haven?

bartaco has carved out a niche for itself by offering a menu that is not only flavorful but also inclusive. The restaurant prides itself on using fresh, high-quality ingredients, many of which are naturally gluten-free. From their tacos to their salads and sides, bartaco has made a conscious effort to accommodate gluten-free diners without compromising on taste or quality.

One of the standout features of bartaco’s gluten-free offerings is their corn tortillas. Unlike many other restaurants that use flour tortillas, bartaco’s tacos are served on soft, warm corn tortillas that are naturally gluten-free. This makes it easy for gluten-free diners to enjoy a variety of taco options without worry.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

For those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease, cross-contamination is a significant concern. bartaco takes this issue seriously and has implemented measures to minimize the risk of cross-contamination in their kitchens. However, it’s important to note that bartaco is not a dedicated gluten-free facility, so there is always a slight risk of cross-contamination.

If you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s recommended to inform your server when you place your order. The staff at bartaco are well-trained and will do their best to accommodate your needs, but it’s always better to err on the side of caution.

Yes, bartaco’s guacamole is gluten-free and is served with plantain chips, which are also gluten-free.

5. How does bartaco handle cross-contamination?

bartaco takes steps to minimize cross-contamination, but they are not a dedicated gluten-free facility. If you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s best to inform your server so they can take extra precautions.
