Union Jacks Gluten-Free Menu: A Culinary Delight for All

Union Jacks, the renowned British-inspired restaurant chain, has long been celebrated for its innovative approach to pizza and sharing plates. With a focus on quality ingredients and bold flavors, Union Jacks has carved out a niche in the culinary world. But what truly sets them apart is their commitment to inclusivity, particularly through their gluten-free menu. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. Union Jacks, however, has risen to the occasion, offering a diverse and delicious gluten-free menu that ensures everyone can enjoy their dining experience to the fullest.

Why Union Jacks Stands Out for Gluten-Free Dining

Union Jacks has gone above and beyond to cater to the gluten-free community. Their gluten-free menu is not just an afterthought; it’s a carefully curated selection of dishes that maintain the same high standards as their regular menu. From gluten-free pizzas to sharing plates and desserts, Union Jacks ensures that every dish is crafted with care, using gluten-free ingredients and preparation methods to avoid cross-contamination.

FAQs About Union Jacks Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the gluten-free menu suitable for celiacs?

Yes, Union Jacks takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for those with celiac disease. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Are all gluten-free dishes clearly marked on the menu?

Absolutely. Union Jacks clearly labels all gluten-free options on their menu, making it easy for diners to identify suitable dishes.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

While Union Jacks offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, they are also happy to accommodate customizations where possible. Just inform your server of your dietary requirements.

4. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Yes, Union Jacks offers a selection of vegan dishes that are also gluten-free. Be sure to check the menu or ask your server for recommendations.

Whether you’re craving a gluten-free pizza, a sharing plate, or a decadent dessert, Union Jacks has you covered.
