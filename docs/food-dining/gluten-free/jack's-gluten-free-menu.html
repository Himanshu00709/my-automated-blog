FAQs About Jack's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Jack's gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Jack's takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. They follow strict protocols to avoid cross-contamination, including using separate cooking utensils and preparation areas.

2. Are all ingredients in the gluten-free menu items certified gluten-free?

Jack's sources certified gluten-free ingredients for their gluten-free menu items. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about any specific dietary concerns to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While Jack's offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, some dishes on the regular menu can be modified to be gluten-free. Please consult with your server to discuss customization options.
