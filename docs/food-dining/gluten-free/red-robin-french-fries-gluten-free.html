Red Robin French Fries: A Gluten-Free Delight

When it comes to dining out, finding gluten-free options can be a challenge, especially when you're craving something as universally loved as French fries. Red Robin, a popular American chain known for its gourmet burgers and bottomless fries, has become a go-to spot for those seeking gluten-free options. But are Red Robin French Fries truly gluten-free? In this article, we’ll dive deep into the gluten-free status of Red Robin French Fries, explore their preparation process, and provide tips for gluten-sensitive diners.

Are Red Robin French Fries Gluten-Free?

Yes, Red Robin French Fries are gluten-free! Red Robin takes pride in offering a variety of gluten-free options, and their famous bottomless steak fries are no exception. The fries are made from whole potatoes, cut in-house, and cooked in a dedicated fryer to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing items. This makes them a safe and delicious choice for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

How Red Robin Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Red Robin has implemented several measures to ensure that their French fries remain gluten-free:

  • Dedicated Fryers: Red Robin uses separate fryers for their French fries, ensuring that they do not come into contact with gluten-containing foods like breaded chicken or onion rings.
  • Gluten-Free Menu: Red Robin offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, which includes their French fries, making it easier for customers to identify safe options.
  • Staff Training: Red Robin staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.

Tips for Gluten-Free Dining at Red Robin

While Red Robin is known for its gluten-free options, it’s always a good idea to take extra precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable gluten-free experience at Red Robin:

  • Communicate with Staff: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. Red Robin staff are trained to accommodate gluten-free requests, but clear communication is key.
  • Check the Gluten-Free Menu: Red Robin’s gluten-free menu is regularly updated, so be sure to review it before placing your order.
  • Avoid Shared Condiments: Some condiments, like ketchup, may be shared with gluten-containing items. Consider asking for individual packets to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Double-Check Your Order: When your food arrives, double-check that it matches your gluten-free order. If you have any doubts, don’t hesitate to ask the staff.

FAQs About Red Robin French Fries and Gluten-Free Options

1. Are Red Robin French Fries cooked in a dedicated fryer?

Yes, Red Robin French Fries are cooked in a dedicated fryer to prevent cross-contamination with gluten-containing foods.

2. Does Red Robin offer a gluten-free menu?

Yes, Red Robin provides a dedicated gluten-free menu that includes their French fries, burgers, and other options.

Red Robin takes gluten-free dining seriously and has measures in place to reduce cross-contamination. However, individuals with celiac disease should always communicate their needs clearly to the staff and take extra precautions.

4. Are Red Robin’s gluten-free buns safe for celiacs?

Red Robin offers gluten-free buns that are prepared separately to avoid cross-contamination. However, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure proper handling.

5. What other gluten-free options does Red Robin offer?

In addition to French fries, Red Robin offers a variety of gluten-free options, including salads, burgers with gluten-free buns, and some appetizers. Be sure to check their gluten-free menu for the latest offerings.

Red Robin French Fries are a gluten-free delight that can be enjoyed by everyone, including those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.
