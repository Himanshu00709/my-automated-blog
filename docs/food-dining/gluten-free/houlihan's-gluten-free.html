How Houlihan's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Houlihan's takes this seriously by implementing strict kitchen protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. Their staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that gluten-free meals are prepared separately and with dedicated utensils.

Yes, Houlihan's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination by using separate utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free meals.
