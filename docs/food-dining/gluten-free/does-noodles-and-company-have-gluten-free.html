Does Noodles and Company Have Gluten-Free Options?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often be a challenge. The need to avoid gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, requires careful consideration of menu options. Noodles and Company, a popular fast-casual restaurant chain known for its diverse noodle dishes, has made strides in accommodating gluten-free diets. In this article, we’ll explore whether Noodles and Company offers gluten-free options, how they handle cross-contamination, and what you can expect when dining there.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Noodles and Company

Noodles and Company has recognized the growing demand for gluten-free options and has taken steps to cater to this audience. The restaurant offers a variety of dishes that can be modified to be gluten-free, ensuring that those with dietary restrictions can still enjoy a flavorful meal. However, it’s important to note that while the restaurant provides gluten-free options, cross-contamination is still a possibility due to the shared kitchen environment.

Gluten-Free Menu Options

Noodles and Company offers several gluten-free dishes, including pasta and rice-based meals. Here’s a breakdown of some of the gluten-free options available:

Dish Description
Gluten-Free Pasta Made with rice flour, this pasta is a great alternative for those avoiding gluten. It can be paired with various sauces and toppings.
Gluten-Free Rice Noodles These noodles are a staple in many Asian-inspired dishes and are naturally gluten-free.
Gluten-Free Zoodles For a low-carb option, zucchini noodles (zoodles) are available and can be customized with your choice of sauce.
Gluten-Free Salads Several salads on the menu can be made gluten-free by omitting croutons or other gluten-containing ingredients.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While Noodles and Company offers gluten-free options, it’s crucial to understand that the restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. This means that cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients is possible. The restaurant takes precautions to minimize this risk, such as using separate utensils and cooking equipment when preparing gluten-free dishes. However, individuals with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease should exercise caution and communicate their dietary needs clearly to the staff.

How to Order Gluten-Free at Noodles and Company

When dining at Noodles and Company, it’s essential to communicate your dietary restrictions to the staff. Here are some tips for ordering gluten-free:

  • Ask for the Gluten-Free Menu: Noodles and Company provides a gluten-free menu that lists all the available options. This can help you make an informed choice.
  • Specify Your Needs: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease so they can take extra precautions during preparation.
  • Double-Check Ingredients: Some sauces and toppings may contain gluten, so it’s important to verify the ingredients before ordering.
  • Consider Cross-Contamination: If you have a severe allergy, you may want to inquire about the kitchen’s procedures for preventing cross-contamination.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Noodles and Company

1. Are all Noodles and Company locations gluten-free friendly?

While most Noodles and Company locations offer gluten-free options, the availability and preparation methods may vary by location. It’s always a good idea to check with your local restaurant before dining.

2. Can I trust the gluten-free pasta at Noodles and Company?

The gluten-free pasta at Noodles and Company is made with rice flour and is generally safe for those with gluten intolerance. However, due to the risk of cross-contamination, individuals with celiac disease should proceed with caution.

3. Are there any gluten-free desserts at Noodles and Company?

As of now, Noodles and Company does not offer gluten-free desserts. However, you can enjoy a variety of gluten-free main dishes and salads.

4. How does Noodles and Company handle cross-contamination?

Noodles and Company takes steps to minimize cross-contamination by using separate utensils and cooking equipment for gluten-free dishes. However, the restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, so there is still a risk of cross-contact.

Conclusion

Noodles and Company has made significant efforts to accommodate gluten-free diners by offering a variety of gluten-free pasta, rice noodles, and salads. While the restaurant takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, it’s important for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten allergies to communicate their needs clearly and be aware of the potential risks. By following the tips outlined in this article, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying meal at Noodles and Company while adhering to your gluten-free diet.
