Ruth's Chris Steakhouse Gluten-Free Menu: A Detailed Guide

Ruth's Chris Steakhouse is renowned for its premium steaks, elegant ambiance, and exceptional service. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Ruth's Chris Steakhouse has made it easier by offering a variety of gluten-free options. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free menu at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse, providing you with all the information you need to enjoy a safe and delicious dining experience.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse

Ruth's Chris Steakhouse takes dietary restrictions seriously, and their gluten-free menu is a testament to their commitment to accommodating all guests. The restaurant ensures that gluten-free dishes are prepared with care to avoid cross-contamination. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary needs so they can take extra precautions.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse

When dining gluten-free at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to your server. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Inform Your Server: Always let your server know about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and ensure your meal is prepared safely.
  • Ask About Preparation: Inquire about how your food is prepared to avoid cross-contamination. For example, ensure that your steak is not cooked on the same grill as items containing gluten.
  • Check Sauces and Dressings: Some sauces and dressings may contain gluten. Always ask for gluten-free options or request them on the side.
  • Double-Check Ingredients: If you’re unsure about any ingredient, don’t hesitate to ask. The staff at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse are well-trained and will be happy to assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Ruth's Chris Steakhouse gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Ruth's Chris Steakhouse takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your condition. They can take extra steps to ensure your meal is safe.

Are the fries at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse gluten-free?

Unfortunately, the fries at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse are not gluten-free as they are cooked in shared fryers. It’s best to opt for other gluten-free sides like mashed potatoes or steamed vegetables.

Can I get gluten-free bread at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse?

Ruth's Chris Steakhouse does not currently offer gluten-free bread. However, you can enjoy other gluten-free options like salads and steaks.

Are the desserts at Ruth's Chris Steakhouse gluten-free?

Some desserts, like the Crème Brûlée and fresh berries, are gluten-free. Always confirm with your server to ensure the dessert you choose is safe for your dietary needs.
