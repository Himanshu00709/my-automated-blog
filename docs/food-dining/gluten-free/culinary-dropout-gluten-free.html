Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can trigger an immune response that damages the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause discomfort, bloating, and other digestive issues. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to improve their health and well-being. Culinary Dropout recognizes the importance of providing safe and delicious options for gluten-free diners, ensuring that everyone can enjoy their meals without worry.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Culinary Dropout

Dining out gluten-free can be a challenge, but with a little preparation, you can enjoy a stress-free meal at Culinary Dropout. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Ask About Preparation: Don’t hesitate to ask how dishes are prepared to ensure there’s no risk of cross-contamination.

  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s always a good idea to check for the latest gluten-free offerings when you visit.
