Why Choose Gluten-Free Sauces?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and even long-term damage to the small intestine. For those who must avoid gluten, finding safe and delicious options can be challenging. Noodles and Company's gluten-free sauces offer a solution, allowing you to enjoy flavorful meals without compromising your health.

How Noodles and Company Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Noodles and Company takes gluten-free dining seriously. They have implemented strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchens. Here are some of the measures they take:

  • Dedicated Cooking Utensils: Separate pots, pans, and utensils are used for gluten-free dishes to avoid cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free meals are prepared in designated areas to minimize the risk of contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all safety protocols are followed.

FAQs About Noodles and Company Gluten-Free Sauces

1. Are all Noodles and Company sauces gluten-free?

No, not all sauces at Noodles and Company are gluten-free. However, they do offer a selection of gluten-free sauces, including Marinara, Pesto, Alfredo, and Spicy Korean BBQ. Always check the menu or ask a staff member for the most up-to-date information.

2. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, Noodles and Company allows you to customize your order. You can choose gluten-free noodles and pair them with gluten-free sauces. Be sure to inform the staff of your dietary restrictions so they can take the necessary precautions.

3. Are Noodles and Company's gluten-free sauces suitable for vegans?

Some of the gluten-free sauces, like the Marinara and Pesto, are vegan-friendly. However, others, such as the Alfredo sauce, contain dairy and are not suitable for vegans. Always check the ingredients list or ask a staff member for clarification.

4. How does Noodles and Company prevent cross-contamination?

Noodles and Company uses dedicated cooking utensils, separate preparation areas, and thorough staff training to prevent cross-contamination. However, if you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it's always a good idea to communicate your needs clearly to the staff.
