Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to more severe health complications. As a result, many people are turning to gluten-free diets to manage their health. Salsarita's recognizes the importance of catering to this growing demographic and has made significant efforts to provide gluten-free options that don't compromise on taste or quality.

How Salsarita's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Salsarita's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free offerings:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Salsarita's uses separate preparation areas for gluten-free ingredients to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that utensils and surfaces are properly cleaned before use.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, making it easy for customers to identify safe options.
  • Ingredient Transparency: Salsarita's provides detailed ingredient information, allowing customers to make informed choices.

FAQs About Salsarita's Gluten-Free Options

1. Are Salsarita's corn tortillas gluten-free?

Yes, Salsarita's corn tortillas are gluten-free. They are made from 100% corn and do not contain any wheat, barley, or rye.

3. Are Salsarita's salsas and guacamole gluten-free?

Yes, all of Salsarita's salsas and guacamole are gluten-free. They are made from fresh, natural ingredients and do not contain any gluten-containing additives.

Currently, Salsarita's does not offer gluten-free desserts. However, their main menu items provide plenty of gluten-free options to satisfy your cravings.
