How Bareburger Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Bareburger takes several precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Bareburger uses separate preparation areas for gluten-free items to avoid cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Gluten-Free Buns: The gluten-free buns are stored separately and handled with care to prevent contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to understand the importance of gluten-free preparation and are knowledgeable about the menu's gluten-free options.
  • Clear Labeling: Menu items are clearly labeled to indicate which options are gluten-free, making it easier for customers to make informed choices.
