Precautions and Cross-Contamination

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Buzzard Billy's takes this issue seriously. The kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, using separate utensils, cutting boards, and cooking surfaces. Additionally, gluten-free dishes are prepared in a dedicated area of the kitchen to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.

It’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions when you arrive at the restaurant. The staff at Buzzard Billy's is well-versed in the menu and can guide you to the safest options. They can also answer any questions you may have about the preparation of your meal.
