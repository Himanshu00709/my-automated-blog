Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Famous Toastery

While Famous Toastery offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take a few precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten-free dietary needs. They can guide you to the safest options and alert the kitchen to take extra precautions.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur in kitchens that handle gluten-containing ingredients. Ask the staff about their procedures to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Review the Menu Carefully: While many dishes are naturally gluten-free, some may contain hidden gluten. Take the time to review the menu and ask questions if you’re unsure.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Famous Toastery

1. Does Famous Toastery have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen?

Famous Toastery does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, but they take precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. Always inform your server about your dietary needs so they can take extra care in preparing your meal.

2. Are the gluten-free pancakes at Famous Toastery made with a separate griddle?

While the gluten-free pancakes are made with gluten-free flour, they may be cooked on the same griddle as regular pancakes. If you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s best to ask the staff about their cooking procedures.

3. Can I customize any dish to be gluten-free?

Many dishes at Famous Toastery can be customized to be gluten-free. However, not all dishes can be modified due to the nature of their ingredients. It’s always best to ask your server for recommendations.

4. Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

While some gluten-free options are clearly marked, others may require you to ask your server for clarification. Don’t hesitate to ask for assistance in identifying gluten-free dishes.
