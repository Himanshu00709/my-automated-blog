FAQs About Saltgrass Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Saltgrass gluten-free menu available online?

Yes, Saltgrass Steak House provides a gluten-free menu that can be accessed online. You can download the PDF version of the menu from their official website or request a copy at the restaurant.

2. Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

While the gluten-free options are not always marked on the standard menu, the staff is well-trained to assist you in identifying which dishes are safe for gluten-free diets. It’s always a good idea to double-check with your server.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Absolutely! Saltgrass is known for its flexibility in accommodating dietary restrictions. You can request modifications to certain dishes to make them gluten-free, such as omitting croutons from a salad or substituting a gluten-free side.

4. How does Saltgrass prevent cross-contamination?

Saltgrass takes cross-contamination seriously and follows strict protocols to minimize the risk. However, it’s important to inform your server about your gluten intolerance so they can take extra precautions in the kitchen.

5. Are there gluten-free dessert options at Saltgrass?

While the dessert menu at Saltgrass is limited in gluten-free options, you can always enjoy a fresh fruit cup or a scoop of gluten-free ice cream to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Conclusion

Dining out gluten-free doesn’t have to be a challenge, especially when you choose a restaurant like Saltgrass Steak House. With a variety of gluten-free options available, from appetizers to main courses and sides, you can enjoy a delicious and worry-free meal. Always remember to communicate your dietary needs with your server to ensure the best dining experience. Whether you’re a steak lover or prefer lighter fare, Saltgrass has something for everyone on their gluten-free menu.
