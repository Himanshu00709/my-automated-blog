Tips for Dining Gluten-Free in Lake Placid

When dining out in Lake Placid, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. Here are some tips for navigating the gluten-free dining scene:

  • Call Ahead: Before visiting a restaurant, call ahead to inquire about their gluten-free options and how they handle cross-contamination.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask your server about the ingredients and preparation methods of your meal.
  • Check Menus Online: Many restaurants in Lake Placid have their menus available online, making it easier to identify gluten-free options before you arrive.
  • Bring Your Own Snacks: If you’re unsure about the availability of gluten-free options, consider bringing your own snacks or gluten-free bread.
