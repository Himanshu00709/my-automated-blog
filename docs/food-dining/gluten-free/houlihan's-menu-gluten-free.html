Houlihan's Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Houlihan's is a popular American restaurant chain known for its diverse menu and vibrant atmosphere. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Houlihan's has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diners, offering a variety of options that cater to their dietary needs. In this blog post, we’ll explore Houlihan's gluten-free menu in detail, providing you with all the information you need to enjoy a safe and delicious meal.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Houlihan's

Gluten-free dining is not just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Houlihan's recognizes this and has taken steps to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are both safe and satisfying.

Houlihan's gluten-free menu is designed to provide a wide range of options, from appetizers to desserts. The restaurant takes care to minimize cross-contamination, but it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure the utmost safety.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Houlihan's gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Houlihan's takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your dietary needs. They can guide you to the safest options and ensure that your meal is prepared with care.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, many of Houlihan's dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. Be sure to communicate your needs to your server, who can assist with modifications.

Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Houlihan's offers gluten-free options for kids, including grilled chicken and steamed vegetables. Be sure to ask your server for the latest gluten-free kids' menu items.

Does Houlihan's offer gluten-free bread or buns?

Houlihan's does not currently offer gluten-free bread or buns, but they do provide lettuce wraps as an alternative for sandwiches and burgers.

Conclusion

Houlihan's commitment to providing a diverse and safe gluten-free menu is commendable. With a variety of appetizers, entrees, and desserts, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Always remember to communicate your dietary needs to your server to ensure the best possible dining experience. Happy dining!
