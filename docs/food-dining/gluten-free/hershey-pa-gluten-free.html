Hershey, PA: A Gluten-Free Guide

Hershey, Pennsylvania, often referred to as "The Sweetest Place on Earth," is a destination known for its chocolatey delights and family-friendly attractions. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating a town famous for baked goods and treats can be a challenge. Fear not! Hershey, PA, is also a haven for gluten-free travelers, offering a variety of dining options, activities, and resources to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. In this guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about enjoying Hershey, PA, gluten-free.

Why Hershey, PA is a Great Destination for Gluten-Free Travelers

Hershey, PA, is more than just chocolate. The town is home to a growing number of restaurants, cafes, and bakeries that cater to gluten-free diets. Additionally, many of Hershey’s attractions, such as Hersheypark and The Hershey Story Museum, offer gluten-free options and accommodations. Whether you’re visiting for a day or planning a longer stay, Hershey has something for everyone, including those with dietary restrictions.

Gluten-Free Activities in Hershey

Hershey offers more than just great food. The town is packed with activities that are perfect for gluten-free travelers. Here are some highlights:

Hersheypark

Hersheypark is a must-visit for families and thrill-seekers alike. The park offers a variety of gluten-free snacks and meals, including gluten-free pizza, fries, and even chocolate treats. Be sure to check with food vendors for their gluten-free options.

The Hershey Story Museum

Learn about the history of Milton S. Hershey and the creation of the Hershey Company. The museum’s café offers gluten-free options, and the exhibits are a great way to immerse yourself in the town’s sweet history.

Hershey Gardens

For a more relaxed experience, visit Hershey Gardens. This beautiful botanical garden is a gluten-free-friendly attraction where you can enjoy nature without worrying about dietary restrictions.

Gluten-Free Shopping in Hershey

If you’re looking to stock up on gluten-free snacks or ingredients, Hershey has you covered. Many local grocery stores and specialty shops offer a wide range of gluten-free products. Here are a few places to check out:

  • Giant Food Stores: Offers a variety of gluten-free products, including bread, pasta, and snacks.
  • Weis Markets: Known for its extensive gluten-free section.
  • Hershey’s Chocolate World: While primarily known for chocolate, they also offer some gluten-free candy options.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Travel in Hershey, PA

Is Hersheypark gluten-free friendly?

Yes, Hersheypark offers several gluten-free options, including gluten-free pizza, fries, and desserts. Be sure to ask staff for assistance in identifying safe options.

Are there gluten-free bakeries in Hershey?

While Hershey doesn’t have a dedicated gluten-free bakery, many local bakeries and cafes offer gluten-free baked goods. The Hershey Pantry is a popular spot for gluten-free pancakes and muffins.

Can I find gluten-free chocolate in Hershey?

Yes, Hershey’s Chocolate World offers some gluten-free chocolate options. Be sure to check labels and ask staff for recommendations.

Conclusion

Hershey, PA, is a fantastic destination for gluten-free travelers. With a wide range of dining options, activities, and shopping opportunities, you can enjoy all that Hershey has to offer without compromising your dietary needs. Whether you’re indulging in gluten-free chocolate, exploring Hersheypark, or dining at a local restaurant, Hershey ensures that everyone can experience the sweetness of this charming town. So pack your bags and get ready for a gluten-free adventure in Hershey, PA!
