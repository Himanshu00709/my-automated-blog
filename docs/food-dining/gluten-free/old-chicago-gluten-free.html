Old Chicago Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom is a beloved restaurant chain known for its wide variety of pizzas, craft beers, and Italian-inspired dishes. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Old Chicago has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diets, offering a range of options that allow everyone to enjoy their meals without worry. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Old Chicago, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Old Chicago

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Old Chicago recognizes this and has taken steps to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe and delicious.

Old Chicago’s gluten-free menu includes a variety of options, from pizzas to salads and appetizers. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination, which is crucial for those with severe gluten sensitivities. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure that your meal is prepared safely.

Gluten-Free Menu Options at Old Chicago

Old Chicago offers a dedicated gluten-free menu that caters to a variety of tastes. Here’s a breakdown of some of the most popular gluten-free options:

Gluten-Free Pizzas

Pizza is the star of the show at Old Chicago, and their gluten-free crust is a game-changer for those who miss this classic dish. The gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour, resulting in a crispy yet chewy texture that rivals traditional crusts. You can customize your pizza with a variety of toppings, including fresh vegetables, meats, and cheeses.

Salads and Appetizers

If you’re in the mood for something lighter, Old Chicago offers several gluten-free salads and appetizers. The Classic Caesar Salad (without croutons) and the Spinach & Artichoke Dip (served with gluten-free tortilla chips) are popular choices. These dishes are packed with flavor and provide a satisfying start to your meal.

Entrees and Sides

While Old Chicago is best known for its pizzas, they also offer a selection of gluten-free entrees and sides. The Grilled Chicken Alfredo (with gluten-free pasta) and the Grilled Salmon are excellent options for those looking for a heartier meal. Additionally, their Garlic Parmesan Fries are a must-try for anyone craving a savory side dish.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Old Chicago

Dining out gluten-free can be a bit daunting, but with a few simple tips, you can enjoy your meal at Old Chicago with confidence:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and ensure that your meal is prepared safely.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: While Old Chicago takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to double-check. Ask about their preparation methods and whether they use separate utensils and cooking surfaces.
  • Explore the Gluten-Free Menu: Old Chicago’s gluten-free menu is extensive, so take your time to explore all the options. Don’t hesitate to ask for recommendations if you’re unsure what to order.

Gluten-Free Beer and Beverage Options

Old Chicago is also known for its impressive selection of craft beers. For those who enjoy a cold brew but need to avoid gluten, Old Chicago offers a variety of gluten-free beer options. These beers are brewed with gluten-free grains like sorghum, rice, or millet, providing a safe and enjoyable alternative for beer enthusiasts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Old Chicago’s gluten-free crust vegan?

No, Old Chicago’s gluten-free crust contains dairy and eggs, so it is not suitable for vegans. However, they do offer a variety of vegan toppings that can be added to their gluten-free pizzas.

Are the gluten-free options at Old Chicago safe for celiacs?

Old Chicago takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but they do not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. If you have celiac disease, it’s important to communicate your needs to the staff and ask about their preparation methods.

Can I order gluten-free pizza for delivery?

Yes, Old Chicago offers gluten-free pizza for both dine-in and delivery. However, be sure to specify your gluten-free needs when placing your order to ensure that your pizza is prepared correctly.

Conclusion

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom has made it easier than ever for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease to enjoy a delicious meal out. With a dedicated gluten-free menu, a variety of safe options, and a commitment to minimizing cross-contamination, Old Chicago is a great choice for gluten-free dining. Whether you’re craving a classic pizza, a hearty entree, or a refreshing gluten-free beer, Old Chicago has something for everyone. So next time you’re planning a night out, consider Old Chicago for a safe and satisfying gluten-free experience.
