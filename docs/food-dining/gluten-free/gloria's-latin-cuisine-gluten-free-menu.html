How Gloria's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Gloria's takes this issue seriously by implementing strict protocols in their kitchen. All gluten-free dishes are prepared in a separate area of the kitchen, and dedicated utensils and cookware are used to avoid any contact with gluten-containing ingredients. Additionally, the staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that every dish meets the highest standards of safety and quality.
