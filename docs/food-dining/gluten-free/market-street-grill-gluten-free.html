How Market Street Grill Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Market Street Grill takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure the safety of their gluten-free dishes:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in a dedicated area to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Trained Staff: The kitchen staff is trained in gluten-free food preparation and handling.
  • Clear Labeling: Gluten-free items are clearly labeled on the menu, making it easy for diners to identify safe options.
