Kona Grill Menu: A Gluten-Free Dining Experience

When it comes to dining out, finding a restaurant that caters to gluten-free diets can be a challenge. However, Kona Grill has emerged as a haven for those who need or prefer gluten-free options. With a diverse menu that combines American, Asian, and sushi-inspired dishes, Kona Grill offers a variety of gluten-free choices that don’t compromise on flavor or quality. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free options available at Kona Grill, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions about gluten-free dining at this popular restaurant.

Gluten-Free Options at Kona Grill

Kona Grill takes pride in offering a wide range of gluten-free dishes that cater to various dietary needs. Whether you’re in the mood for sushi, a hearty salad, or a flavorful entrée, Kona Grill has something for everyone. Below, we’ve broken down the gluten-free options by category to help you navigate the menu with ease.

Appetizers

Start your meal with one of Kona Grill’s gluten-free appetizers. The Edamame is a classic choice, steamed to perfection and lightly salted. Another popular option is the Shrimp and Lobster Dynamite, a flavorful dish made with shrimp, lobster, and a spicy dynamite sauce. Be sure to inform your server of your dietary restrictions to ensure that your dish is prepared without any gluten-containing ingredients.

Sushi

Sushi lovers will be pleased to know that Kona Grill offers several gluten-free sushi rolls. The Spicy Tuna Roll and the Salmon Avocado Roll are both gluten-free and made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Additionally, the Rainbow Roll is another excellent choice, featuring a variety of fish and avocado. Always double-check with your server to confirm that the sushi is prepared without soy sauce or other gluten-containing condiments.

Salads

For a lighter option, Kona Grill’s salads are a great choice. The Kona Chopped Salad is a gluten-free favorite, made with mixed greens, avocado, tomato, cucumber, and a tangy vinaigrette. The Asian Grilled Salmon Salad is another standout, featuring grilled salmon, mixed greens, and a flavorful Asian-inspired dressing. Be sure to ask for the dressing on the side to ensure it’s gluten-free.

Entrées

When it comes to main courses, Kona Grill offers several gluten-free entrées that are sure to satisfy. The Grilled Salmon is a popular choice, served with a side of seasonal vegetables and your choice of starch. The Filet Mignon is another excellent option, cooked to perfection and served with mashed potatoes and asparagus. For a taste of Asia, try the Thai Coconut Curry, made with your choice of protein and served with jasmine rice.

Desserts

No meal is complete without dessert, and Kona Grill has you covered with gluten-free options. The Crème Brûlée is a classic choice, featuring a rich custard topped with a caramelized sugar crust. The Seasonal Sorbet is another refreshing option, perfect for those looking for a lighter treat.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Kona Grill

While Kona Grill offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take certain precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server of your gluten-free dietary needs. They can guide you through the menu and ensure that your meal is prepared safely.
  • Ask About Preparation: Some dishes may be prepared with shared equipment or ingredients that contain gluten. Be sure to ask about the preparation process to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Check for Gluten-Free Sauces: Many sauces and dressings contain gluten. Always ask for gluten-free alternatives or request that your dish be prepared without sauce.
  • Review the Menu Online: Kona Grill’s menu is available online, making it easy to review gluten-free options before you visit the restaurant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kona Grill’s sushi gluten-free?

Many of Kona Grill’s sushi rolls are gluten-free, but it’s important to confirm with your server. Some rolls may contain soy sauce or other gluten-containing ingredients, so always double-check before ordering.

Does Kona Grill have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen?

Kona Grill does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, but they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. Be sure to inform your server of your dietary needs so they can take extra care when preparing your meal.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, many dishes at Kona Grill can be customized to be gluten-free. Your server can guide you through the menu and suggest modifications to accommodate your dietary needs.

Are there gluten-free dessert options at Kona Grill?

Yes, Kona Grill offers gluten-free dessert options such as Crème Brûlée and Seasonal Sorbet. Be sure to confirm with your server that the dessert is gluten-free before ordering.
