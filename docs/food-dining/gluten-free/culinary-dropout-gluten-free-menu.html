FAQs About Culinary Dropout’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Culinary Dropout’s gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Culinary Dropout takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free dishes are safe for those with celiac disease. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

2. Are there gluten-free options for vegetarians?

Absolutely! Culinary Dropout offers several vegetarian dishes that are also gluten-free, such as the Grilled Artichoke and the Seasonal Vegetable Plate.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

In many cases, yes. Culinary Dropout is known for its flexibility and willingness to accommodate dietary needs. Just let your server know, and they’ll do their best to customize your order.

4. Are the gluten-free desserts made in a separate kitchen?

While Culinary Dropout takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always best to ask your server about specific dishes, especially if you have severe gluten sensitivities.
