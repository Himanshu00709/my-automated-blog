Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten intolerance, or wheat allergies. Consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues for these individuals, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. Beyond medical necessity, many people choose gluten-free diets for personal health reasons, such as reducing inflammation or improving energy levels. Restaurants like Buca di Beppo play a crucial role in ensuring that everyone can enjoy a meal without compromising their health or dietary preferences.

How Buca Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Buca di Beppo takes this issue seriously by implementing strict protocols in their kitchens. Here’s how they ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in designated areas to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils and cookware are used exclusively for gluten-free meals.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care and attention to detail.
  • Clear Labeling: The gluten-free menu is clearly labeled, and staff are knowledgeable about the ingredients in each dish.
