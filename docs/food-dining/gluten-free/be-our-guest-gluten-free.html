Be Our Guest Gluten-Free: A Comprehensive Guide

Disney World is a magical place where dreams come true, and for those with dietary restrictions, it’s no different. The "Be Our Guest" restaurant, located in the heart of Magic Kingdom’s Fantasyland, is a must-visit for any Disney enthusiast. But what if you’re gluten-free? Fear not! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about enjoying a gluten-free meal at Be Our Guest.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Disney

Disney World is renowned for its commitment to accommodating guests with dietary restrictions, including gluten-free options. The chefs at Be Our Guest are well-trained in handling gluten-free requests, ensuring that your meal is not only safe but also delicious. Whether you’re celiac or simply avoiding gluten, you can dine with confidence at this iconic restaurant.

Gluten-Free Menu Options at Be Our Guest

Be Our Guest offers a variety of gluten-free options across its breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus. Here’s a breakdown of what you can expect:

Breakfast

Start your day with a magical breakfast that includes gluten-free options like:

  • Gluten-Free Mickey Waffles
  • Egg White Omelet with Gluten-Free Toast
  • Fresh Fruit Platter

Lunch

For lunch, you can enjoy a variety of gluten-free dishes, such as:

  • Grilled Steak with Pommes Frites (gluten-free fries)
  • Braised Pork with Seasonal Vegetables
  • Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Dinner

Dinner at Be Our Guest is a truly enchanting experience, with gluten-free options like:

  • Herb-Crusted Lamb Rack
  • Pan-Seared Sea Scallops
  • Gluten-Free Chocolate Mousse for dessert

How to Order Gluten-Free at Be Our Guest

When dining at Be Our Guest, it’s important to inform your server about your gluten-free needs. The chefs are more than happy to accommodate your dietary restrictions and will prepare your meal in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination. You can also speak directly with a chef to discuss your options and any specific concerns you may have.

Cross-Contamination and Safety Measures

Disney takes cross-contamination seriously, especially when it comes to gluten-free dining. At Be Our Guest, the kitchen staff follows strict protocols to ensure that gluten-free meals are prepared safely. This includes using separate utensils, cookware, and preparation areas. However, it’s always a good idea to double-check with your server or chef to ensure that all precautions are being taken.

What to Expect: A Gluten-Free Dining Experience

Dining at Be Our Guest is more than just a meal—it’s an experience. The restaurant is designed to make you feel like you’ve stepped into the Beast’s castle from "Beauty and the Beast." The ambiance is enchanting, with grand ballrooms, stained glass windows, and even a chance to meet the Beast himself. And the best part? You can enjoy all of this while sticking to your gluten-free diet.

Gluten-Free Dining Tips at Disney World

Here are some additional tips to make your gluten-free dining experience at Disney World even better:

  • Make a reservation in advance and note your dietary restrictions.
  • Download the My Disney Experience app to view gluten-free options at other restaurants.
  • Don’t hesitate to ask questions or request modifications to your meal.
  • Consider bringing gluten-free snacks, just in case.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Be Our Guest

Is Be Our Guest safe for celiacs?

Yes, Be Our Guest is safe for celiacs. The chefs are trained to handle gluten-free requests and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

Can I order gluten-free desserts at Be Our Guest?

Absolutely! Be Our Guest offers gluten-free dessert options, including a decadent chocolate mousse.

Do I need to make a special request for gluten-free meals?

Yes, it’s recommended to inform your server about your gluten-free needs when you arrive. You can also note your dietary restrictions when making a reservation.

Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Be Our Guest offers gluten-free options for kids, including gluten-free Mickey waffles and chicken tenders.

Conclusion

Dining at Be Our Guest is a magical experience, and being gluten-free doesn’t have to take away from that magic. With a variety of gluten-free options, a dedicated kitchen staff, and a commitment to safety, you can enjoy a delicious and worry-free meal at this iconic Disney restaurant. So, go ahead—be our guest, and indulge in a gluten-free feast fit for royalty!
