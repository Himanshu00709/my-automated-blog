Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Oregano's gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Oregano's takes great care to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchen. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

Can I customize my gluten-free pizza or pasta dish?

Absolutely! Oregano's allows you to customize your gluten-free dishes with a variety of toppings and sauces. Just let your server know your preferences, and they'll be happy to accommodate you.

Are there any gluten-free dessert options besides cookies and cheesecake?

While the gluten-free cookie and cheesecake are the most popular dessert options, Oregano's occasionally offers seasonal gluten-free desserts. Be sure to ask your server about any special gluten-free dessert offerings.

Does Oregano's offer gluten-free beer or other beverages?

Yes, Oregano's offers a selection of gluten-free beers and other beverages. Ask your server for recommendations, and they'll be happy to guide you.
