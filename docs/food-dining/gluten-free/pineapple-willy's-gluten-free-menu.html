Tips for Gluten-Free Dining at Pineapple Willy's

While Pineapple Willy's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s always a good idea to take extra precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and alert the kitchen staff to take extra care with your order.
  • Ask About Preparation: Don’t hesitate to ask how dishes are prepared. For example, ensure that fryers are dedicated to gluten-free items to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Check for Hidden Gluten: Some sauces and marinades may contain gluten. Always double-check with your server to confirm that all components of your dish are gluten-free.

FAQs About Pineapple Willy's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Pineapple Willy's gluten-free menu suitable for celiacs?

Yes, Pineapple Willy's takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, making it a safe option for those with celiac disease. However, it’s always best to inform your server about your condition so they can take extra care.

2. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Absolutely! Pineapple Willy's offers gluten-free options for kids, including grilled chicken and steamed vegetables. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

3. Can I get gluten-free bread or buns?

Yes, Pineapple Willy's offers gluten-free bread and buns upon request. Just let your server know, and they’ll be happy to accommodate your needs.

4. Are the fries gluten-free?

The fries at Pineapple Willy's are cooked in a shared fryer, which may pose a risk of cross-contamination. If you’re highly sensitive, it’s best to opt for a different side dish.
