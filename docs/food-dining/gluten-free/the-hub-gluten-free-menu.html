Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including damage to the small intestine. Others may experience gluten sensitivity, which can cause symptoms like bloating, headaches, and fatigue. Even for those without these conditions, a gluten-free diet can offer benefits such as improved digestion and increased energy levels. The Hub's gluten-free menu ensures that everyone can enjoy a meal without compromising their health or dietary preferences.

1. Is the Hub’s gluten-free menu suitable for celiacs?

Yes, the Hub takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! The Hub offers a variety of vegan and gluten-free dishes, ensuring that those with multiple dietary restrictions can still enjoy a delicious meal.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, the Hub is happy to accommodate customizations. Simply inform your server of your dietary needs, and they will ensure your meal is prepared accordingly.
