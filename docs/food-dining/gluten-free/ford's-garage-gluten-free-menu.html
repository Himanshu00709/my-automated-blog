Ford's Garage Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Ford's Garage, a popular restaurant chain known for its automotive-themed decor and hearty American cuisine, has made significant strides in catering to diverse dietary needs. Among these, their gluten-free menu stands out as a testament to their commitment to inclusivity. Whether you're a celiac sufferer, gluten-sensitive, or simply choosing a gluten-free lifestyle, Ford's Garage offers a variety of options that don't compromise on flavor or quality. In this article, we'll explore the gluten-free offerings at Ford's Garage, providing you with all the information you need to enjoy a safe and delicious dining experience.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Ford's Garage

Before diving into the menu, it's essential to understand how Ford's Garage approaches gluten-free dining. The restaurant takes great care to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free items are prepared separately from gluten-containing foods. This is crucial for individuals with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, as even trace amounts of gluten can cause adverse reactions. Ford's Garage staff are trained to handle gluten-free requests with the utmost care, making it a reliable choice for gluten-free diners.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Ford's Garage

While Ford's Garage offers a variety of gluten-free options, it's always a good idea to take extra precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable gluten-free experience:

  • Communicate Clearly: Inform your server about your gluten-free needs and any allergies or sensitivities you may have. This will help them guide you to the safest options on the menu.
  • Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to ask about ingredients, preparation methods, and potential cross-contamination risks. The staff at Ford's Garage are well-trained and will be happy to assist you.
  • Double-Check Sauces and Dressings: Some sauces and dressings may contain gluten, so always verify with your server before ordering.
  • Consider Cross-Contamination: While Ford's Garage takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, it's always a good idea to remind your server to take extra care when preparing your meal.

FAQs About Ford's Garage Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Ford's Garage gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Ford's Garage takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, but it's always best to inform your server about your celiac disease so they can take extra care in preparing your meal.

2. Are the gluten-free buns at Ford's Garage made in-house?

Yes, many items on the menu can be customized to be gluten-free. Just inform your server of your dietary needs, and they will guide you through the options.

4. Are there gluten-free dessert options at Ford's Garage?

Yes, Ford's Garage offers gluten-free dessert options such as flourless chocolate cake and a fresh fruit plate.
