Why Lucky 13 is a Great Choice for Gluten-Free Diners

Lucky 13 has earned a reputation for being a welcoming spot for all types of diners, including those with dietary restrictions. The restaurant takes pride in offering a diverse menu that caters to various dietary needs, including gluten-free options. Moreover, Lucky 13’s commitment to quality ingredients means that even their gluten-free options are made with the same care and attention to detail as their regular menu items. This dedication to quality and inclusivity makes Lucky 13 a standout choice for gluten-free diners.

FAQs About the Lucky 13 Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Lucky 13 gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Lucky 13 takes great care to minimize the risk of cross-contamination. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

While the gluten-free menu is primarily focused on accommodating gluten-free diners, there are a few vegan options available. Be sure to check with your server for the most up-to-date information.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free meal?

Absolutely! Lucky 13 is known for its flexibility and willingness to accommodate special requests. Feel free to ask your server about customizing your meal to suit your preferences.

4. Are the gluten-free buns and crusts made in-house?

Lucky 13 sources its gluten-free buns and crusts from trusted suppliers to ensure quality and safety. While they’re not made in-house, they’re carefully selected to meet the restaurant’s high standards.
