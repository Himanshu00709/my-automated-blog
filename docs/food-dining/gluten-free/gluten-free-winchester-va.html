Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. Even for those without celiac disease, gluten sensitivity can cause discomfort, bloating, and fatigue. Adopting a gluten-free diet can significantly improve quality of life for these individuals.
