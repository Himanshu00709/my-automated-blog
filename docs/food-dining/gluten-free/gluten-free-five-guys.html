Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys: A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, the rise of gluten-free awareness has led many restaurants, including Five Guys, to offer more accommodating options. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Five Guys, how to order safely, and what to expect when dining at this popular burger joint.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Five Guys

Five Guys is renowned for its customizable burgers, fries, and milkshakes. While the restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free menu, it does offer several gluten-free options that can be safely enjoyed by those avoiding gluten. The key is knowing what to order and how to communicate your dietary needs to the staff.

Five Guys takes food allergies seriously and has implemented procedures to minimize cross-contamination. However, it’s important to note that the restaurant is not a gluten-free facility, so there is always a risk of cross-contact. If you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, it’s crucial to weigh the risks before dining.

How to Order Gluten-Free at Five Guys

When ordering gluten-free at Five Guys, communication is key. Here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Specify Your Dietary Needs: Inform the staff that you have a gluten allergy or intolerance. They are trained to handle such requests and will take extra precautions.
  • Choose a Lettuce Wrap or Bowl: Instead of the traditional bun, opt for a lettuce wrap or ask for your burger or hot dog to be served in a bowl.
  • Double-Check Toppings: While most toppings are gluten-free, it’s always a good idea to confirm with the staff to avoid any hidden sources of gluten.
  • Avoid Cross-Contamination: Request that your food be prepared on a clean surface and with clean utensils to minimize the risk of cross-contact.

Five Guys’ Commitment to Food Safety

Five Guys has a strong commitment to food safety and customer satisfaction. The restaurant uses separate fryers for their fries, which are cooked in 100% peanut oil, making them a safe option for those avoiding gluten. Additionally, Five Guys provides detailed allergen information on their website, allowing customers to make informed decisions about their meals.

However, it’s important to remember that Five Guys is not a gluten-free facility. While they take precautions to prevent cross-contamination, there is always a risk, especially in a busy kitchen environment. If you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, it’s essential to assess your comfort level before dining.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys

1. Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free. They are cooked in a dedicated fryer using 100% peanut oil, which eliminates the risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing items.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Five Guys?

No, Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can order your burger or hot dog in a lettuce wrap or as a bowl to avoid gluten.

3. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Most Five Guys milkshake flavors are gluten-free. However, you should avoid the malted milk flavor, as it contains gluten.

4. How does Five Guys prevent cross-contamination?

Five Guys takes several precautions to prevent cross-contamination, including using separate fryers for fries and training staff to handle allergen requests carefully. However, the restaurant is not a gluten-free facility, so there is always some risk.

5. Is Five Guys safe for people with celiac disease?

While Five Guys offers gluten-free options and takes precautions to prevent cross-contamination, the restaurant is not a gluten-free facility. Individuals with celiac disease should assess their comfort level and communicate their needs clearly to the staff.

Conclusion

Five Guys offers a variety of gluten-free options that can be safely enjoyed by those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease, provided proper precautions are taken. From their famous fries to customizable burgers and hot dogs, there are plenty of ways to enjoy a meal at Five Guys without compromising your dietary needs. However, it’s important to communicate your requirements clearly to the staff and be aware of the potential for cross-contamination. With a little planning and care, you can enjoy a delicious and safe gluten-free meal at Five Guys.
