How Fuzzy's Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity, cross-contamination is a major concern. Fuzzy's Taco Shop takes several steps to minimize the risk of cross-contamination in their kitchens:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Fuzzy's staff are trained to prepare gluten-free items in designated areas to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Gluten-free dishes are prepared using separate utensils to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: Employees are educated about the importance of gluten-free safety and how to handle gluten-free orders properly.

While Fuzzy's takes these precautions, it's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary needs when placing your order. This ensures that they take extra care in preparing your meal.
