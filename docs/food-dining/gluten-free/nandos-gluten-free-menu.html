Nando's Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Nando's, the renowned South African restaurant chain famous for its flame-grilled PERi-PERi chicken, has become a global favorite. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Nando's has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diets, offering a variety of delicious options that cater to these needs. In this article, we’ll explore Nando's gluten-free menu in detail, providing you with all the information you need to enjoy a safe and satisfying meal.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Nando's

Nando's is committed to providing a diverse menu that caters to various dietary requirements, including gluten-free options. The restaurant ensures that gluten-free dishes are prepared with care to avoid cross-contamination. However, it’s always advisable to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions when ordering to ensure the highest level of safety.

FAQs About Nando's Gluten-Free Menu

Is Nando's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Nando's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform the staff about your condition to ensure extra care is taken during preparation.

Are Nando's chips gluten-free?

Yes, Nando's PERi-PERi chips are gluten-free, but always double-check with the staff as recipes can change.

Can I get gluten-free bread at Nando's?

Nando's does not currently offer gluten-free bread, but you can enjoy their gluten-free options without bread.

Are Nando's sauces gluten-free?

Most of Nando's PERi-PERi sauces are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to check the label or ask the staff for confirmation.
