Why Choose Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity over the years, not just among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity but also among individuals seeking a healthier lifestyle. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in some people, leading to digestive issues, fatigue, and other health problems. By opting for gluten-free meals, you can enjoy a wide range of dishes that are both delicious and safe for your dietary requirements.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in East Hampton

1. Are gluten-free options clearly labeled on menus?

Yes, most restaurants in East Hampton clearly label their gluten-free options on their menus. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

2. Can I find gluten-free options at casual dining spots?

Absolutely! Many casual dining spots in East Hampton, such as Bostwick's Chowder House and Golden Pear Café, offer a variety of gluten-free options, from salads to sandwiches and baked goods.

3. Are there gluten-free options for breakfast?

Yes, several cafés and bakeries in East Hampton, like Mary's Marvelous and East Hampton Gourmet, offer gluten-free breakfast options, including muffins, pancakes, and pastries.
