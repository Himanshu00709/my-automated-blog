FAQs About Bubba's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Bubba's gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Bubba's takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu items are safe for those with celiac disease. The kitchen staff is trained to prevent cross-contamination, and gluten-free items are prepared separately.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

While Bubba's gluten-free menu is primarily focused on accommodating gluten sensitivities, there are a few vegan options available. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Absolutely! Bubba's is happy to accommodate customizations to make your meal gluten-free. Just inform your server of your dietary needs, and they will guide you through the options.
