Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Barrio Queen

For those unfamiliar with gluten-free dining, it's important to understand what makes a dish gluten-free. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. At Barrio Queen, the chefs take extra precautions to ensure that gluten-free dishes are prepared in a separate area of the kitchen to avoid cross-contamination.

Additionally, Barrio Queen's staff is well-trained in handling gluten-free requests. They are knowledgeable about the ingredients used in each dish and can provide detailed information to help you make informed choices. This level of care and attention makes Barrio Queen a trusted destination for gluten-free diners.
