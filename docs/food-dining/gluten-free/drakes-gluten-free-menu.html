Drake's has earned a reputation for being a gluten-free-friendly restaurant, and for good reason. The restaurant takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free options are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Additionally, the menu is designed to be inclusive, offering a wide range of options that cater to different dietary needs without compromising on flavor or quality.

Cross-Contamination Prevention

One of the biggest concerns for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity is cross-contamination. Drake's takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free menu items are safe to eat. These measures include:

  • Dedicated Cooking Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in separate areas of the kitchen to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Separate Fryers: Gluten-free items like wings and fries are cooked in dedicated fryers to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: All staff members are trained on gluten-free protocols, including how to handle and prepare gluten-free items safely.
