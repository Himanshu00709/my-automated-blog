The Gluten-Free Preparation Process

At Northern Taphouse, the preparation of gluten-free dishes is taken very seriously. The kitchen is equipped with separate utensils, cookware, and preparation areas to prevent cross-contamination. Additionally, the staff follows strict protocols to ensure that gluten-free orders are handled with care. Here’s a breakdown of the preparation process:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in a dedicated area of the kitchen to avoid any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Special Utensils: The kitchen uses separate utensils and cookware for gluten-free dishes, ensuring that there is no cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: All staff members undergo rigorous training to understand the importance of gluten-free preparation and to handle gluten-free orders with care.

FAQs About Northern Taphouse Gluten-Free Options

1. Is the entire menu at Northern Taphouse gluten-free?

No, Northern Taphouse offers a separate gluten-free menu with a variety of options. However, the regular menu contains gluten-containing items, so it’s important to specify your dietary needs when ordering.

2. How does Northern Taphouse prevent cross-contamination?

Northern Taphouse takes several measures to prevent cross-contamination, including using separate preparation areas, utensils, and cookware for gluten-free dishes. The staff is also trained to handle gluten-free orders with care.

3. Are there gluten-free options for vegetarians and vegans?

Yes, Northern Taphouse offers a variety of gluten-free options that cater to vegetarians and vegans. The menu includes dishes like gluten-free vegetable stir-fry and vegan gluten-free pizza.

4. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While some dishes can be customized to be gluten-free, it’s best to choose from the dedicated gluten-free menu to ensure that your meal is prepared safely and without cross-contamination.
