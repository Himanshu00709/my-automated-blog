First Watch Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

First Watch, the popular daytime café known for its fresh and healthy breakfast, brunch, and lunch options, has become a go-to spot for those with dietary restrictions, including gluten intolerance or celiac disease. With a commitment to offering wholesome and nutritious meals, First Watch has curated a selection of gluten-free items that cater to a variety of tastes and preferences. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free options available at First Watch, provide tips for dining out with gluten sensitivities, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Why Choose First Watch for Gluten-Free Dining?

First Watch has earned a reputation for its farm-to-table approach, sourcing fresh ingredients and preparing meals from scratch daily. This dedication to quality extends to their gluten-free offerings, ensuring that guests with gluten sensitivities can enjoy a safe and satisfying dining experience. The café’s menu is clearly labeled, making it easy to identify gluten-free options, and the staff is well-trained to accommodate dietary needs.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at First Watch

While First Watch offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take precautions when dining out with gluten sensitivities. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you to the safest options and alert the kitchen to take extra precautions.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: While First Watch takes care to prevent cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to double-check with the staff about their preparation methods.
  • Stick to Clearly Labeled Items: First Watch clearly labels gluten-free items on their menu, making it easier to identify safe choices.
  • Ask About Substitutions: Many dishes can be modified to be gluten-free. Don’t hesitate to ask if a gluten-free alternative is available.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Are all gluten-free items at First Watch certified gluten-free?

While First Watch takes great care to offer gluten-free options, not all items are certified gluten-free. It’s important to communicate with your server and ask about specific preparation methods to ensure your meal is safe.

2. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many items on the menu can be customized to be gluten-free. For example, you can request gluten-free toast or buns for sandwiches and burgers. Always inform your server of your dietary needs.

3. Does First Watch have a separate gluten-free kitchen?

First Watch does not have a separate gluten-free kitchen, but they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. Be sure to communicate your needs with the staff to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

4. Are the gluten-free options at First Watch suitable for celiacs?

While First Watch offers gluten-free options, individuals with celiac disease should exercise caution due to the risk of cross-contamination. Always inform your server of your condition and ask about preparation methods.

So, the next time you’re looking for a gluten-free meal, consider stopping by First Watch for a fresh and flavorful experience.
