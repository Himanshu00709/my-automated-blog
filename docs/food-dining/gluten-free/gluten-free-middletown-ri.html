Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with these conditions. Even for those without a medical necessity, many people choose to go gluten-free to improve digestion, reduce inflammation, or simply explore a healthier lifestyle. Whatever your reason, Middletown, RI, has plenty to offer.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Living in Middletown, RI

1. Are there any dedicated gluten-free bakeries in Middletown?

While Middletown doesn't have a dedicated gluten-free bakery, many local bakeries offer gluten-free options. Clemson's Market and Whole Foods Market are great places to find gluten-free baked goods.

2. Can I find gluten-free options at local farmers' markets?

Yes! Middletown's farmers' markets often feature vendors who sell gluten-free products, including fresh produce, baked goods, and snacks. Be sure to ask vendors about their ingredients to ensure they meet your dietary needs.

3. How do I know if a restaurant is truly gluten-free?

Always communicate with restaurant staff about your dietary restrictions. Many restaurants in Middletown are knowledgeable about gluten-free diets and can provide detailed information about their menu items. Look for restaurants with gluten-free certifications or dedicated gluten-free menus.
