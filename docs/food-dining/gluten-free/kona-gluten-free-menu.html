Enter Kona, a restaurant that has embraced the gluten-free movement with open arms. In this blog post, we’ll take a deep dive into the Kona gluten-free menu, exploring its offerings, nutritional benefits, and why it’s a top choice for gluten-free diners.

FAQs About Kona’s Gluten-Free Menu

Is Kona’s gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Kona takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free dishes are prepared in a way that minimizes the risk of cross-contamination. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Kona offers several vegan and gluten-free options, such as the quinoa salad and the avocado toast. Be sure to check with your server to confirm that the dish meets your dietary needs.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, Kona is happy to accommodate customizations to make your meal gluten-free. Just let your server know your preferences, and they will guide you through the options.
