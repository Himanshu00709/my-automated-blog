Metro Diner has become a beloved spot for those seeking hearty, homestyle meals with a modern twist. This guide will walk you through Metro Diner's gluten-free offerings, ensuring you can enjoy a delicious meal without worry.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten intolerance, or those who choose to avoid gluten for health reasons. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with sensitivities. Metro Diner recognizes the importance of providing safe and delicious options for gluten-free diners, ensuring that everyone can enjoy their meals with confidence.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Metro Diner

While Metro Diner offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Inform Your Server: Always let your server know about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you to the safest options and alert the kitchen staff.
  • Ask About Preparation: Inquire about how your food is prepared to avoid cross-contamination. Metro Diner takes precautions, but it’s always good to double-check.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s a good idea to ask about any new gluten-free items or changes to existing options.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Metro Diner’s gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Metro Diner takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your condition. They can guide you to the safest options and ensure your meal is prepared with care.

Are the gluten-free buns and pancakes made in-house?

Metro Diner sources its gluten-free buns and pancake mix from trusted suppliers to ensure quality and safety. These items are prepared separately to avoid cross-contamination.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, many items on the menu can be customized to be gluten-free. For example, you can request a gluten-free bun for your burger or ask for gluten-free toast with your breakfast.

Does Metro Diner offer gluten-free desserts?

While Metro Diner’s dessert menu is limited in gluten-free options, you can always enjoy a fresh fruit bowl or ask your server about any seasonal gluten-free desserts.
