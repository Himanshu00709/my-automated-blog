Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it’s a necessity for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for individuals with these conditions. Not Your Average Joe's understands the importance of offering safe and tasty gluten-free options, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a meal without worry.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Not Your Average Joe's

While Not Your Average Joe's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Inform Your Server: Always let your server know about your gluten-free requirements. They can guide you to the best options and ensure your meal is prepared safely.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur in kitchens, so it’s important to ask about the restaurant’s practices to avoid gluten exposure.
  • Check the Menu: Not Your Average Joe's clearly labels gluten-free items on their menu, making it easier to identify safe choices.

3. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Not Your Average Joe's offers gluten-free options for kids, including gluten-free pasta and chicken tenders. Be sure to inform your server of any dietary restrictions for your child.

4. Does Not Your Average Joe's offer gluten-free bread?
