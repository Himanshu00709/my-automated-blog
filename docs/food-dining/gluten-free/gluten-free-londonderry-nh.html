Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or wheat allergies. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with these conditions. Even for those without a medical necessity, many people choose a gluten-free diet for its potential health benefits, such as improved digestion and increased energy levels.
