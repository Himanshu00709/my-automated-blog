Famous Toastery has built a reputation for its dedication to quality and customer satisfaction. FAQs About Famous Toastery’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Famous Toastery gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Famous Toastery takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free options are safe for those with celiac disease. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Famous Toastery offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the quinoa salad and avocado toast, which can be customized to suit vegan preferences.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free meal?

Yes, Famous Toastery is known for its flexibility. You can customize most dishes to fit your dietary needs, whether it’s adding extra vegetables or swapping out ingredients.

4. Are the gluten-free options clearly labeled on the menu?

Yes, all gluten-free items are clearly marked on the menu, making it easy for diners to identify their options.
