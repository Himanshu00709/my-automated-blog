Old Chicago Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like a daunting task. However, Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diners. With a dedicated gluten-free menu, Old Chicago ensures that everyone can enjoy their delicious offerings without compromising their health. In this blog post, we’ll dive deep into the Old Chicago gluten-free menu, exploring the options available, the precautions taken, and what makes this restaurant a great choice for gluten-free dining.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Old Chicago

Old Chicago is a popular chain known for its wide variety of pizzas, pastas, and craft beers. Recognizing the growing demand for gluten-free options, the restaurant has developed a menu that caters to those with gluten intolerance or celiac disease. The gluten-free menu at Old Chicago is designed to provide a safe and enjoyable dining experience, with a range of options that don’t skimp on flavor.

Made with a specially formulated gluten-free crust, these pizzas are available in a variety of toppings and styles. Precautions and Cross-Contamination

Old Chicago takes the issue of cross-contamination seriously. The restaurant has implemented strict protocols to ensure that gluten-free dishes are prepared separately from gluten-containing items. This includes using dedicated utensils, cookware, and preparation areas. Additionally, staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.

However, it’s important to note that while Old Chicago makes every effort to provide a safe dining experience, the restaurant cannot guarantee a completely gluten-free environment due to the shared kitchen space. If you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, it’s always a good idea to inform your server and discuss your dietary needs in detail.

FAQs About Old Chicago’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Old Chicago’s gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Old Chicago takes precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, but they cannot guarantee a completely gluten-free environment. If you have celiac disease, it’s important to communicate your needs to the staff and make an informed decision.

2. Are the gluten-free pizzas cooked in a separate oven?

Old Chicago uses dedicated pans and utensils for gluten-free pizzas, but they are cooked in the same oven as regular pizzas. The restaurant takes steps to prevent cross-contamination, but those with severe gluten sensitivities should be aware of this.

3. Can I get gluten-free beer at Old Chicago?

Yes, Old Chicago offers a selection of gluten-free beers. Be sure to ask your server for the current gluten-free beer options available at your location.

4. Are the gluten-free pasta dishes made with fresh ingredients?

Yes, Old Chicago uses fresh ingredients for their gluten-free pasta dishes. The pasta itself is gluten-free, and the sauces are made with care to avoid gluten-containing ingredients.

Conclusion

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom has made a commendable effort to cater to gluten-free diners with a dedicated menu that offers a variety of delicious options. From gluten-free pizzas and pastas to appetizers and salads, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. While the restaurant takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, it’s important for those with severe gluten sensitivities to communicate their needs and make informed choices. Whether you’re a long-time fan of Old Chicago or a first-time visitor, the gluten-free menu ensures that you can dine with confidence and enjoy a flavorful meal.
