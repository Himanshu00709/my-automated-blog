FAQs About J. Alexander's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is J. Alexander's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

J. Alexander's takes great care to avoid cross-contamination, but it's always best to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure the safest dining experience.

2. Are there gluten-free bread options available?

Yes, J. Alexander's offers gluten-free bread upon request. Be sure to ask your server for this option when you arrive.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Many dishes can be modified to be gluten-free. Your server will be happy to assist you with any customizations.

4. Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

While not all gluten-free items are explicitly marked, the staff is well-trained to guide you through the menu and identify gluten-free options.
