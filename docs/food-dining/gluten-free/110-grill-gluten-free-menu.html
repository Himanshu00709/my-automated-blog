Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at 110 Grill

While the 110 Grill is known for its gluten-free options, it’s always a good idea to take a few precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Let your server know about your dietary restrictions. The staff at 110 Grill are well-trained and will guide you through the menu.
  • Ask About Preparation: If you’re unsure about how a dish is prepared, don’t hesitate to ask. The kitchen staff will be happy to accommodate your needs.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: While 110 Grill takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to double-check, especially if you have severe gluten sensitivity.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is the entire 110 Grill menu gluten-free?

No, not all items on the 110 Grill menu are gluten-free. However, the restaurant offers a dedicated gluten-free menu with a wide variety of options to choose from.

Does 110 Grill use separate cooking equipment for gluten-free dishes?

Yes, 110 Grill takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination by using separate cooking equipment and preparation areas for gluten-free dishes.

Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many dishes on the 110 Grill menu can be customized to be gluten-free. Be sure to inform your server of your dietary restrictions so they can guide you through the options.

Are the gluten-free buns at 110 Grill made in-house?

No, the gluten-free buns at 110 Grill are not made in-house. They are sourced from a trusted supplier to ensure they meet gluten-free standards.
