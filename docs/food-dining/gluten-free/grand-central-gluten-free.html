The Importance of Gluten-Free Options

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to severe health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. Even for those without these conditions, reducing gluten intake can sometimes lead to improved digestion and overall well-being. Grand Central Gluten-Free recognizes the importance of providing safe and tasty options for everyone, regardless of their dietary restrictions.
