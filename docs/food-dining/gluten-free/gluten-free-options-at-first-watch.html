Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term complications. As a result, many restaurants, including First Watch, have adapted their menus to accommodate gluten-free diets. However, it’s important to note that cross-contamination can still occur in kitchens, so always communicate your dietary needs to the staff.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at First Watch

While First Watch offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s essential to take precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips:

  • Communicate with Staff: Always inform your server about your gluten-free needs. They can guide you to the safest options and alert the kitchen to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Check the Menu: First Watch clearly marks gluten-free items on their menu, but it’s always a good idea to double-check with your server.
  • Be Mindful of Cross-Contamination: While First Watch takes precautions, cross-contamination can still occur. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s best to discuss this with the staff.
  • Explore Customizations: Many dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. Don’t hesitate to ask for substitutions or modifications.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at First Watch

1. Does First Watch have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen?

No, First Watch does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. However, they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. Always inform your server about your dietary needs.

2. Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

Yes, First Watch clearly marks gluten-free items on their menu, making it easier for customers to identify safe options.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! Many dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. Just let your server know your preferences, and they will accommodate your request.

4. Are the gluten-free options suitable for celiacs?

While First Watch offers gluten-free options, cross-contamination is possible. If you have celiac disease, it’s best to discuss your needs with the staff to ensure a safe dining experience.
