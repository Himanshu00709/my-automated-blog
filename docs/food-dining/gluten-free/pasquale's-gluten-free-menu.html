FAQs About Pasquale's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Pasquale's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Pasquale's takes great care to avoid cross-contamination in their kitchen. All gluten-free dishes are prepared separately, and the staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with the utmost care.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Absolutely! Pasquale's offers several vegan and gluten-free options, including salads, pasta dishes, and even desserts. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free order?

Yes, Pasquale's is happy to accommodate customizations to their gluten-free dishes. Whether you want to add extra toppings to your pizza or substitute ingredients in your pasta, the kitchen staff will do their best to meet your needs.

4. Are the gluten-free desserts made in-house?

Yes, all of Pasquale's gluten-free desserts are made in-house using high-quality, gluten-free ingredients. This ensures that each dessert is fresh and delicious.
