Why Gluten-Free Matters

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, an autoimmune disorder where the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. For others, gluten sensitivity can cause a range of symptoms, from bloating and headaches to fatigue. Beyond medical necessity, many people choose gluten-free diets for perceived health benefits or personal preference. Regardless of the reason, having access to safe and delicious gluten-free options is crucial for a positive dining experience.

Cross-Contamination and Safety

One of the biggest concerns for those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance is cross-contamination. Not Your Average Joe's takes this issue seriously and has implemented protocols to minimize the risk. The kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders separately, and gluten-free items are prepared in designated areas to prevent cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
