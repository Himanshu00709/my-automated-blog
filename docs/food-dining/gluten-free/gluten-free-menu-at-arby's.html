Gluten-Free Menu at Arby's: A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Arby's, known for its roast beef sandwiches and curly fries, has made strides in offering gluten-free options. In this article, we'll explore the gluten-free menu at Arby's, providing you with all the information you need to make informed choices while dining at this popular fast-food chain.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Arby's

Arby's has recognized the growing demand for gluten-free options and has taken steps to accommodate customers with dietary restrictions. While Arby's is not a gluten-free restaurant, they do offer several menu items that are free from gluten-containing ingredients. It's important to note that cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen, so those with severe gluten allergies should exercise caution.

Gluten-Free Menu Items at Arby's

Below is a list of gluten-free items available at Arby's. Please note that menu items may vary by location, and it's always a good idea to check with your local Arby's for the most accurate information.

Menu Item Description
Roast Beef Arby's classic roast beef is gluten-free when served without the bun. Be sure to ask for it in a lettuce wrap or on a plate.
Turkey Breast Similar to the roast beef, the turkey breast is gluten-free without the bun. Opt for a lettuce wrap or a plate.
Ham Arby's ham is also gluten-free when served without the bun. Choose a lettuce wrap or a plate for a gluten-free option.
Side Salad Arby's side salad is a great gluten-free option. Be sure to ask for a gluten-free dressing, such as the balsamic vinaigrette.
Chopped Farmhouse Salad This salad is gluten-free when ordered without croutons. Choose a gluten-free dressing to complete your meal.
Potato Cakes Arby's potato cakes are a gluten-free side option. They are made from shredded potatoes and are fried in a dedicated fryer.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Arby's

When ordering gluten-free at Arby's, it's essential to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to ask the staff about the ingredients and preparation methods. They can provide valuable information to help you make safe choices.
  • Specify No Bun: Most of Arby's sandwiches can be made gluten-free by removing the bun. Ask for your sandwich in a lettuce wrap or on a plate.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: While Arby's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it's always a good idea to remind the staff of your gluten-free needs.
  • Choose Safe Sides: Stick to gluten-free sides like the side salad or potato cakes to avoid any potential gluten exposure.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at Arby's

1. Is Arby's a gluten-free restaurant?

No, Arby's is not a gluten-free restaurant. However, they do offer several gluten-free menu items. Cross-contamination is possible, so those with severe gluten allergies should be cautious.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Arby's?

As of now, Arby's does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can request your sandwich in a lettuce wrap or on a plate to make it gluten-free.

3. Are Arby's fries gluten-free?

Arby's curly fries and crinkle-cut fries are not gluten-free due to the seasoning and potential cross-contamination. However, the potato cakes are a gluten-free alternative.

4. What dressings are gluten-free at Arby's?

Arby's offers several gluten-free dressings, including balsamic vinaigrette, light Italian dressing, and ranch dressing. Always check with the staff to confirm the current gluten-free options.

5. Can I trust the gluten-free options at Arby's?

While Arby's makes an effort to provide gluten-free options, cross-contamination is always a risk in a fast-food environment. If you have celiac disease or a severe gluten allergy, it's best to proceed with caution and communicate your needs clearly to the staff.

Conclusion

Arby's has made significant strides in offering gluten-free options for customers with dietary restrictions. While the menu is not entirely gluten-free, there are several items that can be enjoyed safely by those avoiding gluten. By following the tips provided and communicating your needs clearly, you can enjoy a satisfying meal at Arby's without compromising your health. Always remember to check with your local Arby's for the most accurate and up-to-date information on their gluten-free offerings.
