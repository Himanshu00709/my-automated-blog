FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Chefs de France

1. Is the entire menu at Chefs de France gluten-free?

No, not all dishes are gluten-free. However, the restaurant offers a dedicated gluten-free menu with a variety of options to choose from.

2. How does Chefs de France prevent cross-contamination?

The kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders separately, using dedicated utensils and preparation areas to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.

3. Can I make a reservation for gluten-free dining?

Yes, it’s recommended to make a reservation and inform the staff about your gluten-free requirements when booking. This allows the kitchen to prepare in advance.

4. Are there gluten-free dessert options?

Yes, Chefs de France offers gluten-free desserts such as Crème Brûlée and seasonal fruit sorbets.
