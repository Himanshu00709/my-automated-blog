FAQs About The Goat’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is The Goat’s gluten-free menu safe for those with celiac disease?

Yes, The Goat takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for those with celiac disease. They use separate preparation areas and utensils to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Yes, The Goat offers several vegan options that are also gluten-free. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! The Goat is happy to accommodate customizations to make your meal gluten-free. Just let your server know your dietary needs.

4. Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

Yes, The Goat clearly marks all gluten-free options on their menu, making it easy for guests to identify suitable dishes.
