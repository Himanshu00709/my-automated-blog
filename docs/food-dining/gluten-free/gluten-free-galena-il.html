From cozy cafes to upscale dining, Galena's culinary scene is a testament to its inclusivity and creativity.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in Galena

1. Are there dedicated gluten-free kitchens in Galena?

While not all restaurants have dedicated gluten-free kitchens, many take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. It's always best to inform your server about your dietary restrictions.

2. Can I find gluten-free beer in Galena?

Yes, several bars and restaurants in Galena offer gluten-free beer options. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

3. Are there gluten-free options for breakfast in Galena?

Absolutely! Many breakfast spots in Galena, such as Otto's Place, offer gluten-free pancakes, waffles, and other breakfast items.

4. Is it safe to eat gluten-free at Galena's farmers market?

Yes, the Galena Farmers Market often features vendors who specialize in gluten-free products. Always check with the vendor to ensure their products meet your dietary needs.
