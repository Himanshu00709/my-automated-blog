BJ's Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, BJ's Restaurant & Brewhouse has made it easier for gluten-free diners to enjoy a delicious meal without worry. With a dedicated gluten-free menu, BJ's ensures that everyone can indulge in their favorite dishes without compromising their health. In this article, we’ll explore BJ's gluten-free offerings, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions.

What Makes BJ's Gluten-Free Menu Stand Out?

BJ's Restaurant & Brewhouse has gone above and beyond to cater to gluten-free diners. Their gluten-free menu is not just an afterthought; it’s a carefully curated selection of dishes that are both safe and satisfying. From appetizers to desserts, BJ's ensures that gluten-free options are available across all categories. What’s more, the restaurant takes extra precautions to avoid cross-contamination, making it a reliable choice for those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance.

Exploring BJ's Gluten-Free Menu

BJ's gluten-free menu is diverse, offering a range of options that cater to different tastes and dietary needs. Below, we’ve broken down the menu into categories to help you navigate your choices.

Appetizers

Start your meal with one of BJ's gluten-free appetizers. The Gluten-Free Pizookie Trio is a fan favorite, offering a sweet and savory combination that’s perfect for sharing. Other options include the Gluten-Free Avocado Egg Rolls, which are packed with flavor and served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Main Courses

When it comes to main courses, BJ's doesn’t disappoint. The Gluten-Free Enlightened Cobb Salad is a fresh and hearty option, while the Gluten-Free Grilled Salmon is perfect for those looking for a protein-packed meal. For pasta lovers, the Gluten-Free Penne Pasta can be paired with a variety of sauces, including marinara and alfredo.

Desserts

No meal is complete without dessert, and BJ's has you covered. The Gluten-Free Pizookie is a must-try, featuring a warm, gooey cookie topped with ice cream. It’s the perfect way to end your meal on a sweet note.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at BJ's

While BJ's takes great care to provide gluten-free options, it’s always a good idea to take extra precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Let your server know about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and inform the kitchen staff to take extra care.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Even though BJ's has a dedicated gluten-free menu, it’s important to ask about cross-contamination risks, especially if you have severe gluten sensitivity.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s always a good idea to check BJ's website or ask your server for the most up-to-date gluten-free options.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is BJ's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

BJ's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your condition so they can take extra care.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, many dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. Just ask your server for options.

Are there gluten-free desserts at BJ's?

Yes, the Gluten-Free Pizookie is a popular dessert option for those avoiding gluten.

Does BJ's offer gluten-free beer?

BJ's does not currently offer gluten-free beer, but they have a variety of other gluten-free beverage options.
