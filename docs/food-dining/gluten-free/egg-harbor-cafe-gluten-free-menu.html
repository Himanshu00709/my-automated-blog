Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Egg Harbor Cafe

While Egg Harbor Cafe takes great care to accommodate gluten-free diners, it’s always a good idea to communicate your dietary needs clearly. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Inform Your Server: Always let your server know about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you to the safest options and alert the kitchen staff.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: While the cafe takes precautions, it’s important to ask about potential cross-contamination, especially if you have a severe allergy.
  • Check for Seasonal Specials: Egg Harbor Cafe often introduces seasonal specials, some of which may be gluten-free. Don’t hesitate to ask about these options.

FAQs About Egg Harbor Cafe’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the gluten-free menu at Egg Harbor Cafe safe for celiacs?

Egg Harbor Cafe takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your condition. They can take extra steps to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

2. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Egg Harbor Cafe offers gluten-free options for kids, including gluten-free pancakes and toast. Be sure to ask your server for the kids’ gluten-free menu.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free order?

Absolutely! Egg Harbor Cafe is known for its flexibility. You can customize your gluten-free order to suit your preferences, whether it’s adding extra veggies or swapping out a protein.

4. Are the gluten-free baked goods made in-house?

While some gluten-free items are made in-house, others may be sourced from trusted gluten-free bakeries. Your server can provide more details about specific items.
