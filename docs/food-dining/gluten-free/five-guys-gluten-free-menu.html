Five Guys Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Five Guys is a popular fast-food chain known for its delicious burgers, fries, and milkshakes. However, for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can be a challenge. Fortunately, Five Guys offers a variety of gluten-free options that allow everyone to enjoy their meals without worry. In this article, we’ll explore the Five Guys gluten-free menu, provide tips for ordering safely, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Five Guys

Five Guys has become a go-to spot for gluten-free diners due to its straightforward menu and commitment to transparency. The restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, but they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. Here’s what you need to know about their gluten-free offerings:

Gluten-Free Burger Options

Five Guys offers a unique way to enjoy their famous burgers without the bun. You can order any burger as a lettuce wrap or in a bowl. Here’s a breakdown of the gluten-free burger options:

Burger Type Gluten-Free Options
Hamburger Lettuce wrap or bowl
Cheeseburger Lettuce wrap or bowl
Bacon Burger Lettuce wrap or bowl
Bacon Cheeseburger Lettuce wrap or bowl

All of Five Guys’ burger patties are gluten-free, and you can customize your toppings to suit your preferences. Just be sure to avoid any sauces or toppings that may contain gluten.

Gluten-Free Fries

One of the highlights of Five Guys’ menu is their fresh-cut fries. The good news is that their fries are gluten-free! Five Guys uses peanut oil to fry their potatoes, and they do not share fryers with any gluten-containing items. This makes their fries a safe and delicious option for gluten-free diners.

Gluten-Free Toppings and Condiments

Five Guys offers a wide range of toppings and condiments, many of which are gluten-free. Here’s a list of gluten-free toppings you can add to your burger or fries:

It’s important to note that some toppings, such as the A1 Steak Sauce and BBQ Sauce, contain gluten. Always double-check with the staff if you’re unsure about a particular topping.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Five Guys

While Five Guys offers several gluten-free options, it’s essential to take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff that you have a gluten allergy or sensitivity. They can take extra care when preparing your order.
  • Opt for a Lettuce Wrap or Bowl: Avoid the buns, which contain gluten, and choose a lettuce wrap or bowl instead.
  • Check Toppings and Condiments: Stick to gluten-free toppings and condiments, and avoid any that may contain gluten.
  • Be Cautious with Shared Equipment: While Five Guys takes precautions, cross-contamination is always a risk in a shared kitchen environment.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free. They are cooked in dedicated fryers using peanut oil, and they do not share fryers with any gluten-containing items.

Can I get a gluten-free bun at Five Guys?

Unfortunately, Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can enjoy your burger as a lettuce wrap or in a bowl.

Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Most of Five Guys’ milkshake flavors are gluten-free, but it’s always a good idea to check with the staff to ensure there are no gluten-containing ingredients in your chosen flavor.

Is Five Guys safe for people with celiac disease?

While Five Guys offers gluten-free options, they do not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. Cross-contamination is possible, so individuals with celiac disease should exercise caution.

Conclusion

Five Guys is a great option for gluten-free diners looking for a tasty and satisfying meal. With their gluten-free burgers, fries, and a variety of toppings, you can enjoy a delicious meal without compromising your dietary needs. Just remember to communicate your needs clearly, choose gluten-free options, and be mindful of potential cross-contamination. Happy dining!
