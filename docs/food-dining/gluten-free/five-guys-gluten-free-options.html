Five Guys Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

When it comes to fast food, Five Guys is a favorite for many. Known for their juicy burgers, hand-cut fries, and customizable toppings, Five Guys has built a reputation for quality and taste. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can be a challenge. The good news is that Five Guys offers several gluten-free options that allow you to enjoy a delicious meal without worrying about gluten. In this guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about Five Guys' gluten-free offerings, from burgers to fries and beyond.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Five Guys

Five Guys is committed to providing a safe and enjoyable dining experience for everyone, including those who need to avoid gluten. While they don’t have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. However, it’s important to note that cross-contamination can still occur, so those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease should proceed with caution.

Gluten-Free Menu Options at Five Guys

Here’s a breakdown of the gluten-free options available at Five Guys:

Item Gluten-Free Status Notes
Burgers (without bun) Gluten-Free All burgers can be ordered without the bun and wrapped in lettuce.
Hot Dogs (without bun) Gluten-Free Hot dogs can also be ordered without the bun and wrapped in lettuce.
Fries Gluten-Free Five Guys fries are cooked in 100% peanut oil and are gluten-free. However, cross-contamination is possible in the fryer.
Toppings Most are Gluten-Free Most toppings, including lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, onions, and more, are gluten-free. Always double-check with staff.
Milkshakes Gluten-Free All milkshakes are gluten-free, but be cautious with mix-ins that may contain gluten.

How to Order Gluten-Free at Five Guys

Ordering gluten-free at Five Guys is straightforward, but it’s essential to communicate your needs clearly to the staff. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose Your Protein: Start by selecting a burger or hot dog. Remember to order it without the bun.
  2. Select Your Toppings: Customize your meal with gluten-free toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and more.
  3. Ask for a Lettuce Wrap: Instead of a bun, ask for your burger or hot dog to be wrapped in lettuce.
  4. Order Fries: If you’re comfortable with the risk of cross-contamination, you can enjoy Five Guys’ famous fries.
  5. Double-Check with Staff: Always inform the staff of your gluten-free needs to ensure they take extra precautions.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While Five Guys takes steps to minimize cross-contamination, it’s important to understand that their kitchen is not entirely gluten-free. Shared cooking surfaces, fryers, and utensils can pose a risk for those with severe gluten allergies or celiac disease. If you’re highly sensitive to gluten, it’s best to consult with the staff and make an informed decision based on your comfort level.

FAQs About Five Guys Gluten-Free Options

1. Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free as they are cooked in 100% peanut oil. However, cross-contamination is possible in the fryer, so those with severe gluten allergies should be cautious.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Five Guys?

No, Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can order your burger or hot dog without the bun and have it wrapped in lettuce instead.

3. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Yes, all Five Guys milkshakes are gluten-free. However, be cautious with mix-ins, as some may contain gluten.

4. Is Five Guys safe for celiacs?

While Five Guys offers gluten-free options, the risk of cross-contamination exists. Those with celiac disease should proceed with caution and communicate their needs clearly to the staff.

5. What toppings are gluten-free at Five Guys?

Most toppings at Five Guys are gluten-free, including lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and more. Always double-check with the staff to ensure safety.

Conclusion

Five Guys offers a variety of gluten-free options that make it possible for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease to enjoy a meal out. From burgers and hot dogs wrapped in lettuce to their famous fries and milkshakes, there’s something for everyone. However, it’s crucial to be aware of the potential for cross-contamination and to communicate your dietary needs clearly with the staff. By following the tips and guidelines in this guide, you can enjoy a safe and delicious gluten-free meal at Five Guys.
