Why Go Gluten-Free?

Gluten-free diets have gained popularity over the years, not only among those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity but also among individuals seeking a healthier lifestyle. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause adverse reactions in some people, leading to digestive issues, fatigue, and other health problems. By eliminating gluten from your diet, you can improve your overall well-being and enjoy a variety of nutrient-rich foods.

Tips for Maintaining a Gluten-Free Diet in Palo Alto

Living gluten-free in Palo Alto is easier than ever, but it still requires some planning and awareness. Here are some tips to help you navigate your gluten-free journey:

  • Research Restaurants: Before dining out, check the restaurant's menu online or call ahead to ensure they offer gluten-free options.
  • Read Labels: When shopping for groceries, always read labels to ensure products are certified gluten-free.
  • Join a Community: Connect with local gluten-free groups or online forums to share tips and recommendations.
  • Educate Yourself: Stay informed about hidden sources of gluten and learn how to identify them in food products.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Living in Palo Alto

1. Are there gluten-free bakeries in Palo Alto?

Yes, Palo Alto has several bakeries that offer gluten-free options. Some popular choices include Mariposa Baking Co. and Flour Craft Bakery.

2. Can I find gluten-free options at farmers' markets?

Absolutely! Palo Alto's farmers' markets often feature vendors who sell gluten-free products, including fresh produce, baked goods, and artisanal items.

3. How do I know if a restaurant is truly gluten-free?

Look for restaurants that are certified gluten-free or have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. Additionally, ask staff about their preparation methods to avoid cross-contamination.
