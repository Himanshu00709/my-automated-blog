Why Gluten-Free Matters at T-Mobile Park

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten intolerance, or those who choose to avoid gluten for health reasons. T-Mobile Park recognizes the importance of providing safe and delicious options for all fans. With a growing demand for gluten-free choices, the ballpark has expanded its offerings to ensure everyone can enjoy the game without compromising their dietary needs.

Tips for a Safe Gluten-Free Experience

While T-Mobile Park offers a range of gluten-free options, it's important to take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience:

  • Communicate with Staff: Inform the staff about your gluten-free needs and ask about their preparation methods.
  • Check Labels: Always check the labels of packaged foods to ensure they are certified gluten-free.
  • Bring Your Own Snacks: If you're unsure about the options available, consider bringing your own gluten-free snacks.
  • Use the Ballpark App: The T-Mobile Park app provides up-to-date information on gluten-free options and their locations.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at T-Mobile Park

1. Are all gluten-free items at T-Mobile Park certified gluten-free?

Not all items are certified gluten-free, but the ballpark takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination. Always check with the staff and read labels carefully.

2. Can I bring my own gluten-free food into T-Mobile Park?

Yes, T-Mobile Park allows fans to bring their own food, including gluten-free snacks, as long as they comply with the ballpark's food policy.

3. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, T-Mobile Park offers gluten-free options suitable for kids, including gluten-free hot dogs, pizza, and ice cream.

4. How can I find gluten-free options at the ballpark?

Use the T-Mobile Park app or ask the staff at concession stands for information on gluten-free options and their locations.
