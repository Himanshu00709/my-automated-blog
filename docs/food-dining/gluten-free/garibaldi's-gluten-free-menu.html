Garibaldi's gluten-free menu is not just an afterthought; it's a carefully curated selection of dishes that maintain the same high standards of flavor and quality as their regular menu. Absolutely! Garibaldi offers several vegan and gluten-free dishes, such as the Vegan Gluten-Free Pasta and the Grilled Vegetable Platter.

3. Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

While Garibaldi's gluten-free menu is extensive, the staff is happy to accommodate special requests whenever possible. Just let your server know your preferences.

4. Are the gluten-free desserts made in-house?

Yes, all of Garibaldi's gluten-free desserts are made in-house using high-quality, gluten-free ingredients.
