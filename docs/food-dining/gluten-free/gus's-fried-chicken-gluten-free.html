The History of Gus's Fried Chicken

Gus's Fried Chicken has a rich history that dates back to 1953 in Mason, Tennessee. Founded by Napoleon "Gus" Bonner, the restaurant quickly became famous for its unique blend of spices and perfectly fried chicken. Over the years, Gus's has expanded to multiple locations across the United States, but the recipe has remained unchanged. The secret to their success lies in the simplicity of their ingredients and the precision of their cooking process.

Is Gus's Fried Chicken Gluten-Free?

For those who follow a gluten-free diet, the question of whether Gus's Fried Chicken is safe to eat is a crucial one. The good news is that Gus's Fried Chicken is indeed gluten-free. The chicken is marinated in a special blend of spices and then fried in peanut oil, which does not contain gluten. Additionally, the batter used to coat the chicken is made from a mixture of cornstarch and other gluten-free ingredients, ensuring that the final product is safe for those with gluten sensitivities.
