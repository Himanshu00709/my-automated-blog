Why Gluten-Free Desserts Matter

Gluten-free diets have become increasingly popular over the years, not just for those with celiac disease but also for individuals who experience gluten sensitivity or simply want to adopt a healthier lifestyle. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause digestive issues and other health problems for some people. As a result, many restaurants have started to offer gluten-free options, and Applebee's is no exception. Their gluten-free desserts are carefully crafted to ensure that everyone can enjoy a sweet treat without compromising their health.
