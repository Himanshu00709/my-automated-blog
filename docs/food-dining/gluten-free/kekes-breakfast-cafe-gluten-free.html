Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Keke's

While Keke's Breakfast Cafe is a great option for gluten-free diners, it's always a good idea to take a few precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Let your server know about your gluten-free requirements when you arrive. The staff is well-trained and will guide you through the menu.
  • Ask About Preparation: Don't hesitate to ask how dishes are prepared to avoid cross-contamination. The kitchen staff is diligent, but it's always good to double-check.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it's a good idea to check for any updates or new gluten-free offerings before your visit.
