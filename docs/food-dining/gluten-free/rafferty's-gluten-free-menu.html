Rafferty's Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Rafferty's, a beloved casual dining restaurant, has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diners. With a dedicated gluten-free menu, Rafferty's ensures that everyone can enjoy a delicious meal without the worry of gluten contamination. In this article, we’ll explore Rafferty's gluten-free offerings, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Rafferty's

Rafferty's has long been known for its hearty American fare, from juicy burgers to savory steaks. But what sets them apart is their commitment to inclusivity. Recognizing the growing need for gluten-free options, Rafferty's has curated a menu that caters to those with dietary restrictions. Their gluten-free menu is designed to offer a variety of choices, ensuring that gluten-free diners don’t feel limited in their options.

It’s important to note that while Rafferty's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, they do not have a completely gluten-free kitchen. Therefore, those with severe gluten allergies should exercise caution and communicate their needs clearly to the staff.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Rafferty's

While Rafferty's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s essential to take a few precautions to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and alert the kitchen to take extra precautions.
  • Ask About Preparation: Don’t hesitate to ask how your food is prepared. Cross-contamination can occur if gluten-free items are cooked in the same oil or on the same grill as gluten-containing foods.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s a good idea to check for any updates or new gluten-free offerings before you visit.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is Rafferty's gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

While Rafferty's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, they do not have a completely gluten-free kitchen. Those with celiac disease should communicate their needs clearly to the staff and exercise caution.

Are there gluten-free dessert options at Rafferty's?

Yes, Rafferty's offers a few gluten-free dessert options, including Flourless Chocolate Cake and a Fresh Fruit Plate.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

In some cases, you may be able to customize your order to make it gluten-free. However, it’s best to check with your server to ensure that the modifications are possible and safe.

Does Rafferty's offer gluten-free bread or buns?

Rafferty's does not currently offer gluten-free bread or buns. However, they do have a variety of gluten-free options that don’t require bread, such as salads and grilled meats.
