How Alamo Drafthouse Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

For those with celiac disease or severe gluten intolerance, cross-contamination is a significant concern. Alamo Drafthouse takes this seriously by implementing strict kitchen protocols. Here’s how they ensure your meal is safe:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free items are prepared in designated areas to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Utensils: Special utensils are used exclusively for gluten-free dishes.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that your dietary needs are met.

FAQs About Alamo Drafthouse Gluten-Free Options

1. Are all Alamo Drafthouse locations gluten-free friendly?

Yes, all Alamo Drafthouse locations offer gluten-free options. However, the specific menu items may vary slightly by location, so it’s always a good idea to check the menu online or call ahead.

2. Can I trust that my meal will be gluten-free?

Alamo Drafthouse takes great care to ensure that gluten-free meals are prepared safely. They have strict protocols in place to avoid cross-contamination, and staff members are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care.

3. Are there vegan gluten-free options available?

Yes, Alamo Drafthouse offers a variety of vegan and gluten-free options. Be sure to check the menu or ask your server for recommendations.

4. Do I need to inform the staff about my gluten-free needs?

While it’s not mandatory, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions. This ensures that they can take extra precautions to meet your needs.
