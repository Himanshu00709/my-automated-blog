Gluten-Free Options at Moe's - A Comprehensive Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Moe's Southwest Grill has made it easier for gluten-free diners to enjoy a delicious and safe meal. With a variety of gluten-free options, Moe's ensures that everyone can savor their flavorful Southwest-inspired dishes without worry. In this guide, we'll explore the gluten-free offerings at Moe's, provide tips for ordering, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Moe's

Moe's Southwest Grill is known for its customizable menu, which allows customers to build their own meals from a variety of fresh ingredients. This flexibility is a boon for gluten-free diners, as it allows them to tailor their meals to avoid gluten-containing items. Moe's has also taken steps to ensure that their gluten-free options are prepared with care to minimize cross-contamination.

Gluten-Free Menu Options

Moe's offers a range of gluten-free options, from bowls and salads to tacos and more. Below is a detailed breakdown of the gluten-free items available at Moe's:

Category Gluten-Free Options
Bowls All bowls can be made gluten-free by choosing a base of rice or lettuce and avoiding gluten-containing toppings.
Salads Salads are naturally gluten-free when you skip the croutons and choose a gluten-free dressing.
Tacos Soft corn tortillas are gluten-free, making them a safe choice for tacos.
Burritos Unfortunately, burritos are not gluten-free due to the flour tortillas. However, you can opt for a burrito bowl instead.
Sides Guacamole, queso, and most salsas are gluten-free. Be sure to check the ingredients of any specialty sauces.
Desserts Moe's does not currently offer gluten-free desserts, but you can enjoy a refreshing drink like iced tea or lemonade.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Moe's

While Moe's offers a variety of gluten-free options, it's important to take precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips for ordering gluten-free at Moe's:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff that you have a gluten allergy or sensitivity. They can guide you through the menu and help you avoid cross-contamination.
  • Choose the Right Base: Opt for rice or lettuce as your base for bowls and salads. Avoid flour tortillas and croutons.
  • Be Mindful of Toppings: Some toppings, like certain sauces and dressings, may contain gluten. Always ask for a list of ingredients if you're unsure.
  • Watch for Cross-Contamination: While Moe's takes precautions, cross-contamination can still occur. If you're highly sensitive, consider visiting during off-peak hours when the kitchen is less busy.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Are Moe's corn tortillas gluten-free?

Yes, Moe's corn tortillas are gluten-free, making them a safe option for tacos and other dishes.

2. Can I get a gluten-free burrito at Moe's?

Unfortunately, Moe's burritos are made with flour tortillas, which contain gluten. However, you can enjoy a burrito bowl with a gluten-free base like rice or lettuce.

3. Are Moe's salsas and sauces gluten-free?

Most of Moe's salsas and sauces are gluten-free, but it's always a good idea to check the ingredients or ask a staff member to be sure.

4. Does Moe's have a dedicated gluten-free fryer?

Moe's does not have a dedicated gluten-free fryer, so items like chips may be at risk of cross-contamination. If you're highly sensitive, it's best to avoid fried items.

5. Can I find gluten-free desserts at Moe's?

Moe's does not currently offer gluten-free desserts. However, you can enjoy a refreshing drink like iced tea or lemonade to round out your meal.

Conclusion

Moe's Southwest Grill is a great option for gluten-free diners looking for a flavorful and customizable meal. With a variety of gluten-free bases, toppings, and sauces, you can create a meal that suits your dietary needs and tastes. By following the tips outlined in this guide and communicating your needs to the staff, you can enjoy a safe and delicious dining experience at Moe's. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty bowl, a fresh salad, or tasty tacos, Moe's has you covered with their gluten-free options.
