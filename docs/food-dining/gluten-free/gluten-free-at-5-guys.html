Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys: A Comprehensive Guide

Five Guys is a popular fast-food chain known for its customizable burgers, fries, and milkshakes. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating fast-food menus can be challenging. However, Five Guys has made strides in accommodating gluten-free diets. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free options available at Five Guys, how to order safely, and what to watch out for to ensure a gluten-free meal.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Five Guys

Five Guys is not a gluten-free restaurant, but it offers several menu items that are naturally gluten-free. The key to safely enjoying a gluten-free meal at Five Guys lies in understanding the ingredients and preparation methods. Here’s a breakdown of what you need to know:

Gluten-Free Menu Items

Five Guys offers a variety of menu items that are naturally gluten-free. These include:

  • Burgers: Five Guys burgers are made with 100% beef and are gluten-free. However, the buns contain gluten, so you’ll need to order your burger without a bun or bring your own gluten-free bun.
  • Hot Dogs: Like the burgers, Five Guys hot dogs are gluten-free, but the buns are not. You can order a hot dog without a bun or bring your own gluten-free bun.
  • Fries: Five Guys fries are cooked in 100% peanut oil and are gluten-free. They are prepared in a dedicated fryer, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Toppings: Most of the toppings at Five Guys are gluten-free, including lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, onions, and more. However, always double-check with the staff to ensure there are no hidden sources of gluten.
  • Milkshakes: Five Guys milkshakes are gluten-free, but be cautious with mix-ins like cookies or brownies, which contain gluten.

Cross-Contamination Risks

While Five Guys takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, it’s important to be aware of the risks. The kitchen is not entirely gluten-free, and there is always a chance of cross-contact with gluten-containing ingredients. Here are some tips to reduce the risk:

  • Communicate with Staff: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can take extra precautions when preparing your meal.
  • Avoid Shared Surfaces: Ask for your burger or hot dog to be prepared on a clean surface to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Check Ingredients: Always double-check the ingredients of any toppings or sauces to ensure they are gluten-free.

How to Order Gluten-Free at Five Guys

Ordering gluten-free at Five Guys is straightforward if you know what to ask for. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose Your Base: Select a burger or hot dog without the bun. You can also opt for a lettuce wrap if available.
  2. Select Toppings: Customize your meal with gluten-free toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, onions, and more.
  3. Ask About Preparation: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance and ask them to prepare your meal on a clean surface.
  4. Enjoy Your Fries: Five Guys fries are gluten-free and cooked in a dedicated fryer, making them a safe option.
  5. Double-Check: Before you leave, double-check your order to ensure it meets your gluten-free requirements.

Yes, Five Guys fries are gluten-free. They are cooked in 100% peanut oil in a dedicated fryer, reducing the risk of cross-contamination.

Yes, you can bring your own gluten-free bun to Five Guys. Simply order your burger or hot dog without a bun and use your own bun.

3. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys milkshakes are gluten-free. However, be cautious with mix-ins like cookies or brownies, which contain gluten.

4. How does Five Guys prevent cross-contamination?

Five Guys takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, such as using a dedicated fryer for fries. However, the kitchen is not entirely gluten-free, so there is always a risk of cross-contact.

Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease and ask them to prepare your meal on a clean surface to avoid cross-contamination.

Conclusion

Five Guys offers several gluten-free options, making it a viable choice for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. By understanding the menu, communicating with staff, and taking precautions to avoid cross-contamination, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying meal at Five Guys. Whether you’re craving a bunless burger, gluten-free fries, or a refreshing milkshake, Five Guys has something to offer for everyone on a gluten-free diet.
