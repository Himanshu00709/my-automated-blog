Why Choose a Gluten-Free Diet?

Before diving into Unforked's gluten-free menu, it's essential to understand why someone might choose a gluten-free diet. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease, consuming gluten can lead to severe digestive issues, nutrient malabsorption, and long-term health complications. Others may have non-celiac gluten sensitivity, experiencing symptoms like bloating, fatigue, and headaches after consuming gluten. Additionally, some people opt for a gluten-free diet as part of a broader health and wellness strategy, believing it to be beneficial for weight management and overall well-being.

FAQs About Unforked's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Are all Unforked locations equipped to handle gluten-free orders?

Yes, Unforked takes gluten-free dining seriously and ensures that all locations are equipped to handle gluten-free orders. However, it's always a good idea to inform the staff of your dietary restrictions to ensure that your meal is prepared safely.

2. Are Unforked's gluten-free options also dairy-free?

While many of Unforked's gluten-free options are also dairy-free, it's important to check the specific ingredients of each dish. Some items, like the lime crema in the Grilled Fish Tacos, may contain dairy. Always inform the staff of any additional dietary restrictions.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Absolutely! Unforked is known for its customizable menu, and you can easily modify many dishes to make them gluten-free. For example, you can opt for corn tortillas instead of flour tortillas or choose a gluten-free dressing for your salad.

4. Are Unforked's gluten-free options suitable for people with celiac disease?

Unforked takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it's important to note that their kitchen is not entirely gluten-free. If you have celiac disease, it's crucial to communicate your needs to the staff to ensure that your meal is prepared with the utmost care.
