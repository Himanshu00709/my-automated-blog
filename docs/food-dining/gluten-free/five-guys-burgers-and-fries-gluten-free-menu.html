Five Guys Burgers and Fries Gluten-Free Menu: A Detailed Guide

Five Guys Burgers and Fries has become a staple in the fast-casual dining scene, known for its customizable burgers, fresh ingredients, and generous portions. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating restaurant menus can be a challenge. Fortunately, Five Guys offers several gluten-free options that allow you to enjoy a delicious meal without compromising your dietary needs. In this guide, we’ll explore the gluten-free menu at Five Guys, including what you can eat, what to avoid, and tips for a safe dining experience.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Five Guys

Five Guys is known for its transparency when it comes to ingredients and food preparation. The restaurant provides detailed allergen information, making it easier for customers to make informed choices. While Five Guys does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination. However, it’s important to note that there is always a risk of cross-contact in a shared kitchen environment.

Gluten-Free Options at Five Guys

Here’s a breakdown of the gluten-free options available at Five Guys:

Burgers

Five Guys’ burgers are made with 100% fresh beef and are free from gluten-containing ingredients. However, the standard burger buns are not gluten-free. To enjoy a gluten-free burger, you can order it as a lettuce wrap or in a bowl. Here’s how you can customize your burger:

  • Lettuce Wrap: Instead of a bun, your burger will be wrapped in fresh lettuce leaves.
  • Bowl: Your burger patty and toppings will be served in a bowl without a bun.

Toppings

Five Guys offers a wide variety of toppings that are gluten-free. You can customize your burger with the following options:

Fries

Five Guys’ fries are a fan favorite and are naturally gluten-free. They are cooked in 100% peanut oil, which is free from gluten. However, it’s important to note that the fries are cooked in the same fryer as other items that may contain gluten, so there is a risk of cross-contamination.

Hot Dogs

Five Guys offers hot dogs, but the standard hot dog buns are not gluten-free. Similar to the burgers, you can order a hot dog as a lettuce wrap or in a bowl to make it gluten-free.

Milkshakes

Five Guys’ milkshakes are a popular treat and are gluten-free. They are made with real ice cream and offer a variety of mix-ins, such as chocolate, strawberries, and bacon. Be sure to check the allergen information for specific mix-ins, as some may contain gluten.

What to Avoid at Five Guys

While Five Guys offers several gluten-free options, there are some items you should avoid if you’re following a strict gluten-free diet:

  • Buns: The standard burger and hot dog buns contain gluten.
  • Cheese Sauce: The cheese sauce used for toppings may contain gluten.
  • Certain Toppings: Some toppings, such as malt vinegar, are not gluten-free.

Tips for a Safe Gluten-Free Experience at Five Guys

To ensure a safe dining experience, consider the following tips:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform the staff about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease so they can take extra precautions.
  • Check the Allergen Menu: Five Guys provides an allergen menu that lists all ingredients and potential allergens. Review this menu before ordering.
  • Opt for Simple Orders: Stick to simple orders like a lettuce-wrapped burger with gluten-free toppings to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Be Aware of Cross-Contamination: While Five Guys takes precautions, cross-contamination is always a possibility in a shared kitchen.

FAQs

1. Are Five Guys fries gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys fries are naturally gluten-free as they are made from potatoes and cooked in peanut oil. However, they are cooked in the same fryer as other items that may contain gluten, so there is a risk of cross-contamination.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Five Guys?

No, Five Guys does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can order your burger or hot dog as a lettuce wrap or in a bowl.

3. Are Five Guys milkshakes gluten-free?

Yes, Five Guys milkshakes are gluten-free. They are made with real ice cream and offer a variety of gluten-free mix-ins.

4. Is Five Guys safe for people with celiac disease?

While Five Guys offers gluten-free options, there is a risk of cross-contamination in the kitchen. People with celiac disease should exercise caution and communicate their dietary needs to the staff.

Conclusion

Five Guys Burgers and Fries offers a variety of gluten-free options that allow you to enjoy a delicious meal without compromising your dietary needs. From lettuce-wrapped burgers to gluten-free fries and milkshakes, there’s something for everyone. However, it’s important to be aware of the risk of cross-contamination and to communicate your dietary needs to the staff. By following the tips outlined in this guide, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying gluten-free dining experience at Five Guys.
