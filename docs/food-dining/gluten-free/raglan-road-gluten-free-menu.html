Raglan Road Gluten-Free Menu: A Culinary Delight

Raglan Road, a renowned Irish pub and restaurant located in the heart of Disney Springs, is a haven for food lovers. Known for its authentic Irish cuisine, lively atmosphere, and exceptional service, Raglan Road has become a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the restaurant offers an impressive gluten-free menu that doesn’t compromise on taste or quality. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free options available at Raglan Road, providing you with a comprehensive guide to dining gluten-free at this iconic establishment.

Why Choose Raglan Road for Gluten-Free Dining?

Raglan Road is committed to providing a safe and enjoyable dining experience for all guests, including those with dietary restrictions. The restaurant’s gluten-free menu is carefully curated to ensure that every dish is free from gluten-containing ingredients. The chefs at Raglan Road are well-trained in handling gluten-free orders, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. Additionally, the restaurant uses separate cooking utensils and preparation areas for gluten-free dishes, giving diners peace of mind.

FAQs About Raglan Road’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Raglan Road’s gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Yes, Raglan Road takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu is safe for individuals with celiac disease. The kitchen staff is trained to prevent cross-contamination, and separate utensils and preparation areas are used for gluten-free dishes.

2. Can I make reservations for gluten-free dining?

Absolutely! Raglan Road accepts reservations, and you can specify your dietary requirements when booking. This allows the kitchen to prepare for your visit and ensure a seamless dining experience.

3. Are there gluten-free options for children?

Yes, Raglan Road offers gluten-free options for children as well. The kids’ menu includes gluten-free versions of popular dishes like chicken tenders and pasta.

4. Does Raglan Road offer gluten-free beer?

Yes, Raglan Road offers a selection of gluten-free beers to complement your meal. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

Whether you’re a local or just visiting Disney Springs, Raglan Road is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a safe and enjoyable gluten-free dining experience. So, the next time you’re in the area, be sure to stop by Raglan Road and indulge in their delectable gluten-free offerings.
