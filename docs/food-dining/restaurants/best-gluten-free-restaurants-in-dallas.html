FAQs

What should I look for in a gluten-free restaurant?

When choosing a gluten-free restaurant, it's important to look for establishments that clearly mark their gluten-free options and have a good understanding of cross-contamination. Many restaurants now offer dedicated gluten-free menus or have staff trained in handling gluten-free orders.

Are gluten-free restaurants more expensive?

While some gluten-free restaurants may be slightly more expensive due to the cost of gluten-free ingredients, many offer affordable options. It's always a good idea to check the menu and prices before dining.

Can I find gluten-free options at non-specialized restaurants?

Yes, many non-specialized restaurants now offer gluten-free options. However, it's important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff to ensure that your meal is prepared safely.
