Gluten-Free Restaurants in North Conway, NH

North Conway, New Hampshire, is a picturesque town nestled in the heart of the White Mountains. Known for its stunning landscapes, outdoor activities, and charming downtown area, North Conway is also a haven for food enthusiasts. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding safe and delicious dining options can be a challenge. Fortunately, North Conway boasts a variety of restaurants that cater to gluten-free diets, offering everything from hearty breakfasts to gourmet dinners. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in North Conway, NH, and provide tips for dining out safely.

Top Gluten-Free Restaurants in North Conway

North Conway is home to several restaurants that are not only aware of gluten-free needs but also go the extra mile to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience. Here are some of the top picks:

1. The Local Grocer

The Local Grocer is a must-visit for anyone looking for gluten-free options in North Conway. This farm-to-table restaurant and market offers a wide range of gluten-free dishes, including salads, sandwiches, and baked goods. Their menu is clearly labeled, making it easy for gluten-free diners to navigate. The Local Grocer also offers a variety of gluten-free products for purchase, so you can stock up on your favorite snacks and ingredients.

2. Flatbread Company

Flatbread Company is a popular spot for pizza lovers, and they offer a gluten-free crust option that is both delicious and safe for those with celiac disease. The restaurant takes great care to prevent cross-contamination, and their gluten-free pizzas are cooked in a separate oven. With a variety of fresh, organic toppings to choose from, Flatbread Company is a great choice for a casual meal with friends or family.

3. May Kelly's Cottage

May Kelly's Cottage is an Irish pub that offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, along with a dedicated gluten-free menu. From traditional Irish dishes like shepherd’s pie to gluten-free fish and chips, May Kelly's Cottage ensures that gluten-free diners can enjoy a taste of Ireland without worry. The staff is knowledgeable about gluten-free needs and is happy to accommodate special requests.

4. The Met Coffee House

For a cozy breakfast or lunch spot, The Met Coffee House is a great choice. This charming café offers a variety of gluten-free options, including sandwiches, salads, and baked goods. Their gluten-free bread is sourced from a trusted local bakery, and they take care to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchen. Pair your meal with a cup of their expertly brewed coffee for the perfect start to your day.

Tips for Dining Out Gluten-Free in North Conway

While North Conway offers plenty of gluten-free dining options, it’s always a good idea to take some precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience:

  • Call Ahead: If you’re planning to visit a restaurant, call ahead to inquire about their gluten-free options and kitchen practices. This can help you avoid any surprises and ensure that the restaurant can accommodate your needs.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask your server about the ingredients and preparation methods. A good restaurant will be happy to provide this information and make any necessary adjustments to your meal.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing foods or surfaces. Ask the restaurant about their procedures for preventing cross-contamination, such as using separate cooking utensils and preparation areas.
  • Bring Your Own Snacks: If you’re unsure about the gluten-free options at a particular restaurant, consider bringing your own snacks or a small meal. This can be especially helpful when traveling or dining in areas with limited gluten-free options.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in North Conway

Q: Are there any dedicated gluten-free restaurants in North Conway?

A: While there are no fully dedicated gluten-free restaurants in North Conway, many establishments offer extensive gluten-free menus and take precautions to prevent cross-contamination. Restaurants like The Local Grocer and May Kelly's Cottage are particularly known for their gluten-free offerings.

Q: Can I find gluten-free baked goods in North Conway?

A: Yes, several restaurants and bakeries in North Conway offer gluten-free baked goods. The Local Grocer and The Met Coffee House are great places to find gluten-free bread, muffins, and other treats.

Q: How can I ensure my meal is gluten-free when dining out?

A: To ensure your meal is gluten-free, communicate clearly with your server about your dietary needs. Ask about the ingredients and preparation methods, and inquire about the restaurant’s procedures for preventing cross-contamination. It’s also a good idea to call ahead and confirm that the restaurant can accommodate your needs.
