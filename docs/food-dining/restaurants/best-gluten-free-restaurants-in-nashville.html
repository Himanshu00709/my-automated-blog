Best Gluten-Free Restaurants in Nashville

Nashville, known for its vibrant music scene and Southern hospitality, is also a haven for food lovers. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding a restaurant that caters to gluten-free diets can be a challenge. Fortunately, Nashville boasts a variety of eateries that offer delicious gluten-free options. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in Nashville, ensuring that you can enjoy the city’s culinary delights without worry.

1. The Wild Cow

Located in East Nashville, The Wild Cow is a vegetarian restaurant that offers a plethora of gluten-free options. Their menu is clearly labeled, making it easy for diners to identify gluten-free dishes. From hearty salads to flavorful grain bowls, The Wild Cow ensures that gluten-free diners have plenty of choices. Their gluten-free vegan mac and cheese is a must-try, offering a creamy and satisfying alternative to the traditional dish.

2. Sunflower Cafe

Sunflower Cafe is another excellent choice for gluten-free dining in Nashville. This vegetarian and vegan-friendly cafe offers a variety of gluten-free options, including their famous gluten-free veggie burger. The cafe also provides gluten-free bread for sandwiches and wraps, ensuring that every meal can be tailored to your dietary needs. Their fresh, locally-sourced ingredients make every dish a delight.

3. Graze

Graze is a farm-to-table restaurant that focuses on fresh, seasonal ingredients. Their menu features a variety of gluten-free options, including gluten-free pasta and pizza. The restaurant takes great care to avoid cross-contamination, making it a safe choice for those with celiac disease. Their gluten-free pizza, topped with fresh vegetables and house-made sauces, is a crowd favorite.

4. Avo

Avo is a vegan restaurant that offers a completely gluten-free menu. Located in the heart of Nashville, Avo is known for its creative and flavorful dishes. From raw zucchini noodles to gluten-free flatbreads, every item on the menu is designed to be both delicious and safe for gluten-free diners. Their gluten-free desserts, such as the chocolate mousse, are the perfect way to end your meal.

5. Epice

Epice is a Lebanese restaurant that offers a variety of gluten-free options. Their menu includes gluten-free versions of traditional Lebanese dishes, such as falafel and hummus. The restaurant is committed to providing a safe dining experience for gluten-free diners, with staff trained to handle dietary restrictions. Their gluten-free pita bread is a standout, offering a perfect accompaniment to any meal.

Gluten-Free Dining Tips in Nashville

When dining out in Nashville, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the restaurant staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable gluten-free dining experience:

  • Call Ahead: Before visiting a restaurant, call ahead to confirm that they offer gluten-free options and can accommodate your dietary needs.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask the server about the ingredients and preparation methods to ensure that your meal is gluten-free.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Ensure that the restaurant has measures in place to prevent cross-contamination, such as separate cooking utensils and preparation areas.
  • Read Reviews: Look for reviews from other gluten-free diners to get an idea of the restaurant’s gluten-free offerings and safety measures.

FAQs

1. Are there dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Nashville?

Yes, there are several dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Nashville, such as Avo, which offers a completely gluten-free menu. Other restaurants, like The Wild Cow and Graze, offer extensive gluten-free options and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

2. How can I ensure that my meal is gluten-free?

To ensure that your meal is gluten-free, communicate your dietary needs clearly to the restaurant staff. Ask about the ingredients and preparation methods, and confirm that the restaurant has measures in place to prevent cross-contamination.

3. Are there gluten-free options for vegetarians and vegans in Nashville?

Absolutely! Many of the gluten-free restaurants in Nashville, such as The Wild Cow and Sunflower Cafe, also cater to vegetarians and vegans. These restaurants offer a variety of plant-based gluten-free options, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a delicious meal.

4. Can I find gluten-free desserts in Nashville?

Yes, many gluten-free restaurants in Nashville offer gluten-free desserts. Avo, for example, has a selection of gluten-free vegan desserts, including chocolate mousse and raw cheesecake.
