Why Gluten-Free Dining Matters

Gluten-free dining is not just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Symptoms can range from digestive problems to skin rashes and even neurological issues. For these individuals, dining out requires careful consideration to avoid cross-contamination and ensure that their meals are truly gluten-free.

Tips for Dining Out Gluten-Free in La Crosse

When dining out gluten-free, it’s important to take certain precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with the Staff: Always inform your server about your gluten-free needs. Ask questions about how the food is prepared and whether there’s a risk of cross-contamination.
  • Check for Dedicated Menus: Many restaurants in La Crosse offer dedicated gluten-free menus. These menus are a great resource for identifying safe options.
  • Look for Certification: Some restaurants are certified gluten-free, meaning they adhere to strict guidelines to prevent cross-contamination. Look for this certification when choosing a restaurant.
  • Be Cautious with Fried Foods: Fried foods are often cooked in shared oil, which can contain gluten. Opt for grilled or baked options instead.
  • Bring Your Own Gluten-Free Bread: If you’re unsure about the bread options at a restaurant, consider bringing your own gluten-free bread to ensure you have a safe option.
