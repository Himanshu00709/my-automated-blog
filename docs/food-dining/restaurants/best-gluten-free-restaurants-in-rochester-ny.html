Best Gluten-Free Restaurants in Rochester, NY

Rochester, NY, is a city that prides itself on its diverse culinary scene. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding a restaurant that caters to gluten-free diets can be a challenge. However, Rochester has a growing number of establishments that offer delicious and safe gluten-free options. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in Rochester, NY, where you can enjoy a meal without worrying about gluten contamination.

1. The Red Fern

Located in the heart of Rochester, The Red Fern is a vegan restaurant that also offers a wide range of gluten-free options. The menu is clearly labeled, making it easy for those with dietary restrictions to find suitable dishes. From their gluten-free pancakes to their hearty grain bowls, The Red Fern ensures that every dish is both flavorful and safe for gluten-sensitive diners.

One of the standout dishes is their gluten-free mac and cheese, made with a creamy cashew-based sauce and gluten-free pasta. The restaurant also offers a variety of gluten-free desserts, including their famous chocolate avocado mousse.

2. Owl House

The Owl House is another fantastic option for gluten-free dining in Rochester. This restaurant is known for its creative and eclectic menu, which includes a variety of gluten-free options. The staff is well-informed about gluten sensitivities and takes extra care to prevent cross-contamination.

Some of the popular gluten-free dishes at Owl House include their gluten-free fried chicken, made with a gluten-free batter and served with a side of mashed potatoes and gravy. They also offer a gluten-free version of their famous mac and cheese, which is a must-try for any visitor.

3. Next Door Bar & Grill

Next Door Bar & Grill is a local favorite that offers a dedicated gluten-free menu. The restaurant is committed to providing safe and delicious options for those with gluten sensitivities. Their gluten-free menu includes a variety of appetizers, entrees, and desserts.

One of the highlights of their gluten-free menu is the gluten-free pizza, made with a house-made gluten-free crust and topped with fresh ingredients. They also offer gluten-free pasta dishes, such as their gluten-free spaghetti with marinara sauce, which is a comforting and satisfying option.

4. The Revelry

The Revelry is a trendy restaurant that offers a variety of gluten-free options. The menu is designed to cater to different dietary needs, and the staff is knowledgeable about gluten-free requirements. The restaurant’s atmosphere is lively and welcoming, making it a great spot for a night out.

Some of the gluten-free dishes to try at The Revelry include their gluten-free fried calamari, made with a gluten-free batter and served with a tangy dipping sauce. They also offer a gluten-free burger, served on a gluten-free bun with all the classic toppings.

5. Good Luck

Good Luck is a modern American restaurant that offers a variety of gluten-free options. The menu is seasonal and focuses on fresh, locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant is known for its innovative dishes and attentive service.

One of the standout gluten-free dishes at Good Luck is their gluten-free flatbread, topped with seasonal vegetables and a variety of cheeses. They also offer a gluten-free version of their famous steak frites, served with a side of gluten-free fries.

Gluten-Free Dining Tips in Rochester

When dining out in Rochester, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the restaurant staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable gluten-free dining experience:

  • Always inform your server about your gluten sensitivity or celiac disease.
  • Ask about the restaurant’s gluten-free preparation practices to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Check if the restaurant has a dedicated gluten-free menu or if gluten-free options are clearly labeled on the regular menu.
  • Consider calling ahead to inquire about gluten-free options and to ensure that the restaurant can accommodate your needs.

FAQs

1. Are there any dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Rochester?

While there are no fully dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Rochester, many establishments offer extensive gluten-free menus and take precautions to prevent cross-contamination. Restaurants like The Red Fern and Next Door Bar & Grill are known for their gluten-free options.

2. How can I ensure that my meal is gluten-free?

To ensure that your meal is gluten-free, communicate your dietary needs clearly to the server. Ask about the ingredients and preparation methods, and inquire if the restaurant has a dedicated gluten-free kitchen or preparation area.

3. Are there gluten-free bakeries in Rochester?

Yes, Rochester has several bakeries that offer gluten-free options. Some popular choices include Flour City Bread Co., which offers a variety of gluten-free baked goods, and Simply Crepes, which has gluten-free crepes and other treats.

4. Can I find gluten-free options at fast-food restaurants in Rochester?

Some fast-food restaurants in Rochester offer gluten-free options, but it’s important to be cautious about cross-contamination. Chains like Chipotle and Five Guys have gluten-free options, but always check with the staff to ensure that your meal is prepared safely.

Conclusion

Rochester, NY, is a city that offers a variety of gluten-free dining options for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. From vegan eateries like The Red Fern to modern American restaurants like Good Luck, there’s something for everyone. By following the tips outlined in this blog post and communicating your dietary needs clearly, you can enjoy a safe and delicious gluten-free meal in Rochester. Whether you’re a local or just visiting, these restaurants are sure to provide a memorable dining experience.
