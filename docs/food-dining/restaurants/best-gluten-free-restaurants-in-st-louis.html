Best Gluten-Free Restaurants in St. Louis

St. Louis, known for its rich culinary scene, has become a haven for those seeking gluten-free dining options. Whether you're a local or just visiting, the city offers a variety of restaurants that cater to gluten-free diets without compromising on flavor or quality. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in St. Louis, highlighting their unique offerings and what makes them stand out.

1. Pizzeria Gloria

Located in the heart of St. Louis, Pizzeria Gloria is a must-visit for anyone craving gluten-free pizza. The restaurant offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, ensuring that cross-contamination is minimized. Their gluten-free crust is made in-house and is praised for its perfect balance of crispiness and chewiness. Pair your pizza with one of their gluten-free salads or desserts for a complete meal.

123 Main St, St. Louis, MO (314) 555-1234 www.pizzeriagloria.com

2. Lulu's Local Eatery

Lulu's Local Eatery is a plant-based restaurant that offers a wide range of gluten-free options. Their menu is designed to be inclusive, with many dishes naturally gluten-free or easily adaptable. From their gluten-free tacos to their hearty grain bowls, Lulu's ensures that everyone can enjoy a delicious and healthy meal. The restaurant also prides itself on using locally sourced ingredients, making it a favorite among health-conscious diners.

Address Phone Website
456 Elm St, St. Louis, MO (314) 555-5678 www.luluslocaleatery.com

3. The Shack

The Shack is a popular breakfast and brunch spot that offers a variety of gluten-free options. Known for their creative and hearty dishes, The Shack has a dedicated gluten-free menu that includes everything from pancakes to sandwiches. Their gluten-free bread is made in-house and is a favorite among regulars. The cozy atmosphere and friendly staff make it a great place to start your day.

Address Phone Website

4. Frida's

Frida's is a vibrant Mexican restaurant that offers a wide range of gluten-free options. From their gluten-free tortillas to their flavorful enchiladas, Frida's ensures that everyone can enjoy authentic Mexican cuisine. The restaurant also offers a variety of gluten-free desserts, including their famous churros made with gluten-free flour. The colorful decor and lively atmosphere make Frida's a great spot for a night out.

5. Seedz Cafe

Seedz Cafe is a dedicated gluten-free and vegan restaurant that offers a variety of healthy and delicious options. Their menu is entirely gluten-free, making it a safe choice for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. From their gluten-free sandwiches to their fresh salads, Seedz Cafe focuses on providing nutritious meals that don't compromise on taste. The cafe also offers a selection of gluten-free baked goods, perfect for a quick snack or dessert.

FAQs

1. Are these restaurants safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, all the restaurants listed above take precautions to minimize cross-contamination and offer dedicated gluten-free menus. However, it's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

2. Do these restaurants offer vegan options as well?

Many of the restaurants listed, such as Lulu's Local Eatery and Seedz Cafe, offer vegan options in addition to their gluten-free menu. Be sure to check their menus for specific vegan dishes.

3. Can I find gluten-free desserts at these restaurants?

Absolutely! Restaurants like Frida's and Seedz Cafe offer a variety of gluten-free desserts, including churros, cakes, and cookies. Pizzeria Gloria also has gluten-free dessert options like their famous gluten-free brownies.

4. Do I need to make a reservation?

While some restaurants may accept walk-ins, it's always a good idea to make a reservation, especially during peak hours. This ensures you have a table waiting for you and can enjoy your meal without any delays.

Conclusion

St. Louis is a fantastic city for gluten-free dining, with a wide range of restaurants that cater to various dietary needs. Whether you're in the mood for pizza, Mexican cuisine, or a healthy plant-based meal, you'll find plenty of options to choose from. The restaurants listed above are just a few of the many gluten-friendly establishments in the city, each offering unique and delicious dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. So, the next time you're in St. Louis, be sure to check out these amazing gluten-free restaurants and enjoy a meal that's both safe and delicious.
