Gluten-Free Restaurants in Pittsburgh: A Comprehensive Guide

Pittsburgh, a city known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse culinary scene, has become a haven for those seeking gluten-free dining options. Whether you're a local or just visiting, finding restaurants that cater to gluten-free diets can be a challenge. However, Pittsburgh has embraced the gluten-free movement with open arms, offering a variety of eateries that provide delicious, safe, and satisfying meals for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in Pittsburgh, highlighting their unique offerings and what makes them stand out.

What Makes These Restaurants Stand Out?

Each of these restaurants offers something unique for gluten-free diners. Let's take a closer look at what makes them special:

Gluten Free Goat Cafe

Located in East Liberty, Gluten Free Goat Cafe is a 100% gluten-free establishment, making it a safe haven for those with celiac disease. The cafe offers a variety of baked goods, sandwiches, and breakfast items, all made with high-quality, gluten-free ingredients. Their menu is constantly evolving, ensuring that there's always something new to try.

Eden

Eden, located in Shadyside, is a vegetarian and vegan restaurant that also caters to gluten-free diets. Their menu features a variety of plant-based dishes, many of which are naturally gluten-free. Eden is known for its creative and flavorful dishes, making it a favorite among health-conscious diners.

Pizza Taglio

Pizza Taglio in East Liberty offers a gluten-free pizza crust that is both delicious and safe for those with gluten sensitivities. The restaurant takes great care to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that their gluten-free options are truly safe to eat. With a variety of toppings to choose from, you can customize your pizza to your liking.

B52 Cafe

B52 Cafe in Lawrenceville is a Middle Eastern-inspired eatery that offers gluten-free pita bread and falafel. The cafe is known for its fresh, flavorful dishes and its commitment to providing gluten-free options. Their menu is diverse, offering something for everyone, whether you're gluten-free or not.

Double Wide Grill

Double Wide Grill in South Side is a popular spot for gluten-free diners. The restaurant offers gluten-free buns for their burgers and sandwiches, as well as a dedicated fryer for their gluten-free fries. With a laid-back atmosphere and a diverse menu, Double Wide Grill is a great option for casual dining.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in Pittsburgh

1. Are there any entirely gluten-free restaurants in Pittsburgh?

Yes, Gluten Free Goat Cafe is one of the few entirely gluten-free restaurants in Pittsburgh. They offer a wide range of gluten-free baked goods, sandwiches, and breakfast items.

2. How do I know if a restaurant is safe for gluten-free dining?

When dining out, it's important to ask about the restaurant's gluten-free practices. Look for restaurants that have a dedicated gluten-free menu, use separate cooking equipment, and are knowledgeable about cross-contamination.

3. Can I find gluten-free options at non-specialized restaurants?

Absolutely! Many restaurants in Pittsburgh offer gluten-free options, even if they are not entirely gluten-free. It's always a good idea to check the menu or call ahead to inquire about gluten-free options.

4. Are there any gluten-free bakeries in Pittsburgh?

Yes, Gluten Free Goat Cafe also functions as a bakery, offering a variety of gluten-free baked goods. Additionally, some other bakeries in the city may offer gluten-free options, so it's worth checking out local bakeries.

5. What should I do if I accidentally consume gluten?

If you accidentally consume gluten and have celiac disease or a severe gluten sensitivity, it's important to monitor your symptoms and seek medical attention if necessary. Drinking plenty of water and resting can also help alleviate symptoms.

Conclusion

Pittsburgh's culinary scene has embraced the gluten-free movement, offering a variety of dining options for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. From entirely gluten-free cafes to restaurants with dedicated gluten-free menus, there's no shortage of delicious and safe options for gluten-free diners in the city. Whether you're in the mood for pizza, Middle Eastern cuisine, or a hearty burger, Pittsburgh has something to satisfy your cravings. So, the next time you're in the Steel City, be sure to check out these fantastic gluten-free restaurants and enjoy a worry-free dining experience.
