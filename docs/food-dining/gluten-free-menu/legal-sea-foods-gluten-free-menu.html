Main Courses

When it comes to main courses, Legal Sea Foods offers a variety of gluten-free options that are sure to satisfy. Preparation Practices and Safety Measures

Legal Sea Foods takes the preparation of their gluten-free dishes seriously. The restaurant has implemented strict protocols to prevent cross-contamination, ensuring that gluten-free meals are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. Here are some of the key measures they take:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Gluten-free dishes are prepared in designated areas to avoid contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Dedicated Utensils: The kitchen uses separate utensils and cookware for gluten-free dishes to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Staff Training: All staff members are trained in gluten-free preparation practices and are knowledgeable about the menu’s gluten-free options.

These measures provide peace of mind for gluten-free diners, allowing them to enjoy their meal without worry.

2. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

While Legal Sea Foods offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions. They can guide you on which dishes can be customized to meet your needs.

3. Are there gluten-free options for children?

Yes, Legal Sea Foods offers gluten-free options for children as well. Be sure to ask your server for the gluten-free kids’ menu when dining with young ones.

4. Does Legal Sea Foods offer gluten-free desserts?

Absolutely! Legal Sea Foods offers gluten-free desserts such as the Flourless Chocolate Cake and Fresh Fruit Plate, ensuring that you can end your meal on a sweet note.
