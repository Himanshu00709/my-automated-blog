Exploring the Uno Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

In recent years, the demand for gluten-free dining options has skyrocketed, driven by the increasing awareness of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity. Uno Pizzeria & Grill, a beloved American restaurant chain, has risen to the occasion by offering a dedicated gluten-free menu. This guide will take you through the Uno gluten-free menu, highlighting the delicious options available, the precautions taken to avoid cross-contamination, and tips for dining out safely.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Uno

Uno Pizzeria & Grill has made significant strides in accommodating guests with gluten sensitivities. Their gluten-free menu is designed to provide a wide variety of options, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a meal without compromising their health. From appetizers to desserts, Uno has carefully curated a selection of dishes that cater to gluten-free needs.

Key Features of the Uno Gluten-Free Menu

  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Options: Uno offers a separate menu specifically for gluten-free items, making it easier for guests to identify safe choices.
  • Cross-Contamination Prevention: The restaurant takes precautions to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, including using separate cooking utensils and preparation areas.
  • Variety of Choices: From pizzas to salads and desserts, the gluten-free menu at Uno is diverse, ensuring that there’s something for everyone.

FAQs About the Uno Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is the Uno gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

Uno takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure additional care is taken during preparation.

2. Are the gluten-free pizzas made in a separate oven?

While Uno uses separate utensils and preparation areas, the gluten-free pizzas may be cooked in the same oven as regular pizzas. Be sure to discuss your concerns with the staff.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free pizza?

Yes, Uno allows you to customize your gluten-free pizza with a variety of toppings. Just make sure to specify that you need a gluten-free crust.

4. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Uno offers gluten-free options for kids, including gluten-free pizza and pasta dishes.
