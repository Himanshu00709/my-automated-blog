Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten-free dining is essential for individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or those who choose to avoid gluten for other health reasons. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in people with gluten-related disorders. When dining out, it's crucial to ensure that the food is prepared in a way that avoids cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.

Del Frisco's Grille takes gluten-free dining seriously, offering a variety of dishes that are free from gluten-containing ingredients. The restaurant also trains its staff to handle gluten-free requests with care, ensuring that your meal is safe and enjoyable.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Del Frisco's Grille

When dining gluten-free at Del Frisco's Grille, it's important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Inform Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten-free requirements. They can guide you through the menu and ensure that your meal is prepared safely.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: Inquire about the kitchen's procedures for avoiding cross-contamination. Del Frisco's Grille takes precautions, but it's always good to double-check.
  • Choose Naturally Gluten-Free Options: Opt for dishes that are naturally gluten-free, such as grilled meats, seafood, and salads. These items are less likely to be contaminated with gluten.
  • Check Sauces and Dressings: Some sauces and dressings may contain gluten. Always ask your server to confirm that the sauces and dressings used in your meal are gluten-free.
