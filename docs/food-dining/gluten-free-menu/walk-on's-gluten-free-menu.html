Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Walk-On's

Gluten-free dining is more than just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Walk-On's recognizes this and has developed a gluten-free menu that caters to these dietary needs without compromising on taste or quality.

Walk-On's gluten-free menu is carefully curated to ensure that all items are free from gluten-containing ingredients. Additionally, the restaurant takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, which is crucial for those with severe gluten intolerance. From appetizers to desserts, Walk-On's offers a variety of options that are both safe and delicious.

FAQs About Walk-On's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Walk-On's gluten-free menu safe for those with celiac disease?

Walk-On's takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your dietary restrictions. They can guide you on the safest options and ensure your meal is prepared with care.

2. Are the gluten-free buns at Walk-On's made in-house?

Walk-On's sources their gluten-free buns from trusted suppliers to ensure they meet gluten-free standards. They are not made in-house, but they are stored and prepared separately to avoid cross-contamination.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many items on the regular menu can be customized to be gluten-free. For example, you can request gluten-free buns for burgers or sandwiches, or ask for gluten-free pasta in pasta dishes. Always inform your server of your dietary needs.

4. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Walk-On's offers gluten-free options for kids, including gluten-free buns for burgers and sandwiches. Be sure to ask your server for the available options.

5. Does Walk-On's offer gluten-free desserts?

Yes, Walk-On's offers a delicious Flourless Chocolate Cake that is naturally gluten-free. It’s a perfect way to end your meal on a sweet note.
