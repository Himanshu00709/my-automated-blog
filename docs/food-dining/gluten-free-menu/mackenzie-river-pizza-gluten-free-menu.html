Why Choose Mackenzie River Pizza for Gluten-Free Dining?

For those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like a challenge. Cross-contamination, limited menu options, and lack of transparency can make the experience stressful. Mackenzie River Pizza addresses these concerns head-on by offering a dedicated gluten-free menu that is both delicious and safe for gluten-sensitive individuals. Their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is evident in every bite, making it a top choice for gluten-free pizza lovers.

FAQs About Mackenzie River Pizza’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Mackenzie River Pizza’s gluten-free crust safe for celiacs?

Yes, Mackenzie River Pizza takes great care to prevent cross-contamination. Their gluten-free crust is prepared separately, and the kitchen staff follows strict protocols to ensure the safety of gluten-sensitive customers.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free pizza?

Absolutely! You can customize your gluten-free pizza with any of the available toppings. Just let your server know about your dietary restrictions, and they’ll ensure your pizza is prepared safely.

3. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, Mackenzie River Pizza offers a gluten-free kids’ menu, including smaller portions of gluten-free pizza and other kid-friendly options.

4. Do they offer gluten-free beer or beverages?

Yes, Mackenzie River Pizza offers a selection of gluten-free beers and other beverages to complement your meal.
