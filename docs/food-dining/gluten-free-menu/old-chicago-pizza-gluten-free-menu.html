Old Chicago Pizza Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom is a beloved restaurant chain known for its deep-dish pizzas, craft beers, and vibrant atmosphere. However, for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. Fortunately, Old Chicago has stepped up to the plate by offering a dedicated gluten-free menu. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free options available at Old Chicago, discuss their preparation methods, and provide tips for a safe and enjoyable dining experience.

Why Choose Old Chicago for Gluten-Free Dining?

Old Chicago has made a concerted effort to cater to the growing demand for gluten-free options. Their gluten-free menu is designed to provide a variety of choices, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a delicious meal without compromising their dietary needs. The restaurant takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, making it a safer option for those with gluten sensitivities.

Tips for a Safe Gluten-Free Dining Experience

While Old Chicago takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the staff. Here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Inform the Staff: Let your server know about your gluten sensitivity or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and inform the kitchen staff.
  • Ask About Preparation: Inquire about how the gluten-free items are prepared to ensure there’s no cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s always a good idea to check for the latest gluten-free options when you visit.

FAQs About Old Chicago’s Gluten-Free Menu

Is Old Chicago’s gluten-free crust vegan?

No, Old Chicago’s gluten-free crust contains dairy and eggs, so it is not suitable for vegans.

Are the gluten-free pizzas cooked in a separate oven?

Old Chicago takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, but it’s always best to ask your server about the specific preparation methods at your location.

Can I get gluten-free beer at Old Chicago?

Yes, Old Chicago offers a selection of gluten-free beers. Be sure to ask your server for the current options available.

Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

Yes, Old Chicago’s menu clearly marks gluten-free items, making it easier for customers to identify safe options.

Conclusion

Old Chicago Pizza & Taproom has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diners, offering a variety of appetizers, pizzas, and desserts that cater to those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. By taking precautions to minimize cross-contamination and clearly marking gluten-free options on their menu, Old Chicago provides a safe and enjoyable dining experience for everyone. Whether you’re craving a classic pepperoni pizza or a decadent gluten-free brownie, Old Chicago has something to satisfy your taste buds. So, the next time you’re in the mood for pizza, consider giving Old Chicago’s gluten-free menu a try!
