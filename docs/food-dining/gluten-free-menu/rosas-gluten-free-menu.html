Nutritional Considerations

Tips for Dining Out Gluten-Free at Rosa's

Dining out gluten-free can be a challenge, but Rosa's makes it easy with their dedicated gluten-free menu. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your dietary restrictions. Rosa's staff are trained to handle gluten-free requests and can guide you through the menu.
  • Ask About Preparation: Ensure that your dish is prepared in a separate area to avoid cross-contamination. Rosa's takes precautions, but it's always good to double-check.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it's a good idea to check for any updates or new additions to the gluten-free menu before your visit.
