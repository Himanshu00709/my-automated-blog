FAQs About BJ's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is BJ's gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

BJ's takes gluten-free dining seriously and has implemented strict protocols to minimize cross-contamination. However, as with any restaurant, there is always a risk of cross-contact. It’s recommended to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

2. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many of BJ's dishes can be customized to be gluten-free. For example, you can substitute regular pasta with gluten-free pasta or request gluten-free bread for sandwiches. Always inform your server of your dietary needs to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

3. Are BJ's gluten-free options clearly labeled on the menu?

Yes, BJ's gluten-free items are clearly marked on the menu, making it easy for diners to identify safe options. Additionally, the staff is well-trained and can provide guidance if you have any questions.

4. Does BJ's offer gluten-free beer?

Yes, BJ's offers a selection of gluten-free beers, allowing you to enjoy a cold one with your meal. Be sure to ask your server for the current gluten-free beer options available.
