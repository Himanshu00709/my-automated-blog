FAQs About Walk-On's Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Walk-On's gluten-free menu safe for those with celiac disease?

Walk-On's takes great care to ensure that their gluten-free menu items are prepared in a way that minimizes cross-contamination. However, it's always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

2. Are there vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

While Walk-On's gluten-free menu is primarily focused on accommodating gluten sensitivities, there are a few vegan-friendly options available. Be sure to check with your server for the most up-to-date information.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, Walk-On's is happy to accommodate customizations to make your meal gluten-free. Just let your server know your preferences, and they will guide you through the available options.

4. Are the gluten-free buns and pasta made in-house?

Walk-On's sources their gluten-free buns and pasta from trusted suppliers to ensure quality and safety. These items are stored and prepared separately to avoid cross-contamination.
