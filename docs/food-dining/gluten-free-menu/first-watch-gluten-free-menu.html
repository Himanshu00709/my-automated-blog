First Watch Gluten-Free Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

First Watch, a popular breakfast, brunch, and lunch restaurant, has become a go-to spot for health-conscious diners. With a focus on fresh, high-quality ingredients, First Watch offers a variety of options for those with dietary restrictions, including a gluten-free menu. In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at First Watch, provide tips for dining out gluten-free, and answer some frequently asked questions.

What Makes First Watch a Great Choice for Gluten-Free Diners?

First Watch is known for its commitment to fresh, seasonal ingredients and its ability to cater to various dietary needs. The restaurant offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, making it easier for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity to enjoy a meal without worry. The staff is also well-trained in handling gluten-free orders, ensuring that cross-contamination is minimized.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at First Watch

While First Watch offers a dedicated gluten-free menu, it’s always a good idea to take extra precautions when dining out. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable gluten-free dining experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and ensure your meal is prepared safely.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: While First Watch takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s important to ask about their kitchen practices to ensure your meal is safe.
  • Check for Seasonal Specials: First Watch often features seasonal specials that may include gluten-free options. Don’t hesitate to ask about these when you visit.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is First Watch’s gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

First Watch takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your dietary needs. They can guide you on the safest options and ensure your meal is prepared with care.

Are there gluten-free bread options at First Watch?

Yes, First Watch offers gluten-free bread for certain dishes, such as the Avocado Toast and Chicken Salad Sandwich. Be sure to request gluten-free bread when ordering.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Absolutely! First Watch is known for its flexibility and willingness to accommodate dietary restrictions. You can customize many dishes to be gluten-free by substituting ingredients or omitting certain items.

Conclusion

First Watch is a fantastic option for those following a gluten-free diet. With a dedicated gluten-free menu, knowledgeable staff, and a commitment to fresh, high-quality ingredients, you can enjoy a delicious and safe meal at First Watch. Whether you’re stopping by for breakfast, brunch, or lunch, there are plenty of gluten-free options to choose from.
