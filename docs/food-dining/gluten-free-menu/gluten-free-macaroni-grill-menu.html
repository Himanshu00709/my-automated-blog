How Macaroni Grill Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Macaroni Grill takes several precautions to ensure that their gluten-free dishes are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. The kitchen staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders separately, and they use dedicated utensils and cookware to prevent cross-contamination. Additionally, the restaurant clearly labels gluten-free items on their menu, making it easier for customers to identify safe options.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Macaroni Grill

When dining at Macaroni Grill, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions. They can guide you through the menu and ensure that your meal is prepared safely. Here are a few additional tips:

  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask your server about the ingredients and preparation methods.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s always a good idea to check for the latest gluten-free options.
  • Be Clear About Cross-Contamination: If you have a severe gluten allergy, make sure to emphasize the importance of avoiding cross-contamination.
