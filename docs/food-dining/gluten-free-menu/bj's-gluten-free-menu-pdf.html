Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at BJ's

While BJ's gluten-free menu is comprehensive, here are some additional tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. They can guide you through the menu and ensure your meal is prepared safely.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Ask about the kitchen’s procedures to prevent cross-contamination. BJ's takes precautions, but it’s always good to double-check.
  • Review the PDF Menu: Before visiting, review the gluten-free menu PDF to familiarize yourself with the options and make a plan.
