Ingredients and Preparation

Johnny's Pizza takes great care in selecting ingredients that meet their high standards for quality and safety. Their gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, tapioca starch, and potato starch, ensuring a light and crispy texture. All gluten-free pizzas are prepared in a separate area of the kitchen to avoid cross-contamination, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care.

FAQs About Johnny's Pizza Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is Johnny's Pizza gluten-free crust safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, Johnny's Pizza takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, making their gluten-free crust a safe option for those with celiac disease. However, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff of your dietary needs when placing your order.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free pizza?

Absolutely! Johnny's Pizza allows you to customize your gluten-free pizza with a variety of toppings, sauces, and cheeses. Just let the staff know your preferences when ordering.

3. Are there any vegan options on the gluten-free menu?

Yes, Johnny's Pizza offers vegan cheese as an option for their gluten-free pizzas. Pair it with your favorite vegetable toppings for a delicious vegan meal.

4. How does the gluten-free crust compare to the regular crust?

While the gluten-free crust has a slightly different texture, it’s still light, crispy, and full of flavor. Many customers find it to be a satisfying alternative to traditional crusts.
