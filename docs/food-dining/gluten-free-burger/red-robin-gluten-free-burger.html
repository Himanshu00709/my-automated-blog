How Red Robin Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for gluten-free diners is cross-contamination. Red Robin takes this issue seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure that their gluten-free offerings are safe for consumption. Here’s how they do it:

  • Dedicated Preparation Areas: Red Robin uses separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free items to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Gluten-Free Buns: The gluten-free buns are stored separately and are only handled by staff wearing gloves to prevent any contact with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Staff Training: Red Robin staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, ensuring that all protocols are followed to maintain safety.

FAQs About Red Robin Gluten-Free Burgers

1. Are all Red Robin burgers gluten-free?

No, not all Red Robin burgers are gluten-free. However, Red Robin offers a dedicated gluten-free menu that includes several burger options. Be sure to specify that you need a gluten-free meal when ordering.

2. Are the fries at Red Robin gluten-free?

Red Robin’s steak fries are cooked in a dedicated fryer, making them gluten-free. However, cross-contamination is always a risk, so it’s important to inform the staff of your dietary needs.

3. Can I customize my gluten-free burger at Red Robin?

Yes, Red Robin allows you to customize your gluten-free burger. You can choose from a variety of toppings and sauces, but be sure to confirm that they are gluten-free.

4. Is the gluten-free bun at Red Robin vegan?

The gluten-free bun at Red Robin contains eggs and dairy, so it is not vegan. If you’re following a vegan diet, you may want to opt for a lettuce wrap instead.
