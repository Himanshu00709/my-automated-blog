Does Mellow Mushroom Have Gluten-Free Pizza? A Comprehensive Guide

What is Gluten and Why Does It Matter?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is harmless, but for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to severe allergic reactions. As a result, many individuals must adhere to a strict gluten-free diet to maintain their health.

When dining out, it’s crucial to ensure that the food you’re eating is truly gluten-free. Cross-contamination, where gluten-free foods come into contact with gluten-containing ingredients, can be a significant concern. This is especially true in pizzerias, where flour is often used liberally in the kitchen.

How Mellow Mushroom Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

Mellow Mushroom takes several steps to ensure that its gluten-free pizzas are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Separate Preparation Areas: Mellow Mushroom uses separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free pizzas to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Dedicated Ovens: Some locations have dedicated ovens for gluten-free pizzas, while others use pizza screens to prevent direct contact with surfaces that may have been exposed to gluten.
  • Staff Training: Employees are trained on the importance of gluten-free preparation and are knowledgeable about the ingredients used in each dish.

However, it’s important to note that while Mellow Mushroom takes precautions, cross-contamination can still occur in a shared kitchen environment. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s always a good idea to inform the staff of your dietary needs and ask about their specific preparation practices.
