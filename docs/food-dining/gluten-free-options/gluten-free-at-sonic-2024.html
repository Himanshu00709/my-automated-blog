As the demand for gluten-free options continues to rise, fast-food chains are stepping up to meet the needs of their customers. Understanding Gluten-Free at Sonic

Gluten-free diets are essential for individuals with celiac disease or gluten intolerance. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those who are sensitive to it. Sonic has recognized the importance of catering to this growing demographic and has introduced several gluten-free options in 2024. However, it's crucial to note that while Sonic offers gluten-free items, cross-contamination can still occur in their kitchens. Always inform the staff of your dietary restrictions to minimize risks.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Sonic

While Sonic offers gluten-free options, it's essential to take precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips:

  • Communicate Clearly: Always inform the staff about your gluten-free needs. Ask them to change gloves and use clean utensils to prepare your food.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Even though Sonic has dedicated fryers for gluten-free items, cross-contamination can still occur. Double-check with the staff to ensure your food is prepared safely.
  • Review the Menu: Sonic's menu may vary by location, so it's a good idea to review the gluten-free options available at your local Sonic before ordering.
