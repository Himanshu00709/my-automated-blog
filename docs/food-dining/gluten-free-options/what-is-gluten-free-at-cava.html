What is Gluten-Free at CAVA? A Comprehensive Guide

CAVA, the popular Mediterranean fast-casual restaurant, has become a go-to spot for health-conscious diners. With its customizable bowls, pitas, and salads, CAVA offers a variety of fresh and flavorful options. But for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating the menu can be a bit tricky. In this guide, we’ll break down everything you need to know about gluten-free options at CAVA, so you can enjoy your meal with confidence.

Understanding Gluten and Its Impact

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, consuming gluten can lead to a range of symptoms, from digestive issues to fatigue and even long-term health complications. Therefore, it’s crucial to identify gluten-free options when dining out. CAVA has made strides in accommodating gluten-free diets, but it’s essential to know what to look for on the menu.

Gluten-Free Options at CAVA

CAVA offers a variety of gluten-free options, from bases to proteins, dips, and toppings. Here’s a detailed breakdown of what you can enjoy:

Gluten-Free Bases

When building your bowl or salad, the base is the foundation of your meal. CAVA offers several gluten-free base options:

  • Greens & Grains: A mix of arugula, spinach, and lentils. Ensure you request no pita chips if you’re highly sensitive to gluten.
  • SuperGreens: A blend of kale, arugula, and spinach.
  • Brown Rice: A hearty and gluten-free grain option.
  • White Rice: Another gluten-free grain choice.

Gluten-Free Proteins

CAVA’s protein options are a highlight of their menu. Here are the gluten-free protein choices:

Protein Description
Grilled Chicken Juicy, marinated chicken breast grilled to perfection.
Braised Lamb Tender, slow-cooked lamb with Mediterranean spices.
Grilled Meatballs Savory beef and lamb meatballs with a hint of spice.

Gluten-Free Dips and Spreads

CAVA’s dips and spreads are a must-try, and many of them are gluten-free:

  • Hummus: Classic, red pepper, or harissa hummus.
  • Tzatziki: A refreshing yogurt and cucumber dip.
  • Crazy Feta: A spicy feta cheese spread.
  • Eggplant & Red Pepper Dip: A smoky and flavorful option.

Gluten-Free Toppings

Toppings add texture and flavor to your meal. Here are some gluten-free options:

Cucumber Tomato Salad A fresh and tangy mix of cucumbers, tomatoes, and herbs.
Pickled Onions Sweet and tangy onions that add a burst of flavor.
Olives Kalamata olives for a briny touch.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While CAVA offers many gluten-free options, cross-contamination is a potential risk, especially in a fast-casual setting. Here are some tips to minimize the risk:

  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask the staff about their gluten-free practices and whether they use separate utensils and preparation areas.
  • Skip the Pita: Avoid pita bread and pita chips, as they contain gluten.
  • Be Cautious with Falafel: Ensure the falafel is cooked in a dedicated fryer to avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing items.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Options at CAVA

Is CAVA’s rice gluten-free?

Yes, both brown and white rice at CAVA are gluten-free. However, always double-check with the staff to ensure there’s no cross-contamination.

Are CAVA’s dressings gluten-free?

Most of CAVA’s dressings are gluten-free, but it’s best to ask the staff for confirmation, as recipes can change.

Can I eat falafel at CAVA if I’m gluten-free?

Falafel is naturally gluten-free, but it’s important to ensure it’s cooked in a dedicated fryer to avoid cross-contamination.

Does CAVA offer gluten-free pita bread?

As of now, CAVA does not offer gluten-free pita bread. However, you can enjoy your meal as a bowl or salad without the pita.

Conclusion

CAVA is a fantastic option for those following a gluten-free diet, offering a wide range of delicious and customizable choices. From gluten-free bases like greens and rice to flavorful proteins, dips, and toppings, there’s something for everyone. However, it’s essential to be mindful of cross-contamination and communicate with the staff to ensure your meal is safe. With this guide, you can confidently navigate CAVA’s menu and enjoy a satisfying, gluten-free meal.
