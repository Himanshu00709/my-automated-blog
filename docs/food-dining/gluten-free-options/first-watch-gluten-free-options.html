First Watch Gluten-Free Options: A Comprehensive Guide

First Watch, known for its fresh and healthy breakfast, brunch, and lunch options, has become a favorite among health-conscious diners. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, navigating restaurant menus can be challenging. Fortunately, First Watch offers a variety of gluten-free options that cater to these dietary needs without compromising on taste or quality. In this guide, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at First Watch, provide tips for dining out, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at First Watch

First Watch takes dietary restrictions seriously and strives to accommodate guests with gluten sensitivities. While the restaurant does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, they offer a range of menu items that can be modified to be gluten-free. It’s important to communicate your dietary needs to the staff to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at First Watch

When dining out with gluten sensitivities, it’s essential to take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Here are some tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable dining experience at First Watch:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform your server about your gluten sensitivity or celiac disease. They can guide you to the best options and alert the kitchen staff.
  • Ask About Preparation: Inquire about how your food is prepared to ensure there’s no cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so always double-check with the staff to confirm that your chosen dish is still gluten-free.
  • Consider Customization: Many dishes can be modified to be gluten-free. Don’t hesitate to ask for substitutions or omissions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Does First Watch have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen?

No, First Watch does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. However, they take precautions to minimize cross-contamination and offer several gluten-free menu options.

Are the gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu?

While some gluten-free options are marked on the menu, it’s always best to confirm with your server to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Yes, many dishes at First Watch can be customized to be gluten-free. Be sure to communicate your dietary needs to your server.

Are the gluten-free buns and breads available at all locations?

Availability may vary by location. It’s recommended to call ahead and confirm if your local First Watch offers gluten-free buns or bread.

Conclusion

First Watch is a fantastic option for those seeking gluten-free dining without sacrificing flavor or variety. By communicating your needs and taking necessary precautions, you can have a safe and satisfying dining experience. Whether you’re craving a hearty breakfast or a light lunch, First Watch has something for everyone, gluten-free or not.
