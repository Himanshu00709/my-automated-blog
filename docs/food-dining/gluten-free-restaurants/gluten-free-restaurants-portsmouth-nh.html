Why Gluten-Free Dining Matters

Gluten-free dining is not just a trend; it’s a necessity for many individuals. Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause severe health issues for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Symptoms can range from digestive discomfort to more serious conditions like nutrient malabsorption and autoimmune reactions. For these individuals, dining out requires careful consideration to avoid cross-contamination and ensure that their meals are truly gluten-free.

Tips for Dining Out Gluten-Free in Portsmouth

While many restaurants in Portsmouth offer gluten-free options, it’s important to take extra precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips for dining out gluten-free:

  • Call Ahead: Contact the restaurant in advance to inquire about their gluten-free options and kitchen practices.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask your server about ingredients, preparation methods, and cross-contamination risks.
  • Check Menus Online: Many restaurants post their menus online, making it easier to identify gluten-free options before you arrive.
  • Bring Your Own Gluten-Free Items: Some restaurants may allow you to bring your own gluten-free bread or buns.
  • Use Gluten-Free Dining Apps: Apps like Find Me Gluten Free can help you locate gluten-friendly restaurants in Portsmouth.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in Portsmouth, NH

1. Are there dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Portsmouth?

While there are no fully dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Portsmouth, many establishments offer extensive gluten-free menus and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Can I find gluten-free seafood options in Portsmouth?

3. Are gluten-free options clearly marked on menus?

Most restaurants in Portsmouth clearly mark gluten-free options on their menus, but it’s always a good idea to confirm with your server.

4. Is cross-contamination a concern in Portsmouth restaurants?

Cross-contamination can be a concern, especially in kitchens that handle gluten-containing ingredients. Always inform your server of your dietary restrictions and ask about their kitchen practices.

5. Can I find gluten-free desserts in Portsmouth?
