Gluten-Free Restaurants in Asheville, NC

Asheville, North Carolina, is a haven for food lovers, and for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, the city offers a plethora of gluten-free dining options. Known for its vibrant food scene and farm-to-table ethos, Asheville has embraced the gluten-free movement with open arms. Whether you're a local or a visitor, you'll find a variety of restaurants that cater to gluten-free diets without compromising on flavor or quality. In this article, we'll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in Asheville, NC, and provide you with all the information you need to enjoy a safe and delicious dining experience.

Top Gluten-Free Restaurants in Asheville

Asheville's culinary landscape is diverse, and many restaurants have gone the extra mile to accommodate gluten-free diners. Here are some of the top spots where you can enjoy a gluten-free meal:

1. Posana

Posana is a must-visit for anyone seeking a gluten-free dining experience in Asheville. This upscale restaurant is entirely gluten-free, meaning you can order anything off the menu without worry. The menu features seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients, and the dishes are as beautiful as they are delicious. From their gluten-free bread to their decadent desserts, Posana is a testament to how flavorful gluten-free cuisine can be.

2. The Bull and Beggar

Located in the River Arts District, The Bull and Beggar offers a sophisticated dining experience with a focus on European-inspired cuisine. While not entirely gluten-free, the restaurant is highly accommodating and offers a variety of gluten-free options. Their knowledgeable staff can guide you through the menu, ensuring a safe and enjoyable meal. The charcuterie board and roasted chicken are particularly popular among gluten-free diners.

3. Plant

For those who prefer plant-based cuisine, Plant is a fantastic option. This vegan restaurant is entirely gluten-free, making it a safe haven for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. The menu is creative and flavorful, with dishes like smoked eggplant dip, mushroom risotto, and raw cheesecake. Plant's commitment to sustainability and health is evident in every bite.

4. Nine Mile

Nine Mile is a Caribbean-inspired restaurant that offers a variety of gluten-free options. Their menu is clearly labeled, making it easy to identify gluten-free dishes. The vibrant flavors and fresh ingredients make Nine Mile a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Be sure to try their gluten-free pasta dishes, which are both hearty and satisfying.

5. The Green Sage Café

The Green Sage Café is a great spot for a casual meal or a quick bite. This eco-friendly café offers a variety of gluten-free options, including sandwiches, salads, and baked goods. Their gluten-free bread is a standout, and their commitment to sustainability makes dining here a feel-good experience.

Gluten-Free Dining Tips in Asheville

When dining out in Asheville, it's important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Call Ahead: If you're unsure about a restaurant's gluten-free options, call ahead to ask about their menu and preparation practices.
  • Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to ask your server about gluten-free options and how dishes are prepared. Many restaurants in Asheville are well-versed in gluten-free dining and can provide detailed information.
  • Check for Cross-Contamination: Ensure that the restaurant takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, especially if you have celiac disease.
  • Use Gluten-Free Apps: Apps like Find Me Gluten Free can help you locate gluten-free restaurants and read reviews from other gluten-free diners.
