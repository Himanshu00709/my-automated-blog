Gluten-Free Restaurants in Seaside, Oregon

In this article, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in Seaside, Oregon, and provide tips for dining out safely.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free in Seaside

While Seaside offers many gluten-free dining options, it’s important to take precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips:

  • Communicate with Staff: Always inform the restaurant staff about your gluten-free needs. Ask about their preparation methods and whether they have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen area.
  • Check Menus Online: Many restaurants in Seaside have their menus available online, often with gluten-free options clearly marked. This can help you plan your meals in advance.
  • Be Aware of Cross-Contamination: Even if a dish is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Ask if the restaurant uses separate utensils, cookware, and fryers for gluten-free items.
  • Bring Your Own Snacks: If you’re unsure about the gluten-free options available, it’s a good idea to bring your own snacks or gluten-free bread as a backup.
