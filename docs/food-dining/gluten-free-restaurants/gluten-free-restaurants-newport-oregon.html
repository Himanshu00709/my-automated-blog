Whether you're a local or a visitor, this guide will help you navigate the best gluten-free restaurants in Newport, Oregon.

What to Look for in a Gluten-Free Restaurant

When dining out gluten-free, it's essential to ensure that the restaurant takes cross-contamination seriously. Here are some tips to help you choose the right place:

  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Menu: A separate menu indicates that the restaurant is knowledgeable about gluten-free needs.
  • Staff Training: Well-trained staff can answer your questions and ensure your meal is prepared safely.
  • Cross-Contamination Practices: Look for restaurants that use separate cooking utensils, fryers, and preparation areas for gluten-free dishes.
  • Positive Reviews: Check online reviews from other gluten-free diners to gauge the restaurant's reliability.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in Newport, Oregon

1. Are there dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Newport?

While Newport doesn't have exclusively gluten-free restaurants, many establishments offer extensive gluten-free menus and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Can I find gluten-free seafood options in Newport?

Yes, Newport is famous for its seafood, and many restaurants offer gluten-free seafood dishes, including gluten-free fish and chips, chowder, and more.

3. Are there gluten-free bakeries in Newport?

While Newport doesn't have a dedicated gluten-free bakery, several cafes and restaurants offer gluten-free baked goods, such as pancakes, waffles, and pastries.

4. How do I know if a restaurant is safe for celiacs?

Look for restaurants that have a dedicated gluten-free menu, well-trained staff, and positive reviews from other celiac diners. Don't hesitate to ask questions about their preparation practices.
