Best Gluten-Free Restaurants in Washington, DC

Washington, DC, is a city known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse culinary scene. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding a restaurant that caters to gluten-free diets can be a challenge. Fortunately, DC is home to a variety of restaurants that offer delicious, safe, and gluten-free options. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in DC, ensuring that you can enjoy a meal out without worry.

1. Ris

Located in the heart of DC, Ris is a fine-dining restaurant that offers a dedicated gluten-free menu. Chef Ris Lacoste is known for her commitment to providing safe and delicious options for those with dietary restrictions. The menu features a variety of dishes, from gluten-free pasta to seafood and steak, all prepared with the utmost care to avoid cross-contamination.

One of the standout dishes is the gluten-free risotto, which is creamy, flavorful, and made with fresh, seasonal ingredients. The restaurant also offers a selection of gluten-free desserts, including a decadent flourless chocolate cake that is a must-try for any chocolate lover.

2. Busboys and Poets

Busboys and Poets is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, offering a diverse menu that includes plenty of gluten-free options. The restaurant is known for its commitment to social justice and community engagement, and its menu reflects this ethos with a focus on fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Gluten-free diners can enjoy a variety of dishes, including gluten-free pizzas, salads, and entrees like the grilled salmon or the vegan Buddha bowl. The restaurant also offers gluten-free bread for sandwiches and burgers, making it a great option for those looking for a casual meal out.

3. Zaytinya

Zaytinya, a Mediterranean-inspired restaurant by renowned chef José Andrés, offers a wide range of gluten-free options. The menu is designed to be shared, with small plates that allow diners to sample a variety of flavors. Many of the dishes are naturally gluten-free, including the mezze platters, grilled meats, and seafood.

The restaurant is also known for its attentive staff, who are well-versed in gluten-free dining and can guide you through the menu to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. Be sure to try the gluten-free pita bread, which is soft, warm, and perfect for dipping into the restaurant’s flavorful spreads and dips.

4. Farmers Fishers Bakers

Farmers Fishers Bakers is a farm-to-table restaurant located in the Georgetown waterfront. The restaurant is committed to sustainability and offers a variety of gluten-free options on its menu. The gluten-free menu includes everything from appetizers to desserts, ensuring that there’s something for everyone.

One of the highlights of the gluten-free menu is the wood-fired pizza, which is made with a gluten-free crust and topped with fresh, seasonal ingredients. The restaurant also offers gluten-free pasta dishes, as well as a selection of gluten-free desserts, including a rich and creamy cheesecake.

5. &pizza

For a quick and casual gluten-free meal, &pizza is a great option. This fast-casual pizza chain offers gluten-free crusts that are made in a dedicated gluten-free facility, ensuring that there’s no risk of cross-contamination. The pizzas are customizable, allowing you to choose from a variety of toppings to create your perfect pie.

The gluten-free crust is thin and crispy, making it a great option for those who prefer a lighter pizza. &pizza also offers a variety of sauces and cheeses, as well as vegan and dairy-free options, making it a versatile choice for anyone with dietary restrictions.

Gluten-Free Dining Tips in DC

When dining out in DC, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs clearly to the restaurant staff. Here are a few tips to ensure a safe and enjoyable gluten-free dining experience:

  • Call Ahead: If you’re unsure about a restaurant’s gluten-free options, call ahead to ask about their menu and preparation practices.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask your server about gluten-free options and how dishes are prepared to avoid cross-contamination.
  • Check Reviews: Look for reviews from other gluten-free diners to get an idea of how well a restaurant accommodates gluten-free diets.
  • Bring Your Own: If you’re particularly sensitive to gluten, consider bringing your own gluten-free bread or crackers to ensure you have safe options.

FAQs

1. Are there any dedicated gluten-free restaurants in DC?

While there are no fully dedicated gluten-free restaurants in DC, many restaurants offer extensive gluten-free menus and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Some of the best options include Ris, Busboys and Poets, and Zaytinya.

2. Can I find gluten-free pizza in DC?

Yes, several restaurants in DC offer gluten-free pizza options. &pizza is a popular choice, offering gluten-free crusts made in a dedicated facility. Farmers Fishers Bakers also offers a delicious gluten-free wood-fired pizza.

3. How do I know if a restaurant is safe for gluten-free dining?

It’s important to communicate with the restaurant staff about your dietary needs. Ask about their gluten-free options, preparation practices, and whether they have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen or area. Reading reviews from other gluten-free diners can also provide valuable insights.

4. Are there gluten-free options for breakfast in DC?

Yes, many restaurants in DC offer gluten-free breakfast options. Some popular choices include gluten-free pancakes, omelets, and pastries. Be sure to check with the restaurant to ensure that their gluten-free options are prepared safely.

Conclusion

Washington, DC, is a fantastic city for gluten-free dining, with a wide range of restaurants offering delicious and safe options for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease. Whether you’re in the mood for fine dining, casual eats, or something in between, you’re sure to find a restaurant that caters to your needs. By following the tips outlined in this article and doing a little research, you can enjoy a worry-free dining experience in the nation’s capital. Bon appétit!
