What to Look for in a Gluten-Free Restaurant

When dining out gluten-free, it's important to ensure that the restaurant takes the necessary precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Here are some tips to help you choose the right gluten-free restaurant:

  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Menu: A restaurant with a dedicated gluten-free menu is more likely to understand the needs of gluten-free diners.
  • Staff Knowledge: The staff should be knowledgeable about gluten-free options and able to answer your questions confidently.
  • Cross-Contamination Prevention: Ensure that the restaurant has protocols in place to prevent cross-contamination, such as separate cooking areas and utensils.
  • Positive Reviews: Look for restaurants with positive reviews from other gluten-free diners. This can give you an idea of the quality and safety of the gluten-free options.

Gluten-Free Dining Tips in Hershey, PA

Dining out gluten-free in Hershey, PA, can be a delightful experience if you follow these tips:

  1. Plan Ahead: Research restaurants in advance and check their gluten-free options online. Many restaurants in Hershey have their menus available on their websites.
  2. Call Ahead: If you're unsure about a restaurant's gluten-free options, call ahead and speak to the staff. This can save you time and ensure a stress-free dining experience.
  3. Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to ask questions about how the food is prepared. A good restaurant will be happy to accommodate your needs.
  4. Bring Your Own Snacks: If you're traveling, consider bringing your own gluten-free snacks, especially if you're unsure about the availability of gluten-free options at your destination.
