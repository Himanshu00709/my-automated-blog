What to Look for in a Gluten-Free Restaurant

When dining out gluten-free, it’s important to ensure that the restaurant takes cross-contamination seriously. Here are some key factors to consider when choosing a gluten-free restaurant:

  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Menu: A restaurant with a dedicated gluten-free menu is a good sign that they understand the needs of gluten-sensitive diners.
  • Staff Knowledge: The staff should be knowledgeable about gluten-free diets and able to answer questions about ingredients and preparation methods.
  • Cross-Contamination Prevention: Look for restaurants that have protocols in place to prevent cross-contamination, such as separate cooking areas and utensils.
  • Positive Reviews: Check online reviews to see what other gluten-free diners have experienced at the restaurant.

2. Can I find gluten-free pizza in Providence?

Yes, Pizza J is a popular spot in Providence that offers gluten-free pizza crust. They are known for their delicious pizzas and take care to accommodate gluten-free diners.

3. Are there gluten-free options for breakfast in Providence?

Absolutely! Julian's is a fantastic breakfast spot that offers gluten-free bread and pancakes. They have a variety of options to start your day off right.

4. How do I know if a restaurant is safe for celiacs?

It’s important to communicate with the restaurant staff about your dietary needs. Ask about their preparation methods and whether they have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen area. Reading reviews from other celiac diners can also provide valuable insights.
