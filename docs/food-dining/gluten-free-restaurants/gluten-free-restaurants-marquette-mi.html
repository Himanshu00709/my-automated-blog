What to Look for in a Gluten-Free Restaurant

When dining out gluten-free, it's essential to ensure that the restaurant takes the necessary precautions to avoid cross-contamination. Here are some key factors to consider when choosing a gluten-free restaurant:

  • Dedicated Gluten-Free Menu: A restaurant with a dedicated gluten-free menu is more likely to understand the needs of gluten-sensitive diners.
  • Staff Knowledge: The staff should be well-informed about gluten-free options and cross-contamination risks.
  • Separate Preparation Areas: Restaurants that have separate preparation areas for gluten-free dishes reduce the risk of cross-contamination.
  • Positive Reviews: Look for restaurants with positive reviews from other gluten-free diners.
