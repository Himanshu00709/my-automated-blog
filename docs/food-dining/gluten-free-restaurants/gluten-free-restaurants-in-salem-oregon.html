FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in Salem

1. Are these restaurants safe for people with celiac disease?

Yes, all the restaurants listed above take precautions to avoid cross-contamination and are knowledgeable about the needs of people with celiac disease. However, it's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions when you arrive.

2. Do these restaurants offer gluten-free desserts?

Many of these restaurants offer gluten-free desserts. Word of Mouth Bistro, for example, is known for its gluten-free baked goods, including cakes and cookies. Be sure to ask your server about the dessert options available.

3. Can I find gluten-free options at casual dining spots in Salem?

Absolutely! Salem has a variety of casual dining spots that offer gluten-free options. Many cafes and fast-casual restaurants also cater to gluten-free diets, so you'll have plenty of choices no matter where you go.
