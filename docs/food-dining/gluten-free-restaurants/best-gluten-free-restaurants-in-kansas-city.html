Gluten-Free Dining Tips in Kansas City

When dining out in Kansas City, it’s important to communicate your dietary needs to the restaurant staff. Many restaurants are accommodating and will go out of their way to ensure that your meal is safe and delicious. Here are some tips for dining gluten-free in Kansas City:

  • Call Ahead: If you’re unsure about a restaurant’s gluten-free options, call ahead to ask about their menu and preparation methods.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t be afraid to ask questions about how your food is prepared. Make sure to ask about cross-contamination and whether the restaurant has a dedicated gluten-free fryer.
  • Check Online Reviews: Many restaurants have online reviews that mention their gluten-free options. This can be a helpful resource when deciding where to dine.
  • Bring Your Own Gluten-Free Bread: If you’re dining at a restaurant that doesn’t offer gluten-free bread, consider bringing your own.

FAQs

1. Are there any dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Kansas City?

While there are no fully dedicated gluten-free restaurants in Kansas City, many restaurants offer extensive gluten-free menus and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination.

2. Can I find gluten-free barbecue in Kansas City?

Yes, some barbecue restaurants in Kansas City offer gluten-free options. Be sure to ask about sauces and preparation methods to ensure they are gluten-free.

3. Are there gluten-free bakeries in Kansas City?

Yes, there are several gluten-free bakeries in Kansas City, including Ibis Bakery and Sweet Love Bakery, which offer a variety of gluten-free baked goods.

4. How do I know if a restaurant is safe for celiacs?

Look for restaurants that have a dedicated gluten-free menu and take precautions to avoid cross-contamination. It’s also a good idea to read online reviews and call ahead to ask about their preparation methods.
