Gluten-Free Restaurants in St. Augustine, FL

St. Augustine, Florida, is not only the oldest city in the United States but also a haven for food enthusiasts. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding safe and delicious dining options can be a challenge. Fortunately, St. Augustine boasts a variety of restaurants that cater to gluten-free diets, offering everything from casual bites to fine dining experiences. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in St. Augustine, ensuring you can enjoy your visit without compromising your health or taste buds.

St. Augustine’s culinary scene is diverse, and many restaurants have embraced the gluten-free movement. Below, we’ve curated a list of top spots where you can enjoy gluten-free meals with confidence.

What Makes These Restaurants Stand Out?

Gluten-free dining is more than just offering a few menu items. It’s about ensuring cross-contamination is minimized and that the staff is knowledgeable about gluten-free needs. The restaurants listed above go the extra mile to provide safe and delicious options for their gluten-free patrons.

1. The Floridian

Located in the heart of downtown St. Augustine, The Floridian is a must-visit for anyone seeking gluten-free Southern cuisine. Their menu features locally sourced ingredients, and they offer gluten-free bread for sandwiches and burgers. The staff is well-trained in handling gluten-free requests, making it a safe choice for those with celiac disease.

2. Collage Restaurant

For a more upscale dining experience, Collage Restaurant offers an array of gluten-free options, including pasta and desserts. Their chefs are meticulous about avoiding cross-contamination, and the ambiance is perfect for a special night out.

3. Mojo's Tacos

If you’re craving Mexican food, Mojo's Tacos on Anastasia Island has you covered. Their gluten-free tacos and bowls are made with fresh ingredients, and they even offer gluten-free chips for dipping. It’s a casual spot with a vibrant atmosphere.

4. Sunset Grille

Sunset Grille, located near St. Augustine Beach, is a great option for seafood lovers. They offer gluten-free seafood dishes and salads, and their waterfront views make for a memorable dining experience.

5. Crave Food Truck

For a unique dining experience, check out Crave Food Truck. Their gluten-free mac and cheese and sandwiches are crowd favorites. The truck moves around, so be sure to check their social media for the latest location.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining in St. Augustine

1. Are gluten-free options clearly marked on menus?

Yes, most restaurants in St. Augustine clearly mark gluten-free options on their menus. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure proper handling.

2. Do these restaurants offer vegan or dairy-free options as well?

Many of the restaurants listed, such as The Floridian and Mojo's Tacos, offer vegan and dairy-free options alongside their gluten-free dishes. Be sure to ask your server for recommendations.

3. Is cross-contamination a concern?

While the restaurants listed take precautions to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always best to communicate your needs clearly with the staff. Most establishments are happy to accommodate special requests.

4. Can I find gluten-free desserts in St. Augustine?

Absolutely! Collage Restaurant and several other spots offer gluten-free desserts, including cakes, cookies, and more. Don’t hesitate to ask about dessert options when dining out.

Conclusion

St. Augustine, FL, is a fantastic destination for gluten-free diners. With a variety of restaurants offering safe and delicious options, you can explore the city’s rich history and culture without worrying about your dietary needs. Whether you’re in the mood for Southern comfort food, fresh seafood, or international cuisine, there’s something for everyone. So, the next time you’re in St. Augustine, be sure to check out these gluten-free restaurants and enjoy a worry-free dining experience.
