Tips for Dining Gluten-Free in Gatlinburg

When dining out gluten-free, it’s essential to take precautions to ensure your meal is safe. Here are some tips to help you navigate gluten-free dining in Gatlinburg:

  • Communicate with Staff: Always inform your server about your gluten-free needs. Ask questions about how dishes are prepared and whether they can accommodate your dietary restrictions.
  • Check Menus Online: Many restaurants in Gatlinburg offer gluten-free menus online. Reviewing the menu beforehand can help you make an informed decision.
  • Look for Gluten-Free Certifications: Some restaurants have gluten-free certifications or labels on their menus, indicating that they follow strict gluten-free protocols.
  • Be Cautious with Cross-Contamination: Even if a dish is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur in the kitchen. Ask if the restaurant has separate preparation areas or utensils for gluten-free meals.
