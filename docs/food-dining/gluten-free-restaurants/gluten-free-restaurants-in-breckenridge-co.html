Gluten-Free Restaurants in Breckenridge, CO

Breckenridge, Colorado, is a picturesque mountain town known for its stunning landscapes, outdoor activities, and vibrant dining scene. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, finding safe and delicious dining options can be a challenge. Fortunately, Breckenridge is home to a variety of restaurants that cater to gluten-free diets, offering everything from hearty breakfasts to gourmet dinners. In this guide, we’ll explore some of the best gluten-free restaurants in Breckenridge, CO, and provide tips for dining out safely.

Top Gluten-Free Restaurants in Breckenridge

Breckenridge has a growing number of restaurants that understand the importance of offering gluten-free options. Here are some of the top spots to enjoy a gluten-free meal:

1. The Hearthstone Restaurant

Located in a historic Victorian home, The Hearthstone Restaurant offers a fine dining experience with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. Their menu features several gluten-free options, including their famous Colorado lamb and fresh seafood dishes. The staff is knowledgeable about gluten-free preparation, ensuring a safe dining experience.

2. Downstairs at Eric's

Downstairs at Eric's is a family-friendly restaurant that offers a wide range of gluten-free options. From their gluten-free pizzas to their extensive selection of burgers and sandwiches, there’s something for everyone. They also have a dedicated gluten-free fryer, making their fries and other fried items safe for those with gluten sensitivities.

3. Empire Burger

Empire Burger is a must-visit for burger lovers. They offer gluten-free buns and a variety of toppings to create your perfect burger. Their menu also includes gluten-free salads and sides, making it easy to enjoy a complete meal without worrying about cross-contamination.

4. Modis

Modis is a contemporary American restaurant that offers a sophisticated dining experience. Their menu includes several gluten-free options, such as their roasted beet salad and grilled salmon. The chefs at Modis are well-versed in gluten-free cooking, ensuring that your meal is both safe and delicious.

5. Breckenridge Brewery & Pub

For those who enjoy a good beer with their meal, Breckenridge Brewery & Pub offers a selection of gluten-free beers. Their menu also includes gluten-free options like their bison burger and quinoa salad. The pub’s casual atmosphere makes it a great spot to relax after a day on the slopes.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free in Breckenridge

When dining out in Breckenridge, it’s important to take a few precautions to ensure a safe gluten-free experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with the Staff: Always inform your server about your gluten-free needs. Ask questions about how dishes are prepared and whether there’s a risk of cross-contamination.
  • Check Menus Online: Many restaurants in Breckenridge have their menus available online, making it easy to check for gluten-free options before you arrive.
  • Look for Gluten-Free Certifications: Some restaurants have gluten-free certifications or labels on their menus, indicating that they follow strict gluten-free preparation guidelines.
  • Bring Your Own Snacks: If you’re unsure about the availability of gluten-free options, consider bringing your own snacks or gluten-free bread to supplement your meal.
