Gluten-Free Outback Menu: A Comprehensive Guide

Outback Steakhouse is a popular restaurant chain known for its hearty meals and Australian-themed ambiance. For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often be a challenge. However, Outback Steakhouse has made significant strides in accommodating gluten-free diets. This guide will walk you through the gluten-free options available at Outback, ensuring you can enjoy a delicious and safe meal.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Outback

Outback Steakhouse has a dedicated gluten-free menu, which is a testament to their commitment to catering to diverse dietary needs. The menu is designed to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, although it’s always advisable to inform your server about your dietary restrictions. The gluten-free options span across appetizers, entrees, sides, and even desserts, making it possible to enjoy a full-course meal without worry.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Outback

Is Outback Steakhouse safe for celiacs?

Outback Steakhouse takes precautions to minimize cross-contamination, but it’s always best to inform your server about your dietary needs to ensure the safest dining experience.

Are the fries at Outback gluten-free?

Outback’s fries are not gluten-free as they are cooked in shared fryers. However, there are plenty of other gluten-free side options available.

Can I get a gluten-free bun for my burger?

Yes, Outback offers gluten-free buns upon request. Be sure to specify your need for a gluten-free bun when ordering.

Are the sauces gluten-free?

Many of Outback’s sauces are gluten-free, but it’s important to check with your server as some may contain gluten.

Conclusion

Outback Steakhouse has made significant efforts to accommodate gluten-free diners, offering a wide range of options across all courses. With this guide, you can confidently navigate the gluten-free menu at Outback and enjoy a delicious, worry-free meal.
