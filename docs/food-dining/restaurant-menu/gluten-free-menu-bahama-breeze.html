Understanding Gluten-Free Dining

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, and it can cause adverse reactions in individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. For these individuals, consuming gluten can lead to symptoms such as bloating, diarrhea, fatigue, and even long-term health complications. As a result, many restaurants have started to offer gluten-free options to cater to this growing demographic.

Bahama Breeze has recognized the importance of providing gluten-free choices and has developed a menu that ensures guests can enjoy their meals without worry. The restaurant takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, but it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions to ensure your meal is prepared safely.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Bahama Breeze

While Bahama Breeze offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take certain precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate with Your Server: Always inform your server about your gluten-free dietary needs. They can guide you through the menu and alert the kitchen staff to take extra precautions.
  • Ask About Preparation: Some dishes may be naturally gluten-free but could be prepared in a way that introduces cross-contamination. Don’t hesitate to ask about the preparation methods.
  • Check for Updates: Menus can change, so it’s a good idea to check for any updates or new gluten-free offerings before your visit.
  • Consider Cross-Contamination: While Bahama Breeze takes steps to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to double-check, especially if you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease.
