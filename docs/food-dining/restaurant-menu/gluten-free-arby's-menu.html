Gluten-Free Arby's Menu: A Detailed Guide

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, fast-food chains like Arby's are increasingly offering gluten-free options to cater to this growing demographic. In this blog post, we'll explore the gluten-free options available at Arby's, provide tips for safely ordering, and answer some frequently asked questions about gluten-free dining at this popular fast-food chain.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Arby's

Arby's is known for its roast beef sandwiches, curly fries, and a variety of other meat-centric offerings. While the menu is not entirely gluten-free, there are several items that can be enjoyed by those avoiding gluten. It's important to note that cross-contamination is always a risk in fast-food environments, so individuals with severe gluten intolerance should exercise caution.

Gluten-Free Options at Arby's

Below is a list of gluten-free items available at Arby's. Please note that this list is based on the most recent information available, and menu items may vary by location. Always double-check with the staff to ensure that the items meet your dietary needs.

Menu Item Description
Roast Beef Arby's classic roast beef is naturally gluten-free. However, it's important to order it without the bun to avoid gluten.
Turkey Breast Similar to the roast beef, the turkey breast is gluten-free when ordered without the bun.
Ham Arby's ham is another gluten-free option, but again, it should be ordered without the bun.
Side Salad Arby's side salad is a safe gluten-free option. Be sure to ask for a gluten-free dressing, such as the Light Italian Dressing.
Chopped Farmhouse Salad This salad is gluten-free when ordered without croutons. Opt for a gluten-free dressing to complete your meal.
Potato Cakes Arby's potato cakes are a gluten-free side option, perfect for those looking for a hearty addition to their meal.

Tips for Ordering Gluten-Free at Arby's

When ordering gluten-free at Arby's, communication is key. Here are some tips to ensure a safe dining experience:

  • Specify Your Dietary Needs: Clearly inform the staff that you require a gluten-free meal due to celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.
  • Avoid Cross-Contamination: Request that your food be prepared separately to minimize the risk of cross-contamination with gluten-containing items.
  • Check Ingredients: Always ask for the ingredient list or allergen information to confirm that your chosen items are gluten-free.
  • Skip the Bun: Most of Arby's sandwiches can be made gluten-free by simply removing the bun. You can also ask for a lettuce wrap as an alternative.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Dining at Arby's

1. Are Arby's fries gluten-free?

Unfortunately, Arby's curly fries and crinkle-cut fries are not gluten-free due to the seasoning and batter used. However, the potato cakes are a gluten-free alternative.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Arby's?

As of now, Arby's does not offer gluten-free buns. However, you can enjoy their meats without the bun or opt for a lettuce wrap.

3. Is Arby's sauce gluten-free?

Arby's classic sauce is gluten-free, but it's always a good idea to double-check with the staff, as recipes can change.

4. Are there any gluten-free desserts at Arby's?

Arby's does not currently offer gluten-free desserts. However, you can enjoy a side salad or potato cakes as a satisfying alternative.

Conclusion

While Arby's may not be the first place that comes to mind for gluten-free dining, there are still several options available for those avoiding gluten. By carefully selecting menu items and communicating your dietary needs to the staff, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying meal at Arby's. Always remember to double-check ingredients and be mindful of cross-contamination to ensure a positive dining experience.
