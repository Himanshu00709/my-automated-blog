Gluten-Free Menu at Red Robin: A Comprehensive Guide

Red Robin, a popular American chain known for its gourmet burgers and bottomless fries, has made significant strides in catering to customers with dietary restrictions. For those who follow a gluten-free diet, whether due to celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or personal preference, Red Robin offers a variety of options that allow you to enjoy a delicious meal without compromising your health. In this blog post, we’ll explore the gluten-free menu at Red Robin, provide tips for dining safely, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Understanding Gluten-Free Dining at Red Robin

Red Robin has taken steps to ensure that gluten-free diners can enjoy their meals with confidence. The restaurant provides a detailed gluten-free menu, which includes a variety of burgers, salads, and sides. However, it’s important to note that while Red Robin takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, the kitchen is not entirely gluten-free. Therefore, if you have severe gluten intolerance or celiac disease, it’s crucial to communicate your needs clearly to the staff.

Gluten-Free Menu Options

Red Robin’s gluten-free menu is extensive, offering a range of options to suit different tastes. Below is a breakdown of some of the most popular gluten-free items available:

Gluten-Free Burgers

Red Robin offers a variety of gluten-free burgers, which can be served on a gluten-free bun or as a lettuce wrap. Some of the standout options include:

  • Royal Red Robin Burger: A classic burger topped with a fried egg, bacon, and American cheese.
  • Guacamole Bacon Burger: A flavorful burger topped with fresh guacamole, bacon, and pepper jack cheese.
  • Banzai Burger: A teriyaki-glazed burger topped with grilled pineapple and cheddar cheese.

Gluten-Free Salads

For those looking for a lighter option, Red Robin offers several gluten-free salads. These salads are made with fresh ingredients and can be customized to suit your preferences. Some popular choices include:

  • Simply Grilled Chicken Salad: A refreshing salad with grilled chicken, mixed greens, tomatoes, and cucumbers.
  • Ensenada Chicken Platter: A hearty salad featuring grilled chicken, black beans, corn, and avocado.

Gluten-Free Sides

No meal is complete without a side, and Red Robin offers several gluten-free options to complement your main dish. Some of the gluten-free sides include:

  • Steamed Broccoli: A healthy and delicious side option.
  • Apple Slices: A sweet and refreshing choice.
  • Side Salad: A simple salad with mixed greens and your choice of dressing.

Tips for Dining Gluten-Free at Red Robin

While Red Robin offers a variety of gluten-free options, it’s important to take certain precautions to ensure a safe dining experience. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Communicate Your Needs: Inform your server about your gluten intolerance or celiac disease. Red Robin staff are trained to handle dietary restrictions and can guide you through the menu.
  • Ask About Cross-Contamination: While Red Robin takes steps to avoid cross-contamination, it’s always a good idea to ask about their procedures and express any concerns you may have.
  • Check the Gluten-Free Menu: Red Robin’s gluten-free menu is regularly updated, so be sure to check it for the latest options and any changes in ingredients.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is Red Robin’s gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

While Red Robin takes precautions to avoid cross-contamination, the kitchen is not entirely gluten-free. If you have celiac disease, it’s important to communicate your needs clearly to the staff and ask about their procedures to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.

2. Can I get a gluten-free bun at Red Robin?

Yes, Red Robin offers gluten-free buns for their burgers. You can also opt for a lettuce wrap if you prefer.

3. Are Red Robin’s fries gluten-free?

Red Robin’s fries are cooked in a dedicated fryer, making them gluten-free. However, it’s always a good idea to confirm with the staff to ensure there have been no changes in their cooking procedures.

4. Does Red Robin offer gluten-free desserts?

Red Robin’s gluten-free dessert options are limited. However, you can enjoy fresh fruit or a gluten-free milkshake as a sweet treat.

Conclusion

Red Robin has made significant efforts to accommodate gluten-free diners, offering a variety of delicious options that cater to different tastes and dietary needs. From gourmet burgers to fresh salads and sides, there’s something for everyone on the gluten-free menu. By following the tips provided and communicating your needs clearly, you can enjoy a safe and satisfying dining experience at Red Robin. Whether you’re a long-time fan of the restaurant or trying it for the first time, Red Robin’s gluten-free menu is sure to impress.
