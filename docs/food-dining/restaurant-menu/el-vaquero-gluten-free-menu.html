Why Gluten-Free Dining Matters

Gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, can cause serious health issues for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Even a small amount of gluten can trigger symptoms such as bloating, diarrhea, fatigue, and long-term damage to the intestines. For these individuals, dining out can be a daunting experience, as cross-contamination and hidden gluten are constant concerns. Restaurants like El Vaquero that prioritize gluten-free options provide a safe and enjoyable dining experience for everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions.

FAQs About El Vaquero’s Gluten-Free Menu

1. Is El Vaquero’s gluten-free menu safe for people with celiac disease?

El Vaquero takes great care to prevent cross-contamination in their kitchen. However, it’s always a good idea to inform your server about your dietary restrictions so they can take extra precautions.

2. Are the corn tortillas at El Vaquero gluten-free?

Yes, the corn tortillas used at El Vaquero are gluten-free. They are made from 100% corn and do not contain any wheat flour.

3. Can I customize my order to make it gluten-free?

Absolutely! El Vaquero’s staff is happy to accommodate special requests. Just let your server know about your dietary needs, and they will guide you through the menu options.

4. Are there gluten-free options for kids?

Yes, El Vaquero offers gluten-free options for kids, including tacos, quesadillas, and rice bowls. Be sure to ask your server for the gluten-free kids’ menu.
