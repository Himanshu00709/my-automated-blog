Does Round Table Have Gluten-Free Pizza? A Comprehensive Guide

Understanding Gluten-Free Pizza

Before diving into Round Table’s specific offerings, it’s important to understand what makes a pizza gluten-free. Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye, which are common ingredients in traditional pizza dough. For a pizza to be considered gluten-free, it must be made with alternative flours such as rice flour, almond flour, or tapioca flour. Additionally, the preparation process must avoid cross-contamination with gluten-containing ingredients.

Gluten-free pizza has become increasingly popular, not only among those with gluten-related disorders but also among health-conscious individuals. Many pizza chains have responded to this demand by introducing gluten-free crust options. But how does Round Table Pizza stack up?

Round Table’s Gluten-Free Pizza Options

Round Table Pizza does offer a gluten-free pizza option, catering to customers who need or prefer to avoid gluten. The gluten-free crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and other gluten-free ingredients. However, it’s important to note that while the crust itself is gluten-free, cross-contamination can occur during preparation, especially in a kitchen that also handles traditional pizza dough.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

One of the biggest challenges for those with celiac disease or severe gluten sensitivity is the risk of cross-contamination. Even if a pizza is made with gluten-free ingredients, it can still come into contact with gluten during preparation. This can happen through shared utensils, cutting boards, or even the same oven used for regular pizzas.

Round Table Pizza acknowledges this risk and advises customers with severe gluten allergies to exercise caution. While the chain takes steps to reduce cross-contamination, such as using separate preparation areas and utensils, the possibility of trace amounts of gluten cannot be entirely eliminated. If you have a severe gluten allergy, it’s always best to consult with the restaurant staff and make an informed decision based on your comfort level.
