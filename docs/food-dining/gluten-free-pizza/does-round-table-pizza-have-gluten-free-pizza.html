Does Round Table Pizza Have Gluten-Free Pizza?

For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, finding a safe and delicious pizza option can be a challenge. Round Table Pizza, a popular pizza chain known for its "Last Honest Pizza," has been a go-to spot for pizza lovers for decades. But does Round Table Pizza offer gluten-free options? In this article, we’ll explore the gluten-free offerings at Round Table Pizza, how they cater to gluten-sensitive customers, and what you need to know before ordering.

Understanding Gluten-Free Diets

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. For people with celiac disease or gluten intolerance, consuming gluten can lead to serious health issues, including digestive problems, nutrient deficiencies, and long-term damage to the small intestine. As a result, many individuals must adhere to a strict gluten-free diet. This has led to an increased demand for gluten-free options at restaurants, including pizza chains like Round Table Pizza.

Round Table Pizza's Gluten-Free Options

Round Table Pizza has recognized the growing need for gluten-free options and has introduced a gluten-free pizza crust to its menu. This crust is made from a blend of rice flour, potato starch, and other gluten-free ingredients, providing a safe alternative for those who cannot consume gluten. However, it’s important to note that while the crust itself is gluten-free, cross-contamination can still occur in the kitchen. Round Table Pizza does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, so there is a risk of gluten exposure during preparation.

Gluten-Free Pizza Toppings

When ordering a gluten-free pizza at Round Table Pizza, you can choose from a variety of toppings. Most of their standard toppings, such as pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, and onions, are naturally gluten-free. However, some toppings, like certain sauces or specialty meats, may contain gluten. It’s always a good idea to check with the staff or consult the allergen menu to ensure that your chosen toppings are safe.

Cross-Contamination Concerns

While Round Table Pizza offers a gluten-free crust, it’s crucial to be aware of the potential for cross-contamination. Cross-contamination can occur when gluten-free food comes into contact with surfaces, utensils, or ingredients that have been exposed to gluten. Since Round Table Pizza does not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen, there is a risk that your gluten-free pizza could be exposed to gluten during preparation. If you have severe gluten sensitivity or celiac disease, it’s important to communicate your needs clearly to the staff and ask about their preparation practices.
