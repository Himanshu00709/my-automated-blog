Bonefish Restaurant Gluten-Free Menu: A Detailed Guide

For those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, dining out can often feel like navigating a minefield. However, Bonefish Grill has made it their mission to provide a safe and enjoyable dining experience for everyone, including those who need to avoid gluten. In this article, we’ll take a deep dive into Bonefish Restaurant’s gluten-free menu, exploring the options available, the precautions they take, and what makes this restaurant a standout choice for gluten-free diners.

Why Bonefish Grill is a Great Choice for Gluten-Free Dining

Bonefish Grill is renowned for its fresh seafood and innovative dishes, but what sets it apart is its commitment to accommodating dietary restrictions. The restaurant has a dedicated gluten-free menu, which is carefully curated to ensure that guests with gluten sensitivities can enjoy a wide variety of dishes without worry. The staff is trained to handle gluten-free orders with care, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.

Exploring the Gluten-Free Menu

The gluten-free menu at Bonefish Grill is extensive, offering a range of appetizers, entrees, and even desserts. Here’s a closer look at some of the standout options:

Appetizers

Start your meal with one of Bonefish Grill’s gluten-free appetizers. The Bang Bang Shrimp is a fan favorite, featuring crispy shrimp tossed in a creamy, spicy sauce. Another great option is the Mussels Josephine, which are steamed in a flavorful white wine and garlic sauce.

Entrees

When it comes to entrees, Bonefish Grill offers a variety of gluten-free options. The Grilled Salmon is a popular choice, served with your choice of sauce and a side of seasonal vegetables. For something a bit different, try the Lily’s Chicken, a grilled chicken breast topped with goat cheese, spinach, and artichoke hearts.

Desserts

Yes, even dessert is possible on a gluten-free diet at Bonefish Grill! The Key Lime Pie is a must-try, with its tangy lime filling and gluten-free crust. Another option is the Crème Brûlée, a classic dessert that’s naturally gluten-free.

FAQs About Bonefish Grill’s Gluten-Free Menu

Is Bonefish Grill’s gluten-free menu safe for celiacs?

Bonefish Grill takes great care to minimize the risk of cross-contamination, but they do not have a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. If you have celiac disease, it’s important to inform your server so they can take extra precautions.

Are the fries at Bonefish Grill gluten-free?

Unfortunately, the fries at Bonefish Grill are not gluten-free as they are cooked in a shared fryer. However, there are plenty of other gluten-free side options available, such as steamed vegetables or a baked potato.

Can I customize my order to be gluten-free?

Yes, many dishes on the regular menu can be customized to be gluten-free. Just let your server know about your dietary restrictions, and they will guide you through the options.

Whether you’re in the mood for fresh seafood, a hearty chicken dish, or a decadent dessert, you’ll find plenty of options to satisfy your cravings. So next time you’re looking for a restaurant that caters to your dietary needs, give Bonefish Grill a try—you won’t be disappointed!
