How Bareburger Ensures Gluten-Free Safety

One of the biggest concerns for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease is cross-contamination. Bareburger takes this issue seriously by implementing strict kitchen protocols. Gluten-free items are prepared separately, and staff are trained to handle gluten-free orders with care. Additionally, Bareburger clearly labels gluten-free items on their menu, making it easy for customers to make informed choices.

FAQs About Gluten-Free Bareburger

1. Are all Bareburger locations gluten-free friendly?

Yes, all Bareburger locations offer gluten-free options. However, it's always a good idea to inform the staff about your dietary restrictions to ensure proper handling of your order.

2. Can I customize my gluten-free order?

Absolutely! Bareburger encourages customization. You can swap out ingredients or add extras to suit your preferences, just make sure to specify that you need a gluten-free meal.

3. Are the gluten-free buns vegan as well?

Some Bareburger locations offer vegan gluten-free buns, but it's best to check with your local restaurant to confirm availability.

4. How does Bareburger prevent cross-contamination?

Bareburger uses separate preparation areas and utensils for gluten-free orders. Staff are trained to follow strict protocols to minimize the risk of cross-contamination.
