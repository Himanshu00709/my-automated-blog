What is Gluta Face Wash?

Gluta Face Wash is a skincare product that is specifically formulated to cleanse the skin while providing additional benefits such as brightening, whitening, and reducing the appearance of dark spots. How to Use Gluta Face Wash

To get the most out of your Gluta Face Wash, it's important to use it correctly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wet Your Face: Start by wetting your face with lukewarm water. This helps to open up your pores and prepare your skin for cleansing.
  2. Apply the Face Wash: Take a small amount of Gluta Face Wash and gently massage it onto your face in circular motions. Focus on areas with more oil or dirt buildup.
  3. Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse your face thoroughly with lukewarm water, ensuring that all traces of the face wash are removed.
  4. Pat Dry: Gently pat your face dry with a clean towel. Avoid rubbing, as this can irritate the skin.
  5. Follow Up with Moisturizer: After cleansing, apply a moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated and to lock in the benefits of the face wash.
