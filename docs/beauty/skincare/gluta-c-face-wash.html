How to Use Gluta-C Face Wash

To achieve the best results, it’s important to use Gluta-C Face Wash correctly. Follow these steps for optimal skin benefits:

  1. Wet Your Face: Start by splashing your face with lukewarm water to open up your pores.
  2. Apply the Face Wash: Take a small amount of Gluta-C Face Wash and gently massage it onto your face in circular motions. Avoid the eye area.
  3. Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse off the face wash with lukewarm water, ensuring that all product is removed from your skin.
  4. Pat Dry: Gently pat your face dry with a clean towel. Avoid rubbing, as this can cause irritation.
  5. Follow Up with Moisturizer: After cleansing, apply your favorite moisturizer to lock in hydration and keep your skin soft and supple.
