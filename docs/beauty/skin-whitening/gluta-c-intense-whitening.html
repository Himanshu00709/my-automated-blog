What is Gluta C Intense Whitening?

Gluta C Intense Whitening is a skincare product that combines the power of two potent ingredients: Glutathione and Vitamin C. Glutathione is a naturally occurring antioxidant in the body that plays a crucial role in skin lightening and detoxification. Vitamin C, on the other hand, is a well-known antioxidant that helps in collagen production and skin brightening. Together, these ingredients work synergistically to reduce melanin production, which is responsible for skin pigmentation, and promote a more even skin tone.

How Does Gluta C Intense Whitening Work?

The primary mechanism of Gluta C Intense Whitening revolves around its ability to inhibit the enzyme tyrosinase, which is responsible for melanin production. By reducing melanin synthesis, the product helps to lighten dark spots, hyperpigmentation, and uneven skin tone. Additionally, the antioxidant properties of both Glutathione and Vitamin C help to protect the skin from oxidative stress caused by free radicals, which can lead to premature aging and skin damage.
