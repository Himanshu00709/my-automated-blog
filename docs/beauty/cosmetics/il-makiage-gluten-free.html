What Does Gluten-Free Mean in Makeup?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. While it's commonly associated with food, gluten can also be present in cosmetics, particularly in products like lipsticks and foundations. For individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity, even topical exposure to gluten can cause adverse reactions. Gluten-free makeup ensures that these individuals can enjoy beauty products without the risk of a reaction.

IL MAKIAGE has recognized the importance of catering to this demographic, ensuring that their gluten-free products are not only safe but also deliver the same high performance as their regular line.
