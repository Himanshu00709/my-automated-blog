How to Use Gluta Milk Soap

Using Gluta Milk Soap is simple, but following the correct steps can enhance its effectiveness. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wet Your Skin: Start by wetting your skin with lukewarm water. This helps to open up the pores, allowing the soap to penetrate deeper.
  2. Lather the Soap: Rub the soap between your hands or on a washcloth to create a rich lather.
  3. Apply to Skin: Gently massage the lather onto your skin in circular motions. Focus on areas that need extra attention, such as dark spots or uneven skin tone.
  4. Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse off the soap with cool water to close the pores and lock in the moisture.
  5. Pat Dry: Pat your skin dry with a soft towel. Avoid rubbing, as this can irritate the skin.
  6. Moisturize: Follow up with a good moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated.
