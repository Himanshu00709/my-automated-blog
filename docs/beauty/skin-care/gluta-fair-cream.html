How to Use Gluta Fair Cream

To achieve the best results with Gluta Fair Cream, it is essential to use it correctly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Cleanse Your Face: Start by washing your face with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and makeup.
  2. Tone Your Skin: Apply a toner to balance your skin’s pH levels and prepare it for the cream.
  3. Apply Gluta Fair Cream: Take a small amount of the cream and gently massage it onto your face and neck in upward circular motions. Avoid the eye area.
  4. Moisturize: Follow up with a moisturizer to lock in the cream and keep your skin hydrated.
  5. Use Sunscreen: During the day, always apply sunscreen to protect your skin from UV rays, which can counteract the effects of the cream.

FAQs About Gluta Fair Cream

1. Is Gluta Fair Cream suitable for all skin types?

Yes, Gluta Fair Cream is formulated to be suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin. However, it’s always recommended to do a patch test before full application to ensure there’s no adverse reaction.

2. How long does it take to see results?

Results may vary depending on individual skin types and conditions. However, most users report noticeable improvements in skin tone and texture within 2-4 weeks of consistent use.

3. Can I use Gluta Fair Cream during the day?

Yes, you can use Gluta Fair Cream during the day. However, it’s crucial to follow up with sunscreen to protect your skin from UV rays, which can cause pigmentation and counteract the cream’s effects.

4. Is Gluta Fair Cream safe for pregnant or breastfeeding women?

It’s always best to consult with a healthcare professional before using any skincare product during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. While Gluta Fair Cream is generally safe, individual circumstances may vary.

5. Can I use Gluta Fair Cream with other skincare products?

Yes, Gluta Fair Cream can be incorporated into your existing skincare routine. However, avoid using it with other products that contain strong exfoliants or acids, as this may cause irritation.
