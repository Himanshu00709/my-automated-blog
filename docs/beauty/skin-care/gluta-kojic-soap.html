What is Gluta Kojic Soap?

How to Use Gluta Kojic Soap

To achieve the best results with Gluta Kojic Soap, it is important to use it correctly. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to incorporate this soap into your skincare routine:

  2. Lather the Soap: Rub the Gluta Kojic Soap between your hands to create a rich lather. Alternatively, you can use a loofah or washcloth to generate lather.
