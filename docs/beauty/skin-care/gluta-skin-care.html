What is Glutathione?

Glutathione is a naturally occurring antioxidant found in the human body. It plays a crucial role in protecting cells from damage caused by free radicals, toxins, and oxidative stress. Over time, however, the levels of Glutathione in the body can deplete due to factors like aging, poor diet, and environmental stressors.
