How to Use Gluta-C Soap Intense Whitening

To achieve the best results with Gluta-C Soap Intense Whitening, it is important to use it correctly. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wet Your Skin: Start by wetting your skin with lukewarm water. This helps to open up the pores and prepare the skin for cleansing.
  2. Lather the Soap: Rub the soap between your hands to create a rich lather. Alternatively, you can use a loofah or washcloth to generate more foam.
  3. Apply to Skin: Gently massage the lather onto your skin using circular motions. Pay special attention to areas with dark spots or uneven skin tone.
  4. Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse off the soap with lukewarm water, ensuring that no residue is left on the skin.
  5. Pat Dry: Pat your skin dry with a clean towel. Avoid rubbing, as this can cause irritation.
  6. Moisturize: Follow up with a moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated and to lock in the benefits of the soap.
